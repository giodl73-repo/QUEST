This reference pack paraphrases and condenses SRD content for workshop use. Mechanics are preserved; flavor text is often rewritten to avoid bulk copying. Where a stat block or condition text is reproduced verbatim, it is marked `(SRD verbatim)`.

Content in `reference/dragonlance/` is NOT from the SRD and is NOT under CC-BY-4.0 — it is our own paraphrase of public lore summaries and must not be redistributed as if it were SRD material.
