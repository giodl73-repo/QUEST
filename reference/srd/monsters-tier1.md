# Tier 1 Monsters (CR 0–3)

Twelve creatures appropriate for a party of levels 1-4. Stat blocks summarized from SRD 5.1; mechanics preserved, some flavor paraphrased.

Format: Name (CR, XP) — AC / HP / Speed / key saves & attacks / notable features.

## Skeleton (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- **AC** 13 (armor scraps) · **HP** 13 (2d8+4) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Str** 10 **Dex** 14 **Con** 15 **Int** 6 **Wis** 8 **Cha** 5
- **Damage vulnerabilities** bludgeoning · **Immunities** poison; exhaustion, poisoned
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft · Passive Perception 9
- **Shortsword** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing
- **Shortbow** +4 to hit, 80/320 ft, 1d6+2 piercing

## Zombie (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- **AC** 8 · **HP** 22 (3d8+9) · **Speed** 20 ft.
- **Undead Fortitude:** If damage (not radiant or crit) would reduce it to 0, Con save DC 5 + damage; on success drops to 1 HP instead.
- **Slam** +3 to hit, 1d6+1 bludgeoning.

## Goblin (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- **AC** 15 (leather armor, shield) · **HP** 7 (2d6) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Nimble Escape:** Disengage or Hide as bonus action.
- **Scimitar** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 slashing.
- **Shortbow** +4 to hit, 80/320 ft, 1d6+2 piercing.

## Kobold (CR ⅛, 25 XP)
- **AC** 12 · **HP** 5 (2d6-2) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attacks vs. creature if ally is within 5 ft of it.
- **Sunlight Sensitivity:** Disadvantage on attacks and Perception (sight) in sunlight.
- **Dagger** +4 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing.
- **Sling** +4 to hit, 30/120 ft, 1d4+2 bludgeoning.

## Cultist (CR ⅛, 25 XP)
- **AC** 12 (leather) · **HP** 9 (2d8) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Dark Devotion:** Adv. on saves vs. charmed/frightened.
- **Scimitar** +3 to hit, 1d6+1 slashing.

## Bandit (CR ⅛, 25 XP)
- **AC** 12 (leather) · **HP** 11 (2d8+2) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Scimitar** +3 to hit, 1d6+1 slashing.
- **Light Crossbow** +3 to hit, 80/320 ft, 1d8+1 piercing.

## Wolf (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- **AC** 13 · **HP** 11 (2d8+2) · **Speed** 40 ft.
- **Keen Hearing & Smell:** Advantage on Perception (hearing/smell).
- **Pack Tactics:** Advantage if ally is within 5 ft.
- **Bite** +4 to hit, 2d4+2 piercing; target DC 11 Str save or prone.

## Giant Rat (CR ⅛, 25 XP)
- **AC** 12 · **HP** 7 (2d6) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Keen Smell · Pack Tactics.**
- **Bite** +4 to hit, 1d4+2 piercing.

## Swarm of Rats (CR ¼, 50 XP)
- **AC** 10 · **HP** 24 (7d8-7) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Swarm:** Occupies another creature's space; can move through 1-inch openings. Resistance to bludg/pierce/slash.
- **Bites** +2 to hit (adv. while at ≥ half HP), 2d6 piercing (or 1d6 below half).

## Giant Spider (CR 1, 200 XP)
- **AC** 14 · **HP** 26 (4d10+4) · **Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.
- **Spider Climb · Web Sense · Web Walker.**
- **Bite** +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing + DC 11 Con save or 2d8 poison (half on success); 0 HP from this = stable but poisoned & paralyzed 1h.
- **Web (recharge 5–6):** Ranged +5, 30/60 ft, target restrained by webs (AC 10, HP 5; vulnerable fire, immune bludg/poison/psychic).

## Ghoul (CR 1, 200 XP)
- **AC** 12 · **HP** 22 (5d8) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **Immunities** poison; charmed, exhaustion, poisoned · **Darkvision** 60 ft.
- **Bite** +2 to hit, 2d6+2 piercing.
- **Claws** +4 to hit, 2d4+2 slashing; non-elf humanoid targets: DC 10 Con save or paralyzed 1 min (repeat end of each turn).

## Animated Armor (CR 1, 200 XP)
- **AC** 18 (natural armor) · **HP** 33 (6d8+6) · **Speed** 25 ft.
- **Antimagic Susceptibility:** incapacitated under antimagic; save vs. dispel magic.
- **False Appearance:** indistinguishable from a normal suit of armor while motionless.
- **Multiattack:** 2 slam attacks.
- **Slam** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 bludgeoning.

## Encounter Budget Reference

Tier 1 daily/encounter XP thresholds per character (combine for party total):

| Lvl | Easy | Med | Hard | Deadly |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 25 | 50 | 75 | 100 |
| 2 | 50 | 100 | 150 | 200 |
| 3 | 75 | 150 | 225 | 400 |
| 4 | 125 | 250 | 375 | 500 |

Adjusted XP multipliers for number of monsters:
- 1 monster: × 1
- 2 monsters: × 1.5
- 3-6 monsters: × 2
- 7-10 monsters: × 2.5
- 11-14 monsters: × 3
- 15+ monsters: × 4

For our tier-1 party (4 PCs, level 3): medium threshold ~600 XP (150 × 4), hard ~900, deadly ~1,600.
