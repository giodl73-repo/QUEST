# Factions of Krynn

Seeded for adventure #0001 (Knights of Solamnia focus). Other factions added as adventures demand.

## Knights of Solamnia

Founded by **Vinas Solamnus** c. PC 1060, the Knights are Krynn's oldest chivalric order and Paladine's sword-arm in Ansalon. Three Orders of ascending honor:

### Order of the Crown
Entry order. Values: **Loyalty.** Duties: service to the Knighthood, obedience to superiors, protection of holdings. A Knight of the Crown may be sworn to a specific lord or house; many are soldiery-career knights who never advance further.

### Order of the Sword
Middle order. Values: **Heroic Courage, Heart, Intelligence.** A Knight of the Sword must have distinguished themselves in defense of the weak or in open battle. More independent action expected; often itinerant on Knightly errands.

### Order of the Rose
Highest order. Values: **Wisdom, Justice.** The Rose Knights are the judges and elders of the Knighthood — not simply the strongest fighters but the most measured. Relatively few at any one time. Admission requires proof of wisdom under pressure and the sponsorship of an existing Rose Knight.

### The Measure & the Oath

Knights are bound by two codes:

- **The Oath:** "Est Sularus oth Mithas" — *my honor is my life.* A short oath sworn once.
- **The Measure:** a vast, arcane code of conduct whose full weight no single Knight can recite. Some Knights interpret the Measure strictly (and are paralyzed by it); others pragmatically.

In the Age of Might the Measure is at its most legalistic. Pre-Cataclysm Knights often appear rigid and slow; reformers don't fully take hold until after the War of the Lance.

### For Adventure #0001

Sir Ilendra Vaenor was a **Knight of the Rose** — young for the order (she was admitted by unanimous recommendation after surviving a Third Dragon War engagement that killed her brother). The fact that a Rose Knight bore a secretly-forged relic is scandalous to the Order; the **Order wants the rose quietly returned and buried** rather than publicly acknowledged. That is the archivist's real motivation.

Our archivist NPC (write them up when adventure #0001's NPCs are authored) is a **Knight of the Crown** serving as clerk to the Rose Council — loyal, not powerful, not likely to explain the curse even if he knows.

## Wizards of High Sorcery

Three orders tied to Krynn's moons:

- **White Robes** — Solinari, Good, healing/protection/divination focus.
- **Red Robes** — Lunitari, Neutral, pragmatic/transformative magic.
- **Black Robes** — Nuitari, Evil, necromancy/domination.

All three orders cooperate to regulate magic on Krynn through the **Test of High Sorcery** — a deadly rite every would-be full wizard must undergo. Failing wizards die or lose access to magic.

**For Adventure #0001:** The **hedge-wizard** who refashioned the Silver Rose was NOT a Wizard of High Sorcery. That's part of the curse's problem — it was shaped by grief-magic outside sanctioned practice. Any Wizard of High Sorcery the party shows it to will recoil.
