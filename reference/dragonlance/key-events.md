# Key Events of Krynn — Reference Dating

Not a full Krynn timeline. A reference for the events our adventures cite. Grown on demand.

## Age-Spanning

| Event | Year (PC/AC) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| The Graygem released | pre-recorded | Age of Dreams; scatters the neutral/evil races |
| First Dragon War | ~ PC 2350 | Age of Dreams end; dragons driven back |
| Kingdom of Ergoth rises | Age of Might begins | Human political rise |
| Founding of the Knights of Solamnia | PC 1060 | Vinas Solamnus; the Oath codified |
| Second Dragon War | ~ PC 1025 | Age of Might |
| **Third Dragon War** | **~ PC 500 (start) — PC ~150 (late skirmishes)** | **Age of Might. Long campaign. The specific skirmishes cited in our adventures fall in the LATE phase (~PC 150). This is when Caen Vaenor died.** |
| Founding of Istar | ~ PC 2645 | Age of Might; will grow to dominate by end of age |
| **Sir Ilendra Vaenor's burial at the Rose Cairn** | **PC 100** | (See `workshop-canon.md`.) |
| The Cataclysm | 0 AC (= 0 PC by different notation) | Mountain of fire; Istar shattered; gods withdraw |

## Age of Might dating convention

"Third Dragon War" is a long campaign, not a single event. Our workshop convention: cite battles as **Third Dragon War, ~PC 150** (the late phase) unless otherwise specified. Caen Vaenor's death occurred in this late phase. Ilendra was ~30 when it happened.

## Dating convention used in QUEST adventures

- Dates in adventure files use **PC/AC convention** (PC = Pre-Cataclysm; AC = After Cataclysm).
- **PC 100** = 100 years before the Cataclysm.
- **AC 10** = 10 years after the Cataclysm.
- The Cataclysm itself is the zero point.
- Our adventures currently sit in the **late Age of Might** (PC 500 — PC 1).
