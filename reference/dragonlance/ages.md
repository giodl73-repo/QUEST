# The Ages of Krynn

Krynn's history is divided into five Ages. Dates are relative to the **Cataclysm** (AC 0 — "After Cataclysm" / PC — "Pre-Cataclysm").

## I. Age of Starbirth (before recorded time)

The gods shape creation. Paladine (Good), Gilean (Neutral), and Takhisis (Evil) establish the cosmic balance. The Graygem of Gargath is created, containing Chaos itself.

**Adventure use:** deep-mythic artifacts; pre-history origins for legendary items.

## II. Age of Dreams (c. 9000 – 4000 PC)

The primal races emerge. Ogres rule, then fall. Dwarves build Kal-Thax (later Thorbardin). Elves establish Silvanesti. The gods walk Krynn more openly. First Dragon War. The Graystone escapes, scattering the neutral and evil races.

**Adventure use:** ancient ruins predating human civilization; elven/dwarven lost artifacts.

## III. Age of Might (c. 4000 PC – 0 AC)

The **Empire of Ergoth** rises and falls. **Istar** emerges as the human religious-political power under the Kingpriests. **Three Dragon Wars** shape the landscape; the Knights of Solamnia are founded after the first (by Vinas Solamnus). Magic is regulated under the Wizards of High Sorcery; the three moons (Solinari white / Lunitari red / Nuitari black) patronize the three robes.

Late in this age, the **Kingpriest of Istar** demands godhood. Paladine sends signs; they are ignored. In **AC 0 (technically 0 PC → 0 AC)**, the **Cataclysm** strikes — a burning mountain hurled from the sky shatters Istar into the Blood Sea, buckles coastlines, and the gods withdraw.

**Adventure use:** **THIS IS WHERE `0001-tomb-of-the-silver-rose` IS SET** — mid-late Age of Might, ~100 years PC. The tomb was sealed then; the Cataclysm arrived a century later and did not disturb it (significant).

## IV. Age of Despair (0 AC – 346 AC)

The gods are silent (they withdrew; most mortals think they left). Clerical magic fails. Humanity decays. Istar's survivors become the Seekers. The Dragonarmies of Takhisis invade in the **War of the Lance** (c. 348-352 AC — technically overlapping into the next age). Takhisis is driven back; Paladine and the true gods reveal themselves; clerical magic returns.

**Adventure use:** ruined-civilization aesthetic; Cataclysm rubble; the despair of a gods-forgotten century.

## V. Age of Mortals (c. 383 AC – present)

After the **Chaos War** (the Summer of Chaos, c. 383 AC), the gods again depart. Mortals inherit a magic-altered world. Great Dragons rise as Overlords. Eventually the gods return (War of Souls, ~422 AC).

**Adventure use:** dragon-dominated regions; lost gods; magic-changed landscape.

- `PC 100` = 100 years before Cataclysm
- `AC 10` = 10 years after Cataclysm
