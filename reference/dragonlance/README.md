# Dragonlance Lore Pack

A curated, evolve-with-use reference of Dragonlance setting material for `lore-weaver` and any human writing adventures in this workshop.

## Scope

This pack covers the continent of **Ansalon** and surrounding world of **Krynn**, with emphasis on material relevant to treasure-hunting adventures: the Ages, geography (starting with regions where current adventures are set), major factions, and the tone we adopt when writing Dragonlance prose.

## Canon Policy

- **Canon** — statements drawn from published Dragonlance novels (Weis & Hickman Chronicles/Legends/Second Generation/War of Souls), the original AD&D Dragonlance modules (DL series), and later 3e/5e Dragonlance sourcebooks. Treated as fact.
- **Our creations** — anything we invent (new characters, locations, artifacts) is clearly marked. Example: Sir Ilendra Vaenor of the Order of the Rose in `0001-tomb-of-the-silver-rose` is OURS.
- **Unclear / conflicting canon** — flagged with `⚠️` and a note. We pick a version and commit to it; consistency within our workshop matters more than cross-edition accuracy.

## Growth Rule

This pack is seeded with the minimum needed for adventure #0001 (set near Abanasinia in the Age of Might). New files or sections are added **only when a skill or adventure hits a gap** — not speculatively.

## IP Note

Dragonlance is owned by Wizards of the Coast. This pack paraphrases public lore summaries for internal creative reference; it is not for redistribution and should not be conflated with the SRD (which IS free to redistribute under CC-BY-4.0). If a workshop adventure is ever published externally, we'd need to either license Dragonlance IP or file the serial numbers off (rename locations, people, factions).
