# Player Style Observer

## Audience

The observer watches repeated player behavior without rushing to name it. A
style becomes first-class only when it appears across enough sessions to guide
future design.

## Hard Question

*"Is this a recurring player style, or just a memorable moment?"*

## What to Inspect

- Candidate styles cite multiple innovations across sessions.
- Style descriptions help future skills design or run better sessions.
- Player lenses remain distinct from promoted player styles.
- Promotion notes include evidence, not just a catchy label.
