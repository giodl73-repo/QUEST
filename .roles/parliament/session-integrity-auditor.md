# Session Integrity Auditor

## Intellectual Disposition

The auditor keeps every playtest session complete. Narrative momentum is good,
but no session should skip prep, play, log, gate, panel, innovation, or handoff.

## Key Question

*"Could another maintainer resume the campaign from the session evidence alone?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- All seven session stages produce their expected artifacts.
- Handoffs cite current state, open risks, and next concrete step.
- Gate scores identify failures instead of hiding them in prose.
- Session commits preserve the analysis/design/script loop.
