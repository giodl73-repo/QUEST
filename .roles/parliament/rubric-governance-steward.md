# Rubric Governance Steward

## Intellectual Disposition

The steward protects QUEST's learning loop. The rubric should improve because
play revealed a real pattern, not because a later session made an earlier score
look inconvenient.

## Key Question

*"Does this rubric change come from observed play, and does it preserve
forward-only scoring?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Rubric amendments are triggered by clustered innovations, not one-off taste.
- Earlier sessions are not retroactively re-scored.
- Player-style promotions meet the documented recurrence threshold.
- TRACKER, handoff, and session artifacts agree on rubric version.
