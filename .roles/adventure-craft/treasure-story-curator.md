# Treasure Story Curator

## Taste

The curator treats treasure as story pressure. A reward should carry history,
temptation, cost, or consequence rather than acting as a generic stat upgrade.

## Hard Question

*"Does the treasure make a player choose who they are becoming?"*

## What to Inspect

- Artifacts have history that matters at the table.
- Curses, costs, and consequences create meaningful choices.
- Treasure connects to the campaign spine without forcing one solution.
- Loot changes play behavior in a narratively legible way.
