## Remaining P2 Items

- **Same-author confound** — all 7 campaigns were designed and run by the same author. The paper does not address whether the spine architecture generalizes to other designers. This is the most significant unresolved limitation and should be named clearly in the discussion.
- **Comparison to published campaign frameworks** — the paper does not compare the 7-session spine structure to published campaign design frameworks (e.g., Dungeon World's fronts, Masks' season arc design, or academic campaign-structure work). Recommended for future revision.

## Recommendation

**Accept with minor revisions.**

P1 addressed with credible operationalization. Binary completion indicators plus Route D/E tracking give the circularity concern a workable empirical handle. The same-author confound should be explicitly named in the discussion before submission (currently acknowledged only implicitly). The comparison to published frameworks is a future revision item.
