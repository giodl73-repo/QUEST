## P1 Checklist — Must complete before resubmission

- [ ] **Conduct inter-rater reliability study**
  - Select 10–15 sessions from the 49-session corpus (stratified: early/mid/late, one per campaign)
  - Recruit second scorer; provide rubric documentation only (no session discussion)
  - Second scorer rates each selected session blind on all 8 dimensions
  - Compute Cohen's kappa or Krippendorff's alpha per dimension and overall
  - Add results to methodology section as a new subsection: "Rubric Reliability"
  - Report number, interpret it, and discuss implications

- [ ] **Fallback (if second-scorer study is not feasible in revision window)**
  - Remove or qualify all uses of "research instrument" as a validated category
  - Add explicit acknowledgment in abstract and limitations: rubric is a design artifact and annotation framework; inter-rater validation is stated as future work with specific protocol described
  - Frame contribution as "instrument under development" rather than "validated instrument"

- [ ] **Update abstract to reflect reliability status** (either the result or the acknowledged gap)
