# Round 2 Recheck Synthesis — glyph-player-style

## P1 Recheck: promotion threshold unjustified

**Status: Resolved**

The revision added a three-subsection calibration analysis (§3.2 Threshold Calibration). The analysis includes: retrospective simulation of a 2/2 threshold (would have promoted 3 false-positive styles with fewer than 8 corpus instances); retrospective simulation of a 4/2 threshold (would have delayed ratification of act-without-announcement by 2 campaigns with no improvement in reliability); false-positive analysis showing 0 false positives at 3/2; and a 10% recalibration trigger for future sessions. The 3+ threshold is now justified empirically, not asserted. P1 is resolved.
