# The Border Watch — Campaign 6

## Central question

*When the orders you're following become wrong, how do you know — and who do you tell?*

## Adventures

0036–0042 (7 adventures)

## Theme

7 patrol actions on a Kharolis border route, each one revealing why the orders exist and who benefits.

## Setting

A Solamnic expeditionary unit patrolling the **Irenvale Crossing** — a disputed border pass 40 miles south of Thorngate Keep (same region as C4, different location, workshop-canon). The unit is assigned to "suppress border irregularities." The irregularities are real. What's irregular is who's profiting from them.

## New resource mechanic — Unit Cohesion Die

- Opens at d12 (12-soldier unit at full strength)
- Degrades by one die size after each session where: (a) a unit member is lost in combat, OR (b) the party defies a direct order
- Die result at session start = number of unit soldiers available to call on in combat
- Between-session carry-over: party HP still carries (end HP + 1 hit die), BUT the unit's capability degrades separately
- Research purpose: tests whether resource exhaustion tracking OTHER PEOPLE (not party HP) produces same character-agency amplification as C4's Supply Die

## End conditions

- **Route A** (3+ unit members loyal to party): Present the field journal through official channels; let a tribunal decide
- **Route B** (5+ unit members loyal): Take the evidence directly to a superior who bypasses the corrupt commander; unit structure survives
- **Route E** (all loyalty + field journal complete + Commander Varet's arc fires): Commander Varet presents the journal herself — the party gave her the vocabulary to act; they receive nothing; the unit survives intact

## Hints (4)

- **H1:** The cache is connected to the commander's supply chain (S1–S2)
- **H2:** Command knew before the patrol began (S3)
- **H3:** The unit was chosen because command expected them to fail or disappear (S4–S5)
- **H4:** The commanding officer is being blackmailed, not corrupt — this is the key inversion (S5–S6)

## Research validation targets

- **RV5:** Does resource exhaustion on unit cohesion (tracking other people) produce the same Character Agency amplification as C4's HP carry-over?
- **RV6:** Does documentary-witness emerge from a field journal PC without being grief-designed?
- **RV7:** Does campaign spine work with a duty/obedience central question (vs. grief/loss)?
- **RV8:** Does PC-authored finale deliverable work as a DOCUMENT (field journal) rather than a speech?
