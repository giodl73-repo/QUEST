## RV5 — Depletion-Amplification Verdict

**CONFIRMED.**

Research question: *Does resource exhaustion tracking other people (unit cohesion) produce the same Character Agency amplification as C4's HP carry-over?*

Evidence:
- C6-S1: Cohesion d12, Character Agency 7/10
- C6-S2: Cohesion d12, Agency 8/10
- C6-S3: Cohesion d12→d10, Agency 9/10 (first depletion)
- C6-S4: Cohesion d10, Agency 10/10 (PRIMARY RV5 TEST)
- C6-S5: Cohesion d10, Agency 10/10
- C6-S6: Cohesion d10→d8, Agency 10/10 (hardest combat)
- C6-S7: Cohesion d8, Agency 10/10

Pattern: Agency scores 7→8→9→10→10→10→10. Monotone increase. Depletion tracks inversely with the only variable that could indicate reduced capability.

**The unit losing soldiers made the PCs more precise, not less.** At d12, Varet asked questions and waited. At d10, she wrote down the deviation in real time. At d8, she read the entire journal aloud in the tribunal room without permission.

The mechanism is different from C4's HP carry-over (which tested physical attrition on emotional choices). Cohesion-Die depletion tests *social* attrition — the party becomes smaller in formation, more visible, more exposed. The response to that exposure was specificity of action.

**Extended finding:** The Cohesion Die reached d8 (not d4 as designed in the target spec). The formal objection path in S4 preserved the die by one step. The party's precision of action (filing the formal objection rather than defying outright) meant the depletion ended at d8 rather than d4. This is itself RV5 evidence: the PCs chose the path that preserved the resource because the resource was people.

## RV6 — Documentary-Witness Field Journal Verdict

**CONFIRMED.**

Research question: *Does documentary-witness emerge from a field journal PC without being grief-designed?*

Evidence:
- S1: Journal margin notes open on Day 1. First non-column content: "Key placement: bottom of order packet, under fold." First discrepancy entry added via PC choice (Persuasion parley).
- S2: Margin note "Arren is right" — first relationship-triggered reception event in a duty-anchored document.
- S3: Journal entry written before leading unit back ("Order received. Deviation logged."). Document as act, not record.
- S4: Formal objection derived from journal. Journal spawns a second legal document. Documentary compound.
- S5: "The arc fires." Self-referential professional self-assessment. Journal applies its own rule to itself. Journal IS the mechanism.
- S6: Journal's document sleeve used to carry physical evidence (Ossian letter). Journal designed to carry evidence without knowing what evidence would be.
- S7: Journal read verbatim in tribunal room. Seven entries. Every margin note. The Ossian letter from the sleeve.

**The journal accumulated reception from Day 1 without being designed to.** The margin was for weather notes. It became the reception layer. The discrepancy column was for professional record. It accumulated until it fired the arc.

**Extended finding:** The documentary-witness player-style can emerge from duty-anchored PCs without grief hooks. The journal did not contain grief. It contained observations, correlations, and eventually a self-referential rule. The professional record becoming the emotional record happened because the facts became personal — not because a grief arc was designed in.

## RV7 — Duty-Spine Emotional Density Verdict

**CONFIRMED.**

Research question: *Does a campaign spine built on duty and obedience (rather than grief and loss) produce equivalent emotional density?*

Evidence: Campaign mean gate 74.6/80. Campaign 5 (professional-spine, grief-adjacent): 73.9/80. Campaign 4 (personal/political spine): comparable. Campaign 3 (faith/institutional spine): higher but shorter runtime.

**The central question "when the orders you're following become wrong, how do you know — and who do you tell?" produced equivalent density at every measured arc-beat.** The inversion moment ("the arc fires") fired at session 5 of 7, which is structurally identical to where grief-arc inversions fire in campaigns 1-3.

**Extended finding:** The duty-spine's emotional density is not produced by duty itself, but by the moment when the character's professional framework becomes inadequate. Varet's inversion fired when she applied her own professional rule (discrepancy > outcome ratio) and found it was telling her to act against command. The emotional weight is in the collision of her professional obligation with her institutional loyalty — not in personal loss.

This confirms that the spine type is not the emotional driver. The driver is the collision of two obligations the character cannot hold simultaneously.

## RV8 — Document-as-Deliverable Verdict

**CONFIRMED and EXTENDED.**

Research question: *Does a PC-authored deliverable function as a DOCUMENT (field journal) rather than a speech, producing equivalent or better final-scene effect?*

Evidence: S7 tribunal scene. Varet read the journal without making an argument. She read what she wrote in the field. Derrath could not challenge the content as advocacy because it was not advocacy — it was dated field records.

**The document produced MORE effect than a speech would have, specifically because it was evidence rather than advocacy.** A speech composed for the tribunal could be challenged on its logic. The field journal's logic was not composed for the tribunal — it was composed in the field, one entry at a time, starting on Day 1 before the pattern was visible. Its credibility was its provenance.

**Extended finding:** The RV8 finding is specific to institutional contexts (tribunals, formal reviews, official proceedings). In a non-institutional context, a speech might produce equivalent effect because the authority mechanisms are different. The field journal's advantage was not the document-form itself but the documentary immunity that provenance creates: you cannot argue with a date.

## What to carry to C7

- **Duty-spine works.** The collision of two obligations (professional + institutional) produces equivalent emotional density to grief-collision. C7 will test: what happens when there are NO designed obligations?
- **The documentary-witness style can emerge without grief hooks.** The journal worked because Varet was the kind of character who writes things down. Not because she lost someone. Watch for this in C7's strangers — none of them have designed practices.
- **Depletion-amplification holds for social resources (cohesion die).** Not just physical resources (HP/Supply Die). C7 has no resource mechanic; this is deliberate — testing whether character agency without any depletion pressure produces the same scores.
- **The Cohesion Die worked because it tracked people, not abstractions.** When Carra went down, the die degraded and Dorn's formation-announcement was the line of the session. The resource was made of characters. That is what made it matter.
- **Formal-objection path preserved a cohesion step.** Design implication for C7: paths that preserve the resource while achieving the goal are better than paths that spend the resource to achieve the goal. The unit arrived at Route E at d8, not d4, because Dorn's clause-reading + Varet's formal-objection preserved the Cohesion Die when outright defiance would have degraded it.
