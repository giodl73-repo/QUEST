## Adventure briefs

### #0001 — Tomb of the Silver Rose *(played S01)*

**Artifact:** Silver Rose of Vaenor (Fear). **Spotlight:** party formation.
**Seeds planted:** Mira Vaenshold-Silversmith; Wena Laen; Nenn Hillfoot; Duren Coalbeard's death context; Vethrenn's warded margin; **Hint 1 (Fresco-Heal)**; Jannet of Crownhold.
**Seeds retrieved:** none (first adventure).
**Outcome:** rose returned to Council; cross-adventure phrase *"I will not look at the rose again"* embedded in Vesk's memory fragment.

### #0002 — The Bone-Collector Trap *(played S02)*

**Artifact:** Varran's Reliquary (Grief). **Spotlight:** Aelric.
**Seeds planted:** Thane Krun Ironhaft *(retroactive — plant in #0003 or #0004)*; Lady Tiran *(pending plant)*; Second moon-silver smith-family *(pending plant #0006)*; Keloth's vault-ledger contains the cross-adventure phrase *"I will not look at the rose again"* (signal missed on dice — recovery via Kessa's notebook).
**Seeds retrieved:** Pella; Bone-Collectors; Brother Laen's sister Wena (deepened).
**Outcome:** Aelric attuned — Varran's Minute preserved; all other Varran-memories permanently consumed. Campaign-permanent fact. Mira-protection contract formal with Rose Council.

### #0003 — The Silversmith's Mirror

**Setting:** Crystalmir-by-the-Fells, Abanasinian hill-town ~3 days' ride from Varduin.
**Hook:** Rose Council's protection contract for Mira. Bone-Collector remnants are closing.
**Artifact (emotion):** Aelwen's Mirror (**Love**) — shows holder their face as the person who loves them most sees them; cost permanent self-image replacement.
**Spotlight:** **Kessa** (Vethrenn retrieves). The Mirror is Aelwen's last work; Kessa's notebook and Vethrenn's commonplace book are the key to understanding it.
**Seeds retrieved:** Mira Vaenshold-Silversmith (S01); Vethrenn-Aelwen connection (recovery path for S02's missed cross-adventure signal — Mira's shop has a receipt-book with the phrase uncredited).
**Seeds to plant:** Thane Krun Ironhaft (mentioned in Mira's records as a buyer of moon-silver ingots — sets up #0004); Lady Tiran of the Purple Rose (mentioned as a Crystalmir visitor who came for Mira's wares); **Hint 2 target confirmation** (Kessa's warded margin-note is now *nearly* unlocked — the ward weakens in Aelwen's shop proximity but does not break until Grom's Reorx-rite in #0004).
**Hints:** setup for Hint 2 (near-unlock only). Hint 1 recovery available (party may re-discuss the Fresco-Heal memory).
**Open questions:** does Mira live at adventure end? Does the party take the Mirror, destroy it, leave it with Mira, or return it to the Council?

### #0004 — The Collapse at Gavrek's Anvil

**Setting:** Gavrek's Anvil, outer hold of Thorbardin, Kharolis foothills. 4 days' ride south of Varduin.
**Hook:** A forge-priest colleague of Duren's writes — Duren's grave has been disturbed.
**Artifact (emotion):** The Wrath-Coin (**Rage**) — closed-fist focus on a named enemy; cost: inability to forgive that enemy.
**Spotlight:** **Grom.**
**Seeds retrieved:** Duren Coalbeard's death (S01 Grom sheet); Thane Krun Ironhaft (from #0003 planting).
**Seeds to plant:** the Kharolis-to-Palanthas silver trade route (setup for #0005 Istar corridor); an old Solamnic dwarf named Yorin who knew Duren (possible future hook).
**Hints:** **Hint 2 — Vethrenn's warded margin-note UNLOCKS.** The Reorx-rite for Duren has enough cosmic weight to break the elven ward. Kessa reads Vethrenn's hypothesis: *"We made the rose to hold. We did not try to release. I think release is possible. I do not know how."*
**Open questions:** Thane Krun Ironhaft's fate (die this session? survive? post-campaign arc?).

### #0005 — The Kingpriest's Circlet

**Setting:** A waystation outside Istar. ~10 days' journey east. Party does not enter Istar proper.
**Hook:** Wena has left her Paladine cloister; joined an Istaran faction. Laen asks retrieval.
**Artifact (emotion):** The Pride-Circlet (**Pride**) — perceive self as greatest admirer sees you; cost: no self-surprise.
**Spotlight:** Institutional (Brother Laen arc delivers at his family's cost) + **Aelric secondary** (a Knight of the Crown confronting Istaran pride).
**Seeds retrieved:** Wena Laen (S01); Lady Tiran of the Purple Rose (from #0003 planting).
**Seeds to plant:** an Istaran named scribe or clerk (a minor witness to the circlet's use — seed for a potential future campaign set at the Cataclysm's approach).
**Hints:** none scheduled here.
**Open questions:** does Wena return to her cloister? Does Lady Tiran survive (circlet-overwear will kill her regardless)?

### #0006 — The Thread at the North Market

**Setting:** A trading-post at the northern edge of Abanasinia. 5 days' ride.
**Hook:** A letter arrives at Varduin for Thera — in Nenn Hillfoot's hand.
**Artifact (emotion):** The Thread-That-Frays (**Longing**) — glimpse of future where named absent has returned; cost: present less real.
**Spotlight:** **Thera.**
**Seeds retrieved:** Nenn Hillfoot (S01 Thera sheet); Adda Hillfoot (S01 Thera grief); the letter-to-Nenn Thera has carried six years (revealed as Adda's-hand-to-Nenn).
**Seeds to plant:** the Second moon-silver smith-family — Nenn commissioned his Thread from a **non-Vaenshold smith-family**. This matters at the finale (#0007). Name the family; place them geographically (an Abanasinian hill-village separate from Varduin and Vaenshold). Seed only; no NPC visit this session.
**Hints:** none scheduled here.
**Open questions:** Does Thera meet Nenn as Nenn, or can she only meet him as the Thread-bound ghost? What does she do with the Thread?

### #0007 — The Vaenshold Vein *(FINALE)*

**Setting:** The Vaenshold moon-silver vein, beneath the abandoned Vaenshold estate. 1 day's ride from Varduin.
**Hook:** Kessa's research (across all prior adventures) points here. The party arrives to confront what they've learned.
**Artifact (emotion):** Aelwen's Silver-Ingot Confession (**Shame**) — not attuned; read. Carries Aelwen's inscription.
**Spotlight:** all four PCs.
**Seeds retrieved:** the Vaenshold line; Aelwen's workshop; the second moon-silver smith-family (from #0006); campaign-level all-active seeds.
**Seeds to plant:** post-campaign hooks: Pella at 14, Jannet of Crownhold, the Cataclysm 15 years away.
**Hints:** **Hint 4 — Aelwen's Confession inscription delivered.** Passive-Perception DC 15 fallback auto-catches for any PC with passive 15+.
**Structure:** approach → Aelwen's above-ground workshop → descent → vein chamber → Confession → A/C/D decision.
**Finale branches:** see spec Section 4.

## Finale branches

### D — Inversion (optimal)
Requires: ≥ 2 of 3 hints caught (Hint 1 already caught at S01; Hint 2 at #0004 if Kessa unlocks; Hint 4 at #0007 auto with passive-Perception 15).
Cost: inverter (Kessa most likely) loses capacity to perform moon-silver magic forever.
Restoration: prior bearers (Aelwen, Baron Edric, Ilendra, anonymous dead). NOT current party (Aelric's Varran-memories remain gone).

### A — Charter (fallback)
Requires: ≥ 1 shelf-reliquary intact + Brother Laen alive + a PC argues for regulation.
Cost: party becomes ongoing Council agents.

### C — Destruction (default)
Requires: nothing. Grom's Reorx-rite at scale. Clean.
