# Relic Signs Manifest — Velantha's Healer's Journal

At least **3 of the following** distributed across **≥2 rooms**:

## Required (≥3, ≥2 rooms)

- [ ] **Absence sign:** The Crypt is the only room in the building with no smell from the congregation's practices. No herbs, no tallow, no ink. After five sessions of navigating the building's smell-map, the absence is the sign. The congregation kept this room clean for thirty years. The journal does not smell of anything except old leather. *DC 10 Perception to notice the smell absence from the entry passage.*

- [ ] **Sourceless light sign:** The congregation placed a smooth stone at the center of the Crypt that emits a faint, steady glow. No fire, no oil, no magic that the party can identify. DC 13 Arcana to attempt to identify the source — the check fails; the stone simply glows. This is how the congregation lit the crypt for thirty years without knowing how to explain it. *Relic sign: the crypt was maintained without fully understanding how.*

- [ ] **Journal warmth sign:** The journal is warm on the shelf — slightly warmer than the room, which is cool. *DC 10 Perception from within 5 ft.* Different from the field satchel's warmth (congregation veneration) and the measuring cup's warmth (correct dosing). This warmth is the warmth of something that has been waiting to be received.

- [ ] **Final entry rewrite sign (H4):** When a qualifying reader reads the final entry, it rewrites. *Passive 15 auto-detects; DC 15 for lower passive.* Maret (passive 17) is the designed auto-reader. The rewrite is the session's H4 delivery.

- [ ] **Congregation completion sign:** The Crypt is finished. The vaulting is complete. The stone is dressed. The proportions are correct for a Mishakal crypt. A character with DC 12 History or Religion recognizes: this room was finished by people who did not have architectural training but who had decades of determination. The finish is not expert but it is thorough. *The relic sign here is the relationship between the completed room and the unfinished building around it.*

## Required (always)

- [ ] **Journal present and observable before it is read.** The party should see the journal on the shelf before Velantha gives it to Calla.

- [ ] **Velantha's presence is felt before she speaks.** The crypt should have one beat of awareness (the light, the warmth, the way the sourceless glow responds slightly to movement) before Velantha speaks directly. She is here; she has been here; the party should know it before she says so.
