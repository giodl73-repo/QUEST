## Scene 6.2 — The Crypt (R3): Velantha's Account

Velantha: *"You came far enough. I wasn't sure anyone would."*

**Cold Pulse 10min-1** — `1d6` → rolls=[5] **total=5** · Renn's warm copper coin is the warmest it has ever been. Three unauthorized acts in one space: the coin (his pact), the letter (Aldric's authorization), the ritual focus. He notes this without comment.

The party asks about what happened (doubt-die for arc-question = 1 → act-first). Velantha answers completely: the fever, the working, the cost, the overextension. She confirms Aldric's account with one correction: *"He softened one thing. He couldn't bring himself to write that I was glad. I wasn't sorry about the working. I was sorry about the silence. Those are not the same."*

Then she speaks about the cost:

*"I reached past what my body could sustain. It was a choice I made knowing what I was spending. The working I used was from the Orders' restricted library. I accessed it through a forged authorization. I spent myself past sustainable, and I did it for people the Orders had decided were not worth the expenditure."*

**🎲 Renn doubt-die (pact line aloud)** — `1d6` → rolls=[4] **total=4** · 4-6 = he speaks.

Renn Ashkeld says, to the room, to the party, to Velantha:

**"She used something that wasn't prayer. I know what that costs."**

The party hears this. Calla hears this. Maret hears this. Dren hears this.

Velantha looks at Renn. *"What was yours?"*

*He tells her. He tells all of them: a collapsed Istaran wayhouse, a transaction with something ancient and unnamed, the power he received and the sister he could not save with it. He says it in three sentences. He has been carrying it for four years. He says it in three sentences in a crypt in front of a woman who did the same category of thing and did not survive it.*

Calla does not ask for clarification. She holds the new observation the way she has held all the others.
