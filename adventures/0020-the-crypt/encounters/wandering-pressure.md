# Wandering Pressure — The Crypt

*Roll Cold Pulse (d6) every 10 min. Roll Wandering Encounter (d6) every 30 min.*

## Cold Pulse (d6, every 10 min)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | The sourceless glow in R3 brightens slightly when a party member enters a new room. Velantha is aware of where everyone is. |
| 2 | From R5: the faint sound of someone placing an object on the offering wall. A congregation member has come to leave something. They do not enter R3. |
| 3 | Cold pulse in the approach (R1) — the temperature drops another degree. |
| 4 | Velantha is quiet for a moment in the middle of speaking. She is listening to something. Then she continues. |
| 5 | From R6's far wall, very faint: someone is reading the inscription aloud in the passage outside. The congregation sends visitors occasionally. |
| 6 | The dried herb in R4 releases its scent briefly — cold remedy, thirty years old, still faintly identifiable. Calla (Nature 13+) or Maret (passive Perception 17) recognize it. |

## Wandering Encounter (d6, every 30 min)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Aldric's new holder.** He appears briefly at R2's vestibule with a fresh ceramic holder. He looks at the party once. He nods. He goes back to the sacristy. The holder will be on the shelf when the party returns. |
| 2 | A congregation member enters the crypt from R2 — come to visit. They stop when they see the party. Velantha says: *"Let them pass."* The congregation member leaves an offering in R5 and goes. |
| 3 | **Doran's final message.** A nave congregation member appears at R2's vestibule with a note. *"Tomorrow morning. I am coming to the building myself."* Doran is done waiting. The party has until morning to decide what to tell her. |
| 4 | Velantha says, unprompted: *"The building doesn't care what it's called. A cathedral or a home. It's stone. The stone doesn't choose. The people do."* |
| 5 | Renn's warm copper coin is the warmest it has ever been. The crypt, the journal, and the letter (if the party has it) are all in proximity. Three unauthorized acts in one space. The coin recognizes the convergence. |
| 6 | The party has been in the crypt for 30 minutes. Velantha says: *"You should go soon. The building needs you above."* She does not mean it as pressure. She means the finale is at hand. |
