# Encounter — Velantha's Account

## Type

Social encounter (extended). Velantha speaks. The party responds. Hint 4 delivered via journal. Arc-completion on "what do you want." Calla receives the journal.

## Velantha's Opening

*"You came far enough. I wasn't sure anyone would."*

She waits. She lets the party speak first.

## Her Account (when asked what happened)

*"I came because they were sick. The same fever as the town. Nobody else came. I had a working that might help. I used it. It worked. I died — not from the working, exactly. From what the working cost. That's the distinction I want you to understand. The Orders have a word for it: an overextension. I reached past what my body could sustain. It was my choice. I made it with full information."*

## On the Promise

*"I promised some of them I would come back. I believed it when I said it. I died instead. I am sorry for that. The healing was real. I cannot apologize for the healing."*

## On the Silence

*"I should have told them it was sorcery. I should have told them gully dwarves are worth saving. I didn't, because I thought a miracle would be more useful than the truth. I was wrong about that. I was right about everything else."*

(This line appears in the journal. She says it here directly as well.)

## On Aldric

*"He was a coward about the Orders and a brave person about gully dwarves. Both things are true. He has been in that room for thirty years making copies of an account he partially understands. That is also a form of keeping faith."*

## Renn's Entry-Point

After her account of the cost:

*"I reached past what my body could sustain. It was a choice I made knowing what I was spending."*

**Renn's doubt-die (pre-rolled in S6 session)** determines whether he says the line aloud or via Awakened Mind or not at all.

If aloud: *"She used something that wasn't prayer. I know what that costs."*

Velantha: *"Yes."* She looks at him for a moment. *"The working I used was from the Orders' restricted library. I accessed it through a forged authorization. What was yours?"*

This is the open door to Renn's disclosure to the party. Whether he walks through: his choice.

## Arc-Completion Gate

Party asks: *"What do you want?"*

**Velantha's arc-completion:**

*"I want the gully dwarves to have somewhere that is theirs. They kept faith for thirty years in a building they didn't build. They deserve the building."*

Pause.

*"I don't care what the town says. I don't care what the Orders say. The faith is real. The building should be theirs."*

She is done. She looks at Calla.

*"You've been carrying both accounts. You know what they don't say."*

She holds out the journal.

*"Someone should speak for what I actually did. Not what they've built up there. What I did. Someone should speak it aloud, in front of the people who need to hear it. Can you do that?"*

**Calla's structural invitation.** The PC-authored finale deliverable is now formally activated.

## Seeds Retrieved

- **aldrics-written-copies:** *"He softened one thing. He couldn't bring himself to write that I was glad. I wasn't sorry about the working. I was sorry about the silence. Those are not the same."*

## Route Implications

- **Route D:** Arc-completion fires; Calla receives journal with the invitation; party proceeds to 0021 with Velantha's explicit request and the PC-authored deliverable formally active.
- **Route C:** Party asks about the ritual but not the want; journal given without invitation; deliverable not formally activated (Calla still has the material but wasn't asked directly).
- **Route A:** Party receives information, takes journal, proceeds without the arc-completion or deliverable activation.
