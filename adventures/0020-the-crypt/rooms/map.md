# Map — The Crypt

## ASCII Map

```
[0019 R6] ──[R1: APPROACH]──[R2: VESTIBULE]──[R3: THE CRYPT (finished)]
                                                    │              │
                                               [R4: JOURNAL]  [R5: OFFERINGS]
                                                              └──[R6: INSCRIPTION]──(back to R2)
```

## Connections

| From | To | Type | Condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0019 R6 | R1 | Passage | Always |
| R1 | R2 | Step down (~3 ft) | Always; one step down into the finished section |
| R2 | R3 | Open arch | Always; the finished crypt is visible from R2 |
| R3 | R4 | Side alcove | Always; DC 8 Perception from R3 to notice the alcove |
| R3 | R5 | East side passage | Always |
| R5 | R6 | Continuing passage | Always |
| R6 | R2 | Loop back | Always; the loop through R4-R5-R6 connects back |

## Notes

The step down into R2 (from the unfinished section to the finished) is the only vertical element. The Crypt is a small space — all rooms are within earshot of each other. Velantha's presence is felt throughout but she speaks from R3.
