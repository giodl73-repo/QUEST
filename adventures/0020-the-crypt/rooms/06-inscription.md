# Room 06 — The Far Inscription

## Read-aloud

> The passage ends at a wall. Carved into it, carefully, by someone who was not a stonecutter: a single sentence in Common. The carving took years — you can see where the letters were corrected, where the tool slipped and was corrected. "SHE CAME WHEN WE ASKED."

*(49 words)*

## Features

- **The inscription** (carved sentence). *"SHE CAME WHEN WE ASKED."* Six words. The congregation's official statement of what happened. Not "she performed a miracle." Not "she saved us." Not "she gave her life." Just: she came. When we asked. **DC 10 History** to note: this is the most accurate characterization of Velantha's act in the building. The congregation, who know the full truth (they felt what was not prayer), chose these six words as the building's only inscription.

- **The carving quality** (evidence of long effort). The letters were corrected multiple times. This took years — probably five to ten years of periodic work by someone who was learning while carving. The final result is clear and readable. **DC 12 Investigation** to note the correction marks and estimate duration.

- **Loop connection** (passage west returns to R2 vestibule).

## Encounter

None.

**Dren's beat:** He reads the inscription. He looks at it for a moment. *"She came when we asked."* He says it quietly. He has Aldis's insignia in his left breast pocket. He has been carrying the question of what it means to come when asked. He does not say anything further. He turns and goes back through the passage.

## GM Notes

This room is the campaign's shortest emotional beat. Six words. The congregation's official record. The party has now seen what the congregation produced in thirty years: a complete crypt, a wall of offerings, and six words on the far wall. That is what Velantha's act looks like from inside.

### Memory Fragment
*The congregation debated the inscription for three years. Some wanted more. Most wanted exactly what they wrote. They couldn't agree on anything more than six words that were true. "She came when we asked." They agreed on those.*
