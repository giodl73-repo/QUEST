# Room 03 — The Crypt

## Read-aloud

> Clean vaulting. Dressed stone. A smooth stone at the center emitting a steady, sourceless glow. Correctly-laid floor. Correctly-proportioned niches in the walls, each one carefully shaped and smoothed. A Mishakal crypt as it should be — by people who had never built one before. A woman's figure near the north wall. She says: "You came far enough. I wasn't sure anyone would."

## Features

- **Velantha** (north wall). See `npcs/velantha.md`. She is present. She speaks.

- **Sourceless glow stone** (center). Smooth, warm to the touch (DC 8 Perception from the entrance — Maret passive 17 auto-detects). **DC 13 Arcana to identify source:** check fails — no source identifiable. The congregation placed it here; they do not know where the glow came from. It has been glowing steadily for thirty years. *Relic sign: sourceless light.*

- **Correctly-proportioned niches** (walls). **DC 12 Religion** to confirm: these are laid out correctly for a Mishakal crypt — the proportions, the orientations, the number of niches. The congregation got it right without formal instruction. They consulted something — possibly a scrap of liturgical text, possibly pure determination. *Relic sign: congregation completion sign.*

- **Side alcove** (east wall, slightly hidden). **DC 8 Perception** to notice. Connects to R4 (Journal). The journal is visible on the shelf from the alcove entrance.

## GM Notes

This is the campaign's penultimate room. Every adventure has been working toward this conversation. Keep it simple: Velantha speaks, the party responds, Velantha asks one question back (she will say "Before you go — what are you going to do?"), and the party's answer shapes the session's close.

**Do not rush the arc-completion.** The question "what do you want?" may not come immediately. Let Velantha deliver her account. Let the party ask about the promise, about Aldric, about the congregation. The arc-completion comes when the party has received everything else and finally asks the right question.
