# Room 01 — Approach

## Read-aloud

> The passage from the Sacristy continues. No congregation marks on these walls. No ink smell, no tallow, no herbs. Clean stone, and ahead: a step down and light. The light is steady and sourceless.

*(35 words)*

## Features

- **No smell** (immediate). After five sessions navigating the building's smell-map, the absence of congregation smells is a relic sign. **DC 10 Perception** to note the absence. *This room has been kept clean.*
- **Step down** (~3 ft) at R2 entry. The finished crypt is lower than the rest of the building — the congregation excavated properly.

## Connections

- **West:** 0019 R6
- **East (step down):** R2 (Vestibule)

## GM Notes

**Cold entry (resource cost):** As the party steps into the approach, the temperature drops noticeably. **DC 8 Con save** as they descend the step. On a failure: 1d4 cold damage. This is the session's designed resource cost — minor, not dangerous, but physical. The Crypt is cool. The party must choose to be here.

### Memory Fragment
*She walked this passage twice in her life. The second time she was being carried. She did not know it was the second time.*
