# Room 02 — Vestibule

## Read-aloud

> A small antechamber. Unfinished — this section is still the congregation's work-in-progress. Through the arch ahead: the finished crypt, clean stone, correct vaulting. The light from inside is steady. Someone is present. You can feel it before you see anything.

*(43 words)*

## Features

- **Velantha's presence** (felt here before she speaks). The sourceless light from R3 reaches this room. The way it moves when a party member steps forward — very slightly, as if noticing — is the first signal that the crypt is inhabited. **No roll needed.** The presence is perceptible.
- **Unfinished walls** (R2 vs R3 contrast). R2 is still rough stone; R3 through the arch is dressed stone, correct proportions. The contrast is immediate. The congregation spent thirty years finishing the one room they could finish.

## Connections

- **West (step up):** R1 (Approach)
- **East (arch):** R3 (The Crypt)
- **South (loop back from R6):** R6 (Inscription) — *loop completion*

## GM Notes

R2 is the threshold. The party should take one breath here before entering R3. Velantha does not speak yet — she is present but waiting for them to come fully in.
