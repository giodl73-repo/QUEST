# Temporal Pressure — Stonfold Holding Variant

The session spans **road approach (Day 1-3) → Stonfold holding (Day 3-4)**. The holding is a working smith-family compound; pressure is craft-rhythm, not dungeon-pace.

## Use

- **Road Pulse** d6 once per day of travel (Days 1-3); atmospheric.
- **Holding Pulse** d6 every **4 hours** inside Stonfold (Day 3 afternoon through Day 4 morning); craft-rhythmic.
- Both tables stop during Scene 06 (the Resolution).

## Road Pulse Table (d6 per day of travel)

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** Hill-road sounds. |
| 2 | **A Stonfold ore-wagon** passes heading to Crystalmir markets. The driver nods at the party; the wagon smells of Stonfold silver (cool, faintly blue). Grom passive 13: different from Vaenshold work. |
| 3 | **A pilgrim** from Varduin-direction — a halfling woman, 40s, recognizes Thera's last name when she introduces herself. *"Hillfoot? From Varduin? I knew a Hillfoot. A man. Gate-tender type. Said he had family he hadn't seen."* She does not know the connection. |
| 4 | **Nothing.** Hill-road sounds. |
| 5 | **The collector's advance scout** (if Scene 01 collector survived and chose to send ahead). A single hired human, watching the road. Thera passive 13: noting the party's direction. No engagement. |
| 6 | **A letter from Brother Laen** delivered by a Solamnic courier on his way north. Confirms the Council's referral to Tova Stonfold. Also: *"The Second House is still active. Felor has a new client. Be careful with the spool."* |

## Holding Pulse Table (d6 per 4 hours inside Stonfold)

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** Forge-sounds. The smell of Stonfold silver. |
| 2 | **Harel approaches Thera** (if Thera is in the courtyard): *"You came. Mira said you might."* Then: *"Is it true you fight?"* Not unfriendly; curious. She is 16; she is asking a real question. |
| 3 | **Grom and Tova forge comparison.** Tova allows Grom to examine her primary forge-work. He runs his thumb along the inside curve of a piece she is shaping. It is honest work. They do not speak for twenty minutes. This is compatible conversation. |
| 4 | **The spool vibrates louder.** Any PC near the archive building: Holding Pulse 4 fires when Nenn passes the archive on his patrol route. The spool vibrates behind the locked archive door. Passive Perception 12: audible through the stone. |
| 5 | **Mira's forge-question.** Mira approaches Kessa specifically: *"The Wrath-Coin. It was destroyed? Entirely?"* She is calculating something. She does not explain. **Seed:** Mira has been thinking about all six artifacts and what the loss of two means for the finale. |
| 6 | **Tova's records offer.** Tova brings Kessa a specific archive record — the third-vein reference — without being asked. *"I thought you might want this. Aelwen wrote about a cold vein. I do not know what it means for you."* This is path-c delivery of the third-vein seed; accelerates Scene 04 if already fired or provides pre-Scene-04 context. |
