# Scene 07 — Aftermath (Stonfold, Day 4 morning)

**Location:** Stonfold courtyard and gate. **Time:** dawn Day 4.

**Read-aloud:**

> Stonfold in the morning: the forges are lit. The Stonfold-family children are doing morning chores that look the same as Varduin's morning chores, because chores look the same everywhere. Mira is at her smaller forge already, and Harel is beside her, and Harel is seventeen months taller than Thera last measured. Tova Stonfold is at the archive door, watching the party prepare to leave. Nenn Hillfoot is at the gate.

**Features:**

- **Tova's final word:** she hands Kessa a leather folder of copied archive records — the third-vein reference, the Aelwen-invoice, and a letter of provenance for the spool's disposition. *"Your Wizard of High Sorcery will want these. I kept originals."* Then, to Grom: *"The Reorx-rite was honest work. Thank you."* Then she goes inside.
- **Mira's cameo:** she walks across the courtyard to speak to the party before they leave. To Thera specifically: *"I did not know he was here when you arrived. Tova told me not to tell you. She thought — she thought you should find it yourself."* She shakes her head slightly; she is not sure Tova was right; she is not sure she was wrong. To Grom: *"The forge feels cleaner."* She goes back to work.
- **Nenn at the gate:** he opens it for them. When Thera passes through, he says: *"If you come back through Stonfold—"* He does not finish the sentence. She may choose to answer it.
- **Harel's growth:** 16 now; she has grown in the three months since S03. She is carrying two forge-tools Mira taught her to use. She looks at the party with the polite confidence of someone who no longer needs rescuing from anyone.
