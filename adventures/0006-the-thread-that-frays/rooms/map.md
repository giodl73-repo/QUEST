## DM notes

- **The holding is a working compound.** 20-30 Stonfold-family members present; children, apprentices, forge-workers. Not hostile; not welcoming; professional.
- **The archive is the only locked building.** Everything else is accessible on request.
- **Rubric v1.4 combat:** Scene 01 road ambush is the session's required dice-weight encounter. Scenes 02-07 are social/atmospheric.
- **Stonfold geography:** 80 miles east of Crystalmir; 3 days from Korrath. The holding is defensible but not fortified; it relies on being remote rather than walled.
- **Mira's presence** is a cameo, not a plot. She is here because she lives here now. Let her feel settled.
