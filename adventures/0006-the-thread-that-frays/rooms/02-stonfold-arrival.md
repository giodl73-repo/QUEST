# Scene 02 — Stonfold Holding, Arrival

**Location:** Stonfold gate and courtyard. **Time:** late afternoon Day 3.

**Read-aloud:**

> Stonfold is not a town. It is a holding — a walled compound of stone longhouses and forge-halls built into the slope of a hill that has a moon-silver vein running through its eastern face. The wall is functional, not beautiful. The gate is iron-banded oak. At the gate: a human man, late fifties, sun-wheat hair going silver, spinning a too-large ring on his left thumb. He is watching the road. When the party comes into view, he stops spinning the ring. He looks at Thera. He says one word. He says: *"Thera."*

**Features:**

- **Nenn at the gate.** He does not move toward her. He does not move away. He waits.
- **Tova Stonfold** steps out from behind Nenn within thirty seconds of the party's arrival. She has been watching. She looks at Grom. Says: *"Forge-priest. The spool will be yours to deal with. Come inside."* Does not wait.
- **The courtyard.** Functional. Three forge-halls (the smell: hot metal, pine-resin, something cooler and blue that is the Stonfold-vein silver's specific scent). A well. A stone building that is probably the archive. Children watching from a second-floor window.
- **Mira Vaenshold** — visible at the far end of the courtyard, working at a smaller forge with Harel. She looks up when the party arrives. Harel waves. Mira nods once. She does not approach immediately; she is mid-work.

**Encounter:** None. This is an arrival scene with one emotional beat at maximum compression.

**Connections:** gate → [3] Nenn-Thera confrontation space; courtyard → [4] archive; courtyard → Mira and Harel cameo.

**GM Notes:**

- **Nenn at the gate is the session's first anchor symptom.** He is not introduced; he is *seen*. Thera recognizes him before the DM names him, if Thera's player is paying attention. Give the player a moment.
- **Do not rush Scene 02 → Scene 03.** The read-aloud ends with *"Thera."* The next beat is Thera's. Silence is correct here.
- **Tova's redirect.** She pulls the party forward deliberately. She is not separating Nenn and Thera — she is giving them space to be separated on their own terms, by routing the rest of the party inside.
- **Mira's cameo.** She should feel like she belongs here. She is not broken; she is working. Harel is taller. Mira's leather apron is the same worn one from S03. This is continuity, not drama.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Thera has rehearsed this meeting seventeen times in her head, in the eight months since Wena told her Nenn might be at Stonfold. Each rehearsal ended differently. In none of them did the first word come from him. In none of them was the first word her name.*
