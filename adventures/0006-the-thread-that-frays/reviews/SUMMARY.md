## Aggregate verdict

**Shippable with P0 fixes.** Three reviewers (Gygax, Schick, Moldvay) score at or above 65; Jaquays (60.2) and Stuart (64.6) want one more pass. The architecture is sound — the Thread is universally praised as the best artifact in the cycle. The gap is in *emotional scaffolding*: Nenn is invisible before Scene 03; Scene 06 doesn't give him enough to do; there is no safety net if the table drifts toward logistics.

All P0 fixes are additive (1-2 lines per location) — no structural changes needed.

## Strongest axes

1. **Curse/consequence (9.2)** — Thread-loss mechanic praised by all 5. Stuart: "the Thread is haunted." Schick: "passes the Blackrazor test."
2. **Treasure-as-story (9.2)** — Aelwen's presence in the Thread is fully felt. The artifact is the story.
3. **Premise clarity (8.8)** — "father and daughter at a gate; both longing for someone dead; the artifact shows what you long for." One sentence.
4. **Player agency (8.2)** — Four overlapping decisions; Option C for Thera; three genuine resolution paths.

## Weakest axes

1. **Dungeon integrity (5.4)** — All reviewers note linear topology. Jaquays: "a corridor with parallel doors." Gygax: "no loop, no shortcut."
2. **Encounter craft (5.4)** — One required combat (road ambush) + 6 social scenes. Stuart gives 3/10. Schick: "parlor scene, not a heist."
3. **Table-readiness (6.4)** — Missing DM cheatsheet (Moldvay). Scene 03 and 06 require active table trust with no mechanical fallback (Stuart). Forge-temperature under-specified (Moldvay).

## Convergent P0 fixes (3+ reviewers)

1. **Plant Nenn before Scene 03** (Stuart + Schick + Moldvay). In Scene 02: Mira mentions "a quiet man, good with gates, been here eight months — Nenn." Tova should reference the spool vibrating for someone in the holding before the party meets Nenn. This earns Scene 03's silence.

2. **Nenn needs one active moment in Scene 06** (Gygax + Schick + Stuart). Currently: "he is at the door. He was not invited." If Thera doesn't initiate, Nenn has nothing to do. Add: if the party moves to leave without resolution, Nenn steps into the doorway: *"She should decide first."* One line; no force; just re-centers attention.

3. **Safety net against logistics-drift** (Stuart + Moldvay). If the party ignores Thera/Nenn and reaches for the Thread as a transaction, no NPC intercedes. Add: Tova will not hand over the Thread unless Thera has spoken to Nenn — she names this condition explicitly when the party first asks for the Thread.

4. **Forge-temperature verification** (Moldvay). In Scene 06 Branch A: add *"Tova confirms the main forge reaches ≥1,800°C at dawn."*

5. **One mid-compound pressure beat** (Gygax + Schick). The party bleeds nothing reaching the spool. The wandering-pressure table has it (Holding Pulse 4: the spool vibrates when Nenn passes the archive), but it's not guaranteed. Consider making Holding Pulse 3 (Grom + Tova forge comparison) mandatory rather than random.

## Divergent P0 suggestions

- **(Jaquays)** Add a second entrance to the archive (climbing-accessible window). Unlocks topology.
- **(Gygax)** Give the Reorx-rite a secondary forge path (Tova's fire-altar, DC 18 without dawn conditions).
- **(Schick)** Cut or defer Blank-Thread Trap — it's unreachable in this adventure.
- **(Stuart)** Nenn should have done something at Stonfold that the party witnesses before meeting him. The gate-hinge he repaired is there — use it explicitly: "This gate-hinge is well-made; someone who knows their work repaired it."

## Go/No-Go

**GO to play with P0 revisions applied.** Estimated fix time: 20 minutes. All six P0 items are additive (1-3 lines each). The architecture, the artifact, and the emotional core are all validated. Apply the fixes, compile to module.md (done), then run S06.
