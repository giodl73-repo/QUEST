# Encounter — Unbound Courier Ambush (Route C)

**Triggers:** The Unbound Conclave courier (seen at the harbor market from Scene 05) has been watching the townhouse. If the party spends more than 90 minutes in the townhouse (Route C pressure timer), or if they were previously spotted and the courier recognized what they came for, two Unbound Conclave agents enter via the private dock.

**Goal of the agents:** Recover the Clause of Witness. Not kill the wardens — the renegades need them to find the other shards. Prefer capture; will fight to subdue.

## Stat blocks

**Unbound Conclave Agent (×2)**

Reskin of Thug (SRD): **AC** 11 | **HP** 32 | **Speed** 30 ft.
**STR** 15 (+2) | **DEX** 11 | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 10 | **WIS** 10 | **CHA** 11
**Saves:** STR +4, CON +4
**Skills:** Intimidation +2
**Multiattack:** 2 attacks with mace (+4, 1d6+2).
**Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attack rolls against a creature if an ally is adjacent to it.

**Agent 1 — "Osvin":** Carries a short staff. Once per day, can cast Thunderwave as a 1st-level spell (save DC 11; 2d8 thunder, pushed 10 ft. on fail). This depletes after one use.

**Agent 2 — "the quiet one":** Has a grappling hook and rope; will attempt to grapple a PC and drag them to the dock-edge (not into the water — that would kill; just threatening proximity). Athletics +4 to grapple.

## Tactics

Round 1: Osvin enters from the dock stairs first, announces "Give us the fragment and no one is hurt." (He means it.) The quiet one flanks toward the nearest PC carrying the shard.

Round 2: If the party refuses, Osvin casts Thunderwave toward the cluster; the quiet one attempts grapple on the shard-carrier.

Round 3+: If reduced below half HP or if the party demonstrates clearly superior force, Osvin calls retreat — "We'll find the rest of them ourselves." They disengage and flee via the dock.

**They do not kill.** If a PC falls to 0 HP, the agents stabilize them and leave. They want the shard, not a war with the Conclave remnant.

## XP and balance

- 2 × Thug CR 1/2 = 200 base XP × 1.5 multiplier = 300 adjusted XP.
- Party budget (Medium, L5, 4 PCs) = approximately 900 XP. This is an Easy encounter.
- **Intentionally Easy.** Route C is the pressure route, not the optimal route. The ambush should feel urgent but not dangerous. The party can win quickly; the point is the time pressure, not the combat itself.

## GM Notes

- This encounter fires only on Route C. If the party recovered the shard cleanly (Route A or D), the courier watches them leave and files a report. The agents will appear in adventure 0009 as a known threat.
- If the party has not yet gotten the shard (ambush interrupts the study scene), they must fight/flee with the shard still in the case. The study scene can still complete if the party holds the dock long enough to finish the negotiation with Henneth.
- Orik is the tactical center of this encounter. His Battle Master maneuvers (Commander's Strike; Menacing Attack) are exactly right for protecting the shard-carrier and creating space.
