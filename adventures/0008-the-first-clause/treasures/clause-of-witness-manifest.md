# Curse Symptoms Manifest — Clause of Witness

Curse: The Clause's weight — the attuned creature has disadvantage on Deception; hears the question "Who witnessed this?" once per day after unseen actions.

The dungeon must express at least 3 of the following symptoms distributed across rooms:

## Required symptoms (≥ 3 of 5 must appear in keyed rooms)

### Symptom 1 — The dimming (visual)
The shard dims when a lie is spoken within 20 feet.
**Required:** At least one scene where the shard dims visibly — preferably before the party knows what it means.
**Fallback:** If the party misses the dimming in its first occurrence, a second opportunity is scripted in the study (Henneth's business partner scene).

### Symptom 2 — The warmth (sensory)
The shard is always warm — warmer than the room, warmer than a hand that has been holding it.
**Required:** The moment the shard is first touched (by any PC) must note the warmth. It is the first thing a touching PC notices.
**Fallback:** If no PC touches it immediately, Orik will instinctively tap the case; Sera will note the ambient temperature near the display.

### Symptom 3 — The question (auditory/interior)
If any PC attunes (or handles for ≥ 1 minute), they hear: *"Who witnessed this?"* — not aloud; interior.
**Required:** If a PC handles the shard for more than a minute in the study or during transport, note the question.
**Fallback:** Tessamine explains this effect when the party delivers the shard; she has held one previously.

### Symptom 4 — The inscription-glow (visual, Hint 1 vehicle)
When Thessaly reads the inscription with genuine attention, the inscription brightens briefly.
**Required:** Scene in Tessamine's archive or the study where Thessaly reads the shard.
**Fallback (Hint 1 recovery path):** If Thessaly does not read it this session, Tessamine reads it aloud during the delivery scene — Hint 1 auto-delivers at passive Arcana 10 for any party member present.

### Symptom 5 — The display case anomaly (visual)
Henneth's display case holds six fragments. The Clause of Witness is the only one that has never collected dust. The others have a fine film; this one is clean.
**Required:** Perception DC 10 (or passive 10) during the study scene notices the clean shard.
**Fallback:** Casual mention in read-aloud as a minor detail; auto-available.

## Per-PC reception table
