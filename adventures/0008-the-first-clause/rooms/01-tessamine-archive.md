# Scene 01 — Tessamine's Archive

**Location:** Red Robe chapter-house, Korrath-by-the-Sea. Third floor; a room that smells of cedar and paper. **Time:** Day 1, arrival.

**Read-aloud:**

> Three walls of shelves, one wall of window. Harbor light through old glass. Archivist Tessamine sits at the table — she didn't rise to greet you. Your folded letter is already between you. She's read it first.

**Features:**

- **Tessamine.** She is smaller than expected. See `npcs/archivist-tessamine.md`. She will brief the party on Henneth's townhouse, the shard's location, the renegade faction, and what she can't yet say. The dimming behavior: she has a shard-fragment reference (a document, not a shard) on her desk; when she says "I have six weeks" — a lie; she has four — the fragment reference dims briefly. Perception DC 10 to notice; Arcana proficiency = automatic.
- **The archive shelf (optional investigation).** Among the sorted materials: a moon-calendar chart with a date circled three months from now. Tessamine did not put this out deliberately; it is part of her working papers. Investigation DC 12 or Hint 2 carrier: Calder sees it first (passive Insight 14 on Tessamine's papers; she is watching the calendar).
- **The brief.** Tessamine gives the party: Henneth's address, a letter of introduction (bearing her seal — the White Robe Conclave remnant's mark), operational information about the renegade faction (name: the Unbound Conclave; courier active in Korrath harbor; not yet hostile but watching).

**Encounter:** None. This is the briefing.

**GM Notes:**

- **The dimming.** Tessamine says she has six weeks; she has four. This is a calculated lie — she is managing the party's urgency. The shard-reference on her desk dims for three seconds. This is Symptom 1 (dimming) from the manifest, delivered before the party knows what it means. They will remember it when they see the real shard dim.
- **Do not explain the dimming.** The DM notes it; narrates it if the party notices; does not explain it. "The document on her desk dims slightly when she says six weeks. You aren't sure what you saw."
- **Thessaly and the archive.** If Thessaly investigates the archive while waiting, she finds Tessamine's reconstruction document (locked in a lower drawer; Investigation DC 16 to find without permission; Tessamine will show it freely if asked directly after trust is established). This is not the arc-completion trigger — it is the structural setup.
- **DM-letter:** See `dm-letter-scene-01.md` before running this scene. The moment Tessamine delivers the arc-completion (if it happens here) requires the DM to stop and witness.

**Connections:** Street (to Henneth's townhouse, Scene 02). Return path: party returns here after recovering the shard.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Tessamine has been in this archive for eleven years. She came here after the Order sent her to Korrath on a survey mission and she decided not to go back. She told the Order she was ill. She was, but not the way she said. The archive was the first place in forty years she had been able to work quietly on what she actually cared about, which was the question of what the compact said and whether it could still matter. She has her answer. She has been waiting for someone to ask it.*

## Manifest symptoms landed

- **Dimming (Symptom 1):** Tessamine's lie dims the shard-reference on her desk. (anchor: Perception 10 / Arcana proficiency automatic; path a — all PCs present)
- **Moon-calendar (Hint 2 carrier):** Archive shelf contains a circled moon-calendar date. (anchor: Investigation 12 or Calder passive Insight 14; path a+b)
