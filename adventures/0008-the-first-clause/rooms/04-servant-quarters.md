# Scene 04 — Servant Quarters / Back Stairs (Optional)

**Location:** Henneth's townhouse, rear ground floor. **Time:** Day 1, morning (if party explores independently).

**Read-aloud:**

> The servant entrance opens from a side alley onto a narrow corridor — storage on the left, a staircase going up on the right. There's a smell of cooking from somewhere above and the sound of someone moving methodically between tasks. Pella's domain; she knows where everything is and notices when something is out of place.

**Features:**

- **Pella's territory.** Pella is here if the party enters via the servant entrance uninvited. She is not alarmed — the door is unlatched during working hours — but she will ask, clearly and politely, who they are and what they want.
- **The household guard.** One of Henneth's two guards rests here during the day shift. He is not aggressive; he is present. A party that looks like they belong will not be questioned; a party that looks suspicious will be asked to wait.
- **The back stairs.** Connect to the study (Scene 03) directly. A party moving quietly (Stealth DC 12) can reach the study without passing through the Entry Hall.
- **Investigative detail.** A shelf in the corridor holds Henneth's household accounts — not the shipping contracts (those are in the study), but the domestic records. Investigation DC 14: the household accounts show a pattern of small irregular purchases from a chandler in the harbor district. This is not significant to the mission; it is Henneth quietly maintaining a candle-lit memorial for his wife.

**Encounter:** None unless the party triggers a hostile response (see study for guard stats).

**GM Notes:**

- **This is the stealth path.** A party that wants to bypass the social encounter in Scene 02 can use this route. It is not recommended — the social approach to Henneth is more direct and produces better outcomes — but it is available.
- **Sera's territory.** Sera will note the alley entrance before the party enters the front door (signature move: she checks perimeters). She may or may not flag it to the party. Let her decide.
- **The household guard.** His name is Bereth. He is not a threat. He has worked for Henneth for four years. If the party treats him with basic courtesy, he will point them toward Pella and let them past.

**Connections:** Scene 02 (Entry Hall, back), Scene 03 (Study, via back stairs), street (external side alley).

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Bereth has been Henneth's household guard for four years. He has never had to do anything more than look convincing. His previous employment was as a harbor dockhands' supervisor, which involved considerably more physical confrontation and considerably less standing in corridors. He considers this an improvement.*
