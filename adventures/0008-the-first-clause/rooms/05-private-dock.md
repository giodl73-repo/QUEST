# Scene 05 — Private Dock

**Location:** Henneth's townhouse, harbor-level, accessed via internal stairs from the study. **Time:** Day 1, whenever needed.

**Read-aloud:**

> The dock is small — space for one vessel and the rope-work to maintain it. The harbor is grey and working, the sounds of commerce filtering in from further out. Henneth uses this dock for weather observation, he said; the stairs behind the bookcase go here. The water is three feet below the dock-level. It smells of salt and old rope and, faintly, of pine resin.

**Features:**

- **The dock itself.** One small sailing vessel moored (Henneth's weather-observation sloop; it is seaworthy but lightly equipped). The party could leave by water if they needed to.
- **The harbor view.** From this dock, Sera can see the harbor market stalls where the Unbound Conclave courier was described as operating. Investigation DC 12 or Perception DC 14: a figure in grey robes who does not match the market-going crowd is visible at the far stall. This is the courier. She is watching the townhouse.
- **The ambush entrance (Route C).** If the Route C pressure activates (courier alerted), the two Unbound Conclave agents enter via the harbor dock rather than the front door. They arrive at the dock level and attempt to recover the shard before the party can return to Tessamine's archive. See `encounters/unbound-courier-ambush.md`.

**Encounter:** None at baseline. Unbound Courier Ambush (Route C only) — 2 agents, medium difficulty for L5 party. See encounters/.

**GM Notes:**

- **The pine resin smell.** Orik notices this (signature move: he smells it on every threshold; this threshold smells of it genuinely — the rope maintenance oil). He goes still for a moment. This is not a plot point; it is his grief landing in an ordinary moment. Do not narrate it heavily. Note it briefly: *"The dock smells of pine resin. Orik's hands go still for a moment, then resume."* Let Orik's player carry it.
- **The courier.** She is visible from the dock if the party looks. She is not yet hostile. She is watching. If the party identifies her and approaches, she will deny being anything other than a merchant. Insight DC 14 reveals the denial. This is the seed for the renegade faction's next adventure (0009).
- **Route C ambush:** Two Unbound Conclave agents, Thugs stat block (see SRD, MM p. 350), one holding a short staff that can cast Thunderwave (1/day). Medium encounter for L5 party. Goal: recover the shard; they will not kill a Conclave operative if they can avoid it — they need the wardens alive to track the other shards. They prefer capture to death.

**P0 fallback:** If the party has the shard and is leaving via the dock (either as the clean exit or during the Route C ambush), they have the shard. The adventure ends at the dock exit. Return to Tessamine's archive is the final scene (Tessamine arc-completion opportunity, even if Hint 1 was not delivered in the study).

**Connections:** Scene 03 (Study, via internal stairs), harbor (water exit), harbor market (courier visible from here).
