# Scene 06 — Tessamine's Archive (Return)

**Location:** Red Robe chapter-house, Korrath-by-the-Sea. Same room as Scene 01. **Time:** Day 1, afternoon/evening.

**Read-aloud:**

> Tessamine is still in the same chair. She has added a shawl. The light is later now — orange, coming through the old glass at a lower angle. She watches you cross the room to her desk and set down whatever you brought, and she does not look at the shard first. She looks at your faces.

**Features:**

- **The delivery.** The party returns the shard (or describes what happened to it). Tessamine confirms its identity by holding it — she has held a shard-fragment before; she knows the warmth. She writes a receipt note and files it.
- **Tessamine's arc-completion.** Available if Thessaly named the inscription's meaning (Hint 1 delivered) earlier in the session, or if any PC says: *"What was the compact actually for?"* and waits. See `npcs/archivist-tessamine.md` for the exact arc-completion moment. She speaks the compact's purpose and gives them her reconstruction document.
- **The conversation about what's next.** Tessamine gives the party the operational brief for the remaining six shards: known locations of two more, suspected locations of two, renegade faction's known movements. This is the seed for 0009.
- **The Hint 1 fallback.** If the inscription was not read in the study, Tessamine reads it aloud now. Any PC present: passive Arcana 10 to understand what she's reading. Hint 1 delivers via her words rather than Thessaly's direct reading. This is Route D prereq 1 still available.

**Encounter:** None.

**P0 fallback — if Tessamine never fires arc-completion:** She gives the operational brief, confirms the shard's identity, and asks them to rest and return the next morning. She will attempt the arc-completion a second time at that meeting — she has not given up on the party being ready; she has just recalibrated her timeline.

**P0 failure state:** Even if Tessamine never fires arc-completion this session, the campaign continues. Route D is still open. The reconstruction document arrives in a later adventure if the party earns it.

**GM Notes:**

- **"She looks at your faces."** This is the read-aloud's most important line. She is assessing whether they understand what they brought her. Let this moment breathe.
- **DM-letter:** See `dm-letter-scene-06.md` if the arc-completion fires here. The DM's job is to witness Thessaly naming the inscription's meaning. Do not narrate over her.
- **Orik smells the pine resin again** — on Tessamine's archive, from some preservation treatment on the shelves. He notes it and says nothing. Third occurrence; the third time is when it becomes a thing.

**Connections:** End of adventure 0008. Party returns to wherever they are staying in Korrath; Tessamine remains at the archive.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Tessamine wrote the letter to Thessaly four months ago. She has been sitting with the question of whether to send it for three months before that. She sent it when she received the news that the renegade faction had sent a courier to Korrath. Not because she was afraid — she does not have enough time left to be afraid — but because the math changed. If the compact is to be understood before it is reassembled, someone who can read it has to be there when it happens. Thessaly can read it. She sent the letter.*
