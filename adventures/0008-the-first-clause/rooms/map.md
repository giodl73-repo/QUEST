# Map — The First Clause

## Adventure topology

This adventure takes place across two connected locations: Tessamine's archive (entry point) and Henneth's townhouse (primary location). The party moves between them; the topology is a building, not a classic dungeon.

```
[01 TESSAMINE'S ARCHIVE]
         |
         | (3 streets; street level)
         v
[02 HENNETH TOWNHOUSE — Entry Hall]
    /         \
   /           \
[03 STUDY]   [04 SERVANT QUARTERS / BACK STAIRS]
   |               |
   v               v
[05 PRIVATE DOCK]  (reconnects to Entry Hall via staff entrance)
```

**Loops:** The townhouse has two entry points (front door via Entry Hall; servant entrance behind Servant Quarters) that both connect to the study. A party that enters via the servant entrance can reach the study without passing through the Entry Hall. Loop: Entry Hall → Study → Servant Quarters → back to street → Entry Hall.

**Vertical:** The study is on the second floor; the dock is on the ground floor; the servant quarters bridge them via back stairs.

**Two paths to the artifact:** Front door (Entry Hall → Study) or servant entrance (Servant Quarters → Study). The dock offers a third path if the party arrives by water.

**Shortcut:** If the party earns Henneth's trust in the Entry Hall, he invites them directly to the Study, bypassing the servant quarters entirely.

## Scale note

Each room represents a distinct social/spatial encounter. Travel between rooms takes one narrative beat. The archive and townhouse are 10 minutes apart on foot.
