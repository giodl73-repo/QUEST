# Scene 03 — The Study (Artifact Location)

**Location:** Henneth's townhouse, second floor. **Time:** Day 1, mid-morning.

**Read-aloud:**

> Six shelves of pre-Cataclysm fragments, each with a small handwritten card. The room smells of old stone and careful attention. A display case runs the length of the east wall behind Henneth's desk — six fragments mounted on velvet backing, small paper labels below each. The third from the left is the one you came for. Even from the doorway, you can tell it's different from the others: the others have acquired the faint dust-film of objects that have been in the same place for years. This one is clean.

**Features:**

- **The display case.** Six fragments mounted on velvet. The Clause of Witness (third from left) is clean; the others have a faint dust layer. This is Symptom 5 (manifest). Henneth's handwritten card: *"Obsidian fragment, pre-Cataclysm. Palanthas estate auction, 3 years ago. Provenance unknown. High Istaran script (unread). 15 gp."*
- **The inscription.** Any PC who reads the fragment up close (or examines the display case) can attempt to read High Istaran — Thessaly reads it automatically (she has History +7 and Arcana +7; this falls under her proficiencies). If Thessaly reads it: Hint 1 delivery (the inscription brightens; see treasure file). Other PCs: Arcana DC 12 or History DC 14 to read High Istaran.
- **Sevel's visit.** During the party's time in the study, Henneth's business partner Sevel arrives unannounced. He is solicitous, slightly too smooth, and lying to Henneth's face about the shipping contract numbers. The Clause of Witness dims visibly while Sevel speaks — this is Symptom 1's second occurrence (primary, since the first was subtle). Perception DC 10 to notice; Arcana proficiency = automatic. Sevel leaves after 8 minutes. Henneth does not mention the numbers discrepancy; he knows and hasn't decided what to do.
- **Henneth's decision.** After Sevel leaves, if the party explains what the shard is (genuine explanation, not a con), Henneth gives it to them. See `npcs/varold-henneth.md` for his behavior matrix and arc-completion.

**Reaction moment (artifact removal):** When the shard is removed from the display case, the case is suddenly noticeably empty — the velvet beneath the other fragments seems darker, as if the warmth that held the whole case together has left with the shard. Henneth looks at the empty space for a moment and says: *"I always thought it was warmer than it should be."* He is not sad; he is resolved. The adventure's shard has a home to go to.

**Encounter:** None at baseline. If the party attempts theft, Henneth calls for staff (2 household guards — see encounters/household-guard.md). If the Unbound Conclave courier has been alerted (Route C pressure), two Unbound Conclave agents arrive at the townhouse at the end of Scene 03 — see encounters/unbound-courier-ambush.md.

**Treasure:** Clause of Witness (display case, mountable). See `treasures/clause-of-witness.md`.

**Connections:** Scene 02 (Entry Hall, back to start), Scene 05 (Private Dock, internal stairs behind bookcase — Perception DC 14 or Henneth points it out; he uses it for weather observation).

**GM Notes:**

- **The shard's warmth.** The first PC to open the display case and reach for the fragment immediately notes the warmth (Symptom 2). Read aloud: *"It is warm — not hot, but the specific warmth of a hand that has been holding it for an hour, even though it has been in the case."* Do not explain.
- **Thessaly and the inscription.** If Thessaly reads it, she reads it aloud quietly to herself (signature move). The inscription brightens. This is Hint 1. If this happens, do not rush the moment — let her read it, let it land, let her turn the shard over in her hands. If she speaks the inscription's meaning aloud to the party, the Tessamine arc-completion becomes available.
- **Sevel and the dimming.** Sevel lies twice. The first lie dims the shard; the second lie dims it again. Henneth notices nothing. Orik notices the first (his Perception is +5 and he has been watching Sevel since he arrived — signature move: counting exits, counting tells). Thessaly notices automatically. The party now has evidence of what the shard does, even if they don't know why.
- **P0 fallback — if Henneth refuses:** If the party's approach fails (attempted deception, no letter of introduction, poor social rolls), the shard is not immediately retrievable. Route C applies: the courier arrives; the party must recover under pressure, without time for the quiet Hint 1 moment. The shard is recoverable via the dock (Scene 05) during the ambush.
- **P0 failure state for the Sevel scene:** No roll needed. Sevel comes; the shard dims; Sevel leaves. This is not a check the party can fail.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *The display case has been in this room for twelve years. Henneth brought the fragment collection from Palanthas when he relocated to Korrath. The fragment that is now the Clause of Witness spent its first year in a storage crate before he unpacked it. He noticed then that the velvet under it was slightly warmer than the velvet under the others. He attributed it to the crate's position near the wall radiator. He has not thought about it since.*
