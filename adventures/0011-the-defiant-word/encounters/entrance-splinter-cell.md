# Encounter — Splinter Cell at Entrance (non-optional)

**Location:** Hidden entrance in the Qualimosti eastern foothills.
**Trigger:** The party finds two Unbound Conclave agents at the entrance before they descend.

**Context:** These are Unbound Conclave agents who did not receive Mira's stand-down order. They are part of a splinter faction within the Unbound Conclave — a group that disagreed with Mira's authority to stand down the entire operation. They know about the White Quill shard. They are trying to break in (they have not found the latch; it requires knowing where to look). They do not know the three-Order requirement.

**Sera's advantage:** She always checks the perimeter. She will have seen the agents from the hillside before the party approached. The party has surprise.

## Stat blocks

**2× Unbound Conclave Splinter Agents (Thug reskin):**
AC 11 | HP 32 | Speed 30 ft.
Multiattack: 2 mace attacks (+4, 1d6+2) | Pack Tactics

Neither has Osvin's Dagger of Warning or Quiet One's Bola. They are not the trained agents. They are competent soldiers, not specialists.

**Adjusted XP:** 2× CR 1/2 = 200 base XP × 1.5 multiplier = 300 adjusted. Per PC (5): 60 XP. **Easy encounter.** This is not a threat; it is information.

## Social resolution

If the party approaches diplomatically (or simply announces themselves with authority): the agents may stand down. Persuasion DC 12 or authority claim ("Mira Coldsteel stood your organization down — you didn't get the message?"). If this works: the agents confirm that yes, they heard about Mira's stand-down, they disagreed with it, and they are operating independently. They will leave (they are not committed enough to fight an organized party). They provide free intel: the Unbound Conclave splinter faction has three cells; this one was tasked with the White Quill shard.

## Combat tactics

**Round 1:** They recognize the party is organized. One attacks (Pack Tactics if possible); one moves to flank. They are not trained to handle five opponents.

**Round 2+:** If reduced to one agent, the survivor surrenders. They are not ideologues; they were hired. Intel under interrogation: three splinter cells; the White Quill shard; they have orders to deliver it to a contact in Palanthas (name unknown).

## What this encounter establishes

The Unbound Conclave did not fully stand down when Mira gave the order. There is a faction that operates independently of Mira's authority. This is a seed for 0012 (The Reckoning Stone) — the renegade leader who has the fourth shard is not Mira; it is the person running these splinter cells.

## Encounter-balancer notes

**Easy encounter — intentional.** S04 is the first binding-threshold session for campaign 2. The Mira stand-down removed the campaign's primary threat. This encounter establishes a new, lower-level threat (splinter cells) without replacing Mira's antagonist role with something equivalent. The party should handle this quickly and cleanly; the session's real weight is the library.
