# Wandering Pressure — The Defiant Word

## Library pressure (no hard clock)

The library has no time pressure — Sevven has been waiting fifty-eight years; he can wait longer. The only pressure is the convergence countdown (83 → 82 → 81 days) and the party's own sense of urgency.

## Library Cold Pulse (d6, every 30 minutes underground)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | A lamp flickers — one of the Continual Flames responding to an air current from the entrance. It resolves on its own. Deva notices; no one else does unless they're watching. |
| 2 | Sevven returns a volume to its place on the shelf after 40 years in the reading room. He notes the date in his sentence-a-day journal. He does not explain why it took 40 years. |
| 3 | Pine resin smell from a preservative oil used on the oak rolling ladders. Orik's hands still for a moment. |
| 4 | Lenne finds a volume titled *"Survey of Compact Artifact Responses, PC 200-215."* Sylaren wrote it. It is the most rigorous pre-Cataclysm artifact documentation the party has seen. Lenne does not put it down for fifteen minutes. Sevven lets her read. |
| 5 | Calder sits at one of the reading desks with the Sylaren correspondence volume and does not read it. He just keeps it in front of him. |
| 6 | Sera finds the emergency exit (air-shaft behind Sevven's bookcase) without being told it exists. She checks the width. She checks if it is maintained. She says nothing about this to anyone. |
