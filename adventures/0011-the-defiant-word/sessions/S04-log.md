# S04 LOG — The Defiant Word

## Session Summary

**First binding-threshold session for campaign 2.** The advisory period is over.

Two splinter-cell agents were at the hidden entrance, trying to find the latch they didn't know how to find. Thessaly told them Mira had stood the organization down; they confirmed they were operating independently, gave free intel (three cells; Palanthas contact, name unknown), and left. No combat. Orik checked the hatch inside-curve on the way down: true, annual maintenance, three hundred years.

Underground: Sevven read the party at a glance — White Robe, Red Robe trained, hall-guard, a ranger positioned near the exit, a Life cleric. He said they had two Orders and needed a third. Lenne read Deva as Black Robe trained from how she handled texts — *"Abjuration school reads differently. You handle them like someone who was taught to think defensively."* Deva said it had been four years. Thessaly asked formally as a White Robe representative. Deva agreed. Three Orders assembled.

In the deep archive, Sevven gave the shard to Calder. He said: *"Read both sides."* Calder read the front inscription. Sevven guided him to the reverse: *"As I wrote above: what any mortal swore, any mortal may unsay. This was always true."*

Calder named the plague village. Precisely. *"Forty-one died. Seventeen survived. I was the delayed response. I do not know if anyone was still operating this protocol or if it was residual institutional structure. I know it was operating."*

The second inscription warmed.

Sevven named the Dalimvar protocol: seven hundred deaths, PC 203, the only dissenting vote, nine years of watching, then she left, and the protocol ran for another three hundred years.

Deva said, from the third corner: *"I still know the words."*

Sera noticed the carved figure in the display case — Sylaren's, from the ruins of Istar, facing outward. Same position as Morreth's figure in the chapel gap. She said nothing. She filed it.

In Sevven's study, Calder read Sylaren's personal note — *"it was the protocol, not you"* — and gave it back. He did not keep it. Lenne filled the empty page in her notebook and three pages after it. Thessaly said: *"Seventy-nine days."*

Four shards. Route D prerequisites: 4/4.
