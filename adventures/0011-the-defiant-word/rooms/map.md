# Map — The Defiant Word

## Topology

The White Quill is underground — five connected rooms beneath the Qualimosti foothills. The entry tunnel is the only known entrance; there is an emergency exit (a low crawlspace to a hillside air-shaft, usable by one person at a time) that Sevven has maintained for sixty years and never used.

```
[ENCOUNTER: Splinter cell at hidden entrance]
        |
        v
[01 ENTRY TUNNEL & ANTEROOM]
        |
        v
[02 MAIN STACKS — Deva works here]
    /         \
   /           \
[03 READING ROOM]  [04 DEEP ARCHIVE — Clause of Defiance]
                         |
                         v
               [05 SEVVEN'S STUDY]
               (air-shaft exit behind bookcase)
```

**Loop:** Main Stacks (02) connects to both the Reading Room (03) and Deep Archive (04). The Reading Room connects back to the Main Stacks only (no shortcut). The loop: Entry → Stacks → Reading Room → Stacks → Deep Archive → Study → Stacks (via corridor).

**Vertical:** The Entry Tunnel descends 20 feet underground. The rest is level.

**Two paths to artifact:** Via Reading Room (social path — Sevven shows them the shard's context before they see it) or via Deep Archive direct (Deva can escort them directly if the Orders are assembled immediately).

**Shortcut:** If all three Orders are assembled in the anteroom (Entry), Sevven escorts them directly to the Deep Archive, bypassing the Reading Room.

**Sensory cues at branches:**
- Reading Room entrance: smells of dust and leather binding
- Deep Archive entrance: cooler; the smell changes to old stone and something mineral
