# Scene 01 — Entry Tunnel & Anteroom (+ Entrance Encounter)

**Location:** Hidden entrance in the Qualimosti eastern foothills. **Time:** Day 2 travel from the chapel.

**Read-aloud (encounter):**

> The entrance is a flat stone set into the hillside — you found it because the ground around it is too level, the kind of levelness that takes maintenance. Two people are standing in front of it, grey-robed, not the agents you know. They have not heard you yet. One has a hand on the stone. They are trying to find the latch.

**Entrance Encounter:** A two-agent Unbound Conclave splinter cell who did not receive Mira's stand-down order. They are trying to break into the White Quill to retrieve the shard. They are not hostile to the party specifically — they don't know the party is here. If discovered: they may try to recruit ("the main faction stood down but we didn't — join us") or fight. Sera's high-point perimeter approach (she will check the hill before the party approaches) gives the party surprise.

**P0 fallback:** If the party talks their way past the encounter (Persuasion DC 12 — "the main faction stood down; you're behind"), the agents leave. If combat: 2-agent encounter, Easy-Medium for 5 PCs (2× Thugs, same stats as Osvin/Quiet One, no special equipment).

**Read-aloud (anteroom, after encounter):**

> The tunnel descends twenty feet under the hill. At the bottom: a room that smells of old paper and cedar oil and something else — underground quiet, the specific silence of a space that has been maintained but not visited much. A reading lamp burns on a low table. Beside it: a small card that says, in careful script, *"Please announce yourselves. The archivist will come."*

**Features:**
- The announcement card. It has been here for three hundred years, replaced when it fades. This is the fifth version.
- Sevven will appear from the Main Stacks within two minutes.
- The lamp is Continual Flame (pre-Cataclysm; the spell predates most living memory). It never burns down.

**GM Notes:**
- **Sera's approach.** She will check the hill from above before the party descends. She will see the two agents. She will signal the party silently (signature move: mark a time, keep position). The party has surprise if they want it.
- **The agents.** They are not well-informed. They know the White Quill has a shard; they do not know the three-Order requirement. They think it is just hidden well.
- **After the encounter.** Orik will check the tunnel entrance structure (inside curve of the stone hatch: well-maintained; someone has been greasing the hinge annually). He notes it.

**Connections:** Main Stacks (02, descending from anteroom).

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *The entrance has been maintained by the same family for three generations. The current maintainer is a farmer who lives two miles east. She checks the hinge annually, in early spring, when the frost lifts. She does not know what is under the hill. Her grandmother told her: "Keep the hinge greased. Someone will need that door someday." She has never met anyone who needed it until now.*
