# Curse Symptoms Manifest — Clause of Defiance

Curse: The right to amend — attuned creature becomes aware once/day of any unjust commitment they are maintaining.

At least 3 of 4 symptoms must land:

## Symptom 1 — The two inscriptions (visual/textual)
The shard has two inscriptions: front (compact clause) and reverse (Sylaren's amendment).
**Required:** When the shard is first shown or examined, both inscriptions are visible. The fact that there are two — in different hands, different dates — is immediately apparent.
**Fallback:** Sevven describes the second inscription before showing the shard.

## Symptom 2 — The second inscription's content (Hint 3 primary)
*"What any mortal swore, any mortal may unsay."*
**Required:** Calder reads it (passive Insight 14 → Sevven points it out directly) OR Religion DC 15 (any PC). The reading triggers Hint 3 delivery.
**Fallback:** Sevven reads it aloud when presenting the shard.

## Symptom 3 — Route D: shard warms when Calder names the village (visual/sensory)
When Calder names the plague village in the presence of all three Orders, the second inscription warms briefly.
**Required:** Route D only. The shard is in the room; it responds to the naming.
**Fallback (Routes A/C):** The shard is given; the second inscription is read; the warmth does not occur. Hint 3 still delivers via the reading.

## Symptom 4 — The three-Order requirement as demonstration (behavioral)
The requirement that all three Orders be present is not a gatekeeping mechanism — it is the compact's own structure demonstrated in miniature. The party cannot receive this shard without representing all three Orders that swore the compact. This is the compact's self-application.
**Required:** The moment all three Orders are present in the room, Sevven presents the shard. This is the symptom.
**Fallback:** Sevven explains the requirement before the party assembles the Orders.
