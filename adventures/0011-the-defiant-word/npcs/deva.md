# Deva

*White Quill assistant archivist, 82. Trained with the Black Robes for four years when she was 22. Left. Has been here since. She is not sure whether she is still a Black Robe; she has never tested the question.*

## Appearance

Deva is precise in the way that people who have been maintaining records for sixty years become precise — she notices inconsistencies, she catches errors, she does not misplace things. She wears her hair in a tight braid. Her robe is unmarked; she has not worn Black Robe robes in sixty years. She keeps them in a chest in her room. She does not know why she keeps them.

She is not warm. She is not cold. She is accurate.

## Stat block

**Non-combatant.** AC 10, HP 10.

## What she knows (public)

- She trained with the Black Robes for four years.
- She left after a philosophical disagreement about how the Orders should respond to the growing tension that preceded the Cataclysm. She thought the Orders were underreacting. The Orders disagreed.
- She has been at the White Quill for sixty years.
- She does not know whether her four years of Black Robe training counts as "a Black Robe present."

## Emotional position

**What she wants:** To not have to decide whether she still counts as a Black Robe. She has been not deciding for sixty years. She will decide if she has to. She would prefer not to.

**What she can't say:** She is quietly proud of the Black Robe training she received. She will not admit this.

**The condition:** She agrees to represent the Black Robes if the party asks directly and acknowledges that her sixty-year absence does not invalidate what she learned and who she was. She will not agree to represent the Black Robes if she is asked to perform it — *"say something in Black Robe style"* or *"do something a Black Robe would do."* She cannot be prompted into it. She can only be respected into it.

## Behavior matrix

| Party action | Response |
|---|---|
| "Can you be our Black Robe?" | "I don't know if I still count. You'd have to decide if I do." |
| "You trained for four years. That counts." | Quiet. Considering. Then: "What do you need me to do?" |
| Tries to get her to demonstrate Black Robe knowledge | "I'm not going to perform it for you." Cold. She goes back to shelving. |
| Lenne acknowledges her as a practitioner | This is the closest thing to a trigger. Lenne reads practitioners by school. If she says "You trained Black Robe — I can see it in how you handle the texts," Deva goes still. Then: "Yes. I did." |
| Thessaly (White Robe) asks formally | This registers for Deva. An active White Robe acknowledging her former standing. She says: "If you're asking as a White Robe representative — yes. I'll stand as Black Robe." |

## Arc-Completion

**The moment:** When the party brings her to the deep archive and presents the three Orders — Thessaly (White), Lenne (Red), Deva (Black) — she stands at the third corner of the triangle the three of them form. Sevven looks at her. She looks at Sevven. She says, quietly: *"I still know the words."* She does not say which words. It is enough.

**What it produces:** Three Orders present. Sevven retrieves the shard.

**What happens if never met:** Mira Coldsteel can be messaged to provide a Black Robe contact instead. Two days' delay. The session waits.
