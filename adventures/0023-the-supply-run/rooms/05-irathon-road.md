# Room 05 — Irathon Road (Return)

## Read-aloud

> The mule tracks go south, toward Irathon Waystation, hours old. The cart is repaired. Tomek's scout is on the cart-bed, awake now, disoriented. The supplies are intact. The pass is quiet. Time to go back.

## Features

- **The mule:** Gone south — toward the supply depot, not north toward the Keep. The ambushers drove it off rather than taking it. It will find its own way to Irathon eventually. Not recoverable this session.

- **Aldric (Tomek's scout) awake:** He was hit from behind and remembers very little of the ambush. He confirms: the attack came from above. Two people. Not Reconstitution standard equipment. He is shaken but ambulatory with help. He will ride on the cart.

- **The return march:** One hour, cracked wheel repaired. The low trail is still exposed. Same DC 14 Stealth group check as on the way out.

- **Maret's token scene (Scene 5 of premise):** When the party arrives back through the postern with the cart, Maret is waiting at the station. The full scene is in the premise — she gives the token to Sava. This room is the approach to that scene; the scene itself fires inside the Keep (Room 03 of 0022).

## Encounter

None (return). Combat is behind them. The scene is resolution.

## Connections

- **North (trail):** back to 03 Choke Point → 02 Low Trail → 01 Postern Exit
- **South (Irathon Road):** to supply depot — not visited this session

## GM Notes

The return is not a free scene. The exposure on the low trail is real — a failed Stealth check means another arrow from the ridge. With a cart and a wounded scout, the group check is harder: disadvantage if pulling the cart through the exposed section (DM's discretion based on party configuration).

**Maret's token reaction moment:** When Sava hands the first supply crate to Maret, Maret takes it without looking at the contents. She looks at Sava. Then she looks at the cord under her healer's apron and removes the token. She hands it across like she hands a suture kit — the same motion, no ceremony. The token is warm from being worn. Sava will notice this. *This is the session's reaction moment.*

**Sava on the return:** She has the supplies. She is bringing them back before the window closes. This is the Elara story but ending differently. She does not narrate this internally — the session log should not over-explain it. It is present in her behavior (she counts the remaining time, she doesn't stop, she gets there).

**Aldric:** He owes the party something but he doesn't know what to call it yet. He will speak well of them to Tomek. This is not a token; it is texture.
