# Room 02 — Low Trail Junction & Trail

## Read-aloud

> The road branches left into shadow. The main road continues south, visible, exposed. The branch is older — narrower, stone underfoot rather than gravel. Boot-prints in the trail dust, going in. Not Solamnic-pattern kit. Lighter boots. Different stride.

## Features

- **Track analysis (Talis's scene):**
  - **DC 12 Survival (Talis, auto with signature move "read the ground"):** The tracks are not Reconstitution pattern — lighter boots, shorter stride, no military spacing. Two to three individuals. The cart's wheel-tracks go ahead of them.
  - **DC 14 Survival (any PC):** The tracks are 2-3 days old. They predate the Reconstitution's current siege-line tightening. Whoever hit the cart arrived before the siege was at full strength.
  - **DC 16 Survival (Talis only, takes a minute):** The lighter boots have a specific sole-wear pattern — high-arch, soft leather, the kind used by mountain guides or mercenary scouts. These are professionals, not regulars.

- **Cold smoke (shortcut trigger):**
  - **Passive Perception DC 14 (from the junction):** A faint smell of old smoke from above — a fire ring on a ledge, cold now. If noticed, the party can attempt to reach the upper ledge (Room 04) before entering the choke point.
  - If Talis makes DC 14 passive (her passive is 16): she spots it automatically. She says: *"There was a watch-fire up there. Recent. Cold."* Whether to climb before entering the choke is a party decision.

- **Exposure:** The low trail is visible from the northeast ridge. Anyone moving along it in daylight can be observed by the Reconstitution. This does not trigger combat — the Reconstitution's standing orders distinguish between garrison resupply parties and military threats — but it is visible. Note for the handoff: the Reconstitution knows the party made a supply run.

## Encounter

None (approach/investigation scene). The pressure comes from the tracks and the exposure, not combat.

## Connections

- **North (main road):** back to 01 Postern Exit
- **South (trail continues):** to 03 Choke Point
- **Up (cold smoke sighted, Athletics DC 12):** to 04 Upper Ledge (shortcut — approach choke from above with Surprise)

## GM Notes

This is Talis's first solo-spotlight scene in the campaign. Her decision order fires: *terrain first* → *state what she sees, do not wait to be asked*. She reads the tracks, she reports the result, she moves.

Her voice-tag fires: "two, maybe three. Forty-eight hours old. Light boots — not military." She does not editorialize. She gives the read.

The cold smoke is the shortcut cue. If Talis sees it and the party climbs to Room 04 before the choke: they approach the ambushers from above with Surprise in combat. If they miss it: they enter the choke at ground level, no Surprise.

### Memory Fragment *(read if party pauses at the junction)*

> This trail is older than the Keep. Before Thorngate existed, someone was walking this path between two places that mattered. The branch is not on any Solamnic map. It is known because people have needed it, not because anyone drew it down.
