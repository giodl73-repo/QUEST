# Room 01 — Postern Exit

## Read-aloud

> Before dawn. The postern opens onto the pass-side, the safe direction, the side the Reconstitution is not watching. Cold air. Stars still visible. The main road south is a darker line through grey stone. Tomek is on the central wall above you. He watches until you are through. He does not say anything.

## Features

- **Pre-dawn light:** The party leaves in darkness. The first half-mile is in shadow from the Keep's walls. DC 14 Stealth (group check) to pass the junction unseen by the Reconstitution's northeast ridge patrol. Failure: the patrol spots movement — one crossbow bolt, +4 to hit, 1d8+2, random target. The patrol does not pursue off the ridge.
- **Tomek on the wall:** His watching is passive Perception DC 10 to notice from below. He does not call out, signal, or interfere. He is logging the departure.

## Encounter

None (departure scene; tension only).

## Connections

- **South (main road):** to 02 Low Trail Junction — half-mile trail, open

## GM Notes

This scene is not a combat scene. Its job is to establish the threshold — the party is leaving the walls for the first time since arrival. The stakes are implicit in the departure.

Sava's signature move fires here: she counts her supplies before leaving (5 potions from garrison, her own healer's satchel, current slot count). She says it internally. If asked, she says it aloud.

### Memory Fragment *(read if party lingers at the postern)*

> The postern has been used at night before. The stone around the bolt is worn smooth from hands reaching for it in the dark. Going out and not coming back is a thing that has happened here. Going out and coming back with something is also a thing that has happened here.
