If Hessa's situation is framed correctly, Davan's grief makes contact with the investigation.*

2. **Contractor's token backup.** Dex escaped with the token. The Hessa investigation seed (0024) needs a physical evidence anchor in the choke point debris — a dropped item from the ambush setup that pre-dates the scouts' departure. Add to 0024 premise before dungeon-smith runs.

3. **Caelith Portent in-fiction.** Three sessions without the mechanic grounded in fiction. S3 should include one moment — in combat or social — where Caelith's Portent [6, 7] fires and is framed in-voice. Either: he offers a die to an ally ("I saw this coming — use it"), or he uses it himself for a check that matters. Low dice are most useful for enemy saves, enemy attack rolls, or a DC6-7 situation. Design the S3 encounter to create such a condition.

4. **Supply Die pressure.** S3 starts with 1 garrison potion. The encounter design should cost enough HP that the 1 potion matters — not just exists. If Sava has 3×L1 entering S3, she can still carry the group; the tension comes from the Supply Die being at d8 and visibly thinning.
