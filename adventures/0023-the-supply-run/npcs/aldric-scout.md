# Aldric (Tomek's Scout)

## Appearance

Mid-twenties. Garrison runner kit. Concussion-pale, eyes not quite tracking. He has been unconscious for two days against a rock face and his head hurts. He is functional. He is not happy about any of this.

## Stat Block

*Non-combatant (1 HP, recovering).* Stabilizes immediately with Spare the Dying or any healing. Can speak within 10 minutes of stabilization; can walk within 30; can ride on the cart-bed immediately.

## Public knowledge

- He rode ahead of the cart as a scout runner. He arrived at the choke point, was struck from behind before he could call out, and woke up against the rock face with a headache and no memory of the ambush itself.
- He confirms: the attack came from above. Two people. Not Solamnic kit. He did not see their faces.
- He has been conscious for maybe two hours total since the strike. He has no water left.

## Behavior matrix

- Wake him (any healing): He asks where he is, how long, and whether the cart made it. In that order.
- Tell him the cart is intact: He exhales. Does not say thank you. That is the garrison's "thank you."
- Ask him about the ambush: He gives what he has — attack from above, two people, not standard kit. That's all.
- Tell him about Pren: He processes this. *"A Reconstitution regular tried to stop it?"* He will report this to Tomek accurately.

## No arc-completion

Aldric is a seed for garrison continuity. He speaks well of the party to Tomek — which is worth something, though Tomek won't say so. Document in handoff: alive, recovered, returned with party.
