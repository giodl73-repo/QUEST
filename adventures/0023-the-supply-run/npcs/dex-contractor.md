# Dex (Mercenary Scout Lead)

## Appearance

Thirties. Lean, efficient, weather-worn. Light kit. Carries the contractor's token on a cord at his belt — a wooden disc with a device neither Solamnic nor Reconstitution. He moves like a man who has been in mountain terrain for twenty years. Not cruel; professional.

## Stat Block

Standard Mercenary Scout — see `encounters/ambush-remnant.md`.

## Role in this session

Dex is Scout A (the lead, behind the cart). He has the contractor's token. If captured before fleeing, he gives the information under Caelith's institutional framing (see below). If he escapes south, the token is lost and the Hessa investigation seed (0024) is weaker.

## Public knowledge

- He was hired through a contractor network to delay Thorngate's resupply. The job: stop the cart, leave the supplies intact, leave no Reconstitution evidence. No killing garrison soldiers.
- He did the job as specified. He is waiting to report completion.
- He has no idea Pren tried to stop the ambush.

## Private knowledge

- The contractor token connects to a network used by Coldforge's field adjutant for plausibly-deniable operations. Dex doesn't know who the adjutant is — he knows the token, not the name.
- The same token network connects to someone inside Thorngate Keep who provided the supply route details. Dex doesn't know who. He received the route from the contractor handler.

## H1 seed (if captured)

If captured and questioned by Caelith (institutional framing: *"who hired the contractor network — name the institution, not the person"*):

*"The job came through the token network. I don't name clients. The token's the authorization — that's all I know."*

He hands over the token if Caelith explains it's evidence of the route-leak rather than of his own guilt. He has professional ethics: he won't take credit for the route leak, which he didn't do.

**The token:** Hessa Var will recognize this device if shown (she uses the same contractor network for her message-carrier). This is Seed 0024 — the investigation thread for the next session.

## Information-holder design

Dex holds one piece of intelligence: the route was leaked from inside the Keep. He cannot name who; he never knew. But the existence of a leak — and the token that proves the contractor network's involvement — is the seed for 0024's investigation.

Information readiness condition: the party demonstrates they are investigating the leak, not prosecuting the contractor. Caelith's framing accomplishes this.

## No arc-completion

Dex is a professional who did a job. He has no arc in this campaign beyond the Hessa seed. Document in handoff: whether captured, whether token recovered.
