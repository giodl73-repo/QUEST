Laen does **not** know about the reliquary's existence before the party's return. If presented with:
- **Keloth's letter to Mira:** immediate Council-level interest. Laen escalates to higher Council within the week. Party may be offered a Council contract to protect Mira (seed for #0003).
- **The shelf-reliquaries:** Laen is unsettled but not immediately decisive. The Council will want them vaulted (like the Rose) or destroyed (if Grom pushes). This is a repeat of the S01 moral question at larger scale.
- **The Varran-reliquary (if attuned):** Aelric is no longer neutral in this. The Council will quietly note that a Knight of the Crown has attuned a Bone-Collector reliquary. Consequences at his next advancement review.
- **The empty reliquary:** Laen will ask to have it. He will not say why. (Canon: the Council wants it to destroy.)
