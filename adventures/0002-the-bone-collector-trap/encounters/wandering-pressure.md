# Temporal Pressure — Winter Wilderness + Stronghold

Two tables, adapted for adventure #0002's setting. Complies with rubric v1.1 Mechanical fairness cross-dimensional-harmlessness anchor (no ability-check disadvantage as primary mechanic).

## Use

- **Cold Pulse** d6 — every **10 minutes of in-fiction time** spent outdoors (Scenes 01-02 travel/pursuit) OR inside the unheated stronghold upper level (Scene 03-04). Stops in lower level (warmer, enclosed) and in Varduin on return.
- **Wandering Encounter** d6 — every **30 minutes** in the stronghold OR every **2 hours** on the wilderness trail. Stops once the party engages the vault climax.

## Cold Pulse Table (d6 per 10 min; rubric v1.1 compliant)

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** The wind moves past. |
| 2 | **A chill.** For three heartbeats the air bites; no mechanical effect, atmospheric only. |
| 3 | **The cold bites.** Each character not in heavy armor and not wearing a winter cloak takes **1d4 cold damage** (no save). Heavy-armor + cloak immune. *(v1.1 compliant: costs HP, not check-disadvantage.)* |
| 4 | **A forgotten name.** One random character is asked to name an NPC from their PC backstory (Adda, Vethrenn, Duren, Varran, etc.). They cannot remember for one minute (roleplay only). The name returns. |
| 5 | **A distant sound.** Carries on the wind — a woman's voice, or a child's, or a dog. Indistinct. Active Perception check (each PC may try; no gating) localizes to roughly the direction of Gavek's Rest. Atmospheric only. |
| 6 | **The silver stirs.** Any PC carrying moon-silver (Pella's ribbon if recovered; the Howl-Stones lieutenant's finger-bone; the Silver Rose's petal — Kessa from S01; the reliquary once taken) feels it warm briefly. A one-minute sensation, no mechanical effect. |

## Wandering Encounter Table (d6 per 30 min in stronghold; d6 per 2 hours on trail)

### Wilderness trail (between Varduin → Mensa's → Howl-Stones → Gavek's Rest)

| d6 | Encounter |
|---|---|
| 1-2 | Nothing. |
| 3 | **A Bone-Collector scout** (1 × Bandit, CR ⅛) crossing the trail ahead. Perception DC 13 to spot first. Combat or stealth. If the scout escapes or is killed silently, no consequence. If the scout flees back to the cell and reports, the cell goes on alert 6 hours sooner. |
| 4 | **Late-spring blizzard.** 1-2 hour delay; no mechanical cost unless the party has no shelter (then Cold Pulses accelerate — roll at 5-minute intervals during the blizzard). |
| 5 | **A Solamnic courier** riding hard north (single rider, CR 0 commoner-equivalent, non-combatant). Carrying messages from Abanasinia to a Solamnic holding near Neraka-border about Bone-Collector raids. Useful intelligence if parleyed (DC 12 Persuasion to get him to stop). Flavor and foreshadowing. |
| 6 | **The wind speaks.** The DM whispers one sentence to a random PC — a memory-fragment from one of the rooms already visited, or from the PC's own backstory. No save, no effect beyond roleplay. |

### Stronghold (inside Gavek's Rest)

| d6 | Encounter |
|---|---|
| 1-2 | Nothing. |
| 3 | **A Cultist on errand** — 1 × Bandit crossing a corridor. Roll Stealth to avoid; if combat, 25 XP. |
| 4 | **Collapse** (rev 3 from #0001 onward: d3-randomized). 1 = upper floor beam falls (Acrobatics DC 12 or 1d4 bludg, prone, in Scenes 03-04 only); 2 = the well shaft rumbles and loose stones fall (climb DC increases from 10 to 15); 3 = a cell door slams shut suddenly (no mechanical cost, dramatic). |
| 5 | **Pella (off-screen) causes a distraction** — she bites, kicks, or breaks something. The party, if listening, catches the commotion from wherever they are (Perception DC 12). This is a gift to the players — it confirms Pella is alive and fighting, AND draws Cultists away from their posts. Next stronghold guard-encounter has -1 creature. |
| 6 | **The silver stirs.** Same as Cold Pulse 6. |

## Pacing Guidance

- Wilderness: the party spends ~4-5 hours on the trail (Scenes 01→02, then 02→03). Expect 2-3 Wandering rolls total. Cold Pulses every 10 min = roughly 25 Cold Pulse rolls — more than needed; roll lazily (every 30 min of real-time DM attention) rather than religiously.
- Stronghold: ~45-90 min in-fiction from entry to climax. 1-3 Cold Pulse rolls (upper level only); 1-2 Wandering rolls.

## Rubric v1.1 compliance

- ✓ **Mechanical fairness cross-dimensional-harmlessness:** no outcome in either table inflicts ability-check disadvantage. Cold Pulse outcome 3 costs HP (clean resource). Outcomes 4-6 are roleplay / atmospheric only.
- ✓ **Atmospheric landing per-PC tracking:** outcomes 4-6 are single-PC affecting; document which PC was affected in the session log.

## Designer notes

- **Pella's off-screen distraction (Wandering 5)** is the session's most novel interrupt. It ties mechanics to her agency as an NPC — she is not just a quest-target, she is a tiny active agent doing her own small work against Keloth.
- **Do not force-trigger.** Dice carry the pressure. If the dice are quiet, so is the tomb.
