## The real decision the adventure asks

Two overlapping:

1. **The trade.** Keloth offers: attune the Varran-reliquary; Pella walks free; the cell continues unharried. Accept / refuse / negotiate / betray.
2. **The reliquary.** Even after the trade question is settled, the artifact remains. Attune (Aelric alone can)? Destroy (Grom's forge-rite)? Keep unattuned? Return to the Rose Council like the Silver Rose?

Party-leveling: ends at L4 (combined XP from S01 + #0002 = ~900 XP).

## Design features unique to #0002

- **Three-location structure** (travel + waystation + trap + stronghold) is a structural flip from #0001's single-dungeon delve.
- **Non-combatant child NPC** with own agency and voice.
- **Moral-complex antagonist** (Keloth) whose surrender-offer creates a gut-check scene.
- **Reliquary = grief-economy inversion** of the Silver Rose (gives one memory, takes four). Builds cross-adventure thematic coherence.
- **Earned shortcut** (the well in the Stable Yard) following Jaquays discipline.
- **Rubric v1.1 compliance built-in:** Cold Pulse redesigned to cost HP (not check-disadvantage); per-PC atmospheric reception documented in each room's GM Notes; inter-PC chain-reactions designed into climax.
