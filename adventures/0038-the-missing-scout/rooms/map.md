## Tactical note — Room 02B-2 (East Valley Holdout)

A shallow cave entrance, 15 ft deep, 10 ft wide. Two guards with shortswords and hand crossbows. Brek is tied to a rock at the rear of the cave.

Guards use half-cover from cave entrance (+2 AC). Taris has advantageous position if Hunter's Mark is applied before they are alerted.

**Stealth approach:** Taris leads, DC 13 group Stealth. On success: PCs get a surprise round.
