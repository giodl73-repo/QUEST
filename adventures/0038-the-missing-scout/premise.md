# 0038 — The Missing Scout

## The adventure in one sentence

Unit scout Brek Aldren didn't return from his advance route; the party finds what happened to him, receives orders to abandon the search, and must decide whether to follow them.

## Setting

Day 3 of patrol. Unit scout Brek Aldren was sent ahead (per standard protocol) at dawn. He has not returned. Standard return window: 2 hours. It is now 4 hours. The party is at the Ridgehead Marker. Captain Veth sends a courier: continue patrol, log the absence, do not divert search resources.

## Hook

The party has a direct order: don't search. Taris is the navigator. He has walked every foot of this route. He knows exactly where Brek would have gone.

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d12

## Scenes

1. **Briefing** — standard orders. Brek assigned as advance scout.
2. **Ridgehead, 4 hours overdue** — Brek hasn't returned. Party waits. Courier arrives with Veth's order: continue patrol, log the absence.
3. **Taris reads the map** — the advance scout route takes Brek east of the ford. East. Same direction as the men Serrak described.
4. **Decision point** — follow orders (continue patrol) or divert east (defy orders, Cohesion d12→d10 risk).
5. **If diverted east:** Taris leads. They find Brek — alive, tied, guarded by two men in unmarked gear. Rescue combat.
6. **If orders followed:** Patrol continues. No combat. Brek is not found this session. The unit returns with 11 soldiers (Brek absent, missing, not dead — Cohesion d12→d10 still triggers per the session-design: Cohesion degrades if a unit member is lost, and Brek is operationally lost this session regardless of party choice).

## Cohesion degradation trigger

**Cohesion degrades from d12 to d10 this session** because Brek is operationally lost (per campaign design brief: "Cohesion d12→d10 if scout is lost"). This applies regardless of whether the party searches — a unit member was lost on their watch.

## Hint placement

- **H2 seed:** The men guarding Brek (if found) are under orders from someone in the command chain — but not Veth. They reference "the arrangement." Taris hears this.

## Spotlight

Navigator Taris Cole. His map shows the east route. His Favored Terrain applies. His arc seed: he has been correcting command's map for two years. This is the first time it directly matters where he leads the unit.

## NPC

- **Brek Aldren** (unit scout, workshop-canon) — young, scared, knows one thing he wasn't supposed to hear.
- **Two unmarked guards** — combat encounter if party diverts east.
