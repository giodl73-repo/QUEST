## Pre-Session

<!-- SURPRISE duty-triggered -->
Brek Aldren. Twenty-two years old. Second tour. He had been assigned advance scout because he was fast and because Taris had marked his reliability on the formation brief. Not a favor. An assessment.

Taris had noted Brek's position in formation before departure. This was the heuristic's logic: when you track formation, you know when someone is missing. He would know by the second waypoint.

<!-- SURPRISE duty-triggered -->
Dorn read the contract before departure. He did not say so. He put it away. He checked Carra's position. Fest. Oln. Brek was at the front of the file, not in Dorn's three, but close enough that Dorn had been tracking him unconsciously. When Brek left as advance scout at dawn, Dorn noted the departure with the part of his mind that never stopped noting these things.

## Scene 0 — Briefing

Standard orders. Brek assigned as advance scout per standard protocol. Captain Veth handed Varet the patrol packet. *"Scout first, formation standard. You're back by nightfall."*

*"The advance route takes Brek east of the ford before coming back,"* Taris said, quietly, to Varet as they formed up. He pointed to the route on his map. *"If something goes wrong east of the ford — that's two miles off route. Nobody would know until he was overdue."*

Varet read the map. *"How long is the return window?"* Taris: *"Standard: two hours. I'm starting the clock now."*

---

## Scene 1 — Ridgehead: Four Hours Overdue

The unit reached the Ridgehead Marker. Taris checked his watch. Four hours since Brek's departure. Two hours overdue.

They waited ten more minutes.

A courier rode in from Thorngate. Handed Varet a sealed note. She read it: *Continue patrol. Log the absence. Do not divert search resources.* Captain Veth's signature.

**Decision point:**

Varet read the order to the unit. She did not add commentary. She looked at Taris.

Taris said: *"His route goes east of the ford. There's a ravine cut at two miles. You can't see into it from the trail."* He showed her the map. He marked it with his finger. He did not say "we should go." He showed her where Brek would be.

Dorn said: *"He's been in that ravine for two hours."* Flat. Informative. Not an appeal.

Lenne said: *"If he's injured and it's been four hours—"* She did not finish the sentence. She did not need to.

**Varet's decision:** *"We divert east. Formal record: scout overdue, search conducted per unit safety protocol. I'll log the order received. I'll log the deviation and my reasoning."* She wrote it in the journal as she spoke: *"Order received: continue patrol, do not divert. Deviation: unit safety protocol. Brek Aldren, advance scout, 4 hours overdue. Route east of ford. Search initiated."*

**Cohesion note:** Order defied → Cohesion d12 → d10. This triggers regardless of outcome. The die size changes at session end.

**Research coding (RV5 pre-test):** Character Agency at the DECISION POINT (before depletion is felt, but after the degradation trigger fires). The party did not agonize. Taris showed the map. Dorn stated a fact. Lenne indicated consequence. Varet wrote it down and moved. Four separate agency contributions arriving at the same choice independently. **v1.9 independent-motivation convergence:** four PCs, different registers, same non-delay action.

**Combat end: Round 2.** Total: surprise round + 2 full rounds. Clean resolution.

**Interrogation (Guard1, willing):**
Varet asked one question: *"Who sent you?"*

Guard1: *"We were paid. We were told the unit wouldn't come east. That was the whole brief. We were told the commander knows this route is flagged, she won't look east."*

He did not know who gave that brief. He had a contact name — *"Ossren's man"* — which meant nothing to the party yet. He had been hired three weeks ago.

**Brek freed.** Taris cut the rope (automatic). Brek was bruised but uninjured. He sat up. He looked at Varet. He said: *"They said you wouldn't look east, Commander. That you knew the route was flagged."*

Varet: *"I didn't know."*

Brek: *"I didn't think so. That's why I'm saying it."*

**H2 delivery:** *"The commander knows this route is flagged."* — Brek repeating what he heard. Implicates Varet in something she is not part of. Her name was used to indicate compliance. She was not compliant. She just didn't know.
