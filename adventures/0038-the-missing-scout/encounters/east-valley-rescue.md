# East Valley Rescue

## Setup

2 Unmarked Guards (Thug stat blocks, MM p.350) vs. party of 4 (L3) + possible unit soldiers.

**Note:** This encounter only occurs if the party defies the order to continue patrol. Defying the order triggers Cohesion d12→d10 regardless of outcome.

## CR / XP Budget

- 2 × Thug (CR 1/2, 100 XP each) = 200 XP
- Party of 4 L3: Medium threshold 600 XP. 200 XP = Easy.
- **Assessment:** Easy encounter with meaningful stakes (Brek's life; order-defiance consequences). The difficulty is moral, not mechanical.

## Terrain

Shallow cave entrance: 15 ft deep, 10 ft wide. Cave grants guards half-cover from outside (+2 AC). Brek tied at rear (40 ft from entrance). Difficult terrain inside (uneven stone).

## Action economy

2 Guards vs 4 PCs. Clear party advantage. Intended to be winnable without significant resource expenditure.

## Key tactical choices

1. **Stealth approach** (DC 13 group): surprise round. Taris's Pass Without Trace (+10 group Stealth) makes this near-automatic.
2. **Direct approach**: Guards alerted; hand crossbows fire (1d6+2) in opening round.
3. **Dorn:** Menacing Attack on first guard to protect Brek's position.

## Guard behavior

- Round 1: both fire hand crossbows from cover.
- Round 2: if one guard downed, the other moves to Brek's position to threaten him.
- Parley option: DC 14 Intimidation (Dorn) — one guard surrenders. He reveals nothing useful except: "We were paid. We were told the unit wouldn't come east." He names no employer.

## Resolution

- Brek freed. Tied with good rope (DC 12 Str or blade).
- Brek's information: "They said you wouldn't look east, Commander."
- **H2 seed delivered** if party asks Brek what he heard.

## Post-encounter

- Party must return to patrol route. Cohesion d12→d10 is already triggered by order-defiance.
- If party did NOT divert: Brek is missing, Cohesion d12→d10 triggers on unit loss.
- Either way: Cohesion degrades this session.
