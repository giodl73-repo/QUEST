# Room 00 — Stonehaven Entry

## Read-aloud

> Stonehaven is larger than expected — a walled market town with a mining settlement running up the eastern hill. The entry gate is busy. The road you've been on for six sessions ends here, spreading out into streets. Each of you has somewhere to go.

## DM notes

- **The four arrive together.** Naturally; they've been traveling together for six sessions. There is no ceremony at the gate.
- **The separation that doesn't happen:** In Room 00, each PC's destination becomes visible. Vess has an address. Brek has nothing left to do. Sera has a name. Tam has a wayshrine. They could each go separately.
  - **Brek does not leave.** He stands at the gate junction looking south toward the mining district. He does not volunteer why he's staying. Per heuristic: he works out what he thinks while doing the thing. He is not doing anything yet.
  - **Tam notices** (Insight passive 13): Brek has resolved his reason for being here and has not moved toward the exit. This is unusual for someone who announced himself as a person with a destination.
- **First Reciprocal Act this session:** Before the group splits toward their destinations, Vess says to Sera, without prompting: *"The man you're looking for — scholar, traveling with a research pack? There was someone at the market gate, rented room above the tanner. Saw him when we came in."* She does not explain how she knows what Sera is looking for. She simply noted it. Per her heuristic: she notices what changes, what's different, who's new.
  - Log: [Vess → Sera → scholar's location, unprompted]. Trigger type: situational (professional observation habit).
