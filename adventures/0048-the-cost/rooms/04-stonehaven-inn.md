# Room 04 — Stonehaven Inn (Session Close)

## Read-aloud

> A good fire. The four of you at a table. Each of you has done what you came here to do, or decided not to. The road was the reason you were traveling together. The road, technically, is done.

## DM notes

- **Session close scene.** The DM does not direct this scene — let the PCs inhabit it.
- **What is true at this point:**
  - Brek has his money (if he took it) or has something else. His reason for being on the road is resolved.
  - Vess has delivered, refused, or redirected. Her contract is complete or forfeited.
  - Sera knows where Mikela went and what probably happened. She knows more than she wanted to know.
  - Tam performed the rite. He still does not know why he was sent on this road specifically. He suspects the answer was the people, not the shrine.
- **RV10 test moment:** Does anyone name what the group has become? Not necessarily with ceremony — even a small naming counts. *"I suppose we're going the same way tomorrow"* is a naming. Silence is not a naming. The DM logs whatever happens.
- **Act-without-announcement finale for S6:** Brek, at some point during the evening, puts the 240 gp on the table — the money Torven gave him — and divides it four ways. He does not explain why. He is not being generous with someone else's money; he is acknowledging that three other people were at the ford with him and had nothing to gain from it.
  - This is his intra-session restraint instance: receives the acknowledgment of the group (they came down the wrong fork for him) and expresses it through action, without announcement.
  - If PCs object: Brek says *"You were there."* That is the whole explanation.
  - Log: [Brek → group → split the money, unprompted]. Trigger type: relationship-driven. Intra-session restraint (v1.12).

End HP + 1 hit die before S7. This is the last regeneration before the finale.
