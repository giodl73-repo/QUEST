# Room 01 — Merchant House (Doss Network Contact)

## Read-aloud

> The merchant house is three stories, solid stone, with two men at the door who do not look like clerks. The house name above the door: Havel-Aldric Trading. The same name as the merchant you met on the road.

## DM notes

- **The connection:** Merchant Havel from Session 1 is a front. "Havel-Aldric Trading" is the Stonehaven face of the Doss network. Havel himself is the network's courier-fixer — he scouts travelers and identifies packages in transit. The travelers met him at the roadblock. He knew what was in Vess's pack. He said nothing.
  - **Vess:** Passive 16 Perception: she recognizes the house name immediately. The merchant she met at the roadblock is connected to the package she's been carrying. She was selected specifically because she was already on the right road.
  - **Brek:** He came with Vess. He sees the door, sees the guards, and says nothing. He positions himself to Vess's left. Per heuristic: interposes between someone and danger without announcing it.
  - Log: [Brek → Vess → positioned without announcement]. Trigger type: relationship-driven.
- **Vess's decision point:** She can deliver the package. She can refuse. She can surrender it to Cassel's investigation (requires getting word to Cassel, which Tam can arrange via Gilean courier network).
  - If she delivers: she is paid. She completes her contract. The package goes to people who will use it badly. But her license is clean.
  - If she refuses: she forfeits the fee (50 gp), but the network loses a shipment.
  - If she redirects: the network loses a shipment AND it goes to investigation. No fee. The most costly personal option.
- **Tam accompanies Vess** (if Brek came). He does not say why. He has his wayshrine to reach. He is here first. Log: [Tam → Vess → accompanied to merchant house without being asked]. Trigger type: relationship-driven.

## Exits

West: Stonehaven market (Room 00).
