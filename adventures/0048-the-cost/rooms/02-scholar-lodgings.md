# Room 02 — Scholar Aldric's Lodgings

## Read-aloud

> A small room above the tanner's shop. Aldric Halven, sixties, documents everywhere. He looks at Sera when she gives the name and does not look away. "Mikela. Yes. I know where she was going. I don't know if she got there."

## DM notes

- **Aldric Halven:** The person Sera has been looking for on this road. He knew her person (Torvens Mikela — a researcher investigating the Doss network's documentation trail, because it intersects with something Sera cares about deeply). Mikela was taken into custody three months ago by the Stonehaven authority (Overseer Crel's operation). Aldric does not know if she is alive.
- **What Aldric tells Sera:** *"She was following the same documentation trail you've been following, from the other end. She found the mill contracts. She found the fraudulent deeds. She was going to bring them to the circuit magistrate at Millhaven. She never arrived."*
  - This is the hardest moment of the campaign for Sera: she has been looking for someone for three sessions, and the answer is uncertain and bad.
  - Sera's heuristic: delays commitment to a course of action until she has more data. She now has data. The commitment cannot wait.
- **What Sera does with this information:**
  - She writes it down. Not because she is a scholar — because she is processing it.
  - She tells Brek. Not because it is useful to him. Because she needs to tell someone who does not require an explanation.
  - Log: [Sera → Brek → told him, unprompted]. Trigger type: relationship-driven.
- **Brek's response:** He is there if Sera went to Room 02. He does not prompt her. He does not ask. He sits in the chair nearest the door and waits. That is all.
  - Log: [Brek → Sera → waited without being asked]. Trigger type: relationship-driven.

## Exits

South: Stonehaven market (Room 00).
