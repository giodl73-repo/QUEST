## Convergent P1

All five lenses: the session delivered what the campaign was designed to produce. Nine Reciprocal Acts in one session. The Havel-Aldric connection (Session 1 NPC as the network scout) was the campaign's best structural surprise — the beginning and the ending connected.

## Convergent P2

Vess's delivery fee forfeiture (50 gp) was a real cost the session required of her. She paid it. Three lenses note this as the campaign's most significant PC sacrifice — more than Brek's fire damage, more than following the wrong fork. She forfeited income and professional standing for people she has known for six days and for a commitment she decided on by herself.

## Divergent

Roleplayer: Tam's sentence ("I suppose we're traveling the same road tomorrow") is the best line in the campaign. It names the continuation without naming what they are. The understatement is the precision.

Improviser: Brek's chair occupancy is the campaign's most refined act-without-announcement. It has no tactical value. It is purely presence-as-care. The session did not design it; it emerged from six sessions of Brek's physical-cover heuristic applied to a non-physical situation.

## Verdict

PASS 76.0 / 80. Campaign is at its peak. S7 needs to close correctly.
