# Grisk, the Watchman

## Appearance
Adult male gully dwarf, broad relative to his height, with the posture of someone who has been in watchman position long enough for it to become a stance. A length of iron construction rod carried in his left hand — not a weapon exactly, but in his hand rather than at his belt. His eyes track the party from the moment they enter his sight line, but he does not advance.

## Stat Block

HP 12. AC 11. Speed 20 ft.
- **Attack:** Iron rod +3 to hit, 1d6+1 bludgeoning.
- **Behavior:** Interposes only. Does not initiate. Retreats to R3 at half HP. Morale breaks below 6 HP.

## Public Knowledge

- He has been here for three weeks. He came with his group.
- He has seen workers come before. They always leave.
- He is here because Velantha healed his grandfather. That is the entire explanation, if he gives it.

## Information Readiness Condition

Grisk does not provide information through persuasion or insight rolls. He provides it through the specific question: *"Why are you here?"* Not "what are you doing" or "who are you" — *why*. The phrasing matters. If the party asks why (in any words that map to "why you specifically, in this building"), he says: *"She came for us. We come back for her."* He says this once. He does not elaborate. If the party presses for more, he points toward R5 (the direction of the inner cathedral) without speaking.

## Behavior Matrix

| Party action | Grisk's response |
|---|---|
| Party enters R1 | Grisk moves from R3 to R2 to warn Torva |
| Party enters R2 without stopping | Grisk positions himself between party and shrines/satchel |
| Party stops and waits | Grisk waits. He does not advance either. |
| Party asks what he wants | He says: "Go." And points at the exit. |
| Party asks why he is here | He says: "She came for us. We come back for her." |
| Mig de-escalates | Grisk takes two steps back. That is his de-escalation signal. |
| Party attacks | Grisk fights to defend the congregation. Retreats at half HP. |
| Party leaves | Grisk returns to R3 (or, on subsequent visit, to R4) to watch. |

## Arc-Completion

**Condition:** The party asks Grisk *why* he is here (not what he is doing, not what he wants — why).

**The act:** He says: *"She came for us. We come back for her."*

He does not say more. He does not need to. The arc completes in this sentence. The DM notes it. Grisk's body language after: he sits down somewhere, or leans against the wall with the rod loosely in his hand. Not hostile. Not friendly. Present.

**Alternative (R4, if Torva encounters happen there):** Torva's version of the same arc-completion: *"She came. So we came."* If both Grisk and Torva say their version, note both in the session log — bilateral minor arc-completion.
