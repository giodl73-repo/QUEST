## Innovation Flags

```
INNOVATION FLAG: Awakened Mind as social bypass
Dimension affected: Character Agency, Mechanical Fairness
What happened: Renn used Awakened Mind (Warlock class feature) to establish first contact
with Mig, bypassing the language barrier entirely. The social encounter resolved without a
Persuasion check, Insight check, or any verbal exchange. The module's social encounter
was designed assuming communication would require either common language or skill checks.
The telepathy feature made both unnecessary. (Scene 3)
Why the rubric missed it: No current dimension captures "class feature replaces skill
architecture." Mechanical Fairness covers cross-character mechanical compounds (v1.4) but
not single-character social bypass. Character Agency covers heuristic-driven decisions but
not feature-driven encounter-shape decisions.
```

```
INNOVATION FLAG: Fumble-as-arc-activation
Dimension affected: Atmospheric Landing, Character Agency
What happened: Calla's documentation-suspension arc fired because her Insight check
critically failed (1 on the die). The arc activated because her categorical vocabulary
broke on contact with the congregation's practice — not because she successfully observed
something unusual. The failure IS the arc beat. (Scene 2.1, repeated Scene 5)
Why the rubric missed it: Player-style documentation-arc and the broader atmospheric
landing dimension both assume arc activation follows a discovery — something noticed,
something encountered. This session established a variant: the arc fires when the observer's
framework fails, not when the observed thing exceeds the framework. The trigger is the
blank, not the content.
```
