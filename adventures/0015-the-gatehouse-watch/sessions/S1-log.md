# S1 LOG — The Gatehouse Watch

## Session Summary

The Witnesses arrived at the Halted Spire under Magistra Doran's commission: assess and clear the site. They found the gatehouse occupied by seven gully dwarves — Velantha's congregation — led by a keeper named Mig who had spent three weeks trying to reach the Nave shrine. The party's threat-assessment (Dren), patient observation (Calla, Maret), and Renn's Awakened Mind contact resolved the gatehouse defense socially. A Ghoul in the crawlspace under the guardroom floor — the congregation's actual fear — was destroyed in two rounds. Mig then led the party to the inner door and stepped through with their cooperation. Route D. The inner cathedral is now accessible. Mig's arc-completion fired.

Three relic signs landed: the herb smell in R1 and R5 (olfactory), the shrine arrangements in R2 and R5 (devotional), and the warmth anomaly at R2 and R6. The tally stone in R4 was read. Reaction moment at R6 fired. The field satchel's manifest is satisfied.

## Scene 1.1 — Exterior Approach

### Setup
The party arrives at the approach road below the Halted Spire. Midmorning. The half-built cathedral looms above Mireth-on-the-Hill: rough-cut stone, open sky where the roof should be, scaffolding on the upper sections. The main gate is at the end of a short road up the hill.
