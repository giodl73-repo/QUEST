## Questions Raised About the Module or the System

1. **The Ghoul's designed threat (paralysis) requires hitting AC.** Against a level 3 party of 4 with AC 14-16, the Ghoul hits roughly 45% of the time — not enough to reliably land its signature ability in a 2-round fight. For the workshop's combat design, note: CR 1 creatures need either higher to-hit bonuses or a secondary threat that fires on miss (lair action, triggered ability, environmental hazard).

2. **The social encounter's fallback path (Dren's second grate Perception attempt via congregation body language) was an unwritten ruling.** Should be written into the module's GM Notes as: "If the party misses the grate (Perception < 10), the congregation's behavior — they watch the grate, Mig sits near it, they don't warn the party — is a directed second prompt. The DM may allow a second Perception check prompted by congregation focus, no re-roll penalty."

3. **Awakened Mind as social bypass is not anticipated by any module.** This will recur across C3 since Renn is carrying it throughout. Consider whether the modules for 0016-0021 should have an Awakened Mind note in their social encounter GM Notes: "A PC with telepathy can bypass language barriers; Mig and the congregation will respond to telepathic contact in images and emotion rather than structured Common."
