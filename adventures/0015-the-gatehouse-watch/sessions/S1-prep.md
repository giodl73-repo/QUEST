## Party-specific threads

- **Maret:** Osel's death (no longer hears the pattern from the next room). The congregation's morning rite in R2 — structured, repetitive, devotional without formal tradition — resonates with her own practice. This is the craft-witness moment. The DM should notice the structural parallel and play Maret's stillness when she sees the congregation's rite.
- **Calla:** Tarrin closed his eyes at the founding verse; she doesn't know if she's telling it right without a witness. The shrine arrangements in R2 are not a tradition she knows — and she cannot categorize them. Documentation-suspension fires here. Play the stillness.
- **Renn:** Mira died before his pact's power could reach her. He made an unauthorized transaction to help someone; so did Velantha. The V.S. initials on the satchel may surface his parallel — not yet, but if he examines the satchel, note his reaction to evidence of an unauthorized act that still had consequence.
- **Dren:** Aldis died asking for a healer who didn't come. When Dren understands Mig is holding a healer's bag — and came here *because a healer came for her people* — play the voice-tag pause before "healer." Do not narrate it. Note it.
