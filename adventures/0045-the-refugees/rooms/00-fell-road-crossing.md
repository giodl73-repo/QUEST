# Room 00 — Fell Road Crossing

## Read-aloud

> A toll station: two officers, a gate pole, a small booth. An elderly woman is explaining something to the officers, who have already decided. Behind her: ten people, adults and children, with packs and handcarts. They are waiting.

## DM notes

- **The situation:** Elder Daven Sorr is presenting travel papers. The papers are valid. The toll officers know the papers are valid. They are processing slowly, asking clarifying questions, requesting additional documentation that doesn't exist for this type of travel. They are not lawbreaking. They are making the passage expensive in time and dignity.
- **Toll officers:** Road-warden regulars. CR 1/2 (Scout stat blocks). They follow orders; they know the rules; they are applying them selectively. They do not want a fight. They want the refugees to go away or pay.
- **Tam's intervention:** Per heuristic, he moves to the front and presents his Gilean seal. He does not confront the officers. He says: *"Gilean circuit clergy. This group has my travel attestation."* He does not have authority to give travel attestation. But a road-warden officer stopping a Gilean priest's travel companions is paperwork he does not want. The officers confer for 30 seconds. The gate opens.
  - Deception DC 10 (Tam's bonus +4) or Persuasion DC 12 (Tam +4). Either approach works. Roll it — the outcome should feel earned.
  - If Tam fails: the officers require a "transit fee" of 2 gp per non-clergy traveler. The four PCs could pay for the refugees (11 people × 2 gp = 22 gp). If they do: log as group Reciprocal Act.
- **Condition:** The officers stamp the papers but say they'll follow the group to ensure "orderly transit." Two officers, 80 ft behind, for the first four miles.

## Exits

South: Scrubland approach (Room 01).
