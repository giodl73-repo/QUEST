# Room 02 — Fell Road Bridge

## Read-aloud

> The bridge is wooden, thirty feet across, over a twelve-foot drop to a dry streambed. It takes the group about two minutes to cross — they must go single-file. The ridge above the west bank is twenty feet up. Two men are coming down it now.

## DM notes

- **Road agents:** Two Bandit stat blocks (CR 1/8 each, AC 12, HP 11, Scimitar +3 1d6+1). They want the refugees' goods — they have done this before, on this bridge, to people moving in groups too slow to run. They are not brave; they are opportunistic.
- **Combat terrain:**
  - Bridge is single-file width (5 ft). Difficult terrain for anyone not at the front.
  - The road agents come from the west ridge (high ground, +1 to hit on first round).
  - Refugees are crossing — they are NOT in combat; they move south as fast as they can.
  - **Brek's position:** His heuristic puts him at the front of physical threats automatically. He will be on the bridge when the agents come down. This is his fight. He anchors the bridge. With his AC 14 + CON + STR + Rage: this is not dangerous for him. The fight is over in 2 rounds.
- **Combat resolution:**
  - Round 1: Brek Rages (bonus action). Reckless Attack (+4 to hit). Greataxe: 1d12+6 (Rage).
  - Agent 1: likely drops in Round 1 (Brek does average 10-12 damage; agents have 11 HP).
  - Round 2: Brek on Agent 2. May not need Frenzy. Frenzy: bonus action attack 1d12+6, but 1 exhaustion after.
  - If Brek uses Frenzy: note the exhaustion. He takes it without complaint. This is act-for-strangers at personal cost.
- **If another PC contributes:**
  - Vess: already in flanking position (she saw this coming). Sneak Attack 2d6+4 if she has advantage or Brek is adjacent. She fires once and does not advance onto the bridge.
  - Tam: moves to the back of the refugee group; heals anyone who takes incidental damage.
  - Sera: cast Shield? Arcane Ward? This is the first moment she might deploy a defensive spell for someone who isn't herself.

## Exits

South: Haverford road (Room 03).
