**Fell Road characteristics:**
- 8 miles, flat scrubland
- No cover except low brush (Stealth DC 14 to hide)
- One bridge crossing at mile 5 (Fell Road Bridge)
- Toll officers follow at 80 ft behind the group for the first 4 miles, then drop back to 200 ft
- Road agents wait at the bridge (they watch from the ridge, time the approach)

**Group movement:** Eleven refugees move at 2 mph (elderly, children). 8 miles = 4 hours. Weather: overcast, cold, no rain.

**Vess's route-scouting benefit:** If Vess scouts ahead (she will, per heuristic), she spots the road agents at the bridge ridge in time for the group to know they're coming (DC 14 Perception, passive 16 — automatic). She doesn't announce this to the group — she positions herself forward of the group's right flank.
