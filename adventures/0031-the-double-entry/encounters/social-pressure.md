# Combat Encounter — Pale's Watcher

## Trigger

This encounter fires only if Pale has sent someone to observe Nenne's meetings — which Tessaly's intelligence does not cover (she does not know Pale monitors his clients). Trigger condition: roll 1d4 during Scene 2 — on a 1, Pale's observer is watching Weavers' Street from the opposite building.

## Composition

**One Guild Watcher** — Use Bandit stat block (SRD, CR 1/8) with modifications

- **AC** 12 (leather) | **HP** 11 | **Speed** 30 ft.
- **STR** 11, **DEX** 14, **CON** 11, **INT** 12, **WIS** 10, **CHA** 10
- **Skills:** Perception +2, Stealth +4
- **Passive Perception** 12
- **Actions:** Shortsword +3, 1d6+2 piercing
- **XP:** 25 (not a significant combat threat)

The watcher is not here to fight. He is here to record who visits Nenne. If he sees Mira enter and exit, he follows her. If he identifies her as guild-adjacent, he reports to Pale.

## Non-Combat Resolution (preferred)

Darro spots the watcher from the alley (Perception DC 12 vs Watcher's Stealth DC 13 — contested). If Darro wins: he signals Cael. Cael positions himself between the watcher and the building. The watcher, observing Cael, decides this particular visit is not worth pressing. He files a note about the visitor description and leaves.

If no one spots the watcher: he follows Mira for two blocks after the meeting. Mira's Perception (passive 13) against his Stealth (+4). If she detects him, she loops a block and loses him. If she does not, he identifies the direction she headed — toward the Cordwainer Lane district. He reports this to Pale.

## Encounter notes

This is an optional complication — it fires on a 1-in-4 chance and is meant to introduce Pale as an active observer of his own clients, not just a passive information seller. If it fires, it seeds S4's premise: Pale is watching and the audit is coming.

If combat is actually forced (watcher is cornered and fights), he is trivial. He knows nothing useful (he is paid per observation, not briefed). He is more valuable let go than captured.

**XP budget:** 25 XP. Not a meaningful combat encounter — it is a story encounter.
