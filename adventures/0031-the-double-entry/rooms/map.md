## DM notes

The house is designed for a single-path social session — Mira goes in through the front, Darro is in the alley as support, Cael is on the street. The map is simple because the session is not an extraction; it is an observation. The complexity is in the conversation, not the space.

The parallel books are in the lower left drawer of the working office desk — Nenne does not offer these, but Mira can see the drawer has a different lock from the other drawers (Investigation DC 12 to notice; DC 15 to notice the drawer has been opened recently and relatched).
