# Room 02 — Working Office

## Read-aloud
> A real working desk — two stacks of accounts, a pen holder with three ink weights, a ruler worn at the edge. Two ledgers visible: one open, one closed with a different lock on the clasp. The woman behind the desk watches you with the expression of someone running a calculation you are not privy to.

## Features

- **The open ledger.** Legitimate accounts, black ink, Nenne's formal hand. Investigation DC 10 to read: textile purchase orders, sales ledgers, factor's commissions. Clean.
- **The closed ledger.** A different lock, a different ink weight visible at the edge. This is the parallel book. Investigation DC 12: the lock is recently oiled; she has been into this ledger within the last two days. Persuasion DC 12 to see the client records it contains.
- **Nenne's desk drawer.** Lower left, fresh-locked since the standard drawers. Investigation DC 15: recently opened and relatched.
- **Nenne herself.** Full NPC file: `npcs/nenne-ashwick.md`. She meets Mira with professional courtesy and active scrutiny.
- **The three signature characteristics (Mira's study target):** The capital N with a left-leading upstroke; the date column formatted with a forward slash between day and month; ink weight heaviest at line bottoms (pressure habit). Investigation DC 13 to identify all three. Mira can record them in the paper case immediately.

## GM Notes

This is Mira's room — the forgery study. The three characteristics are the session's deliverable for the RV3 accumulation. They do not require action; they require observation and documentation. Mira photographs, records, updates the case.

The moment Nenne shows the client ledger is the moment the session pivots. She is showing something she did not intend to show fully, and she knows it. Her Insight (+4) is working against Mira's Deception the whole time.

The office window overlooks the side alley — Darro is visible at ground level if anyone looks down. Nenne does not look out the window during Mira's visit. She is focused on the visitor.

### Memory Fragment (optional)
> *The pen holder has three ink weights because Nenne uses different weights for different categories. Black for legitimate income. Red for corrections. The third ink, unused in two months, was purchased for a category she decided not to create after all.*
