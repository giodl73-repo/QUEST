# Vel

## Appearance

An older woman. Practical. She does not introduce herself.

## Notes

Vel has worked for Nenne for twelve years. She has perfect professional discretion — not because she has been instructed to be discreet, but because she does not find other people's business interesting. She answers questions directly and without elaboration. She is not a source of information. She is not a threat.

If questioned: she gives Mira's cover identity to the Revenue Intelligence review without commentary. She describes the visit accurately. She noticed nothing unusual because she was not trying to notice anything.

She is an ambient NPC — she fills the house's surface without revealing its interior. She is the house's equivalent of Nenne's dried-flower arrangement in the reception room.
