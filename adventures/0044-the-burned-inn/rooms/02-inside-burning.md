# Room 02 — Second Floor (Inside)

## Read-aloud

> The smoke is thick at the ceiling; below knee-level it is passable but worsening. The floor to the right has burned through — a black hole where three boards should be. To the left: a closed door. The east end of the building is visible through a gap in the wall — orange and roaring.

## DM notes

- **Navigation:** Brek must check the back room (Maret said there was a girl). Athletics DC 10 to move through the room without disturbing the floor integrity. Failure: the floor creaks and shifts but holds (2d4 bludgeoning, reduced to 1d4 if CON save DC 10 passed).
- **Back room:** Empty. No girl. A travel bag, a record-ledger, and a small wooden box nailed shut. Brek has approximately 2 rounds to make a decision about whether to take anything.
  - **The ledger:** If taken, it becomes a prop in S2's post-fire scene. It is an inn ledger with traveler names and dates. Sera may review it.
  - If Brek takes nothing: fine. He came for the person. He leaves with the person.
  - If Brek takes the ledger: it is because he saw it and picked it up. He may hand it to Sera without comment when he gets out.
- **Getting Maret out:** She can move on her own feet (she is stunned and hurt but functional). Brek's route back is the way he came — window and stable roof. Maret cannot leap alone (hands burned). Brek lowers her to the stable roof (STR check DC 8 — automatic for Brek).
- **Fire timing:** If Brek spent rounds in the back room, add 1d4 fire per round spent past round 5. He exits with Maret on or before round 10 under normal play.

## Exits

West: window to stable roof (Room 01).
East: burned-through floor (impassable without fall).
