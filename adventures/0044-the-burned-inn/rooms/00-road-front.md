# Room 00 — Road Front

## Read-aloud

> The inn is burning. Orange light, black smoke straight up. A boy stands in the road holding an empty bucket, frozen. Ground floor windows are sheets of flame. At the second-floor window: a woman shouting, inaudible over the noise.

## DM notes

- **Len (stable boy, 14):** Frozen. The bucket was full; he must have dropped it. He will not respond to logical instructions. He needs someone to tell him one specific thing to do (Persuasion or just direct command). Tam's heuristic fires here — he reads Len without announcing it, tells him to go to the well and get the rope. Len does it.
- **Fire assessment (no roll required):** Ground floor impassable. Second floor partially accessible via stable roof. The building will collapse in 20 minutes (16 combat rounds, but DM uses minutes for narrative time).
- **Brek's action:** Per his heuristic, he acts on physical threats first without consulting. He looks at the stable roof, looks at the window, and goes. He does not say "I'll go in." He just moves. DM narration: *"Brek crosses the road without discussion."* He does not ask permission. He does not announce heroism. He goes.
- **If another PC tries to go instead of or alongside Brek:** Let them. If Vess or Tam goes in: valid data. If Sera goes in: note — she has the lowest HP and the most to lose; this is a player-style instance.
- **Maret's lockbox:** Vess's passive Perception 16 spots it near the stable door. A small iron-bound box, carried out before the fire reached the storage area. This is notable — Maret got out her valuables before she got out herself. Vess does not comment but logs it mentally.
