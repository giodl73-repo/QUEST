# Room 03 — Aftermath

## Read-aloud

> The building collapses ten minutes after they clear the roof. The stable and storage building stand. Maret Sunn sits against the stable wall, hands wrapped in cloth. Smoke goes straight up. The road is quiet.

## DM notes

- **Maret's arc fires here.** See npcs/maret-sunn.md for full arc-completion conditions.
  - She looks at Brek. She does not thank him immediately. She is looking at the ruins of the inn. Then she says: *"My son helped me build that. Eight years ago. He was fourteen."* She does not say where her son is now. She does not ask if they want to know. She says it because Brek came out carrying her, and that earned a real sentence.
  - This is her arc-completion. She names her son. She does not explain why. She offers it without asking for anything in return.
- **Sera's documentation moment:** Does Sera write down what Maret said? Or does she write down the fire's structural pattern? Or does she write down nothing and just watch? This is the RV9 secondary test — log whatever she does.
  - If Sera reviews the inn ledger (if Brek took it): she finds a name she recognizes. A traveler logged 12 days ago — name matches someone she has been looking for. H2 seed activates. She does not say anything. Her hand stops on the page.
- **Sleeping arrangements:** The stable is intact. All four travelers and Maret are there tonight. First time they sleep in close proximity.
- **HP carry-over note:** End of S2 HP + 1 hit die (d12 for Brek if damaged) before S3.
