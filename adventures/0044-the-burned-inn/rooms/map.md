**Interior fire timeline (20 minutes):**
- Rounds 1-4: ground floor fully engulfed; rear window accessible
- Rounds 5-10: second floor east end actively burning; rear room reaching smoke-critical
- Round 11+: second floor floor integrity compromised (DC 14 Athletics to move quickly; failure = fall through)
- Round 16: structural collapse — anyone inside takes 6d6 damage

**Stable roof route:** Athletics DC 12 to climb. From roof: leap to rear window (DC 10 Athletics). Fire damage inside: 1d4 fire at start of each turn on rounds 5+.
