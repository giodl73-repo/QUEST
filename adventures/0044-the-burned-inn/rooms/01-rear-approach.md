# Room 01 — Rear Approach / Stable Roof

## Read-aloud

> Stable roof thatched and dry — the fire is still at the front. Peak is twelve feet up; the inn's second-floor rear window is seven feet away. The innkeeper leans out, hands burned, watching the ground she cannot reach.

## DM notes

- **Climbing the stable roof:** Athletics DC 12. Failure by 5+: fall, 1d6 damage, try again. Brek: +6 Athletics, succeeds on anything but a 1.
- **Leaping to the window:** Athletics DC 10 (shorter gap, slightly uphill). Automatic for Brek. Anyone else: DEX save DC 8 to land on the sill without falling back.
- **Maret's condition:** She cannot climb down — her hands are burned (1d4 fire, already taken; she can hold but cannot grip reliably). She is lucid. She is not panicking. She says to whoever reaches the window: *"There's a girl in the back room. Go left, not right — the floor on the right is gone."*
  - **The girl:** There is no girl. Maret is confused from smoke inhalation. The DM should NOT correct this in-scene — let whoever is inside make the decision based on incomplete information. Brek's heuristic will have him check (he acts, not deliberates). The back room is empty.
- **Fire timeline:** Increases urgency but does not prevent success. Brek inside: takes 1d4 fire damage per round starting round 5. He has 32 HP. He can take 6 rounds of fire damage before it becomes serious.

## Exits

West: ground (jump down, 12 ft, 1d6 if not controlled — DC 10 Athletics to land clean).
East: inside (Room 02, second floor).
