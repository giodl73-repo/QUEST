# 0044 — The Burned Inn

## The adventure in one sentence

The only inn for twenty miles is burning; the innkeeper is trapped inside; Brek goes in without being asked.

## Setting

**Wayfarers' Rest, Mile 32** (workshop-canon, south Abanasinia). A substantial inn with a stable and a storage building, set at the road's edge. The travelers arrive at dusk to find the main building on fire. The stable and storage building are not yet burning but are at risk. The innkeeper — **Maret Sunn**, a woman in her fifties — is visible through a second-floor window, shouting, unable to reach the ground.

## Hook

The four travelers from S1 arrive together (or close together) at dusk. The inn is burning. There are no other travelers present who will act. The innkeeper is trapped. The building will collapse within 20 minutes.

## Scenes

1. **Arrival** — the fire is visible from a quarter-mile. Black smoke, orange glow. No one is fighting it. The inn's stable boy (Len, 14) is standing in the road with a bucket, frozen.
2. **Assessment** — the building's ground floor is fully engulfed. The second floor is burning at the east end. There is a rear window accessible by climbing the stable roof (Athletics DC 12). The front entry is not passable.
3. **Brek goes in** — Brek does not discuss this. He uses the stable-roof route. He does not announce he is doing it for anyone. He goes in because someone is trapped. First major Reciprocal Act.
4. **The others act** — what the other three do while Brek is inside. Sera: manages the stable animals (potential secondary crisis — horses spooked, may injure someone). Vess: scouts the rear of the building, finds the innkeeper's storage lockbox outside (she moved it herself before the fire reached). Tam: manages Len (the stable boy), who is panicking and may need Healing Word.
5. **Brek comes back out** — carrying Maret Sunn. The building collapses approximately 3 minutes later. Maret is alive, with burns on her hands and smoke inhalation.

## NPC arc

**Maret Sunn** — arc fires when Brek comes back out carrying her. See npcs/maret-sunn.md.

## Hint placement

- No seeds planted. RV9 primary test session: Brek's act is situational (no backstory). Log trigger type.
