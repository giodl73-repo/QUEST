# Keep Pressure Table

*Temporal pressure for the interior investigation. The party is inside Thorngate during an active siege, asking questions in garrison space, looking at a garrison member's bunk. Time costs trust. Roll once per scene transition (d6).*

## Pressure Table (d6)

| Roll | Event |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | **Undisturbed.** The garrison is occupied with its own morning duties. No one notices the investigation. The keep moves around the party without touching it. |
| 3 | **Asholt walks by.** The Warden crosses the Great Hall or the west wing passage at a distance — not looking for anyone, doing his morning circuit. He does not acknowledge the investigation. DC 12 Stealth if the party is actively searching visible areas (Wil's bunk, the log lectern) when he passes. On a failed check, he sees them and makes a note of it — he does not interfere now, but the GM should mark: Asholt is aware the party is in the garrison quarters. |
| 4 | **A garrison member notices.** A wall-watcher coming off the night shift sees the party near Wil's bunk or the west wall. They say nothing — but they look, and they remember. If the confrontation later turns public (shouting, combat audible below), this garrison member is the one who reports to Asholt that "the party was already up there earlier." |
| 5 | **Wil suspects he's been found.** The party's investigation has been visible enough that word travels — someone saw them at the log, or in the garrison quarters. Wil moves from his normal watch position to the upper ledge (Room 04) and takes the more exposed, harder-to-approach position. Party loses the Path A approach advantage; Path B (battlement traverse) becomes the only unobserved approach. |
| 6 | **Hessa's window narrows.** Asholt is making a pre-assault inspection of the arrow stock — his routine. He will reach the west wall base within the hour. Hessa tells the party quietly: *"You have less time than I thought."* This accelerates the investigation sequence — the party must commit to the confrontation before the stock inspection, or the discovery becomes Asholt's before Hessa can control the sequence. |

## Notes

Roll this table at each of the following scene transitions: (1) Room 01 → Room 02, (2) Room 02 → Room 03, (3) Room 03 → Room 04 approach decision. Three rolls maximum. A result of 5 or 6 on the final roll collapses the timeline toward the confrontation and removes the party's ability to delay.

**Result 5 note:** If Wil relocates to Room 04 early (upper ledge), Path A (west wall direct via ladder) becomes observed — he can see anyone climbing the iron ring ladder. Path B (battlement traverse from Arrow-Walk) remains the unobserved approach. The party can still split: one path to draw attention, one to close the escape route.
