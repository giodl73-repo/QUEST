# Minor Loot & Incidental Treasures

Non-magical items, coins, and setting-grounding objects scattered through the tomb. None requires attunement. Values given in gp unless noted.

## Room 03 — The Pillar Hall

- **Attendants' gravegoods** (4 skeletons × 1 pouch each): 25 gp total in old Ergothian mintage — coins from the late Age of Might, heavy-stamped, not counterfeitable. A collector or historian would pay double (~50 gp) for a full set.
- **The Notched Longsword.** Masterwork Solamnic arming sword, a chunk missing from the blade. 15 gp as scrap. **Key to Room 06 Option C** — fits the sarcophagus lid slot.
- **A scored pillar-rubbing** (if taken): worthless as loot, valuable as evidence of the rose's work.

## Room 04 — The Scriptorium

- **Book I (A Knight's Elegy, annotated):** 0 gp value; roleplay artifact. If sold to a Solamnic archivist (not Brother Laen, who will quietly burn it), 30 gp from a historian.
- **Book II (On Memory):** Ergothian philosophical treatise, intact. 15 gp to a scholar.
- **Book III (Aelwen's Letter, sealed copy):** 0 gp value; the most important evidence in the tomb. Cannot be "sold" — but a character presenting it to Brother Laen changes the negotiation entirely (see his NPC notes).

## Room 05 — The Rose Stair

- **Silver rose-petal** (pressed into the brazier ashes): 25 gp raw silver value. Unsettles anyone who later attunes to the full rose.
- **Bone-Collector's signet** (on desiccated corpse OR on ghoul, depending on encounter): 10 gp to the right fence; faction clue for Neraka. **Seeds adventure #0002.**

## Room 06 — The Sarcophagus Chamber

- **Ilendra's Silver Pendant** (on her remains, inside sarcophagus; only accessible via Option A or D). A simple rose pendant, 40 gp. **NOT CURSED.** The Vaenor family sigil at a simpler scale — her birth-pendant, not the artifact. A character who wears this pendant and meets a surviving Vaenor-Meryn kin is recognized (roleplay beat).
- **Ilendra's sword** (inside sarcophagus, on her remains). Masterwork Knight of the Rose longsword, 50 gp. Functionally identical to a standard longsword (+0, non-magical). Sentimental value: the Order would pay 150 gp to retire it with honors, no questions asked.
- **Ilendra's armor** (the suit that animates as the Animated Armor). Once defeated and de-animated, it is ordinary Knight of the Rose ceremonial plate — 75 gp to a merchant, 250 gp to the Order. It no longer fits Ilendra because she has been dead for a century; no one living can wear it without tailoring.

Note that **the 80 gp fee from Brother Laen is separate** from the tomb's coin-loot. He pays for retrieval; he will not begrudge incidental treasure taken from the cairn, though he would be unhappy if any Order sword or armor were sold rather than returned.
