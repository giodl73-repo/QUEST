Pale's Investigation active check (20) finds inconsistency in two of the pages — the ink weight is wrong and one page has a tiny tear at the margin.

This is the professional cover that Pale can accept without confirming that he found the forgery. He accepts it. He closes the ledger. He says the review is complete.

He found it. He chose not to call it. This is more frightening than if he had failed the check.
