# Room 02 — Forgery Workspace (Back Room)

## Read-aloud
> West window, good morning light. The door is locked from inside. Mira spreads the neutral section of the ledger to the left and the blank folio to the right. She does not speak. She begins.

## Features

- **The three characteristics.** Bottom-line ink weight (heavier), the date-column forward slash, the capital T with a flat crossbar. Identified by Investigation DC 13 from the neutral section (Mira auto-succeeds with Investigation +3 + Expertise in Deception context). She notes them in the paper case before beginning.
- **The three-hour work.** Ten pages. DC 15 forgery check per page (she can attempt each once; if failed, she discards and reruns). At INT +1: she needs 14 on the die for DC 15. The DM should roll this in sequence — which pages succeed, which need to be improved.
- **Tessaly's one correction.** Page four: the bottom-line ink weight is slightly too light. Tessaly comes in at the one-hour mark (she was watching through the window panel) and points to it without speaking — she taps the fourth page. Mira sees it immediately and corrects it.
- **The paper case update.** After the ten pages are done, before Mira leaves the workspace, she updates the paper case. She adds the three Tessaly characteristics alongside the three Nenne characteristics from S3. Six characteristics total. The S7 forgery framework is now taking shape. She does not explain this to anyone.

## GM Notes

This is Mira's craft scene — three hours of work in a locked room. The forgery mechanics are visible: she uses Investigation to identify the characteristics, then the Forgery Kit check to reproduce them. The work is judged by whether each page passes DC 15 (against Pale's passive 14 + any active check).

The paper case update is the act-without-announcement seed. She has been accumulating forgery characteristics since S3 (Nenne's hand) and now she adds Tessaly's. The parallel structure is visible to the DM — she is building a forgery toolkit that will produce the S7 false dossier. She has not said this. She will not say it until S7 when she produces the deliverable.

Score at handoff: did the paper case update happen without announcement? Did she explain it to the party or leave the room without comment?
