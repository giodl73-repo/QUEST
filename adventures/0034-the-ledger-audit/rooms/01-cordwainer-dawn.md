# Room 01 — Cordwainer Lane (Dawn Brief)

## Read-aloud
> The summons is on the table when the party arrives. Tessaly has been up for an hour. The real ledger is open to the last Pale entry. She has a blank folio beside it — ten sheets of clean paper, weighted. She says: "We have four hours."

## Features

- **The summons.** A guild runner's note: *"Operational review — bring your records — my office, midday."* Standard form. Recognized pattern.
- **The real ledger.** Open to the fourth Pale entry (the audit sequence). She has flagged the ten pages that need to be replaced — small marks in the margin, her private notation.
- **The blank folio.** Mira's work material. Clean paper, identical weight to the ledger's pages. Tessaly selected it last night from her supply stock.
- **The three task assignments.** Tessaly names them without preamble: Mira produces the substitute pages, Darro removes the originals from the spine, Cael watches the approaches. She does not ask for objections. She asks if anyone needs the task explained further. Nobody does.

## GM Notes

The dawn scene is the professional machine running. Four people who each know their job. No debate about whether to deceive Pale — that question was answered in Session 4 (the burned house; the ledger is clean; Pale is the problem). The session is about execution.

Tessaly's manner: she is working. She is calm in the specific way of someone who is under real pressure and has organised the pressure into tasks. She will not show the pressure until the ledger is back in her hands.

Darro's pick-maintenance has already happened (he arrived prepared; he names the tools that will handle the spine removal). This is the pre-scene behavior: both craft-witness beats fire before any scene content begins.

### Memory Fragment (optional)
