## DM notes

The forgery workspace (02) is in the back room of Tessaly's office — a room that exists in the building but has not been keyed previously. It has a single table, good daylight from a west window, and a lock on the door that Tessaly uses when sensitive work is in progress. Mira works here for three hours. Nobody else enters. This is the craft-witness space.

Pale's office (03) is standard merchant-professional: a large desk, two side tables for the transcriptionists, shelves of records organized by year. He is behind the desk when the party arrives. The two transcriptionists are present but peripheral — they are there to record any admissions, not to engage. The party should be aware of them.
