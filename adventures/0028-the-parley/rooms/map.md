## Note on Rooms 03 and 04

Rooms 03 and 04 are the same physical location — the parley ground at the camp's edge. They are separated by narrative function: Room 03 is the arrival and setup (who is here, what the conditions are, hard-liner combat if not already resolved in Room 02). Room 04 is the accounting scene itself (Davan speaks, Asholt gives token, Saren responds). The DM may run them as one extended scene; the separation is for clarity of beats.

## Scale

One square ≈ 10 feet. No-man's-land: 250 feet of open ground (25 rounds of movement at speed 30, or 5 dashes). The parley ground at the camp edge is a 30-foot circle marked by Saren's soldiers with flags. Asholt's walk from the east postern to the parley ground: approximately 3 minutes at walking pace. He does not rush.

## DM notes

**The postern crossing is the point of commitment.** Once the party is outside the wall, they cannot return quickly — Hessa bars the gate behind them (security protocol). The return requires the gate to be opened from inside. This is not a trap; it is the scene's architecture. The party is outside, in the open, carrying a parley flag. This is the most exposed they have been in the campaign.

**Asholt's walk:** When the party returns from Room 03 to fetch Asholt (or sends a message back), he crosses alone. No garrison escort. No weapons drawn. He carries the other half of his token. The party watches a 58-year-old man in garrison officer's uniform walk across 250 feet of killing ground toward the camp of the person who has been besieging him for 36 days. This is the visual of his arc.

**Hard-liner position:** The combat in Room 02 or 03 is brief — 2–3 soldiers who do not have Saren's authorization to interfere with a Solamnic parley flag. They act anyway. They are Reconstitution hard-liners who believe the siege should end in a military outcome. Saren, when she sees the flag approach, sends her own soldier to call them off if they intercept at the camp edge (her authority on her own ground). If the combat happens in the no-man's-land (Room 02), she has no jurisdiction. The party fights with whatever HP they have.
