## Accounting Speech Design Note

The speech emerges from six sessions of accumulated content. The DM writes it from these structural pieces:
- "The Oath was supposed to tell me who deserves shelter. It doesn't tell me this." (S4)
- "The Oath tells you about the letter. It doesn't tell you about the people." (S5)
- "I know what it costs to be right about the letter." (S6)
- At the table: name both sides' costs — Saren's 20 years, Asholt's 20 years, garrison's 36 days, refugees' 3 years.
- At the table: name Orel. The speech names what following the letter cost Davan personally. This is what makes it a speech, not a lecture.
