# 0042 — The Reckoning

## The adventure in one sentence

Commander Varet Senn presents the field journal at a military tribunal — the party gave her the vocabulary to act; she acts under her own authority; the journal is read verbatim.

## Setting

Day 7. The unit presents itself at Thorngate Keep for formal review (either they requested it, or they were summoned following Ossren's report from S4). The tribunal is composed of three officers — two neutral, one aligned with Ossian's network (Colonel Derrath, workshop-canon — present to manage the outcome, unaware that the journal exists in its current form).

## Hook

The journal presentation is the finale. It is not a speech. Varet opens the journal to the discrepancy column and reads it. She does not argue. She does not appeal. She reads what she wrote.

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d6 or d4

## Routes

- **Route A:** 3+ unit members loyal to party. Present journal through official channels. Let tribunal decide. Journal read but outcome uncertain — Derrath may suppress.
- **Route B:** 5+ loyal. Derrath bypassed — journal given directly to a senior officer (General Sel Hadren, workshop-canon) who has the authority to act. Outcome more certain.
- **Route E:** All loyalty + journal complete + arc fires. Varet presents the journal herself. She does not wait for permission. She reads it aloud in the tribunal room. That is the finale. The party gave her the vocabulary; she speaks it under her own authority.

## Scenes

1. **Tribunal assembly** — three officers. Derrath present, nervous. Arren Vole in formation. Cohesion at d6/d4 — the unit is visibly reduced.
2. **Varet presents the journal** — Route E: she reads the discrepancy column aloud. Every entry. Verbatim. This is logged verbatim in the session log.
3. **Ossren's testimony** — Lieutenant Ossren (S4) is present. He tells the truth. "I was told it was logistics."
4. **Taris shows the map** — second showing. The first time was to the unit (S5). This time it is to the tribunal. Evidence.
5. **Lenne's log transfer** — she hands the medical log to the tribunal clerk. "These are the ones I couldn't explain."
6. **Dorn reads the contract clause** — second reading. This time in front of Derrath. Flat. Complete.
7. **Resolution** — Derrath attempts to intervene. Arren Vole's arc fires. Arren's arc-completion: he speaks as himself. General Sel Hadren is summoned by the neutral officers.
