# 0046 — The Demand

## The adventure in one sentence

A local authority wants Vess specifically — for the package she's carrying — and the others don't know what's in it.

## Setting

**The town of Greyford, Mile 68** (workshop-canon, south Abanasinia — two days south of Haverford). Greyford is a substantial market town that serves as a regional hub for the Stonehaven authority. The town watch is an extension of the Stonehaven toll operation. The Overseer for this district is **Warden Cassel**, a careful, methodical man who has been waiting for a specific package to come through this road.

## Hook

The four travelers enter Greyford for resupply. Within an hour of arrival, Vess is approached by two town watch officers: *"The package you're carrying has been flagged. Warden Cassel wants to speak with you."* The others are not addressed — but they are standing there when it happens.

## Scenes

1. **The approach** — the watch officers find Vess in the market district. Professional, not aggressive. They have her description (she's been on this road before; she was noted). The others are with her.
2. **The meeting with Cassel** — Warden Cassel is measured, organized, and has been waiting for this package to appear on this road. He explains: the package's sender is under investigation for road-toll evasion and illegal goods transport. He is not accusing Vess of wrongdoing — she is a courier. But he needs the package, or he needs her to open it here so he can verify contents.
3. **Vess's dilemma** — she cannot open the package (courier code). She cannot surrender it (contract). She can be detained while a formal seizure order is filed (3 days in Greyford). The others do not know what's in it.
4. **H2 delivery** — Brek recognizes Cassel. He has encountered this authority before — Cassel works for the same organization whose representative wronged Brek's family. He doesn't announce this. He watches Cassel very carefully.
5. **Resolution** — the others can help Vess negotiate for transit under bond (they vouch for her), pay for a legal review (costs 15 gp and 12 hours), or physically extract from the situation. Cassel's arc fires when he admits the package was never meant for Vess — he was testing the road for someone else.

## NPC arc

**Warden Cassel** — arc-completion NPC. Admits the package was never meant for Vess; he was using her delivery as a test shipment to trace a larger network. Fires when someone asks the right question. See npcs/warden-cassel.md.

## Hint placement

- **H2 delivery:** Brek has encountered Cassel's organization before. He knows Cassel is connected to Stonehaven authority. He says nothing — yet. Log as H2 planted-silent.
