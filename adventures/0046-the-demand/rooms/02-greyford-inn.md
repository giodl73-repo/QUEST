# Room 02 — Greyford Inn (The Steady Wheel)

## Read-aloud

> The inn is clean, well-run, and full of the kind of quiet that settles over a town where everyone is watched. A fire. Three tables. Two men at the far table who arrived before you did and have not ordered food.

## DM notes

- **Cassel's plainclothes observers:** Brek notices them immediately (Perception +3, passive 13 — they are not hiding particularly). He does not say anything. He sits with his back to the wall, which means facing them. He is not threatening them; this is his heuristic. Vess notices them 30 seconds after Brek sits — passive 16.
- **If PCs discuss their situation here:** The conversation is probably overheard. Tam knows this — he subtly changes the subject if anyone starts discussing specifics.
- **Post-meeting decision point:** The four need to decide what to do. Options:
  1. **Accept transit under bond** — they all vouched, Cassel accepted, they continue south. Clean resolution. Vess's package issue follows her legally but she makes the delivery.
  2. **Pay for legal review** — 15 gp, 12 hours. Vess can be cleared formally. Expensive. Requires party to contribute or Vess to have the funds.
  3. **Extract** — slip out of Greyford. See map for routes. Leaves the flagged status active.
- **Research note:** The discussion itself is Reciprocal Act evidence. Who speaks first? Who makes the case for helping Vess? Nobody has been asked to help; they are just here, and Vess needs help.
