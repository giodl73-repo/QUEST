# Room 00 — Market District

## Read-aloud

> Greyford: market stalls, a smithy, three inns. Less than an hour in, two watch officers step out of the crowd and stop Vess. Professional, not rough. "The package you're carrying has been flagged. Warden Cassel would like to speak with you." They do not address the others.

## DM notes

- **Watch officers:** Two Soldiers (Scout stat blocks + town livery). They are polite because Cassel is methodical and does not want unnecessary incidents. They will not search Vess without her consent and without Cassel's presence.
- **The others:** The four are together. This happens in front of Brek, Sera, and Tam. They are not asked to come or to leave. If they follow: the officers allow it (no authority to stop them).
- **Brek's H2 moment:** When they arrive at the Warden's Hall and Brek sees Cassel's insignia (the Stonehaven authority crest — a trade scale over a road marker), his jaw tightens. He does not speak. Tam's Insight passive 13 picks up the change. If Tam asks later: Brek says *"I know that crest."* That is all he will say before the H2/H3 content surfaces in S5.
  - Log: H2 delivered. Brek recognizes the authority. Silent. Does not share.
- **Vess's reaction:** Professional. *"I'm a licensed courier. I'll speak with the Warden."* She does not volunteer the contents. She does not run. She has a contract; she expects the contract to protect her.
