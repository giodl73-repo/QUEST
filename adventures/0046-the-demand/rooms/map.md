**Greyford layout notes:**
- Market district: busy, 40+ traders, easy to lose tail (Stealth DC 13)
- Warden's Hall: 6 watch officers on duty at any time; Cassel's office is on the second floor
- Inn: The Steady Wheel — functional, not memorable; Cassel has 2 plainclothes men watching it
- Town gate: guarded but not fortified; a legal transit arrangement is easier than forcing it

**If PCs choose extraction (bypass legal route):**
- Stealth DC 13 through market, then DC 14 at the north gate (which they entered from); south gate easier (DC 11) but takes them off the main road
- Cassel will not pursue past Greyford's jurisdiction (the Stonehaven authority stops at the district line, 3 miles south)
- Extraction leaves Vess's package issue unresolved — she is flagged on this road
