## Aggregate Verdict

**Shippable as-is, with one-line revisions recommended.** Four of five reviewers (Jaquays, Schick, Moldvay, Stuart) score at or above the 65 shippable threshold. Only Gygax falls below (59.8), and his own weights amplify dungeon-integrity (1.3) — the axis where *"this is not a dungeon; it is a quest with a dungeon inside it"* dominates his math.

## Strongest axes (above 8.5 avg)

1. **Treasure-as-story (9.4)** — near-perfect across the panel. Schick 9, Moldvay + Stuart 10, Jaquays 10. The Wrath-Coin is a *character*; the adventure's spine is the coin's own intention. Aelwen's diptych (journal page + apron-scrap) is cited by all five reviewers as load-bearing.
2. **Curse/consequence (9.2)** — Stuart + Schick score 10; everyone else 8-9. Inherited Rage re-keying and the Voidmouth trap are consensus wins. Mechanical AND philosophical, which is what Stuart specifically asks for.
3. **Premise clarity (8.8)** — tight hook; *"the hammer that should be buried is not"* is cited by three reviewers as exemplary.
4. **Lore grounding (8.6)** — Dragonlance setting-native; Aelwen's arc across three adventures; Thorbardin kinship law mechanically meaningful.

## Weakest axes (below 8.0 avg)

1. **Dungeon integrity (7.0)** — divisive. Stuart + Gygax 6; Jaquays 7; Schick + Moldvay 8. Gygax: *"not a dungeon, a quest with a dungeon inside it."* Jaquays: *"loop is scenically present but tactically hollow."* Stuart: *"linear with branching... feels like an elevator, not a descent."*
2. **Encounter craft (7.6)** — the Voice of the Thane mechanic specifically draws fire. Gygax + Jaquays both flag it as *"save-based action tax, not a tactical decision."*
3. **Table-readiness (7.8)** — Moldvay 7 (the Scene 2 kinship misdirection specifically); Stuart 8 (prose-unevenness); Schick 9 (craft-witness silent reception needs one clarifying line).
4. **Player agency (8.0)** — Scene 6 mandatory frame called out by Gygax + Jaquays + Schick. Spotlight-PC dominance by Grom: Schick + Stuart note it.

## Strongest Consensus

1. **The Wrath-Coin is a signature-level artifact.** All 5 reviewers score treasure-as-story + curse/consequence at 9+. Aelwen's letter-fragment diptych (cross-adventure) + Voidmouth + re-keying mechanic = the best-received artifact in the workshop.

2. **Aelwen is canon-present.** 4 of 5 reviewers cite Aelwen's cross-adventure voice as a strength. Setting is doing work across adventures.

3. **Scene 3 Reorx-rite is moral foundation.** All 5 reviewers cite it positively: Hint 2 + Hint 3 simultaneous delivery; craft-witness reception on Grom; the 7-strike sequence itself.

4. **Jarek's guilt-psychology is textured.** 4 of 5 reviewers cite him positively — flinch, sleepless-three-years, craft-witness-adjacent surrender path.

## Most Dissent

**Dungeon integrity (6-8 spread).** Four different framings of the same cap:
- **Gygax (6):** "Not a dungeon."
- **Jaquays (7):** "Loop is scenically present, tactically hollow."
- **Stuart (6):** "Linear with branching; elevator, not descent."
- **Schick + Moldvay (8):** "Sound topology; verticality matters."

The philosophical disagreement: should an adventure with social + combat + ritual scenes be *scored as a dungeon* or *scored as itself*? Gygax and Stuart say "as itself, and it falls short of being a true dungeon." Schick and Moldvay say "as itself, and it works." Jaquays splits the difference.

**Scene 6 frame (combat vs political vs Option-C).** Gygax wants pure combat; Jaquays wants bypass-option; Schick wants Grom-centric-as-designed; Stuart wants Krun's voice fleshed; Moldvay wants the kinship signaling explicit. Five different fixes to the same scene.

## Convergent Suggestions (3+ reviewers agree)

### P0 — must-fix (4+ reviewers)

1. **Scene 6 attunement-race mechanics need a pre-written flowchart** (Gygax + Jaquays + Schick + Stuart). Five parallel intervention options under table-pressure need DM-managed timeline. Recommend: mini-flowchart in Scene 6 showing Krun's action-economy (free interact → attunement ends next turn) and PC intervention windows.

2. **Scene 2 "cousin" kinship-seed needs DM-facing note** (Moldvay explicit; Gygax + Stuart implicit). One-line parenthetical before Krun's speech: *"(Krun names Grom 'cousin' without explanation. This is intentional kinship-seed. Scene 4 resolves. Do not telegraph surprise.)"*

3. **Hint 3 reception needs one clarifying sentence** (Schick + Stuart + implicit Moldvay). Add to Scene 3: *"Grom knows this the way he knows stone or fire — without words, with absolute certainty. The knowing lands in his hands."*

4. **Voice of the Thane mechanic refinement** (Gygax + Jaquays + Stuart). Replace save-based action-tax with reaction-conditional: *"When a creature within 5 feet attacks Krun with melee, he may use his reaction to force Wis DC 14 save or the attack misses."*

### P1 — should-fix (3 reviewers)

5. **Scene 1 skip → Jarek-appears-Scene-6 note** (Schick + Jaquays + Moldvay flag the Scene-1 conditional branching leaving Scene 6 under-scaffolded). One-liner: *"If Scene 1 skipped (alternate trail / dusk arrival), Jarek arrives Scene 6 unsummoned."*

6. **Kruntharrak alternate-resolution path** (Jaquays + Gygax + Stuart). The current frame funnels party to Scene 6. Recommend adding: if party destroys coin at Gavrek's Anvil forge post-Scene 5 (courier to Krun), they may avoid Scene 6 entirely. Trade-off: Krun remains a political threat; no Grom-closure.

### P2 — authorial-call (2 reviewers)

7. **Krun's voice needs prose-weight** (Stuart specific; Gygax adjacent). Add 3-sentence internal monologue before Krun speaks at Scene 6: *"Three years he's waited for this. Three years he's carried the thought 'Who did it?' with him to every meal. Today, finally, he will know."*

8. **Non-Grom PC Option-C spawn option** (Schick + Gygax). If Thera disarms the coin or Kessa identifies the 6th grievance, their call should matter. Add: Option C can spawn for any PC making a major Scene 6 decision, not Grom-only.

## Divergent Suggestions (single-lens)

- **(Gygax) Collapse the shrine hub.** Two scenes instead of 2-4. Shrine: arrival + rite. Archive moved to Kruntharrak as evidence-search.
- **(Jaquays) Vertical shaft expansion.** Multiple levels in Lower Ore-Shaft — a semi-collapsed lower chamber where the destruction-plan forge-setup lives. Mini-dungeon structure.
- **(Schick) Krun's stat-block clarification.** Voice-of-the-Thane Kessa-advantage note — add *(she has Solamnic kin-witness training)* clarification.
- **(Moldvay) Hilda's death-cause consistency.** Scene 4 vs Scene 3 GM Notes — "fever" vs unspecified. Pick one.
- **(Stuart) Sigga mechanical-anchor.** Religion DC 10 self-check for Grom's hearth-greeting; success = Sigga's Scene 3 confession lands with intimacy; failure = she measures him colder.
- **(Stuart) Voidmouth explicit DM advisory.** Dedicated GM Notes entry warning of the empty-coin-refills trap.
- **(Stuart) Jarek craft-witness surrender path.** If Grom invokes the inside-curve teaching, Jarek surrenders without a roll.

## Recommended revision pass (P0 action items)

Estimated fix-time: ~30-45 minutes of module.md + source-file edits. All P0 items are one-line additions or small reformats, not structural changes.

1. **Scene 2:** kinship DM-note ("cousin is intentional; Scene 4 resolves").
2. **Scene 3:** Hint 3 reception clarifier ("Grom knows this the way he knows stone or fire").
3. **Scene 6:** attunement-race flowchart block + Voice-of-the-Thane reaction-rewrite.
4. **Scene 6 / Encounters Appendix:** non-Grom Option C note.
5. **GM Notes:** Scene 1 skip → Jarek-Scene-6 note.
6. **Treasure wrath-coin.md:** visual-appearance detail (coin size; player-facing description).

## Go/No-Go verdict

**GO to play at S04** with P0 revisions applied. Adventure scores **66.6/80 weighted — shippable on design-review alone** (first time in workshop history). Four of five reviewers at or above 65. Gygax's 59.8 is philosophical disagreement (it isn't a dungeon in his sense) not structural failure.

**Post-S04** the panel should reconvene to score the *played* session — where the attunement-race, kinship-reveal, and Option-C Reorx-exile happen in real-time under dice pressure.
