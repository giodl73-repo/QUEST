# Scene 01 — Kharolis Road Ambush (conditional)

## Read-aloud

> The road climbs toward Gavrek's Anvil through winter-bare pines. Low sun; long shadows; the silence of a road that has not carried caravans in weeks. Halfway up a switchback, a wagon sits sideways across the track — axle broken, cargo tarped, no driver visible. The nearest cover is the pines themselves, twenty yards off.

## Features

- **The staged wagon.** Empty; axle is broken *deliberately* (Investigation DC 12 spots the clean chisel-mark). Tarped cargo is rocks.
- **The pines.** Cover for four ambushers.
- **Jarek Stoneforge + 3 hired hill-dwarf mercenaries.** Positions: Jarek behind the wagon's axle (cover); three mercs in the pines at 20 ft, 25 ft, 30 ft flanking.
- **Jarek's order:** stall, don't kill. Krun wants time. Jarek will withdraw at half-HP (15/30).

## Encounter

**Triggered** if party passes the wagon without a Perception check (passive 12 catches Jarek's plait behind the axle).

### Enemies

> **Jarek Stoneforge (CR ½, 100 XP)** — see `npcs/jarek-stoneforge.md`. AC 15, HP 30.

> **Mercenary hill-dwarf × 3 (CR ⅛, 25 XP each, Bandit variant).** AC 13 (leather+shield), HP 11 each. Warhammer +3 (1d8+1). Darkvision 60 ft.

**Encounter XP:** 100 + 3×25 = 175 base × 2 (4 creatures) = **350 XP.** Medium for 4×L3.

### Tactics

- **Round 1:** Jarek declares: *"We mean no death. Turn back. Kruntharrak has nothing for you."* The mercs move from cover but do not attack first round unless PCs attack. If party turns back (Wisdom save / choice), Jarek lets them pass. They will not turn back.
- **Round 2+:** mercs engage melee. Jarek uses mining charge once if party clusters (≥ 3 within 10 ft; dwarves immune to surprise). Jarek melee with pick + hammer; will Blood-call-intercept on Krun's behalf is unavailable (Krun not in scene).
- **Jarek withdraws at HP ≤ 15.** He takes a dying merc as cover and fades east. If pursued: Stealth +5, cover-rich terrain. Easier to let him go.

### Intel (if Jarek captured OR mercs interrogated)

- **Intimidation DC 14 / Persuasion DC 16 on Jarek:** he names Krun as his employer. Will not confess to Duren's murder. **If the party names the half-kinship** (they cannot yet; Scene 2 reveal), Jarek's will breaks — he confesses everything.
- **Intimidation DC 10 on a merc:** Krun paid them 10 gp each to "slow down travelers." They do not know why.

## Treasure

- Jarek's kit: warhammer, miner's pick, studded leather, shield, 18 gp.
- Merc kits: hammer, leather, shield, 3 gp each.
- **Wagon's hidden compartment** (Investigation DC 15 by Thera): 40 gp in Thorbardin coin + a letter addressed to "J. from K." (unsigned), reading: *"Delay them. Not bodies. Three days is enough."*

## GM Notes

- **This scene can be skipped** if the party (a) takes the alternate southeastern trail (Survival DC 12 to find it, costs half a day), (b) arrives after dusk (Jarek stands down — he will not fight in the dark against Kessa's and Grom's darkvision), or (c) negotiates at first contact (Persuasion DC 18 convinces Jarek to let them pass — he judges it not worth the cost).
- **If Scene 1 is skipped (any of a/b/c), Jarek still appears at Scene 6** — either as Krun's backup at Kruntharrak (guilt-driven to re-engage) OR encountered at Scene 5's shaft (monthly maintenance trip). He is not a one-scene NPC; his guilt-psychology is central. Do not let Scene 1 skip erase him.
- **Jarek's voice is a tell.** He will not say Duren's name. He will not meet Grom's eyes. Grom's Insight DC 12 catches the flinch.
- **Capturing Jarek alive changes Scene 5 and Scene 6.** His evidence becomes Thorbardin-admissible. Strongly preferable to party outcomes.
