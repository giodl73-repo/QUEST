# Temporal Pressure — Gavrek's Anvil + Road Variant

This adventure spans **road → shrine → grave → shaft → mine-hold → return** (~24 in-fiction hours, with one night between Scenes 4/5 and Scene 6). Wandering pressure is lighter than dungeon delves; more like a hill-town's texture.

## Use

- **Shrine Pulse** d6 every **2 hours of in-fiction time**. Atmospheric, rarely mechanical.
- **Road Encounter** d6 once per road-segment (3 segments total: Varduin→Scene 1 approach, Scene 1→shrine, shrine→Kruntharrak).
- Both tables stop during Scene 6's Krun confrontation.

## Shrine Pulse Table (d6 per 2 hours; Scenes 2-5, 7)

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** The forge-smoke continues. |
| 2 | **A traveling priest arrives.** An unnamed Reorx-priest-on-pilgrimage (Vell's rank). Stays an hour. Leaves. Not plot-relevant. |
| 3 | **The forge-hearth flickers.** Any PC near the forge notices a momentary drop in heat. Grom (craft-witness) reads it as *"something is listening in the hold east of here."* Anchor-level ambient: **the Wrath-Coin is at Kruntharrak and Krun is checking it.** Passive perception-style delivery. |
| 4 | **Vell approaches Grom.** Asks a question about Duren's teachings. The question is simple; the asking is the point. *"How do you know the inside-curve is the honest one?"* Grom may answer. |
| 5 | **Sigga appears with tea.** No dialogue. Simply present. A moment of silence. |
| 6 | **Jarek's flinch (if observable).** If Jarek is within the party's observation range (Scene 2 when he watches with Krun; Scene 3 on the slope; or lurking during travel), Thera passive 13 catches him flinching when Grom is named. |

## Road Encounter Table (d6 per road-segment)

| d6 | Encounter |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** |
| 2 | **A Thorbardin trader** passes with a cart of ore. Willing to share news. Insight DC 12 picks up that he is scared of Kruntharrak — he will not travel there alone. *"Thane Krun has been different the past year."* |
| 3 | **A Solamnic courier** heading north. Passes; can carry messages for the party if requested. One-way. |
| 4 | **Weather:** a mountain-squall kicks up; visibility to 50 ft for 1 hour; disadvantage on Perception in the weather. Flavor; no combat result. |
| 5 | **A pilgrim family** camped by the road. Dwarves; three adults, two children. Invite the party to share their fire. Kessa History DC 12: they mention *the shrine-warden Sigga Coalbeard has been writing letters lately* — interesting detail about Sigga's recent activity. Casual, not confrontational. |
| 6 | **Jarek's second ambush** (only fires if Scene 1 was skipped or Jarek survived Scene 1 and chose to re-engage). Same as Scene 1 stats but with only 2 mercs; Jarek is desperate. XP 125 base × 2 = 250. Easy-Medium. |

## Pacing guidance

- **Day 1:** Party rides from Varduin. Scene 1 (road ambush) fires midday. Arrive Gavrek's Anvil late afternoon. Scene 2 + Sigga meeting. Krun visits. Scene 4 (archive) OR Scene 5 (shaft) OR neither — party discretion. Sleep overnight. ~2-3 Shrine Pulse rolls.
- **Day 2 dawn:** Scene 3 (Reorx-rite). Hints 2 + 3 delivered. Party discovers hammer's absence. Sigga's confession.
- **Day 2 mid-morning to afternoon:** Scene 4 (archive; if not done Day 1); Scene 5 (shaft; if not done Day 1). ~2 Shrine Pulse rolls.
- **Day 2 dusk:** travel to Kruntharrak; Scene 6. **Tables OFF during Scene 6.**
- **Day 2 late night:** return ride; Scene 7 (aftermath at shrine). ~1 Shrine Pulse roll (likely nothing).
- **Day 3 dawn:** Laen's courier arrives. Session ends.

Expect **~4-6 total Shrine Pulse rolls**; **~3 Road Encounter rolls** (one per segment).
