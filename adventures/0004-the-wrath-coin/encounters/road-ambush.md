# Encounter — Road Ambush (Scene 1, conditional)

**Location:** Kharolis road, halfway up a switchback to Gavrek's Anvil.
**Trigger:** Party passes the staged wagon without diverting or spotting the ambush.

## Adversaries

### Jarek Stoneforge (CR ½, 100 XP)
- AC 15, HP 30, Speed 25 ft
- Multiattack: warhammer + miner's pick (bonus)
- **Warhammer:** +4, 1d8+2 bludgeoning
- **Miner's pick (bonus):** +4, 1d6+2 piercing; crit embeds (Str DC 13 to free)
- **Mining charge** (1/rest): 10 ft radius, Dex DC 13, 2d6 thunder + prone on fail; dwarves immune to being surprised by this
- **Blood-call intercept** (1/short rest): not usable — Krun not present

### Mercenary hill-dwarf × 3 (CR ⅛, 25 XP each)
- AC 13, HP 11, Speed 25 ft
- **Warhammer:** +3, 1d8+1 bludgeoning
- Darkvision 60 ft

## Encounter XP

- Jarek + 3 mercs: 100 + 75 = 175 base × 2 (4 creatures) = **350 XP**. Medium for 4×L3.

## Tactics

**Round 1:**
- **Jarek:** Declares intent to stall; announces *"We mean no death. Turn back."* Moves to cover behind wagon. If party attacks: melee nearest + bonus-pick.
- **Mercs:** Move from pines to flank. Will not attack first unless PCs do.
- **PC initiative:** Likely to attack (per heuristics: Aelric will not yield to an obstacle; Kessa will analyze the stage; Thera will position for Sneak Attack; Grom will Bless the party).

**Round 2-3:**
- **Jarek** uses mining charge if party clusters ≥3 within 10 ft.
- **Mercs** engage melee; each can take ~1 hit from a smite-loaded Aelric.
- **Grom** likely Bless-then-Guiding-Bolt on Jarek.
- **Kessa** Magic Missile at mercs (efficient 3-drop).
- **Thera** Sneak Attack with advantage from hidden positioning.

**Jarek's withdrawal:**
- HP ≤ 15 (half): Jarek announces *"Enough. We go."* Uses cover + Stealth to fade east. Mercs cover him (or surrender).
- If party pursues in difficult terrain: Stealth +5. Let him go preferred — this feeds Scene 5 cleaner.

## Surrender branch

At HP ≤ 10 Jarek will surrender if given the option. He will not confess to Duren's murder **unless** the party names the kinship (which they cannot yet unless they skipped ahead — Scene 2 disclosure required). He will confess employer = Krun only.

## Loot

- Jarek's kit (if killed/captured): warhammer, miner's pick, studded leather, shield, 18 gp, thieves' tool-pouch with mining-charge-fuse components.
- Merc kits: each has warhammer, leather, shield, 3 gp.
- **Wagon hidden compartment** (Investigation DC 15 by Thera): 40 gp Thorbardin coin + **"J. from K." letter** (the delay-order).

## GM Notes

- **Objective: stall, not kill.** Jarek's own tactical goal is to slow the party by 3 days. He will not press engagements that risk TPK.
- **Dawn-tier or dusk-tier adjustments:** if party arrives before dawn, Jarek's mercs are still warming up — give them disadvantage Round 1 (not yet fully set). If at dusk/night, Jarek prefers not to engage — he will try Persuasion first (DC 15 to convince party to turn around; impossible given their mission).
- **Rubric v1.3:** no anchor-level symptoms in this encounter; narrative-only. Jarek's reluctance to meet Grom's eye (atmospheric pre-reception) fires whether or not combat happens.
