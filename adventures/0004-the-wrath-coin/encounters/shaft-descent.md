# Encounter — Lower Ore-Shaft Descent (Scene 5, conditional)

**Location:** Lower Ore-Shaft at Gavrek's Anvil; 120 ft below shaft-head.
**Trigger:** Party descends while Jarek is present (daylight, most likely); or party descends at night (uncontested).

## Adversaries (if Jarek intercepts)

### Jarek Stoneforge (CR ½, 100 XP)
- See `npcs/jarek-stoneforge.md`. Full stats.
- **Mining charges remaining:** 1 (used 1 in Scene 1 — assume used if Scene 1 fired).

## Encounter XP

- Jarek solo: **100 XP**. Easy for 4×L3.

## Tactics

### Jarek's ambush setup

- Jarek descended earlier in the day; is hiding in a side-shaft at the 80-ft mark.
- **Stealth check:** Jarek +5. Against party's passive 13 average → roll.
- If Jarek wins: surprise round on the first PC down.

### Round 1 — if Jarek surprises

- Jarek **uses his remaining mining charge** to seal the shaft-head (10 ft radius; DC 13 Dex). This both damages any PC in the area AND isolates the party from easy escape.
- Party at shaft-head takes: 2d6 thunder + prone on fail; half + no prone on save.
- **Half the party may be above the charge-blast** (if still descending); they take no damage but are separated.

### Rounds 2-3 (combat)

- **Jarek** melee PCs in the shaft-bottom with warhammer + pick.
- Tight stonespace favors dwarves (no disadvantage from space for Grom or Jarek; others at disadvantage for being in cramped quarters with a dwarf-sized opponent).

### Jarek's withdrawal

At HP ≤ 15: Jarek retreats to a side-shaft. If pursued, **Stealth +5** in cave conditions.

At HP ≤ 10: Jarek will surrender if offered. **Persuasion DC 16** to flip him (or Intimidation DC 14 with the commission-letters from Scene 4). His surrender unlocks the half-kinship confession he secretly knows about.

## If shaft sealed, party digging out

- Athletics DC 15, 3 successes (1 action per PC per round = ~3-4 rounds to break open).
- OR Kessa uses Magic Missile on the rubble (1 HP per 2 force damage; ~2 slots × L1 = enough).
- OR Thera uses thieves' tools (DC 15 — unlocking the rubble-bolt Jarek emplaced).
- Total delay: 3-5 rounds. No combat during digging (Jarek has fled).

## Loot

- Jarek's kit (if killed/captured here): same as Scene 1.
- **Duren's tools + moon-silver ingot** (shaft floor; Investigation DC 12 + Grom Stonecunning): **3 oz moon-silver + chisel + tongs + oil-cloth bundle with destruction-plan.**
- **The cut beam** (cannot transport; Investigation DC 13 / Stonecunning 11 confirms sabotage).
- **Thunder-stone residue** (Investigation DC 15; small sample).

## If party descends at night (Jarek absent)

- Full evidence recovery uncontested.
- No combat.
- Party returns to shrine before dawn Scene 6 departure.

## GM Notes

- **This scene's weight is atmospheric, not tactical.** Combat with Jarek here is incidental; the point is the evidence.
- **Grom's reception of the sabotage:** let it land. He will realize the hearth-greeting saved his life. Craft-witness in its most painful form. Sigga may later tell him: *"Duren sent you for water because he knew. It was his last teaching."*
- **Rubric v1.3 positional-redundancy:** the murder-evidence is in three separable forms (cut beam, thunder-stone residue, destruction-plan bundle). No single-roll fragility. Any of three paths succeeds.
