## DM notes

This session's map is a social geography — locations linked by decision rather than physical access. The party's movement between locations is the session's structure. No locked doors, no climbing, no infiltration. The challenge is in the conversations and the reading of behavior.

The Anchor Bell interior: standard tavern layout, mid-day quiet. Three booths along the east wall; Pell is in the back booth, facing the door (he is also professional about exits). The party enters facing his position immediately.

The coffee house: four tables, a proprietor who does not involve herself in conversations. Morning light from east windows. Pell sits with his back to the light (same habit as Nenne from S3 — Havenmere professionals learn the same things).
