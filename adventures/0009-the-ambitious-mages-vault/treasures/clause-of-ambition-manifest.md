# Curse Symptoms Manifest — Clause of Ambition

Curse: The Measure — attuned creature aware once/day when claiming more than their share; disadvantage on Persuasion when overreaching.

At least 3 of 4 symptoms must land in the adventure:

## Symptom 1 — The vibration (sensory)
The shard vibrates when held by someone genuinely ambitious.
**Required:** Lenne picks it up in front of the party (Scene 03, research floor). The vibration is visible — her hand trembles slightly, half a second.
**Fallback:** Orik holds it; it vibrates for him too (grief-form ambition). Any PC with genuine ambition triggers it.

## Symptom 2 — Lenne's eight-year habit (behavioral)
Lenne has been holding this shard for eight years and working alone in a tower, past safe limits, because stopping feels like failing. This is the curse's visible symptom in an unbound user — the shard does not enforce the Measure, so without attuned discipline it becomes a mirror for unchecked ambition.
**Required:** Scene 03 or 04 — Lenne describes her research pace or working conditions. The party can see the tower's state: stacked failed experiments, no social calendar, meals that suggest irregular eating, notes that cover the walls.
**Fallback:** Scene 05 (tower roof) — Lenne mentions she hasn't been to the foothills market in four months.

## Symptom 3 — The inscription's challenge (textual)
*"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* This clause challenges every reader to measure their own claim.
**Required:** Any PC who reads the inscription (Thessaly: automatic) pauses. The inscription does not dim or glow — it simply reads as a reckoning.
**Fallback:** Orik picks it up and the inscription faces him; even without Arcana proficiency, he understands "more than your share" in his bones.

## Symptom 4 — The vibration stops (sensory, post-arc-completion)
After Lenne gives the shard freely (Route D arc-completion), the vibration stops before she finishes handing it over.
**Required:** Route D only — the shard recognizes that the Measure has been acknowledged and stops vibrating mid-handoff.
**Fallback (Routes A/C):** The vibration continues; the shard is simply transferred without acknowledgment.
