# Scene 01 — Tower Exterior / Approach

**Location:** Kharolis foothills mining road, one mile from Volenn's Tower. **Time:** Day 2 travel from Korrath; midday.

**Read-aloud (road encounter):**

> The road narrows where it enters the foothills. Two figures in grey robes step from behind a cart that has been turned sideways across the track. One holds a short staff. The other is already moving to your left flank, and he has rope.

**Road Encounter (non-optional):** Osvin and the quiet one from Korrath. They are two days ahead of the party and have had time to prepare. They know the party is coming; they chose this bottleneck on purpose.

See `encounters/road-ambush.md`. Medium difficulty for L5 party — harder than the Korrath non-encounter. They have had time to study the party's tactics.

**Read-aloud (tower arrival, post-encounter):**

> The tower is five floors of grey Kharolis stone, slightly off-center on its foundation from some old settling. A spiral stair is visible through a narrow window on each floor. On the northeast face: a utility ladder, iron rungs set into the stone, worn smooth. On the east face: a cargo pulley and rope. The main door is solid wood, unlocked. It smells of preserved herbs before you open it.

**Features:**

- **Tower exterior.** The tower is functional, not beautiful. No decorative elements; no signage. A Red Robe works here.
- **Utility ladder.** Northeast face; rungs set into stone; worn smooth. Perception DC 10 to notice from the road. It connects to the research floor (Scene 03) via a window-hatch that is secured from the inside — but the hatch latch is a simple toggle bar, reachable with a thin blade. Thieves' Tools DC 12 or Athletics DC 14 to open from outside.
- **Cargo lift.** East face; rope-and-pulley; rated for 400 lbs. Controlled from inside floors 1 and 3. Cannot be operated from outside.

**GM Notes:**
- **Orik after the road encounter.** He will count the tower's approaches before entering (signature move). He will note: main door (unlocked), utility ladder (northeast, bypasses ground floor), cargo lift (east, inside-only). He will say: *"Three ways in. She knows about the ladder — the rungs are worn smooth."*
- **Sera after the road encounter.** She will check the treeline for a third agent. There is none (Osvin and the quiet one were the full team assigned to this task). She finds no one; she notes the absence.
- **The Unbound Conclave agents, captured or fled.** Whatever happened on the road: the party now has information about who sent them. If Osvin was captured: he will confirm the Unbound Conclave knew the party was coming; confirm the shard is their target; deny knowing where the other shards are. If both fled: they will regroup and appear in adventure 0010.

**Connections:** Scene 02 (main door), Scene 03 (utility ladder, bypasses 02).

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Volenn built the cargo lift forty years ago when he moved in. He was not young then; he had been a traveling scholar for twenty years before he decided he needed walls. He designed the pulley wrong on the first attempt and the rope snapped on the third trial run. He was annoyed for a week and then fixed it. The lift has worked perfectly since. Lenne has never thought about this. She uses it every day.*
