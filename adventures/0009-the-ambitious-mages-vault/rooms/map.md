# Map — The Ambitious Mage's Vault

## Topology

Volenn's Tower is 5 floors, roughly cylindrical, in the Kharolis foothills. The adventure is vertical rather than horizontal — floors connect via a central stair, with two optional external paths (a utility ladder on the northeast face, and a rope-and-pulley cargo lift on the east side for moving heavy materials).

```
ROAD ENCOUNTER (non-optional, before tower)
        |
        v
[01 TOWER EXTERIOR / APPROACH] ← utility ladder → [03 Research Floor]
        |
        v
[02 GROUND FLOOR — Entry / Lenne's meeting space]
        |
        v
[03 RESEARCH FLOOR (Floor 2) — Shard location; Lenne's workspace]
   |              |
   v              v
[04 OLD STUDY     [cargo lift]
    (Floor 3) —  
    Hint 2]
        |
        v
[05 TOWER ROOF (Floor 5 — top) — Lenne's thinking space]
```

**Loop:** Utility ladder (exterior northeast) bypasses floors 1–2 and connects exterior to research floor (03) directly. A party that climbs the ladder can reach the shard without passing through the ground floor. Loop: exterior → ladder → research floor → stair → ground floor → exterior.

**Vertical:** The entire adventure. Every room is a different floor. The cargo lift (east face) can carry a person and connects floors 1–3.

**Two paths to artifact:** Central stair (through ground floor, then up) or utility ladder (exterior bypass to research floor directly).

**Shortcut:** If Lenne trusts the party (ground floor goes well), she leads them upstairs directly — bypassing the need to navigate the stair alone.

**Sensory cues at each branch (from exterior):**
- Central stair entrance: smells of preserved herbs and lamp-oil; the door is solid wood, unlocked.
- Utility ladder: smells of nothing particular; the ladder rungs are worn smooth with use; someone goes up and down this regularly.
