# Scene 05 — Tower Roof (Optional)

**Location:** Volenn's Tower, top floor / roof. **Time:** Day 2 late afternoon.

**Read-aloud:**

> The roof is the only part of the tower that looks like someone made a choice about. A ring of stone parapet, waist-height. A wooden bench, weathered and steady. The Kharolis foothills spread east and south — mining roads, small holds, the distant line of the mountains. You can see the road you came in on. There is no one on it.

**Features:**

- **Lenne's bench.** She comes here when she needs to stop working and cannot make herself stop. She has not told anyone this. If the party finds her here (she sometimes retreats to the roof when thinking): she is not hiding. She is doing the closest thing she does to resting.
- **The view.** Sera will note the road (empty; the agents did not regroup this quickly), the nearest mining hold (3 km east, visible), and the treeline. She counts approach vectors: four. She says nothing.
- **The sky.** If the party visits at night: the three moons are in different phases. Solinari is waxing. Lunitari is gibbous. Nuitari is not visible (it is always dark; only magic-sensitive beings can see it, and Thessaly can). She notes all three positions against the moon-calendar data from Scene 04.
- **Where Orik's arc-completion can happen.** If it hasn't fired in Scene 03, Lenne comes here after the conversation stalls, and Orik follows. The roof is quieter than the research floor. There is no research on the roof — nothing to show, nothing to defend. Orik speaks here if he speaks at all.

**Encounter:** None.

**P0 fallback for arc-completion:** If Orik has not yet spoken and they are on the roof, the DM offers: *"Lenne looks at the road. She says: 'I haven't been to the market in four months.' She is not asking for sympathy. She is noting a fact."* This is the opening — Orik can take it or not. If he takes it: arc-completion available. If not: Route A or C.

**Connections:** Scene 04 (stair down).

**GM Notes:**
- **The sky and Nuitari.** Thessaly sees Nuitari (it is not fully dark; partially gibbous). She says, quietly: *"Three months.'"* She is calculating the convergence date against the current moon positions. She does not explain this to anyone immediately — she is still processing. Calder watches her. He knows that look too, now.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Lenne has been on this roof four hundred and twelve times in eight years. She counted for the first two years, then stopped counting, then started estimating. She does not know what the count proves. She is not sure it proves anything. She comes up here when she needs to not be solving a problem for a few minutes. She is usually back downstairs within twenty minutes. She does not think of this as rest.*
