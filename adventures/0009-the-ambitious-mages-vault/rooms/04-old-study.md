# Scene 04 — Volenn's Old Study (Floor 3)

**Location:** Volenn's Tower, third floor. **Time:** Day 2 afternoon (after Scene 03).

**Read-aloud:**

> Volenn's study has not been touched much since he died. A desk that faces east — toward the foothills, not the road. A moon-calendar on the south wall, four feet wide, hand-drawn with decades of annotation. A single note on the desk: *"Convergence in 3 months (est.). Check the ward on the third shard."* The note is Volenn's handwriting.

**Features:**

- **The moon-calendar.** Four feet wide, covering Krynn's three moons (Solinari, white; Lunitari, red; Nuitari, black). Dozens of annotations in Volenn's hand — dates, observations, theoretical notes about moon-alignment and Order power. **Hint 2 delivery:** A specific date is circled, three months from now. Thessaly (passive Arcana 18) reads the annotation: *"Triple convergence — all three moons full simultaneously. Last occurred 300 years ago. First occurrence since the Cataclysm. Compact clauses written to align with this event?"* This is Hint 2: the Conclave Compact's seven clauses were designed to be spoken whole during a triple-moon convergence — the power alignment that would make the restored compact binding rather than merely symbolic. The party now knows when they must be at the Tower of High Sorcery ruins for the finale.
- **Volenn's note.** *"Convergence in 3 months (est.). Check the ward on the third shard."* He knew about the convergence; he was planning to address it; he died before he could. "The third shard" refers to what the party knows as the Clause of Ambition — he called them shards too.
- **The ward on the third shard.** Investigation DC 14 or Thessaly Arcana DC 10: Volenn added a ward to the shard at some point — a passive detection ward that would have notified him if someone with "renegade intent" handled the object. The ward is dormant (he is dead) but still detectable. This is why the Unbound Conclave has not been able to study the shard effectively — the ward registers their intent and makes the shard inert for them.

**Encounter:** None.

**Connections:** Scene 03 (stair down); Scene 05 (stair up, tower roof).

**GM Notes:**
- **Hint 2 delivery.** Lenne points them here ("Volenn was obsessed with moon-alignment — if the shard connects to that, his old study is the place to look"). Thessaly finds the calendar immediately. The circled date is three months from now. The party now has a deadline for the finale.
- **Calder and Volenn's note.** *"Check the ward on the third shard"* — Calder will read this as a dying man's to-do list. He will think about Tessamine. He does not say this aloud.
- **The ward and the Unbound Conclave.** The party can now explain to Lenne why the Conclave agents could not study the shard effectively — Volenn warded it against renegade intent. Lenne didn't know about the ward. This is a significant piece of information.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Volenn sat at this desk every morning for thirty years. He faced east because he liked watching the light come up over the foothills. He was not sentimental about it. He told himself it helped him think. He never verified this claim. He did some of his best work at this desk. He did some of his worst work here too. He did not distinguish between the two while he was doing it.*
