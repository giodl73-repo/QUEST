# Scene 02 — Ground Floor (Entry / Meeting Space)

**Location:** Volenn's Tower, ground floor. **Time:** Day 2 midday/afternoon.

**Read-aloud:**

> The ground floor is the only room in the tower that looks like anyone planned for visitors. One table, two chairs, a shelf of reference texts. Everything else is storage — crates and boxes, organized by a system only one person knows. Lenne Stormwatch stands at the top of the stair, looking down at you. She has not come down yet.

**Features:**

- **Lenne at the top of the stair.** She heard the party approach (she always hears people on the road). She has had 10 minutes to decide how she feels about this. She is evaluating. She says: *"You're not Unbound Conclave."* It is not a question. She has dealt with Unbound Conclave before — they do not knock.
- **The two chairs.** She does not use them often. The reference texts on the shelf are Volenn's, not hers — she has her own shelf upstairs.
- **What Lenne will say here.** She will tell the party: she knows about the Unbound Conclave (they have approached her twice before); she has the resonant shard; she has been studying it; she is not giving it to strangers without knowing why. If the party explains the compact: she listens. If they produce the reconstruction document: she reads it carefully and returns it. She does not trust easily, but she is not dishonest.
- **What Orik sees.** The storage crates have delivery dates — the oldest is three years ago. The most recent is four months ago. She has not had a delivery in four months. She is working from what she has.

**Encounter:** None here. If the party arrives having been in combat, Lenne notices and asks what happened. She will treat anyone who needs healing (she has a healer's kit; she is not a healer, but she is systematic).

**Connections:** Scene 01 (exterior); Scene 03 (stair, if Lenne trusts the party — shortcut: she leads them up; if not: they navigate the stair alone).

**GM Notes:**
- **"You're not Unbound Conclave."** This line does two things: it tells the party she knows the faction exists and has dealt with them, and it signals that their approach (by the main door, openly) has already distinguished them from the renegades. This is goodwill before any negotiation has begun.
- **Calder and the healer's kit.** If anyone was hurt in the road encounter: Calder has already begun treating them. Lenne will note this. A cleric of Mishakal who does not wait to be asked is someone she can calibrate against.
- **The storage crates.** Orik's "what did I miss last time?" decision-order will read these. He will know she has been isolated without saying it.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *The two chairs were Volenn's. He kept them for the three or four students he cycled through over the years — including Lenne, who sat in the left one for six years while he taught her. After he died she kept both chairs, even though she has no students. She does not sit in either of them herself. She works at a standing desk upstairs.*
