# Encounter — Tower Intrusion (Route C, conditional)

**Trigger:** Only if Osvin and the Quiet One survived the road ambush and were not captured. They regroup, wait two hours, then attempt the utility ladder while the party is inside.

**When:** Sera's passive Perception (16) detects them on the ladder during Scene 03 or 04 — she hears the rungs. She has 1 round to warn the party before the first agent reaches the window-hatch.

## Stats

Same as road encounter, minus any damage taken. The highwayman is not present — he fled after the road encounter regardless.

**Osvin:** Still without Thunderwave. He has a shortsword now (he discarded the staff on the road). He is angrier and more focused.

**Quiet One:** Has a second bola.

## Tactics

They are here for the shard. They know Lenne is in the tower. They are not here to kill anyone — but they are prepared to knock people unconscious to complete the mission.

Round 1 (after Sera's warning): Osvin at the hatch, forcing it open (Athletics +4 vs. Quiet One's previous DC 12 latch-hold). If the party has not relocked the hatch from inside: he enters first round. If the party has relocked it: one round to force it (Athletics DC 14 to hold it closed from outside).

**Lenne's response (key):** When the agents enter, Lenne immediately attempts Counterspell readied against Osvin's first action. She knows these people — they have been here before. She says: *"Not this time."* She will fight alongside the party against the Unbound Conclave agents. **This is the Route C trigger for her potential Route D arc-completion shift** — she sees the party defend the tower, she fights beside them, and afterward she is more likely to give the shard freely.

## What Lenne gains from this

If the party helps defend the tower: Lenne says, after: *"They've tried twice. They can't study it — something in the shard rejects them."* She is now actively on the party's side, even before the arc-completion fires. Route C can escalate to Route A or D in the aftermath.

## XP

2 Thugs = 200 base XP × 1.5 multiplier = 300 adjusted. Per PC: 75 XP.
