# Wandering Pressure — The Ambitious Mage's Vault

This adventure has two pressure systems: the road (the agents are ahead of the party) and the tower (Lenne's resistance is time-gated only by the agents' potential return).

## Road pressure (before arrival)

The party is two days behind the agents. The agents had the shard location from Tessamine's information leak (the Unbound Conclave's network in Korrath). The party cannot catch up on the road — but the ward on the shard means the agents couldn't take it. The party arrives to find the situation unresolved.

**This means:** The road ambush is the agents' second attempt (the first was at the tower, failed). The ambush is two days of frustration made physical.

## Tower Cold Pulse (d6, every hour in the tower)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | Wind shifts; the tower's heating system (a ward on the ground floor) responds with a faint hum. Lenne doesn't notice; she's used to it. |
| 2 | A bird settles on the parapet (roof, if party is there). Sera notes it: not a carrier bird; wrong species for messaging. Just a bird. |
| 3 | Pine resin smell from a box of preserved samples on the research floor shelf. Orik's hands go still for a moment. |
| 4 | Lenne pauses her explanation mid-sentence to write something down. She does not apologize for interrupting herself. The party waits. |
| 5 | The cargo lift groans — the east wind catches the rope. Nothing is wrong. It groans like this every time. |
| 6 | Calder sees one of Lenne's failed experiments on the shelf. He recognizes the method — systematic documentation of negative results. He was taught this by his order's first physician. He does not say anything. |

## Route C timer

If Osvin and the Quiet One survived the road ambush (both fled or were released), they regroup:

- **2 hours after the road:** They attempt the utility ladder. Sera's passive Perception (16) gives the party 1 round warning.
- **If the party stalls in the tower without resolving Lenne:** This is the pressure. The route C encounter fires after 2 hours of inactivity.
- **If the arc-completion fires:** The party is already moving; no Route C timer.
