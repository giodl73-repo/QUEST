## Summary

The Clause of Ambition is in Lenne Stormwatch's tower in the Kharolis foothills. She inherited it from her mentor Volenn Ashford eight years ago and has been studying it ever since, cataloguing its response to intent. She is brilliant, overworked, and does not know it. Two Unbound Conclave agents (Osvin and the Quiet One) have been at the tower first, failed to take the shard (Volenn's ward rejects renegade intent), and are waiting on the road.

The party fights the agents on the approach. Then they negotiate with Lenne. Orik has something specific to say.

---

## Hook

From Tessamine's reconstruction document: *"Clause of Ambition — last holder: Magister Volenn Ashford, Red Robe, Kharolis foothills. Tower location: [coordinates in archaic notation].  Volenn died 8 years ago."*

The party travels two days from Korrath. The agents are two days ahead.

## Setting & Background

**Volenn's Tower** — 5 floors, grey Kharolis stone, Kharolis foothills. A working wizard's tower, maintained by one person for eight years. It smells of burnt parchment and preserved herbs. Lenne Stormwatch is the only inhabitant.

**Lenne Stormwatch** — Red Robe wizard, 29. Volenn's apprentice; inherited the tower at 21. She has been working alone since. She is not reclusive by philosophy — she has simply been busy, and stopping to find people feels like losing time, and she has not examined this reasoning.

**The Clause of Ambition** — Inscribed: *"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* It vibrates when held by someone with genuine ambition. Lenne has been holding it for eight years.

**The Unbound Conclave** — Same faction as Korrath. Two agents (Osvin + the Quiet One) are on the road, waiting. They cannot study the shard because Volenn's ward makes it inert for them. They are angrier and better prepared than at Korrath.

## Scenes

### Road Encounter (non-optional)

**Read-aloud:**

> The road narrows where it enters the foothills. Two figures in grey robes step from behind a cart that has been turned sideways across the track. One holds a short staff. The other is already moving to your left flank, and he has rope.

This is Osvin and the Quiet One. They have been waiting two days. See Encounters Appendix.

**After the encounter:** The party continues to the tower. Whatever the outcome (agents fled, captured, downed), the party arrives at the tower.

---

### Scene 01 — Tower Exterior

**Read-aloud (arrival):**

> The tower is five floors of grey Kharolis stone, slightly off-center on its foundation. A spiral stair is visible through a narrow window on each floor. On the northeast face: a utility ladder, iron rungs set into stone, worn smooth. On the east face: a cargo pulley and rope. The main door is solid wood, unlocked. It smells of preserved herbs before you open it.

**Features:**
- Utility ladder (NE): worn smooth (someone uses it regularly). Connects to research floor via window-hatch. Thieves' Tools DC 12 or Athletics DC 14 to open from outside.
- Cargo lift (E): inside-operated only. Floors 1 and 3.

**GM Notes:**
- Orik counts entries before entering: main door (unlocked), utility ladder (northeast, bypasses ground floor), cargo lift (east, inside only). *"Three ways in. She knows about the ladder — the rungs are worn smooth."*
- Sera checks treeline: no third agent. The road agents were the full team.

---

### Scene 02 — Ground Floor

**Read-aloud:**

> One table, two chairs, a shelf of reference texts. Everything else is storage — crates and boxes, organized by a system only one person knows. Lenne Stormwatch stands at the top of the stair, looking down at you. She has not come down yet.

**Features:**
- Lenne at the stair. She says: *"You're not Unbound Conclave."* Not a question. She heard the combat. She has been watching the treeline.
- Storage crates: oldest delivery 3 years ago; most recent 4 months ago. Orik notices.
- Two chairs: Volenn's. Lenne does not use them. She has a standing desk upstairs.
- If Lenne trusts the party (open explanation): she leads them upstairs directly (shortcut). If not: they navigate alone.

**GM Notes:**
- "You're not Unbound Conclave" signals she has dealt with them before. The party's approach (main door, openly, after fighting on the road) has distinguished them.
- Calder: if anyone is hurt, he has already begun treating them. Lenne notices a cleric who doesn't wait to be asked.

---

### Scene 03 — Research Floor

**Read-aloud:**

> The research floor is organized the way a mind is organized when it has been working alone for a long time — perfectly logical to one person, impenetrable to anyone else. Notes cover the east wall floor to ceiling. A standing desk faces north. On the desk, in a padded cradle: the shard. It is slightly larger than the one in your pack. When Lenne picks it up to show you, her hand trembles — half a second, then still.

**Features:**
- The shard on a padded cradle. Lenne picks it up (habit). Vibration visible (Symptom 1).
- Notes wall: 3 years of documentation. Investigation DC 12: she has been cataloguing the vibration response correctly. She lacks the origin context.
- The inscription: Thessaly reads automatically. *"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* Lenne: *"'Share of the sky.' That's lunar. Volenn had a moon-calendar. Third floor."*
- Orik reads the room: standing desk only; bedroll in corner; failed experiments on shelf (she kept the failures); meals that suggest irregular eating.
- **DM-letter:** See `dm-letter-scene-03.md`. Orik's arc-completion (naming the hall) requires witnessing. **Read the DM-letter before this session.**

**Lenne's decision (see NPC section for behavior matrix):**
- Route A: trade for reconstruction document + network connection.
- Route C: agents intrude via ladder (Sera passive 16 hears them; 1 round warning). Lenne counterspells and fights alongside party.
- Route D: Orik names the hall. Lenne hands the shard over. Vibration stops mid-handoff (Symptom 4).

**Reaction moment (artifact removal):**
When the shard leaves Lenne's hand (any route), the padded cradle on the desk is suddenly wrong — there is an indentation in the padding in exactly the shape of where the shard has rested. Eight years. The indentation will not spring back.

**Encounter:** Tower Intrusion (Route C conditional — see Encounters Appendix).

**Treasure:** Clause of Ambition.

---

### Scene 04 — Volenn's Old Study

**Read-aloud:**

> Volenn's study hasn't been touched much since he died. A desk facing east — toward the foothills, not the road. A moon-calendar on the south wall, four feet wide, hand-drawn with decades of annotation. A single note on the desk: *"Convergence in 3 months (est.). Check the ward on the third shard."* The note is Volenn's handwriting.

**Features:**
- Moon-calendar: **Hint 2 delivery.** Thessaly (passive Arcana 18) reads a circled date annotation: *"Triple convergence — all three moons full simultaneously. Last occurred 300 years ago. First occurrence since the Cataclysm. Compact clauses written to align with this event?"* The party now has a deadline: three months for the finale.
- Volenn's note: *"Convergence in 3 months (est.). Check the ward on the third shard."* He knew; he died first.
- Volenn's ward: Investigation DC 14 or Thessaly Arcana DC 10 detects it — a passive ward that makes the shard inert for renegade intent. This is why the agents couldn't study it.

**GM Notes:**
- Lenne points them here: *"Volenn was obsessed with moon-alignment — if the shard connects to that, his old study is the place to look."*
- Calder reads the note as a dying man's to-do list. He thinks of Tessamine. He does not say this.
- Thessaly, after seeing the calendar: quietly, to herself: *"Three months."* She is calculating. The party will want to know what she calculated, when she's ready to say.

---

### Scene 05 — Tower Roof (Optional)

**Read-aloud:**

> A ring of stone parapet, waist-height. A wooden bench, weathered and steady. The Kharolis foothills spread east and south — mining roads, small holds, the distant line of the mountains. You can see the road you came in on. There is no one on it.

**Features:**
- Lenne's bench: she comes here when she needs to stop. She does not think of this as rest.
- Arc-completion fallback: if Orik hasn't spoken, Lenne comes here after the conversation stalls. She mentions she hasn't been to the market in four months. This is the opening.
- Three moons visible at night: Thessaly sees Nuitari (magic-sensitive). She says: *"Three months."* This is the first time she's said it where the party can hear.

## Treasures

### The Clause of Ambition

*Slightly larger than the Clause of Witness. When held by someone with genuine ambition, it vibrates — felt before heard.*

**Appearance:** Obsidian shard, 12 cm, same tablet origin as the Clause of Witness. Inscription: *"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* Sigil of Archmage Toven of the Red Robes (PC 190–218) — a small stylized flame.

**Presence / Desire:** Wants to be used. Has been vibrating for eight years in Lenne's hands, measuring her ambition continuously.

**Passive properties:**
- Vibrates (half-second tremor, felt before heard) when held by a creature with genuine ambition.
- Arcana DC 12 to identify as magical compact fragment. Thessaly: automatic.
- Insight DC 14 (held ≥1 min): the vibration is recognition, not power — the shard measures, not fuels.
- Hint 2 connection: "share of the sky" = lunar. Connects to moon-calendar in Scene 04.
- Route D Symptom 4: vibration stops when Lenne releases the shard freely. Felt by Lenne; visible to party.

**Curse — The Measure:**
Attuned creature is aware once/day when claiming more than their share (inward recognition only; shard does not enforce). Disadvantage on Persuasion checks when overreaching.

**Provenance:**
1. Archmage Toven, Red Robes (PC 190–218)
2. Tower of High Sorcery, Istar (destroyed PC 220)
3. Multiple intermediate holders, ~200 years
4. Magister Volenn Ashford (30–8 years ago)
5. Lenne Stormwatch (8 years ago – present)

## NPCs

### Lenne Stormwatch

*Red Robe wizard, 29. Brilliant, overworked. Does not know it.*

**Combatant (reluctant).** AC 12 (Mage Armor 15) | HP 38. Spells: Counterspell, Fireball, Hold Person, Misty Step, Shield.

**Emotional position:**
- **Wants:** To understand what the shard is and what she spent eight years on.
- **Can't say:** She is afraid the work was a waste.
- **Condition:** Someone tells her the work was worth doing even if the object is not what she thought.

**Arc-completion:** Orik names the hall. Lenne goes still. Then picks up shard. Says: *"Eight years. I thought it was a power source."* Hands it to Orik. Vibration stops mid-handoff. Then: *"Volenn's old study — third floor. If it connects to moon-alignment, look at the calendar."*

**Behavior matrix:**

| Party action | Response |
|---|---|
| "We're here for the shard" | "No." Flat. Conversation is over if that's the opening. |
| "Can we see your research?" | Considers. Usually yes — she is proud of the work. |
| Explains what the shard is | Listens carefully. Asks two clarifying questions. Does not immediately give. |
| Shows reconstruction document | Reads, compares to trial data. "Three of my patterns match your clauses." Goodwill gain. |
| Orik names the hall | Goes still. Then arc-completion. |
| Agents intrude | Immediately hostile to agents. Works with party. After: more open to Route A or D. |
