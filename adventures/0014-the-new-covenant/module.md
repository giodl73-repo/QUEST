# The New Covenant — DM Module

*The tower has been waiting three hundred years. The three moons are in alignment for the first time since the Cataclysm. Six shards. An opening sentence that has never been spoken aloud. The campaign ends here.*

**Tier:** 2  |  **Party:** 5 PCs + witnesses  |  **Expected playtime:** 1 session

## Summary

The ruins of the Tower of High Sorcery at Istar. The triple-moon convergence creates a three-hour window when the inner chamber is accessible. The compact-wardens have six shards, assembled witnesses, Lenne's accountability language, Sylaren's amendment, and Thessaly's opening sentence.

One combat encounter at the waterline: the Conclave Neutralists, a separate opposition faction who believe compact restoration would illegitimately legitimize the Orders. Hard encounter; finale combat load; Lenne's Projected Ward and Orik's Battle Master get their showcase.

Then the descent. The outer chamber. The vault. The speaking.

**Three-hour convergence window.** If combat exceeds 90 minutes, the descent becomes more difficult (partial flooding). Route C if the window closes before the speaking completes.

---

## Hook

Lenne's logistics: the ruins are accessible during the convergence. The witnesses have been assembled. The compact has been composed over the course of seven sessions. The sail from Palanthas takes three days.

---

## Encounters

### Waterline Combat — Conclave Neutralists (Hard)

**Context:** A faction of scholars and former mages who oppose compact restoration because they believe it would give any Order a legitimacy claim they dispute. Legitimate concern; wrong method.

**Social resolution (very hard):** Persuasion DC 17 or Lenne presenting accountability clause text directly (gives advantage on check). If convinced: they stand aside. Possible but unlikely.

**2× Conclave Neutralist Mage (CR 3):** AC 15 (Mage Armor), HP 38. Fireball (L3, 8d6, DC 14, 1/day), Counterspell (reaction), Hold Person (concentration), Web (L2, concentration).

**1× Conclave Neutralist Fighter (CR 3):** AC 16, HP 52. 2× Longsword +6 (1d8+4). Action Surge (1/short rest).

**Adjusted XP:** 5250 (Hard for 5 PCs). 1050 XP/PC.

**Tactics:** Mage 1 opens with Web. Mage 2 holds Counterspell. Fighter targets isolated party members.

**Lenne vs. Mages Counterspell:** Both mages have Counterspell. Lenne has more L3 slots than them combined; she wins the duel if she saves her slots.

**Retreat trigger:** Both mages below 15 HP → disengage, withdraw. Do not pursue.

**90-minute timer:** If combat takes longer than 90 min (unlikely for 5 PCs), the descent will be partially flooded. Not unnavigable but urgent.

## Scenes

### Scene 01 — Entry Passage

**Read-aloud:**

> The stairs descend below the waterline. During the convergence they are dry. Pre-Cataclysm stone, preserved. The air is cold and smells of old stone and a warded space unsealed after three hundred years.

Orik checks the stairs (craft-witness x6). Sound. Safe. He is the first one down.

### Scene 02 — Outer Chamber

**Read-aloud:**

> High ceiling. Moon-alignment carvings: Solinari, Lunitari, Nuitari — the convergence position, all three full. The chamber was designed for thirty. There are seven of you, plus witnesses.

Witnesses assemble: Mira, Deva, Vorn, coordinator, witnesses-by-letter (Sevven, Morreth's chapel record). Lenne and Vorn have a final exchange about the accountability language. Calder blesses the chamber.

### Scene 03 — Inner Vault

**Read-aloud:**

> The inner vault. A stone platform at the center. Six indentations in a circle, each shaped for a shard. At the center: a seventh indentation, larger, smooth — where the full tablet rested. Empty. Above: a shaft to the sky. All three moons visible simultaneously. The first time since the Cataclysm.

**THE SPEAKING (Route D):**

**Opening sentence** (Thessaly, from the reconstruction document margin — first time spoken aloud):
[Hold space. The DM does not know this sentence. Read dm-letter-scene-03.md.]

**Six clauses (one per PC; shard placed as clause is spoken):**

1. Thessaly: *"Let no mortal act without witness."* — Witness shard placed. It brightens (same as Session 1).
2. Orik: *"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* — Ambition shard placed. It vibrates briefly.
3. Sera: *"Let no Order forget the cost of what it asks."* — Grief shard placed. Room temperature. Sera reads it. Enough.
4. Calder: *"Let any mortal amend what any mortal has sworn. And as Sylaren wrote, twelve years later: what any mortal swore, any mortal may unsay. This was always true."* — Defiance shard placed. Second inscription honored.
5. Lenne: *"Let the Orders answer to those they serve."* + accountability language (three sentences). — Reckoning shard placed. The weight is placed with it.
6. All five together: *"Let no covenant hold anger as its foundation."* — Forgiveness shard placed.

**After the sixth shard:** The seventh space fills with a quality of stillness. The witnesses in the outer chamber feel it through the stone. The convergence window begins to close.

**DM-letter:** See `dm-letter-scene-03.md`. Read before the session. Read again before Scene 03. The opening sentence is the hold-space moment of the entire campaign.

## DM Cheatsheet

### Three-Route Outcomes

| Route | Condition | Opening sentence? | Compact complete? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A (Charter)** | Combat cleared; speaking begins; Thessaly doesn't speak opening sentence | No | Yes (without it) |
| **C (Default)** | Convergence window closes before speaking completes | N/A | No; campaign ends unresolved |
| **D (Inversion)** | Thessaly speaks opening sentence; all six clauses spoken; Sylaren's amendment + accountability language incorporated | Yes | Yes |

### Attunement suggestion

Before the speaking: any PC who has not yet attuned should consider attuning during the outer chamber scene. The compact's speaking is more resonant if spoken by someone bearing what it requires. Thessaly attuning at minimum; the Witness clause requires *"let no mortal act without witness"* and she is the Witness shard's keeper.
