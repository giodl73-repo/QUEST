## Open questions

1. **Attunement before the speaking.** Does Thessaly attune the Witness shard? Does Sera attune the Grief shard? The compact is more resonant if spoken by someone bearing what it requires. 2. **The opening sentence.** Thessaly has it written. The witnesses will hear it for the first time. What is it? (The player decides; the DM does not know.)
3. **The combat.** Hard encounter. Two mages with Counterspell; one fighter with Action Surge. Lenne's Projected Ward and Counterspell are essential. Orik's Menacing Attack + Commander's Strike creates space. Calder holds heals until after the mages' first round. Sera finds the high point.
4. **The speaking.** Six clauses, one per PC. Calder speaks the Defiance clause with Sylaren's amendment incorporated. Lenne speaks the Reckoning clause with her accountability language. All five speak Forgiveness together.
5. **Route D condition.** Thessaly speaks the opening sentence. All six clauses spoken. Sylaren's amendment spoken as part of the Defiance clause. Accountability language spoken as part of the Reckoning clause. Forgiveness clause spoken together. All shards placed. All witnesses present.
