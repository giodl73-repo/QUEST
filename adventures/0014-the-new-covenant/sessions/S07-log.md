# S07 LOG — The New Covenant

## Session Summary

**THE CAMPAIGN IS COMPLETE.**

Dawn at the Istar waterline. The convergence window was three hours. The Conclave Neutralists were at the waterline — three of them, two mages and a fighter — and they were not subtle about it. The fight took three rounds. Lenne Counterspelled the Web (L2) and then the Fireball (L3). Orik went through the center; Menacing Attack on the fighter (frightened, Thessaly protected); Commander's Strike so Sera fired twice in Round 1. Calder dropped Bless on the right three targets before the mages had a second action. Thessaly made her Wisdom save against Hold Person (total 17 vs DC 14; Bless +1d4). The mages retreated when both fell below 15 HP.

Thessaly and Sera had both attuned on the voyage: the Witness shard (Thessaly) and the Grief shard (Sera). Neither announced this. Sera received her first stranger's grief on the ship the night before. She noted she did not mind.

In the inner vault, Thessaly read the opening sentence from the reconstruction document margin — the first time she had spoken it aloud:

*"We speak this not to bind the Orders to each other but to bind each Order to the people who live beneath the consequences of what the Orders do, and to bind the right of those people to hold the Orders accountable for those consequences."*

Then six clauses, one per PC. The Witness shard brightened when Thessaly placed it (same as Session 1). The Ambition shard vibrated when Orik placed it (same vibration as Lenne's eight years; he held it a second longer than necessary). The Grief shard was room temperature and Sera read it and it was enough. Calder spoke the Defiance clause with Sylaren's full amendment incorporated — not as a footnote but as the clause's completion: *"what any mortal swore, any mortal may unsay; this was always true."* Lenne spoke the Reckoning clause with the full accountability language: three specific sentences naming what the Orders must demonstrate, how, and to whom. All five spoke Forgiveness together.

The seventh space filled with the quality of stillness that had been missing from this room for three hundred years.

Mira Coldsteel said: *"It's done."*

Orik checked the first stair step on the way up.

### Scene 03 — Inner Vault (THE COMPACT SPEAKING)

**Thessaly's opening sentence (spoken for the first time):**
*"We speak this not to bind the Orders to each other but to bind each Order to the people who live beneath the consequences of what the Orders do, and to bind the right of those people to hold the Orders accountable for those consequences."*

**The six clauses:**

1. **Thessaly (Witness shard):** *"Let no mortal act without witness."* — Shard brightens. Campaign echo: Session 1.

2. **Orik (Ambition shard):** *"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* — Shard vibrates. He holds it a moment. He puts it down.

3. **Sera (Grief shard):** *"Let no Order forget the cost of what it asks."* — Room temperature. She reads it. Enough.

4. **Calder (Defiance shard):** *"Let any mortal amend what any mortal has sworn."* + Sylaren's amendment: *"And as Sylaren wrote, twelve years after she swore it: what any mortal swore, any mortal may unsay. This was always true."* — Shard warms briefly. Session 4 Hint 3 honored.

5. **Lenne (Reckoning shard):** *"Let the Orders answer to those they serve."* + Accountability language: *"The Orders demonstrate accountability through: publicly naming the costs of their decisions to the communities affected; accepting amendment requests from any mortal affected by an Order's action; and providing account to a council of non-Order witnesses within forty days of any significant Order action. This is not courtesy. This is the compact's requirement."* — Weight acknowledged.

6. **All five together:** *"Let no covenant hold anger as its foundation."* — Forgiveness shard placed. Sixth.

**The seventh space fills.** Not an object. A quality of stillness. The witnesses feel it through the stone.

Mira: *"It's done."*

The convergence window begins to close. Orik is at the stairs. He checks the first step. Sound.
