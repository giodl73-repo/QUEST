# Scene 02 — Outer Chamber

**Read-aloud:**

> The outer chamber is pre-Cataclysm: high ceiling, stone carved with the three moon-alignment symbols (Solinari, Lunitari, Nuitari — the alignment that is currently, for the first time since the Cataclysm, converging). Tessamine's reconstruction document describes this as the "witnessing chamber" — where the Orders stood when the compact was sworn. It is large enough for thirty people. There are seven of you.

**Features:**

- **The moon-alignment carvings.** Thessaly (Arcana 18): the symbols show the convergence position — all three moons at full, all three in alignment. The carving was designed for this moment. The chamber knew it was coming.
- **The witnesses assembled.** Mira, Deva, Vorn, the coordinator, Lenne (as Red Robe), Thessaly (as White Robe), the party. The witnesses know their role: to be present. Not to approve; not to swear. To witness.
- **Vorn and Lenne discuss.** Before entering the vault, Vorn and Lenne have a brief exchange about the accountability clause language. Lenne reads it aloud. It is good. It is the best single piece of compact language in Tessamine's reconstruction or anyone else's. Vorn says: *"Yes. That's right."*

**GM Notes:**
- **This chamber is the pause before the vault.** The witnesses gather. The speakers understand what they are about to do. This is not a scene with a decision; it is a scene with a preparation.
- **Calder** will ask if anyone needs healing before they enter the vault. Nobody does. He blesses the chamber anyway.

**Connections:** Entry Passage (01, above); Inner Vault (03, through the door).
