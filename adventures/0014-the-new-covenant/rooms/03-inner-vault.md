# Scene 03 — Inner Vault (THE COMPACT SPEAKING)

**Read-aloud:**

> The inner vault is smaller than the outer chamber. A stone platform at the center, slightly raised. Six indentations in a circle, each shaped for a shard. At the center: a seventh indentation, larger, smooth — where the full tablet rested. Three hundred years of emptiness. Above: a narrow shaft to the sky, aligned with the convergence position. All three moons are visible simultaneously. The first time since the Cataclysm.

**The Speaking — Part 3 (Route D):**

Thessaly reads the opening sentence from the reconstruction document margin. She reads it to the witnesses, to the vault, to the empty center. This is the first time anyone has heard it.

Then the six clauses are spoken, one at a time, with each shard placed as it is named:

1. **Witness** (Thessaly places; reads): *"Let no mortal act without witness."* — Selindra's clause. The shard brightens when placed (the same brightening as when Thessaly first read it in Henneth's study, Session 1).

2. **Ambition** (Orik places; reads): *"Let no Order claim more than its share of the sky."* — Toven's clause. The shard vibrates when placed — the same vibration Lenne felt for eight years.

3. **Grief** (Sera places; reads): *"Let no Order forget the cost of what it asks."* — Davan's clause. Room temperature. It does not react. Sera reads it. That is enough.

4. **Defiance** (Calder places; reads, then adds): *"Let any mortal amend what any mortal has sworn."* — Sylaren's clause. Then, as part of the speaking: *"And as Sylaren wrote, twelve years later: what any mortal swore, any mortal may unsay. This was always true."* Hint 3's promise honored in the speaking itself.

5. **Reckoning** (Lenne places; reads, then adds the accountability language): *"Let the Orders answer to those they serve."* Then: Lenne speaks three sentences of accountability language she and Vorn drafted. Specific. Numbered. Who the Orders answer to; how they demonstrate this; what "answering" means in material terms.

6. **Forgiveness** (all five speak together): *"Let no covenant hold anger as its foundation."*

After the sixth shard: the seventh space fills. Not with an object — with a quality of stillness that has not been in this room for three hundred years. The witnesses in the outer chamber will feel it through the stone.

**Route D confirmation:** The compact is spoken with Sylaren's amendment incorporated. The accountability language is part of the Reckoning clause. The Forgiveness clause is last and spoken together. The opening sentence was heard for the first time. All six shards are placed. The witnesses are present.

**DM-letter:** See `dm-letter-scene-03.md`. The opening sentence is the hold-space moment. Thessaly has been composing it for three sessions. The witnesses do not know what she is going to say. The DM holds space.

**P0 fallback — Route A:** If Thessaly does not speak the opening sentence (she freezes, changes her mind, or the session doesn't reach it): the compact is spoken clause-by-clause without the opening sentence. It is still a valid compact. It does not have the same weight. The center space fills, less fully.

**P0 fallback — Route C:** If the convergence window closes before the speaking completes (the combat took too long): the party can return at the next window — three hundred years from now, per Volenn's calendar. Route C is a permanent Campaign End without resolution. The party has what they gathered; the compact is not spoken.
