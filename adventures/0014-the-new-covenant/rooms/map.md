# Map — The New Covenant

## Topology

The Tower of High Sorcery ruins. Underwater approach by boat; a waterline access point during the convergence; descending stairs into the inner chamber.

```
[APPROACH — waterline combat encounter]
        |
        v (descending stairs, dry during convergence)
[01 ENTRY PASSAGE]
        |
        v
[02 OUTER CHAMBER — where witnesses assemble]
        |
        v
[03 INNER VAULT — the compact's center; six indentations + seventh empty space]
```

**No loops.** The finale is linear. The party moves in one direction: down, then speaking.

**Vertical:** Descending from sea level into the preserved inner chamber.

**The vault (Scene 03):** Six indentations in a stone platform at the center — each shaped for a specific shard. The seventh, center space is a smooth depression, larger, shaped for a tablet rather than a shard. This is where the original compact rested. It is empty.

**Time pressure:** The convergence window is three hours. If the combat takes longer than 90 minutes, the approach to the chamber will be affected by rising water. This is not an instant fail but a pressure mechanism.
