# Scene 01 — Entry Passage

**Read-aloud:**

> The stairs descend below the waterline. During the convergence they are dry — the tide has pulled back further than any chart records. Pre-Cataclysm stone, preserved. It has been three hundred years since anyone walked these stairs. The air is cold and smells of old stone and something else — the specific quality of a warded space that has been sealed for a very long time.

**Features:**

- **The ward residue.** Investigation DC 12 or Lenne Arcana auto: a preservation ward was active here for three hundred years; it is no longer active (the compact's shards are present; the ward recognized the condition and released). The chamber was waiting for this moment.
- **The witnesses.** They are here or arriving: Mira Coldsteel (she sailed with the party), Deva (she made her own way), Vorn (she arranged a fast vessel from Palanthas), the coordinator (she kept her word), Sevven's letter (carried by Thessaly). Morreth is not present; his chapel is in the witness-list by name. Six witnesses. Plus the party: five speakers.
- **Lenne's notebook.** She has all seventeen pages. She has used twelve. The accountability language is complete.

**GM Notes:**

- **Orik reads the stairs** (craft-witness x6 — sixth consecutive session). The stone is sound. The descent is safe. He is the first one down.
- **The witnesses** assemble in the outer chamber. They are not performing witnesses; they are present witnesses. This is the distinction the compact's design requires.

**Connections:** Approach (above, waterline); Outer Chamber (02, below).
