# Encounter — Research Center Defense (Route C)

**Trigger:** Party attempts to take the shard by force, search for it without Vorn's permission, or threatens a researcher. Vorn's protective detail mobilizes.

**Why Hard:** These are trained professionals, not hired thugs. They have worked with Vorn for years; they believe in the research. They are defending something they think matters.

## Stat blocks

**Vorn Haleth's Research Guard (×3):** CR 1, AC 14 (light armor + Dex), HP 22, Speed 30.
STR 14, DEX 16, CON 12, INT 13, WIS 12, CHA 10.
**Attacks:** 2× shortsword +5 (1d6+3) or hand crossbow +5 (1d6+3, 30/120 ft.).
**Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attacks if ally is adjacent to target.
**Trained defense:** Once per short rest, can use Dodge as bonus action.

**Senior Researcher (Mage-guard, ×1):** CR 4, AC 12 (Mage Armor 15), HP 40.
**Spells:** Fireball (L3, 1/day), Hold Person (concentration), Sleep, Web (concentration).
**Counterspell (reaction):** +5, auto-succeeds vs. L3 or lower.

**Adjusted XP:** 3× Guard (200 XP) + 1× Mage-guard (1100 XP) = 1700 base. ×2.5 multiplier (4 enemies) = 4250 adjusted. Per PC (5): 850 XP. **Hard encounter.** Most dangerous fight of the campaign.

**Encounter-balancer check:**
- Fireball threat: same as Mira's at S03. Lenne's Projected Ward essential. Party should have learned from Mira.
- Action economy: 4 enemies × roughly 2 attacks each = 8 attacks/round vs. party 5 actions. Enemy-heavy. The guards' Pack Tactics amplifies this.
- Counterspell duel: Lenne vs. Mage-guard. Lenne has 3 L3 slots. Mage-guard has 1 Fireball (L3) + Counterspell. If Lenne saves her L3 slots for this: she can stop both Fireball and Hold Person. If she uses them elsewhere: one or both land.

**Tactics:**
Round 1: Mage-guard targets the party's cluster with Fireball if possible (or Web if tight quarters). Three guards advance, seeking Pack Tactics adjacency.
Round 2+: Guards focus on whoever has the shard (Thessaly, most likely). Mage-guard holds Counterspell for Lenne's Counterspell.

**Retreat trigger:** Mage-guard reduced to 10 HP or Counterspell spent. Vorn, from her study: *"Stand down. Let them go."* She will not let her people die for a shard she might have given willingly. Route C ends with the party having the shard but Vorn not as a witness.

**Capture yield (Mage-guard):** Confirms Vorn's purpose; confirms three other splinter cells; confirms she was the Palanthas contact for all of them.
