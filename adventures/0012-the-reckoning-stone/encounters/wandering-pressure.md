# Wandering Pressure — The Reckoning Stone

## Research center pressure

No hard clock. Vorn has been expecting the party. She will not rush them out. But if the party is evasive, dishonest, or attempts to probe the building for the shard without her knowledge, her staff will note it and Route C will trigger.

**Route C trigger conditions (any one fires combat):**
- Party asks to see the shard without engaging with the research conversation.
- Party attempts to access the Archive without Vorn's permission.
- Party uses Detect Magic to scan the building without asking.
- Party uses Thieves' Tools on any door.
- Party threatens or intimidates a researcher.

## Research Center Cold Pulse (d6, every 30 minutes)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | A researcher asks Lenne a question about the Clause of Ambition's vibration behavior. She has read the trial data and wants a second opinion. Lenne spends fifteen minutes discussing methodology. This is not a distraction; it is two people doing science together. |
| 2 | Orik checks the east wall structurally (service stair access, ceiling load). He does not say why. Vorn's senior researcher notices and says nothing. |
| 3 | Pine resin smell from a preservative oil on the archive shelves. Orik's hands go still. |
| 4 | Calder finds the Dalimvar voting minutes again — in Vorn's primary-source archive. Sylaren's dissent is in the margin, in the original. He holds it. Then he puts it down. |
| 5 | Thessaly is standing in front of the twelve-year summary panel and has been for four minutes without moving. She is reading it for the third time. Nobody mentions this. |
| 6 | Sera does a full perimeter assessment of the building's exits without anyone asking her to. She reports to Orik: four exits, two observed by staff, two unobserved. He nods. Neither explains this to the others. |
