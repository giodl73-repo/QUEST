## Summary

Vorn Haleth has been studying the compact's history for twelve years in a converted merchant house in Palanthas. She has the Clause of Reckoning. She trained the splinter cells. She has already decoded Tessamine's records. She has been expecting this party.

She is not the enemy. She is a mirror: someone who has been right about everything the compact was supposed to do, wrong in how she pursued it. Thessaly is her counterpart: wrong about one thing she did, right about what needs to be fixed.

Hint 4 is on the east wall of the Public Research Room. It has been there for three years. The session's job is for Thessaly to perform the reckoning Vorn has been waiting for someone to perform.

Mira's message: *"Vorn Haleth. Palanthas merchant district, no sign on the building. She has the shard and she has twelve years of research. She trained the splinter cells. She will not recognize my authority to stand anyone down. She will receive you."*

## Setting & Background

**Vorn Haleth** — Research archivist, 44. Founded The Reckoning Archive. Twelve years studying the compact; concluded it was a protection mechanism, not a governance framework. Trained field operatives who she called researchers. The Palanthas contact for the splinter cells.

**The Reckoning Archive** — A four-story converted merchant house. No external sign. Six permanent staff; three trained as security. A private archive on floor 2. Vorn's study on floor 3 with the shard.

## Scenes

### Scene 01 — Reception

**Read-aloud:** A converted merchant house with no external sign. Orderly. Staff member at desk says: *"Vorn Haleth is expecting you."*

Vorn told her staff to receive the compact-wardens. The reception is prepared, not hostile.

---

### Scene 02 — Public Research Room

**Read-aloud:**

> Six researchers at long tables. Twelve years of compact documentation covers the east wall — working notes, post-it cross-references, overlapping timelines. At the center: a panel titled *"Compact Intent: 12yr Summary."* Vorn is at the north end. She says: *"You found it faster than I expected."*

**Hint 4 delivery (auto-delivered, visible from doorway):**

The summary panel reads: *"The compact was a protection mechanism, not a governance framework. The seven clauses are the minimum acknowledgment that the Orders must provide to the people who live under the consequences of what the Orders do."* Passive Investigation 10. Thessaly/Lenne auto (Arcana 18). Calder auto (Insight 14: recognizes this as what the Dalimvar protocol violated).

**Route C trigger:** If party attempts forced access, scanning without permission, or threatening researchers → Research Center Defense (see Encounters Appendix).

**GM Notes:** Lenne will read the research and say: *"You've been doing this alone for twelve years."* Vorn: *"Yes. But you have the same conclusion in less time."* This is not small talk; it is two researchers recognizing each other.

---

### Scene 03 — Private Archive (Optional)

Available if the party wants to verify Vorn's work. Primary-source documents including Dalimvar voting minutes, PC 199 (Sylaren's dissent in the margin). Lenne confirms the research is rigorous. Calder will hold the voting minutes and put them down.

---

### Scene 04 — Vorn's Study

**Read-aloud:**

> Vorn's study. A desk covered in active research. Three notebooks open simultaneously. A locked glass case on the south wall holds the Clause of Reckoning — visible through the glass, obsidian, noticeably heavy even in its cloth wrapping. She sits and looks at the party. She does not open the case yet.

**Features:** The shard in its case (room temperature; weight visible even through glass). Three open notebooks (Lenne reads them immediately; most recent entry: *"The reckoning is performed in the speaking"*).

**Vorn's assessment:** She will ask some version of: *"How do I know you understand what the reckoning requires — not of the Orders, but of you?"*

**Route D (arc-completion):** Thessaly names the Test wound — the spell, the cost, her sister who doesn't know. Vorn goes quiet. Then she names the Crystalmir family her researcher frightened. She gives the shard. *"This clause is for both of us."*

**Route A (fallback):** After 20 minutes without the arc-completion condition being met: Vorn offers a trade — her full twelve years of research documented as the compact's historical foundation, credited in the record. The party agrees. Shard given.

**DM-letter:** `dm-letter-scene-04.md`. Thessaly naming the Test wound is the hold-space moment. Read before the session.

## Treasures

### The Clause of Reckoning

*Fifth shard. Heavier than it looks. The last clause sworn at the compact signing. Toven's open-handed flame sigil.*

**Front:** *"Let the Orders answer to those they serve."* Archmage Toven of the Red Robes (PC 190-218; also swore the Clause of Ambition). Sigil: open flame.

**Passive:** Heavier than it appears (characteristic of the stone, not a spell). Room temperature.

**Hint 4 delivery (via Research Room panel + inscription):** The compact was a protection mechanism, not a governance framework. The accountability is in the act of speaking — each Order acknowledges they answer to the people. The reckoning is performed in the speaking.

**Route D:** When Thessaly names the Test wound, the shard's weight becomes noticeable to everyone in the room. Acknowledged, not lighter.

**Curse — The weight of responsibility:** Attuned creature becomes aware once/day of who they owe an accounting to. Inward recognition; no compulsion. Specific: "This person. This action. They are waiting."

## Encounters Appendix

### Research Center Defense (Route C)

**3× Research Guard:** CR 1, AC 14, HP 22. 2× Shortsword +5 (1d6+3). Pack Tactics. Dodge as bonus action (1/short rest).

**1× Senior Researcher (Mage-guard):** CR 4, AC 15, HP 40. Fireball (L3, 8d6, DC 14, 1/day), Hold Person (concentration), Web. Counterspell (reaction, +5, auto L3 or lower).

**Adjusted XP:** 4250 (Hard for 5 PCs). Per PC: 850 XP.

**Lenne vs. Mage-guard Counterspell:** The Mage-guard will Counterspell Lenne's Counterspell. Lenne has 3 L3 slots; Mage-guard has 1 Fireball (L3) and Counterspell. If Lenne saves her L3 slots: she can stop both Fireball and Hold Person.

**Retreat trigger:** Mage-guard at 10 HP or Counterspell spent → Vorn calls: *"Stand down. Let them go."*

**Route C outcome:** Shard recovered. Vorn not a witness at the compact.
