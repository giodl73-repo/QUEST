# Scene 05 — Roof Garden (Optional)

**Read-aloud:**

> The roof is a small garden — container plants, a bench, a view of Palanthas from the merchant district's fourth floor. The city is visible: the harbor to the south, the scholarly district to the east, the palace towers to the north. From here you can see the distance to the convergence deadline as a quality of sky rather than a number.

**Features:** Vorn's one concession to not-working. She comes here sometimes. The party can come here after the arc-completion; it is a place to process what happened before going back to the work.

**GM Notes:** Thessaly will calculate the convergence date against the sky (seventy-nine days). Orik will check the building's roof structure. Lenne will look at the research center's layout from above — she is already thinking about the compact's design questions Vorn raised. Sera will look at the harbor road. Calder will be quiet.

**Connections:** Scene 04 (Vorn's Study).
