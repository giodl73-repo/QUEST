# Scene 02 — Public Research Room

**Read-aloud:**

> Six researchers at long tables, working. Twelve years of compact-related documentation covers the east wall — a different display from the White Quill's library; this is working notes, not archive. Post-it cross-references, annotation flags, overlapping timelines. At the center of the east wall: a large panel titled *"Compact Intent: 12yr Summary."* Vorn is standing at the north end. She says: *"You found it faster than I expected."*

**Features:**

- **Vorn's twelve-year summary panel.** Investigation DC 10 (or passive 10): the summary reads, in the largest text: *"The compact was a protection mechanism, not a governance framework. The seven clauses are not governance — they are the minimum acknowledgment that the Orders must provide to the people who live under the consequences of what the Orders do."* **This is Hint 4.** Auto-delivered to Thessaly (Arcana 18) and Calder (Insight passive 14 — he recognizes this as exactly what the Dalimvar protocol violated).

- **The researchers.** Six of them. They are a mix of scholar and security. Perception DC 12: three of the six have something concealed under their robes (wands, a shortsword, one alchemical flask). They are Vorn's protective detail. They are not aggressive unless Route C triggers.

- **Vorn's opening.** She says: *"You found it faster than I expected."* She means the compact, the shards, the White Quill. She is impressed and slightly unsettled. She expected more time.

**Encounter:** None at baseline. **Route C trigger:** If the party attempts to take anything by force, search for the shard without Vorn's permission, or threatens a researcher — combat. See `encounters/research-center-defense.md`.

**GM Notes:**
- **Lenne reads the twelve-year summary.** She has been doing the same work, less rigorously, for eight years. She will say, quietly: *"You've been doing this alone for twelve years."* Vorn: *"Yes."* Beat. *"But you have the same conclusion in less time."*
- **Hint 4 is delivered here** — the summary panel is visible from the doorway. The party does not need to ask for it; it is the most prominent thing in the room.

**Connections:** Scene 01 (Reception); Scene 03 (Archive, locked); Scene 04 (Vorn's Study, stair up).

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Vorn put the summary panel up three years ago, after seven years of research, when she finally had enough confidence in the conclusion. She has been adding to it since. The current version is the fourth draft. She has found that explaining the argument to herself on the wall helps her see the gaps. This is a technique she developed alone.*
