# Scene 04 — Vorn's Study

**Read-aloud:**

> Vorn's study is the most personal room in the building. A desk covered in active research. Three notebooks open simultaneously, cross-referenced. A locked glass case on the south wall holds the Clause of Reckoning — visible through the glass, obsidian, noticeably heavy even in its cloth wrapping. She sits in the desk chair and looks at the party. She does not open the case yet.

**Features:**

- **The locked case.** The shard is visible through the glass. Vorn has the key. She will open it when the arc-completion condition is met or when Route A is agreed.
- **The three open notebooks.** Lenne will read them. They are the most recent three years of Vorn's research. The most recent entry, dated last month: *"If the compact speaks the right words, it doesn't need enforcement mechanisms. The accountability is in the act of speaking — each Order acknowledges, in public, that they answer to the people. The reckoning is performed in the speaking."*
- **The arc-completion space.** This is where Thessaly will name the Test wound — if she does. Vorn is waiting for it. She will not ask. She will observe whether Thessaly is capable of performing the reckoning she is asking the Orders to perform.

**Arc-completion available here (Route D):** Thessaly names the Test wound. Vorn gives the shard. She names the Crystalmir family her researcher frightened. *"This clause is for both of us."*

**P0 fallback — Route A:** If Thessaly does not name the wound, Vorn will offer Route A after 20 minutes of conversation: *"Your research is further ahead than mine on the shards. I want that documented in the new compact's record. Give me that, and I'll give you the shard."* This is a real exchange. The party can agree.

**P0 failure state — Route C:** If the party tries to take the shard by force (breaking the case, threatening Vorn): she casts Hold Person on the nearest aggressor. Her staff mobilizes from the Research Room (2 rounds to arrive). See `encounters/research-center-defense.md`.

**Connections:** Scene 02 (Research Room, stair down); Scene 05 (Roof Garden, stair up).

**DM-letter:** See `dm-letter-scene-04.md`. Thessaly naming the Test wound is the hold-space moment. Her sister doesn't know. Vorn is the first person who will know. Hold space for Thessaly to say the thing she has carried since her Test.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Vorn bought the Clause of Reckoning from an antiquities dealer in Palanthas twelve years ago. She paid more than she should have. She did not negotiate; the weight of the shard in her hand was enough to tell her this was what she had been looking for. She has been working ever since. She has not taken a proper holiday in eight years. She does not think of this as a sacrifice. She thinks of it as the minimum.*
