# Scene 03 — Private Archive (Optional)

**Read-aloud:**

> The private archive is more tightly organized than the public room — the same chronological structure but with primary-source documents rather than working notes. A cabinet along the west wall holds original compact-related materials that Vorn has acquired over twelve years: letters, official records, one personal diary (origin unknown). The cabinet is not locked. She trusts that people who got this far deserve to see what she has.

**Features:** Vorn's primary-source collection. If the party wants to verify her research conclusions, it is all here. Investigation DC 10: the collection is rigorous and honest — she has included materials that contradict her conclusions as well as those that support them. She has annotated the contradictions. She is a real scholar.

**GM Notes:** This scene is available if the party wants to verify Vorn's work before trusting her. If they do: Lenne's Arcana auto-confirms the compact-fragment cross-references are accurate. Calder will find the Dalimvar records again — the original Orders' voting minutes, PC 199, with Sylaren's dissent recorded in the margin. He will hold this for a moment. Then he will put it down.
