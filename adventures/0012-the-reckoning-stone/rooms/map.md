# Map — The Reckoning Stone

## Topology

A four-story converted merchant house in Palanthas. The party enters through the public-facing ground floor. The archive and Vorn's study are on floors 2-3. The roof garden is floor 4.

```
[01 RECEPTION (floor 1)] — public entry
        |
        v
[02 PUBLIC RESEARCH ROOM (floor 1)] — researchers visible
    /               \
   /                 \
[03 PRIVATE ARCHIVE]  [04 VORN'S STUDY (floor 3)] — Hint 4 + arc-completion
(floor 2; locked)          |
                           v
                  [05 ROOF GARDEN (floor 4)] — optional
```

**Loop:** Public Research Room → Archive (locked, key with Vorn) → Vorn's Study → back to Research Room via main stair. A service stair connects Archive directly to Reception.

**Vertical:** Four floors. Each floor is a distinct environment.

**Two paths to shard:** Public path (Reception → Research Room → Vorn grants access → Archive) or Vorn's invitation (she escorts directly to Archive if trust established).

**Shortcut:** If Vorn trusts the party after the Research Room conversation, she escorts them to her Study (floor 3) where the shard is kept — skipping the Archive entirely.
