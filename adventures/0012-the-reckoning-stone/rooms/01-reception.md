# Scene 01 — Reception

**Read-aloud:**

> A converted merchant house with no external sign. The inside is orderly — a reception desk, a waiting area, shelves of reference volumes. A staff member at the desk looks up and says: *"Vorn Haleth is expecting you. Please follow me."*

**Features:** Vorn has been watching the road. She told her staff to receive the compact-wardens when they arrived. The reception is not hostile; it is prepared. The staff member leads them to the Public Research Room.

**GM Notes:** Orik reads the building (signature move): service stair behind the desk; upper floors accessible; windows on north and south; the roof garden accessible from a separate stair (he sees the roofline). He notes the staff member is not armed but is not a civilian scholar — the way they move suggests martial training. He says nothing.

**Connections:** Scene 02 (Public Research Room).
