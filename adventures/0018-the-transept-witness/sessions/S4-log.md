## Scene 4.4 — Morren's Space (R3): The Full Account

### Setup
Morren is in his chair. Blue-green light on his hands. The floor near him is slightly shinier from thirty years of pacing. The practice stone is beside his chair.

### Ritual Focus Identification

The party shows Morren the ritual focus.

**🎲 Renn Arcana (Aldric's notation mark DC 10)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[15] mod=+3 **total=18** · Makes it clearly. Renn had suspected this was a formal marking system. Confirmed: Orders-style field station notation.

Morren looks at it. He says: *"That is Aldric's mark. He was stationed at Mireth thirty years ago. He is still in this building."*

*Seed planted: aldric-identified-by-ritual-focus.* The party now knows Aldric Voss is in the Sacristy.

*The focus hums once — faint, brief — when Renn holds it in R3.* The transept is the closest point to where it was used. Renn is the party member whose working most closely resembles what it was used for. The hum is recognition; no further comment from either party. Relic sign: reaction moment.

Maret positions at the R3 entrance (Doubt-die = 5 — she maintains tactical awareness while the party listens). She will hear the account.

### The Account

Morren sets the focus down and begins.

**Stage 1:** She came because we were sick. The same fever as the town. She brought her bag.

**Stage 2:** She said she could not promise it would work. She said she needed our permission to try it — she asked. I had never been asked before.

**Stage 3 (Hint 2 auto-delivery):** *"She held the stone. It changed temperature when she used it. Not cold. Not warm. It went through both, quickly, the way water changes when you add something to it. I had never felt anything like prayer do that. I had felt prayer. This was not that. This was different. It worked."*

*Hint 2 delivered. All four party members have passive Perception ≥ 11. They receive this. The stone was not prayer. She used High Sorcery. The distinction the congregation has known for thirty years is now the party's fact.*

**Cold Pulse 10min-3** — `1d6` → rolls=[4] **total=4** · A bird from the crossing tower passes through R5, startles at the party, returns upward. The account continues.

**Stage 4:** She promised some of us she would come back.

**Wandering Encounter 30min** — `1d6` → rolls=[3] **total=3** · A Bell Tower congregation member appears at R1's entrance. They see the party with Morren. They withdraw. *Sharr will be told.* Morren pauses for one breath, glances at the entrance, and continues.

**Stage 5:** She showed me the stone before she began. She showed me the mark on the base. She said: 'If anyone asks what this is, show them this mark. It belongs to someone who knows what it is.'

**Stage 6:** She used the working. It worked. She sat down where she had been standing. She said: 'I need to rest.' She did not get up.

**Stage 7:** *"The last thing she said was: she had a paper, she said. A forged paper. Someone had written it for her. She had used it to get the stone. She tried to burn it before she began. She said she didn't know if it had burned. She said: 'If you find a man who marks his tools the way that stone is marked — tell him it worked. Tell him it was worth it. Tell him I'm sorry I can't come back.'"*

*"She didn't come back."*

Silence.

**🎲 Calla Insight (receiving the arc-completion DC 13)** — `1d20+5` → rolls=[12] mod=+5 **total=17** · Makes it. She reads the moment: something is coming that the congregation doesn't have. She is still.

**Morren's arc-completion fires:** *"She did not call it a miracle. She called it the right thing to do."*

He looks at his hands. He looks at Calla. He says: *"I hope that's useful to you."*

Then: *"There is a man in the sacristy. He has been copying my account for twenty years. He was the one who gave her the working. He regrets it differently than I do."*

*Seed planted: dispensation-letter-exists.* She burned the paper — or tried to. Aldric wrote it. He has been in the building for thirty years.

*Hint 3 planted:* Aldric gave her the working. The party will find him in the Sacristy (0019).

*This is Stage 3's anteroom. She has the source material. She knows what it costs to carry it. She does not speak.*
