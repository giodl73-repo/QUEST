# Wandering Pressure — The Transept Witness

*Roll Cold Pulse (d6) every 10 minutes. Roll Wandering Encounter (d6) every 30 minutes.*

*The Transept is a quiet space. Temporal pressure here is contemplative rather than structural.*

---

## Cold Pulse Table (d6, every 10 min)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | The coloured light shifts as a cloud passes outside. For a moment the blue-green deepens, then returns to its usual cast. |
| 2 | From the Arcade Passage (R4): the congregation member rehearsing the account gets a word wrong. A pause. They try it again. |
| 3 | Morren turns the practice stone over in his hands — checking his shorthand, making sure the sequence is right before he begins. |
| 4 | A bird from the crossing tower above passes through R5, startles at the party, and returns upward. |
| 5 | A congregation member from the nave enters R2 briefly, sees the party with Morren, and leaves. Word will reach the nave congregation: the party is with the elder. |
| 6 | The smell from the Sacristy passage (R5 east, blocked) intensifies slightly: old ink and cold stone. |

---

## Wandering Encounter Table (d6, every 30 min)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | Morren pauses mid-account and says: *"You're the first people who have asked me to tell all seven stages in order."* He continues. |
| 2 | One of the congregation observers in R2 quietly leaves and returns with a cup of water for the party. Wordless. The same gesture Veth's congregation made in the nave. |
| 3 | A congregation member from the Bell Tower (one of Sharr's group) appears at R1's entrance, looks at the party, sees they are with Morren, and withdraws. They will tell Sharr. Sharr's response: he says nothing but moves to R6 (gallery of 0016) and looks toward the transept. |
| 4 | The ritual focus hums once — faint, brief — if the party is holding it. No cause visible. The transept is simply close enough to where it was used. |
| 5 | Doran's second message arrives via a nave congregation relay: a note at the R1 entrance. *"Report required. Two days."* The deadline has shortened by one day. |
| 6 | Morren finishes his account (if not already done). If done: he says nothing further. He waits to see what the party does with it. |
