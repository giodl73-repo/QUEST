# Room 04 — Arcade Passage

## Read-aloud

> A vaulted passage connecting the two transept arms. Quieter here — the congregation's voices from the main space are muffled. One gully dwarf is walking slowly back and forth, speaking under their breath. Rehearsing. Getting some of it wrong.

*(42 words)*

## Features

- **Imperfect account rehearsal** (one congregation member, non-combatant). They are practicing Morren's account. Getting Stage 4 wrong — they have the words but not the sequence. **DC 12 Insight** to notice they are trying to memorize rather than understand. *This is the third location where Calla has seen imperfect oral transmission (after R2/R5 of 0016 and R4/R3 of 0016). Each location, the gap has gotten smaller. Here, the gap is almost close enough to name.*

- **Vaulted ceiling** (original construction). The arcade was partly completed before the workers fled. It has original stonework — more finished than most of the building. **DC 12 History** to recognize: this section was expensive to build. The transept's arcade was a priority in the original plan.

- **Loop connection** (both ends). R2 on the north, R5 on the south. The passage creates the room loop.

## Encounter

None. The congregation member does not engage unless addressed.

**Documentation-suspension fifth activation (Calla Fosse):** If Calla passes through this room and hears the rehearsal, she has now witnessed five forms of the same practice across 4 sessions. The gap she has been identifying (what is the tradition she keeps encountering) now has a specific shape: it is imperfect oral transmission of a single witnessed event, thirty years ago, by people who were not there. She has been performing the same thing for Thornfield.

## Connections

- **North (arch):** R2 (North Arm)
- **South (arch):** R5 (Crossing)

## GM Notes

The arcade is the session's Jaquays room — it creates the loop and rewards parties who explore before going directly to Morren. The congregation member rehearsing here is the session's best atmospheric beat for Calla outside R3.

### Memory Fragment
*She kept rehearsing the wrong word. The right word was "working." She kept saying "working" but meaning "miracle." The difference matters and she knows it matters and she keeps getting it wrong. Morren has corrected her three times. She will try again.*
