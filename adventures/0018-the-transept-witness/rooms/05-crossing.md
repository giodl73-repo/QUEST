# Room 05 — The Crossing

## Read-aloud

> The intersection of nave and transept — the building's center. Above: the unfinished crossing tower, open to sky. The space is larger than the other rooms. Two arches face east; one leads toward the sacristy passage, stone-blocked but present. The light here is mixed: the nave's ordinary stone-grey and the transept's blue-green, meeting at the crossing without blending.

*(60 words)*

## Features

- **Mixed light** (R5 is at the intersection). The nave's natural light and the transept's blue-green meet here. They don't blend — the boundary is visible on the floor, a line where one light ends and the other begins. *Relic sign: wrong-light sign, second location.*

- **The Sacristy passage** (east, blocked). Stone-blocked, not congregation-blocked. The original construction left this passage unfinished. It is physically present; a medium creature can clear the blocking in 10 minutes of work (**DC 11 Athletics** or simple effort). This leads toward 0019 (Sacristy). It is accessible from Route D (Nave congregation's trust) or by physical clearance. Sensory cue at entrance: the smell of old ink and cold stone coming through the gaps.

- **Open crossing tower** (above). Forty feet up. Birds nest in the gaps where the tower was never finished. Through the tower shaft: sky and scaffolding visible.

- **Loop completion** (R3→R5 and R4→R5). Both arms of the loop end here.

## Encounter

None. The crossing is a transitional and strategic space.

**Renn and the Sacristy smell:** The old ink and cold stone from the Sacristy passage is detectable here at **DC 11 Perception** (Renn: passive Perception 10 — misses; active search: DC 11, he will investigate if he notices). If he smells it: *"Someone has been writing in there for a long time."* He does not know yet that it is Aldric. He will know after Morren's account.

## Connections

- **North (arch):** R3 (Morren's Space)
- **West (arch):** R4 (Arcade Passage)
- **East (blocked passage):** 0019 Sacristy — 10 min clearance; DC 11 Athletics

## GM Notes

R5 is the strategic room — the party sees the Sacristy path for the first time. The smell tells them something is there. Morren's account will tell them what.
