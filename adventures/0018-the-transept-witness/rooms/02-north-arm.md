# Room 02 — North Transept Arm

## Read-aloud

> Blue-green windows on the east wall — the congregation set coloured glass in the frames. The light through them is the colour of growing things, not of white marble or divine grace. Two congregation members sit near the south arch, watching quietly. Through the south arch: an older gully dwarf in a chair. He has seen you come in. He says: "Sit down."

## Features

- **Coloured glass windows** (east wall, four frames). Blue-green, the colour of healing herbs. **DC 13 Religion** to identify: Mishakal's colour is white or silver — these are liturgically incorrect. **DC 10 History** to note: the glass quality is inconsistent with new construction; these were salvaged from somewhere else. *Relic sign: wrong-light sign — strongest here.*

- **Two congregation observers** (near south arch). They have heard Morren's account many times. They are watching the party's faces — not Morren's words. **DC 12 Insight** to read them: they have heard this story fail to land before. They are watching to see if this is different.

- **Northwest corner rope barrier** (near R6). A single rope across the entrance to the northwest corner. No sign. **DC 12 Perception** to notice the unstable floor visible through the barrier. *Mechanical hazard entrance: flagged by Morren; congregation knows to stay out.*

- **Morren visible through south arch** (R3, in his chair). He can be seen from here. His left wrist has no cord (he is not the one who carries relics; he is the one who carries the account). He is waiting.

## Encounter

The party passes through toward Morren. The congregation observers watch. They do not obstruct.

**Unstable floor (R6):** If a party member moves toward the northwest corner — curiosity, tactical assessment, or Perception fumble — the rope barrier is visible at DC 12. If missed or ignored, see R6.

## GM Notes

R2 is the session's staging area. The wrong-light relic sign lands here. The congregation observers watching the party's faces is the session's craft-witness equivalent for the Roleplayer lens — the congregation is watching the party the way Calla watches things. What they're watching for: are these people capable of receiving the account without making it about themselves?

**Dren's note:** He will secure the room (Heuristic 1). He will note the rope barrier and investigate R6's instability if prompted. Let him; the hazard exists.
