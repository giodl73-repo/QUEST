# Room 01 — Transept Entry

## Read-aloud

> The passage from the nave opens into a wider space. The light here is different — blue-green, coming from windows on the far wall. Not wrong, exactly. Almost right. Through the arch ahead, a wider room; through that, a figure seated near the windows. The congregation members here don't block the way. They watch you come in.

*(56 words)*

## Features

- **Wrong light** (from R2 windows, visible from R1). The coloured glass in R2's windows casts blue-green light into the entry passage. The wrong-light relic sign begins here — visible but not yet fully present. **DC 10 Perception** to notice the light's colour is unusual for a Mishakal cathedral.
- **Congregation observer** (one, near the west wall). They are watching the party without hostility. If Renn uses Awakened Mind: they respond with a single image — an old gully dwarf sitting in a chair, waiting.
- **Side passage** (south wall, slightly obscured). DC 11 Perception to notice. Connects to R4 (Arcade Passage). The loop route.

## Encounter

None. Passage room; congregation members present but non-interactive unless addressed.

## Connections

- **West:** 0016 nave east arch
- **East (arch):** R2 (North Transept Arm)
- **South (side passage):** R4 — DC 11 Perception to notice

## GM Notes

R1's job: deliver the wrong-light signal and let the party see Morren at distance before they reach him. The blue-green light is the session's first relic sign. Morren is visible — a seated figure near the far windows.

Just stone openings and the sky beyond. The congregation put the glass in later. They chose the colours.*
