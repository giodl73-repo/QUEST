# Room 06 — Unstable Floor

## Read-aloud

> The northwest corner. A rope strung across the entrance — a single loop tied to two wall-rings, low enough to step over but clearly present. Beyond it: the floor looks normal. It is not. A 10×10 section of flagstone over an unfilled construction drainage trench. If you step on it, it will give.

*(53 words)*

## Features

- **Rope barrier** (entrance). Congregation-installed, not construction-era. **DC 12 Perception** to notice from R2. Once seen: clearly deliberate. Morren, if asked, says: *"Stay out of that corner. The floor goes."*

- **Unstable flagstones** (10×10 ft section). If a party member steps on the unstable section:
  - **DC 13 Dex save** or the floor crumbles.
  - On failure: 5 ft drop, **1d6 bludgeoning damage**.
  - On success: they catch themselves on the edge.
  - **Climbing out:** DC 8 Athletics (easy; 5 ft height).
  - **Party awareness:** if one PC falls through, the others know immediately (sound of cracking stone, a cry). The encounter is over in one round.

- **Below the unstable section** (after falling). A dry construction cavity approximately 5 ft deep. Construction debris. One notable item: a trowel with a mason's mark different from the usual construction tools — this trowel came from the gully dwarf settlement. One of the congregation brought it at some point and it fell through when the floor began to fail. *Incidental item: evidence that the congregation has been doing maintenance work on the building.*

## Encounter

**Mechanical hazard.** Not a combat encounter but a resource test:
- Calla, already at 19/22 HP, falling through would reduce her to 13/22 HP (if she fails the Dex save and takes 1d6). Meaningful for a secondary character at partial HP.
- The hazard fires IF the party explores this corner — Dren (Heuristic 1: secure position) will check the perimeter. He will see the rope barrier if his Perception is ≥ 12 (passive 13 — he auto-detects it). He will not step over the barrier.
- If Calla steps over out of curiosity (Heuristic 1: pursue information) — her Dex save is +2 (Dex 14 = +2 modifier, no proficiency on Dex saves). DC 13 vs +2 = meaningful chance of failure.

## Connections

- **South:** R2 (North Arm) — rope barrier at entrance

## GM Notes

R6 exists to satisfy the panel mandate (mechanical hazard in 0018) and to give Dren something to do during a primarily social adventure. He will detect the barrier. He will report it to the party. He is the person who notices things like this.

The trowel is not a plot item — it is character texture. The congregation has been maintaining this building as best they can. The trowel got lost in the floor cavity thirty years ago. It is still there.

### Memory Fragment
*The floor has been giving since the second year. The drainage trench under it was never filled — the workers left before they could. The congregation put the rope up after the third person stepped through and had to be helped out. Morren thinks of it as the building telling them something about how things left unfinished behave.*
