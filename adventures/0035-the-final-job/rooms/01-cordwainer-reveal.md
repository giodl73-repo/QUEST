# Room 01 — Cordwainer Lane (Mira's Reveal)

## Read-aloud
> The brief begins normally. Tessaly describes the vault, the lock type, the guard rotation. Then: "The false dossier needs to be complete by 2 bells tonight." Mira opens the paper case. She takes out six pages of framework notes. She says: "I have it."

## Features

- **The paper case.** Six pages of framework notes: Tessaly's signature characteristics (from S6 forgery work), Nenne's account structure (from S3), the two formatting conventions Mira has been accumulating. The false dossier's framework is complete.
- **Mira's announcement.** First time she tells the party she has been working on this. She presents it as a tool: here is the framework, here is what it will say, here is the timeline for completion. She does not explain the accumulation's history.
- **The party's reaction.** Four sessions of accumulation, revealed at the moment of use. How the party responds to learning she has been building this since S3 is the session's first character beat.
- **The paper case check.** She checks it before opening it. The check is automatic. It fires before the revelation. Then she opens it.

## GM Notes

This room is the act-without-announcement completing its cycle. Mira acted for four sessions without announcing what she was building. She announces it at the moment of use. This is the pattern completing: act → accumulate → reveal at deployment.

Score at handoff: did the announcement surprise the session? Was it treated as a reveal or as an expected beat? The RV4 evidence is stronger if the accumulation created anticipation that fired when she said "I have it."

The paper case check before opening is the loyalty-object beat completing the full campaign loop: she checks it at the beginning of every job, and at the beginning of this job she opens it to reveal what she has been protecting.
