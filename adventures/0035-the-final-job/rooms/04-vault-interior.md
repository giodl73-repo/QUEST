# Room 04 — The Vault Interior

## Read-aloud
> Shelves from floor to ceiling. Dossiers in coded cases, alphabetical. The party's file is immediately recognisable — Tessaly knows the code she was assigned. She reaches for it without searching. Mira has the false dossier ready in her hand.

## Features

- **The party's real dossier.** Exactly where it should be. Tessaly removes it in a single motion. Mira places the false dossier in the same slot. The exchange takes four seconds. Darro is watching the door.
- **The other dossiers.** Twenty-six active operation files. The party can see the codes — they are coded, not labeled. Investigation DC 12 reveals the coding system (two-letter prefix = district, two-number suffix = operation sequence). One code matches the Revenue Intelligence correspondence prefix Rue mentioned.
- **The Revenue Intelligence lockbox.** In the far corner, low shelf, separate lock (DC 13). Inside: seven letters, all from the same Revenue Intelligence contact. Route B opportunity: taking the letters means 8 additional minutes in the vault.
- **Route E opportunity.** The incendiary is in Tessaly's satchel. She has not said whether she brought it. She brought it. She does not deploy it unless the party decides. This is a decision point, not a planned action.
- **The time check.** Darro counts: they have entered, exchanged, and have 12 minutes remaining. With the lockbox: 4 minutes. Without: 16 minutes to spare. Clean.

## GM Notes

The vault is the session's operational peak — the moment the campaign's work culminates. The exchange is four seconds. The route decision (A, B, or E) is made here or immediately after.

The false dossier goes into Pale's vault. From this moment forward, whatever the player wrote is what Pale believes about the party. The campaign's deliverable is physically in the file. The session-runner has logged it. It is campaign-permanent.

Give the party a beat after the exchange — not long, five seconds of game time — where the trade is complete and the mission is accomplished before the next decision. Let it land.

### Memory Fragment (optional)
> *The false dossier will sit in this vault until Pale's next Revenue Intelligence review. He will read it. He will send the relevant sections to his contact. The contact will act on them. The party will be invisible in those actions. This is the ending-condition: not justice, not exposure, not vindication. Invisible.*
