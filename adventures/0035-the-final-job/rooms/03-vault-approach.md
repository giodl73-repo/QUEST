# Room 03 — Vault Approach (Alley to Corridor)

## Read-aloud
> After midnight. The alley door is a standard commercial lock — newer than the building, replaced within the year. Darro has the tension wrench seated before anyone says anything. The click is quiet. Below: stone stairs going down into the dark.

## Features

- **The alley door.** DC 12 Thieves' Tools (Darro DC 8; with Expertise, he auto-succeeds against DC 8 in favorable conditions). Silent.
- **The service stair.** Fifteen feet of stone stair. No lights. Darro leads; Cael last; Mira second carrying the false dossier in the paper case.
- **The vault corridor.** The guard has just passed (Darro timed the circuit from outside). Twenty minutes. The vault door is directly ahead.
- **Cael's position.** He is at the stair head — between the party and the exit, as always. Left hand to chest, withdrawn. His contract says: clean exit is as important as clean entry. He is at the clean-exit position.

## GM Notes

The approach is clean. This is the professional machine at peak operation: four people who each know their role, executing without discussion. Darro leads because the physical path is his domain. Cael anchors because exits are his domain. Tessaly and Mira have the objective (the ledger and the false dossier).

The campaign's accumulated craft-witness and loyalty-object beats fire here automatically: Darro's picks are ready (he checked them before leaving Cordwainer Lane); Tessaly's satchel is over one shoulder (the real ledger inside, the exchange target); Mira's paper case is inside her jacket; Cael's contract is in his breast pocket. All four objects are present for the final job.
