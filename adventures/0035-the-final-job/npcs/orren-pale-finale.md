# Orren Pale — Arc-Completion (S7)

## Arc-Completion

**Adventure:** 0035 (this session)
**Condition:** The false dossier is in the vault when Pale reviews it.
**The act:** He reads the false dossier. He does not detect the substitution. He sends the relevant sections to his Revenue Intelligence contact with his standard endorsement: *"Operational team profile attached per your request. Subject party: compliant, no independent intelligence-gathering activity, recommend continued operational use."*
**What it means:** The false dossier is campaign-permanent. Whatever the player wrote is what Pale reported. Revenue Intelligence has wrong information. The party is invisible.

## Pale's arc in retrospect

Pale's arc is the inverse of a trust-marker arc. Each session, the party gathered evidence of his betrayal (Aldras's account, the burned house, the test-assignments, the audit). Each session, his arc moved toward the reveal of his disposal plan. In S7, instead of the party confronting him with what they know, they exit the frame — they make his plan irrelevant by making themselves invisible to it.

He does not know what happened. He will not know until the Revenue Intelligence investigation concludes and the party is not among its outcomes. By then, the party is gone.

This is the campaign's "exits the frame" resolution — not justice, not exposure, not vindication. Professional exit.
