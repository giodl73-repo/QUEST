# 0037 — The Cache

## The adventure in one sentence

The second patrol brings the unit back to the staging cache — which has been fully restocked since yesterday — and Medic Lenne's medical log starts showing "cause: unclear."

## Setting

Same route: Irenvale Crossing. Day 2 patrol. Orders are identical to Day 1. The staging cache from session 1 has been restocked overnight. Lenne notices. She writes it down.

## Hook

The cache was half-full yesterday. Today it is full. The unit did not resupply it. Someone else did, overnight, without a record.

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d12 (no degradation from S1)

## Scenes

1. **Briefing** — Veth issues the same orders. Nothing changes. The key is mentioned as "standard issue."
2. **Second pass: Ridgehead Marker** — Taris: fresh mule tracks, both directions, overnight.
3. **The Cache, morning** — The party detours to check it before patrol (if they choose). It is full. Lenne counts supplies. She opens her medical log.
4. **Mid-route: injured local** — A mountain farmer, Serrak, with a crossbow wound. Recent. He was not involved in yesterday's skirmish — the wrong direction.
5. **Evening cache check** — The unit returns to cache. Serrak's wound matches a wound type in yesterday's smugglers' confiscation fight — but nobody from that fight came this way.

## Hint placement

- **H1 confirmation (if missed in S1):** The cache restocking shows the system is active independent of the patrol.
- **Lenne's medical log:** First "cause: unclear" entry. She cannot explain Serrak's wound within the briefing she was given.

## Spotlight

Lenne Orr. She heals Serrak. She asks one question before she does: "How did this happen?" His answer doesn't fit. She heals him anyway. She writes it down.

## NPC

- **Serrak** (farmer, workshop-canon) — injured, scared, knows more than he says. Does not trust soldiers. Will answer if Lenne is alone with him.
- **Arren Vole** — second observation: "Cache was emptier yesterday."

## Research notes

RV6 primary test: does Lenne's medical log accumulate atmospheric content (the "cause: unclear" entries) without being designed to? Her heuristic says she writes clinical entries. The discrepancy is already in her sheet. Does she write it?
