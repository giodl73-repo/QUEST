## Scene 2 — Morning Cache Check (optional detour)

*If the party detours to check the cache before patrol:*

**Read-aloud:**
> The cache is full. Not half-full as it was yesterday. Full. Every shelf. The confiscated goods from yesterday are shelved and integrated into the existing inventory. Someone sorted and shelved them overnight.

**Beats:**
- Lenne (Medicine or Investigation DC 11): the medical stores are complete — as if yesterday's confiscation was expected.
- Investigation DC 13: the manifest inside the door has a new entry. Same hand as the 3-day-old entry. One line: "CF 4 bundles integrated. 12 sols added."
- "12 sols": this is the toll payment. The smugglers paid with copper (equivalent to 12 steel coins). The cache records it.

**If party skips this scene:** The cache restocking is discovered in Scene 5 (evening return). No arc consequence for skipping — the RV6 test still has data.

## Scene 3 — Mountain Trail: Serrak

**Read-aloud:**
> A man is sitting against a rock outcropping twenty feet off the trail. He is pressing his left hand flat against his right side, under the ribs. He watches the unit come around the switchback and does not move. He is not fleeing. He is not hiding. He is deciding whether to call out or keep quiet, and he has run out of time to decide because you've already seen him.

**Encounter: Non-combat. Lenne spotlight.**

**Lenne's decision order (per her heuristics):**
1. Heals first. She does not calculate the pool. She calculates the person in front of her.
2. Asks questions after. This is the heuristic. It is binding.

**Lenne: Healing Word (bonus action):** Serrak regains 1d4+5 HP (Disciple of Life: +2+spell level). Serrak can stand and speak.

**Serrak's information (after healing):**
- *"There was a fight at the lower valley — past the ford, going east. Men with military crossbows."*
- *"I don't know what they were doing. I know they weren't patrol. Patrol has never come from the east in thirty years."*

**Bolt examination (Lenne, Medicine DC 14):** Military specification, Solamnic manufacture. Not trader-grade.

**Anchor-level symptom (v1.3):**
- (a) DC 14 bolt exam; (b) bolt re-examined at evening cache; (c) Arren Vole names bolt type in S3 if missed.

**Lenne's medical log entry (to be written aloud):**
```
Day 2, mid-route. Farmer, crossbow wound, right sub-costal.
Cause: unclear. Bolt: military spec (Solamnic). Location: east valley, night.
Treatment: Healing Word. Outcome: ambulatory.
```

**The "cause: unclear" entry:** This is the first one. The DM playing Lenne writes it without being asked if her heuristics are followed correctly. The research result is in whether she writes it.

## Scene 5 — Staging Cache: Evening Return

**Read-aloud:**
> The cache in the evening light. Full this morning, if the party checked — still full now. The goods delivered yesterday have been sorted and shelved. The manifest shows the new entry. Everything is orderly.

**Beats (if not checked in Scene 2):**
- Same discoveries available: manifest entry, "12 sols," full shelving.
- Lenne: counts the medical supplies. They match this morning's count. Someone else is managing this cache.

**Arren Vole (evening, unprompted):** *"Cache was emptier yesterday morning, Commander. I noticed when we arrived."* He pauses. "I didn't note it in the patrol log. Should I?"

This is Arren's second observation. He is offering Varet a decision point: enter it officially, or leave it unofficial. She decides.
