# Room 03 — Mountain Trail: Serrak

## Read-aloud

> A man is sitting against a rock outcropping twenty feet off the trail. He is pressing his left hand flat against his right side, under the ribs. He watches the unit come around the switchback and does not move. He is not fleeing. He is not hiding. He is deciding whether to call out or keep quiet, and he has run out of time to decide because you've already seen him.

## DM notes

**Serrak** (Commoner stats, MM p.345): AC 10, HP 4 (wounded), Speed 30 ft.
- Crossbow bolt wound, right side, below ribs. Recent — 3-5 hours. He pulled the bolt himself. The wound is ragged.
- Lenne's Medicine DC 12: wound is survivable with treatment. Without treatment: Serrak fails a DC 13 Con save in 1d4 hours and begins taking 1d4 damage/hour.
- **If Lenne heals him first and asks later:** He tells her. "There was a fight at the lower valley — past the ford, going east. Men with military crossbows." East is away from the patrol route. East is where the fleeing smuggler went yesterday.
- **If Lenne asks first and heals second:** He says nothing until healed. He is in pain. He is a farmer. He learned to not answer soldiers until they've done something for him.
- **If asked about the men:** "They came down from the upper trail at night. I was moving sheep. They weren't patrol — patrol wears blue straps. These men wore nothing I recognized. They were arguing."

## Key detail

The crossbow bolt Serrak pulled out is in his pack. Lenne can examine it. Medicine DC 14: the bolt is military specification, Solamnic manufacture. Not the same as the bolts the smugglers used yesterday — those were trader-grade. This bolt came from a supply depot.

**Anchor-level symptom (v1.3 fallbacks):**
- (a) Medicine DC 14 bolt identification.
- (b) Bolt is described a second time if Lenne examines the wound during evening cache check.
- (c) Arren Vole can note the bolt type in session 3 if missed here.

## What this room establishes

Someone with Solamnic military supply access was east of the ford last night. The fleeing smuggler from yesterday's encounter went east. Lenne's medical log gets its first "cause: unclear" entry.

## Exits

Trail continues to ford and cache.
