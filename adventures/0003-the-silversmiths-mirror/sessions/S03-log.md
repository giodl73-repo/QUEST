# S03 LOG — The Silversmith's Mirror

## Session Summary

The Varduin Muster arrived in Crystalmir-by-the-Fells to protect Mira Vaenshold-Silversmith, Aelwen's great-great-granddaughter, from Kann of Neraka's Bone-Collector remnants. Thera's reflex eye caught Lorn's surveillance in the market without being spotted herself, which produced near-Full-Intel prep for the night assault. Mira opened about the Mirror across a fire in her hearth-room, and Kessa — reading Mira's sales ledger on Mira's own invitation — caught the cross-adventure phrase *"I will not look at the rose again"* that the party had missed on the S02 vault-wall ledger. Pevra was ambushed in the alleys and yielded; the night assault itself broke in two rounds when Thera one-shot Lorn at the window, Kessa's Magic Missiles burned Kann down from the street, and Grom's Guiding Bolt dropped Vend at the threshold. When Mira revealed the Mirror in the lower chamber at dawn, all four PCs' doubt-dice landed against looking: Kessa catalog'd, Thera found no profit, Grom named the object unmendable, Aelric held to the Measure. Consensus went to the Reorx-rite. Grom's rite failed its Religion check at the Crystalmir smith's forge — and Kessa burned her held Portent [14] to replace the roll. The obsidian shattered along Aelwen's unconverged polishing-lines; the silver un-forged; Mira watched, said *nyet*, then nodded. The Mirror is gone.

### Scene 1.0 — Crystalmir Arrival

**Setup (read-aloud):**

> The road lifts into Crystalmir at midday. Slate roofs. A market square with an old well at its center, stones worn smooth by three centuries of hands. Market stalls — wool, cheese, tin — under canvas sun-shades. The air smells of cooking-smoke and rain-wet stone. Above the street that descends toward the silversmith's shop, a carved stone arch: a half-bloomed rose, chisel-clean.

**Beats:**

Grom reaches the arch first and stops under it. He touches the stone. *"Thou'rt seen,"* he says in Dwarvish. Then he traces the half-bloomed rose with a thumb along the inside curve of the carving. Duren's habit. Inside-curve is the honest one.

Thera drifts into the market without any obvious objective. She buys a small wheel of cheese she does not need, hands a copper across, looks over the seller's shoulder while paying, and catalogs the square.

**🎲 Thera Perception (active; reflex)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[20] mod=+3 **total=23 CRIT** · Thera spots the man near the well — traveler's cloak, too-clean boots for a road-weary man, eyes tracking the silversmith's door in a specific rhythm. **Lorn.** She does not react. Kessa looks up at the arch and takes her silver rose-petal out of her pocket to compare.

**🎲 Kessa Arcana (arch-mark match)** — `1d20+5` → rolls=[15] mod=+5 **total=20** · Clean. **Resolution:** Party enters Crystalmir having confirmed (a) the smith-mark → Silver Rose linkage, (b) Bone-Collector surveillance presence (Lorn), (c) the town's awareness of their mission. ### Scene 2.0 — Mira's Shop, Upper Floor
