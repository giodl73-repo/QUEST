# Encounter — Scout Intel (Scene 3, conditional)

**Location:** Scene 3, Crystalmir alleys.
**Trigger:** varies by party's Scene 1 behavior. See Scene 3 file for full logic.

## Adversaries (full intel tier — up to 2 scouts)

### Lorn (CR ¼, 50 XP — Scout variant)
- **AC** 13 (leather armor)  ·  **HP** 16 (3d8+3)  ·  **Speed** 30 ft
- Str 11, Dex 14, Con 12, Int 11, Wis 13, Cha 11
- Skills: Stealth +6, Perception +5, Survival +5, Investigation +3
- **Senses:** passive Perception 15
- **Multiattack:** 2 shortsword attacks
- **Shortsword:** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing
- **Longbow:** +4 to hit, range 150/600, 1d8+2 piercing

### Pevra (CR ¼, 50 XP — Bandit variant, slightly modified)
- **AC** 12 (studded leather)  ·  **HP** 11 (2d8+2)  ·  **Speed** 30 ft
- Str 11, Dex 13, Con 12, Int 10, Wis 11, Cha 10
- **Scimitar:** +3 to hit, 1d6+1 slashing
- **Light crossbow:** +3 to hit, range 80/320, 1d8+1 piercing
- **Tactical note:** retreats to inn at HP ≤ 5 if she hears Idra's name.

## Encounter XP math

- Two scouts (Lorn + Pevra): 50 + 50 = 100 base × 1.5 (2 creatures) = **150 XP adjusted.**
- Easy for 4×L3 party.

## Tactics

### Full intel tier — party ambushes both scouts
- Thera's Stealth DC 14 to approach silently.
- If all 4 PCs approach silently (group Stealth DC 12): surprise; scouts caught flat-footed Round 1.
- Opening round: Thera's Sneak Attack at advantage; Aelric closes from opposite angle.
- Scouts respond Round 2 by drawing weapons. Pevra fires light crossbow; Lorn melee or retreat.
- **Goal: subdue at least one.** A captured scout (knock-out / non-lethal / surrender at low HP) delivers the intel listed in Scene 3.

### Partial intel tier — Pevra solo, Lorn already fled
- One scout only. Same stats. Solo combat (25 XP adjusted).
- Pevra will surrender at HP ≤ 5 if treated respectfully (no intimidation-escalation).

### No intel tier — no scouts in alley
- Walk-through only. Party learns Kann is at the inn from ambient observation (window-watching).

## Intel from a captured scout

**Intimidation DC 12 or Persuasion DC 14.** Pevra is more forthcoming than Lorn (Pevra is not a zealot; Lorn is a Nerakan hunter more committed). If the party mentions **Idra** to Pevra, she flinches and will give:

- Kann leads (5 total: Kann + Pevra + Lorn + Vend + Gorr)
- Inn-based
- Assault planned for "tonight, deep" (after midnight)
- Client in Istar (Pevra does not know the name)
- Kann carries a personal moon-silver piece — Pevra knows its significance but will not elaborate further unless Persuasion DC 16 (her loyalty to Kann breaks at 16; she confirms it is NOT Vaenshold-vein and is Stonfold-family; **this is the seed for #0006/#0007**)

## Loot

- Lorn: shortsword + longbow + 15 arrows + 5 gp; **the scout's map** in his pocket (sketches the shop with entry points marked — essential for Scene 5 prep).
- Pevra: scimitar + light crossbow + 15 bolts + 4 gp; a small leather bag with three worry-stones (Idra's; Pevra kept them after Idra died).

## GM Notes

- **The map is Scene 5's prep payoff.** Do not withhold it unreasonably; if the party killed Lorn, they should find it via cursory search.
- **Pevra is a grieving character, not a mook.** Her responses to the party should be tuned to her state. If the party is harsh, she clams up. If they are decent, she gives more.
- **If both scouts are captured:** the party has essentially won Scene 5 before it begins. That's a legitimate reward for Scene 1's good play.
