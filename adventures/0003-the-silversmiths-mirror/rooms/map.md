## Topology Summary

- **7 keyed scenes** across 4 real locations.
- **Loop:** [1] ↔ [2] via shop door; [2] ↔ [3] via back service door; [3] ↔ [1] via alleys. Circular.
- **Vertical:** stair [2]→[4] then [4]→cellar then cellar→[6]. Full vertical descent; three levels.
- **Branch cues:** [3] alleys smell of old mortar (distinguishing from the market square's cooking-smoke); the cellar trapdoor is under a rag-rug that Mira has not moved in a decade.
- **Stealth path to [6]:** only Mira can open the hidden door without Investigation DC 13.
- **Shortcut earned:** if the party spots the scouts in [3] before the night-assault, they can prepare [2] — the assault becomes less punishing.

## DM notes

- **Crystalmir is a lived-in town**, not a dungeon. NPCs in the market, the inn, the well-gatherers. Do not let combat break out in the square unless it is the night-assault.
- **Bone-Collector remnants (4-5)** are scattered across [3] (alleys), the local inn (posing as traders), and the woods west of town. Their leader **Kann** is at the inn.
- **Mira does not live alone.** Her apprentice **Harel** (15, orphaned, Mira took her in eight years ago) sleeps in the upper room above the shop. Harel is a seed — she knows more than Mira has told her, and she is the mirror's most plausible "most-lover" target for Mira specifically.
- **The lower chamber's hidden door** bears silver-filigree matching the mirror. A Kessa Arcana DC 10 identifies the filigree as Vaenshold work; Kessa will recognize the pattern.
- **Rubric v1.2 anchor-level symptom:** the mirror's presence causes the shop-signage (silver-filigree letters above the door) to dim by one perceptible degree when the lower chamber is breached. Passive-Perception DC 14 fallback; active Perception DC 12.
