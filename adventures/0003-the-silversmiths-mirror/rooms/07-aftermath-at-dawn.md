# Scene 07 — Aftermath at Dawn

## Read-aloud

> The assault-dead are laid in the alley. Dawn begins — grey over the fells, then orange. Mira has made tea. Harel is downstairs; she has not slept; she has been polishing the silver cups on the rack, because that is what her mother would have done if her mother had lived. Mira watches her and does not correct the clockwise-circle motion that is different from Mira's own.

## Features

- **The shop in dawn-light.** The front door still stands. Back service door splintered but repairable. The hearth-room fire is out. The kitchen is cold.
- **Mira has made tea.** She offers cups to the party. Small; Vaenshold-silver; the ones she would normally only sell.
- **The bodies in the alley.** Townsfolk are beginning to gather. The town-watch has been summoned by Mira's neighbors. One watchman is Harel's cousin's boy; he will not challenge the party.

## The scene

### Mira's decision

Mira tells the party, one sentence:

*"I will leave Crystalmir today for Stonfold. Harel will come with me. I will close the shop. I will write to Brother Laen by the first courier. Your part in this is done. I have said."*

She gestures. Harel bundles small possessions. Mira takes the clothbound mirror — **if the party has left it with her** — and places it in a wooden box, sealed. She does not look at it. She places the box on the counter.

### The party's choices — what to do with the mirror

Four sub-branches:

**A. Take the Mirror attuned (a PC has attuned in Scene 6).** The mirror travels with the party. Campaign-continuity: the attuner's self-image is permanently altered. Enter in `CLAUDE.md#campaign-continuity`.

**B. Take the Mirror unattuned (no PC attuned in Scene 6).** The mirror travels wrapped in cloth; party considers later. Feasible path if the party is cautious.

**C. Destroy the Mirror** via Grom's Reorx-rite. Grom needs a 1,800°C forge. Crystalmir has a smith's forge — Mira can vouch for its temperature (she has worked at it). **Religion DC 15** at dawn (Grom's +3 proficiency; Bless from the party = advantage or straight). On success: mirror destroyed. Obsidian back shatters; moon-silver un-forges; a small bright vapor escapes. Mira watches; says *nyet* once; then nods. She will leave for Stonfold an hour sooner. The destruction is **not shameful** — she has been asking for this for thirty years without knowing how to ask.

**D. Leave the Mirror with Mira.** She takes it to Stonfold. Tova Stonfold (her mother's cousin) agrees to receive it. The mirror is not destroyed but it is also out of the Bone-Collector client's reach. The Council contract is fulfilled. **But the mirror still exists for the finale — it must come back to the Vaenshold vein.** Consequence: the party will need to retrieve it at #0007.

## GM Notes

- **Let Mira's decision land.** She has made a choice — to leave — that has been thirty years in the making. Do not have the party talk her out of it. She is not flexible here.
- **Harel is a quiet character in this scene.** She has been through a night. She will speak if asked; she will not volunteer. She polishes the silver cups. That is her answer.
- **The Bone-Collector survivors (Kann, if escaped; Pevra if surrendered; Gorr if surrendered).** Their fate shapes future encounters. Kann-escaped = returns in #0006 or #0007. Kann-captured = bargains for Istaran client name. Pevra-surrendered = possible turn-coat; Grom may vouch for her.
- **Aelwen's journal page in the party's hands.** It is **Hint 4 pre-plant.** The ingot-reference IS the link to the finale. Kessa should record it in her notebook.
