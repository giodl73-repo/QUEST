## The real decision the adventure asks

Three overlapping:

1. **Does Mira live?** The Bone-Collector assault threatens her and Harel. Defense choices matter.
2. **Does a PC attune the mirror?** The Scene 6 revelation. Option C spawn required if attunement attempted. Permanent campaign-continuity consequence.
3. **Where does the mirror end up?** Taken (attuned or not), destroyed via Grom's rite, or left with Mira. Each routes the #0007 finale differently.
