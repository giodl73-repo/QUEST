## Innovation Flags

```
INNOVATION FLAG: Relic recognition through behavioral change, not status
Dimension: Atmospheric Landing, Module Fidelity
What happened: The dispensation letter's warmth failed for Maret in R4 (fumble, total 6)
and succeeded for Maret in R3 after she answered Aldric's question honestly. The relic
recognizes the act of having practiced without institutional sanction — not the status of
being such a person. Maret became recognizable by *doing* the thing (answering honestly
that she practices without permission) rather than by *being* the type of person who does it.
Why the rubric missed it: Relic recognition in previous sessions (field satchel warmth,
measuring cup calibration, ritual focus hum) was based on static properties (healer, practitioner,
unauthorized worker). This session showed a relic recognizing a dynamic change: the PC was
not-recognized, then performed an act, then was recognized. Recognition as consequence of act,
not identity.
```
