# S5 LOG — The Sacristy

## Session Summary

*Renn Ashkeld, whose power comes from a pact he made without the Orders' sanction — the same category of unauthorized working that Velantha used — entered Aldric Voss's room and smelled what he expected to smell: thirty years of someone making records no one authorized, in a space no one knew about, for the sake of something that needed to be remembered.* The party cleared the east passage from 0018 and found Aldric mid-sentence. He set his quill down. He had been composing his response for thirty years. Calla tried Thunderwave on the dispensation letter; it was unchanged; the ceramic holder shattered; Aldric made a note to make a new one. The party asked if he regretted giving Velantha the ritual. He said no. They asked what he did regret. Arc fired. He gave them the copy and pointed them toward the Crypt.

## Scene 5.1 — East Passage (R1) and Main Sacristy (R2)

### Setup
The east passage opened after 10 minutes of clearance.

> The cleared passage opens into a narrow corridor. The smell hits immediately: old ink and cold stone. Ahead, light from a small lamp. The sound of a quill stopping.

### Beats

**🎲 Renn Perception (ink confirmation DC 11)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[18] mod=+3 **total=21** · He confirms what he smelled in S4. He was right. He says nothing.

Aldric has stopped writing. He is waiting.

> Walls covered in handwriting — floor to ceiling, organized in columns, the same shorthand repeated in different inks at different ages. A gully dwarf at a writing table, quill down, watching you. In the far corner: a ceramic holder on a stone shelf. He says: "I thought you'd come eventually."

**🎲 Renn Arcana (Red Robe shorthand DC 12)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[13] mod=+3 **total=16** · He recognizes the notation system: Red Robe field station shorthand. The same system on the ritual focus's base mark. He holds this information.

**🎲 Calla Investigation (copies wall DC 12)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[7] mod=+3 **total=10** · MISSES. She sees the copies but can't extract their content — she cannot read Red Robe shorthand. She is standing in the written record she cannot make of Morren's account. She is aware of the irony.

**🎲 Calla Insight (Aldric tone DC 12)** — `1d20+5` → rolls=[15] mod=+5 **total=20 CRIT** · She reads him completely. He is not confessing. He is reporting. He has been composing this statement for thirty years. The composure is not evasion; it is the composure of someone who has thought through every word this conversation will need.

**Cold Pulse 10min-1** — `1d6` → rolls=[4] **total=4** · Aldric pauses mid-statement. He looks at the Crypt direction. He continues.

**🎲 Renn Insight (Aldric's private regret DC 13)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[11] mod=+3 **total=14** · Makes it. He reads that Aldric's regret is not about the act. It is something about the story afterward. He files this and waits for the right question.

### Resolution
Main sacristy engaged. Aldric ready to deliver his statement.

## Scene 5.2 — R4 (Letter's Corner): Indestructibility Test

### Beats

Before the statement: the party examines R4.

> A stone shelf with a ceramic holder on it. Scorch marks on the shelf and the wall behind it. A bowl with old ash at the shelf's edge. The letter is in the holder, slightly warm, unmarked.

**🎲 Dren Perception (scorch marks age DC 10)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[7] mod=+3 **total=10** · Exactly makes it. He estimates: thirty years of attempts. The most recent marks are approximately six months old. Someone tried again recently.

**🎲 Maret Perception (letter warmth DC 12)** — `1d20+5` → rolls=[1] mod=+5 **total=6 FUMBLE** · She does not detect the letter's warmth. She is standing in front of the ceramic holder and feeling nothing. The warmth is there — the letter is always warm. She simply is not, yet, in the state the letter recognizes. She has not yet answered the question she is about to be asked.

Calla examines the letter. She considers using Thunderwave to destroy it (Heuristic 2: preserve access to information — she wants to know if destroying the evidence is even possible). She casts Thunderwave at the letter's corner.

**🎲 Calla Thunderwave (L1 slot, letter vs indestructibility)** — 1 L1 slot spent. The sound wave expands. The ceramic holder shatters. The bowl of ash scatters. **The letter is unchanged.** Not a mark. Not a crease. Unchanged. Calla: L1 3/4 remaining.

Aldric, who has followed them to R4, watches this without expression. He says: *"I've tried everything."*

**🎲 Dren Arcana (understand indestructibility DC 10)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[18] mod=+3 **total=21** · He understands: the act the letter authorized was performed and cannot be undone. The authorization is now permanently part of what happened. It shares that permanence.

**🎲 Renn Arcana (letter indestructibility DC 10)** — `1d20+3` → rolls=[12] mod=+3 **total=15** · He understands the same thing but with a different resonance. His pact is also permanent. He is holding a piece of paper that knows what he knows.

*The letter is warm when Renn picks it up. He notices. He sets it down.*

*Relic sign: warmth sign — the letter recognized Renn.*

### Resolution
Letter indestructibility confirmed. L1 slot spent (Calla). Letter permanently with the party (or leaving with them). Aldric needs a new ceramic holder.

## Scene 5.3 — Main Sacristy: Aldric's Statement and the Arc

### Beats

Back to R2. Aldric delivers his statement.

**Hint 3 delivery:**

*"I gave her the ritual. I knew the prohibition. It was classified for humanoid use only — gully dwarves were specifically excluded in the revision after the Cataclysm. Someone decided they were not capable of consent. I did not agree with that classification. She did not ask me to agree; she asked me to give her the working. I gave it to her. I wrote the authorization letter. I knew it was forged under the Orders' rules. I gave it anyway because she was right about the gully dwarves and I was a coward about the Orders."*

Hint 3: the Orders' man consented and has been here ever since. Delivered.

**Cold Pulse 10min-2** — `1d6` → rolls=[3] **total=3** · The congregation member in R3 turns a page.

**Maret's spotlight:**

After the statement, Aldric looks at the party. His eyes go to Maret — she positioned herself carefully when entering, she scanned the room first, she has the posture of someone who performs a daily practice. He says: *"What do you practice, and who gave you permission?"*

**🎲 Maret doubt-die** — `1d6` → rolls=[1] **total=1** (1-3 = wait). She pauses. She looks at her hands. Aldric waits.

Then she answers honestly: Osel gave her the practice. Osel had no institutional affiliation. She has been practicing for six years without knowing what it is for.

**🎲 Maret Insight (Aldric recognizes parallel DC 12)** — `1d20+5` → rolls=[8] mod=+5 **total=13** · She sees that he already knew the answer before asking. He was not asking for information — he was asking for confirmation. He wanted to meet someone who had done the same thing he did: practiced without permission because the practice was right.

Aldric says: *"Then you know what it costs."*

*After saying it, Maret picks up the letter again. It is warm in her hands now. She answered the question. She has made an unauthorized act: she has been practicing something she does not have permission to practice, given by someone who did not have the authority to authorize it. The letter recognizes this. She holds it for three seconds and sets it back down.*

**Renn's disclosure (via Awakened Mind):**

**🎲 Renn doubt-die** — `1d6` → rolls=[3] **total=3** (1-3 = watches and telegraphs via Awakened Mind). He does not say the line aloud. He sends it to Aldric directly, in the space between his ears: *"She used something that wasn't prayer. I know what that costs."*

Aldric receives it. He looks at Renn. He says: *"Yes."* One word. Then he continues.

*No one else in the room heard this exchange. Calla, who has two CRIT Insights on NPCs, notices Aldric looking at Renn differently afterward — something has been acknowledged between them. She does not ask.*

<!-- SURPRISE --> *Renn's disclosure happens silently via Awakened Mind rather than as a statement to the party. The pact remains the party's unspoken subtext. Aldric now knows. The party does not. This is Renn's choice.* <!-- SURPRISE -->

**Arc-completion gate:**

Party asks: *"Do you regret giving her the ritual?"*

Aldric: *"No. She was right."*

Party follows up: *"Then what do you regret?"*

**Wandering Encounter 30min** — `1d6` → rolls=[4] **total=4** · Aldric says: *"When she came to me, she didn't ask whether the Orders were wrong. She asked whether the gully dwarves needed medicine. She already knew the answer to the second question."*

He is quiet for a moment. Then:

**Aldric's arc-completion fires:**

*"I regret that I let them call it a miracle. She would have hated that. She never asked for a cathedral. She asked for medicine."*

He looks at the current copy on the table — mid-sentence, stopped at the word "permission." He adds: *"I have been trying to write that into the record for thirty years. I cannot find the right place to put it. I hope you can."*

He selects a copy from the archive and holds it out. **🎲 Calla receives copy** — `1d20+5` → rolls=[7] mod=+5 **total=12** · She receives it. Not at Morren's intensity (23 in S4) but solid. She takes the copy. She now has both: the oral account (Morren, in her memory) and the written account (Aldric's copy, in her hands). The gap between them is where Velantha's own account lives.

*"She said she left something in the Crypt. I've never gone to look. I thought it wasn't my place. It is yours now."*

He picks up his quill. He begins writing again.

### Resolution
Hint 3 delivered. Seeds planted. Aldric's arc-completion fired. Copy given. Crypt direction given. Party proceeds to R6.
