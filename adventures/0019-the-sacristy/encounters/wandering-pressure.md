# Wandering Pressure — The Sacristy

*Roll Cold Pulse (d6) every 10 min. Roll Wandering Encounter (d6) every 30 min.*

*The Sacristy is the quietest space in the building. Pressure here is entirely atmospheric.*

## Cold Pulse (d6, every 10 min)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | Aldric's quill scratches — he has picked it back up and is writing even while the party is present. The sound fills the room. |
| 2 | From R4: the faint smell of old ash from the bowl beside the letter's holder. |
| 3 | The congregation member in R3 turns a page. |
| 4 | Aldric pauses mid-statement, looks at the Crypt direction, continues. |
| 5 | From the Crypt passage (R6): the sourceless light pulses once, very slightly. |
| 6 | Aldric says, without looking up from the copy: *"She would have told you herself, if she could."* He continues writing. |

## Wandering Encounter (d6, every 30 min)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | Aldric sets down the quill, goes to R4, checks the letter, returns. He does not explain this. It is a daily habit. |
| 2 | The congregation member in R3 brings Aldric a cup of water without being asked. They look at the party once and leave. |
| 3 | Doran's final message arrives — delivered to R2's entrance by a nave congregation member. *"Tomorrow. Final deadline. — D."* The message is urgent. The party has one day. |
| 4 | Aldric says: *"When she came to me, she didn't ask whether the Orders were wrong. She asked whether the gully dwarves needed medicine. She already knew the answer to the second question."* He continues. |
| 5 | Renn's copper coin is warm in his pocket. The sacristy's proximity to the letter — another permanent unauthorized act — has intensified it. He notices without rolling (passive Perception 10 exceeded). |
| 6 | Aldric finishes the current copy. He selects a blank page. He begins again. The same account, thirty years, still going. |
