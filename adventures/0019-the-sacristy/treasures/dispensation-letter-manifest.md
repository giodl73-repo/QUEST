# Relic Signs Manifest — Velantha's Dispensation Letter

At least **3 of the following**, distributed across **≥2 rooms**:

## Required

- [ ] **Indestructibility demonstration sign:** Evidence in at least one room that the letter has been through destruction attempts — scorch marks on the shelf around the ceramic holder; a bowl of ash that the letter was buried in and emerged from; the ceramic holder showing repair marks from when Aldric broke it in frustration. The destruction has been thorough and repeated. The letter is unchanged.

- [ ] **Copying sign:** Aldric's handwritten copies cover the walls — the same account, over and over, in Red Robe shorthand. At least one room must show the copying practice as active (fresh ink, organized by date). This is the relic sign of the letter's permanence expressed through Aldric's response to it: since he cannot destroy the record of what happened, he has been making additional records.

- [ ] **Warmth sign:** The letter is warm. In the room where it sits, the air near the ceramic holder is slightly warmer than elsewhere. **DC 12 Perception** to notice without picking it up. Anyone who picks up the letter: immediate warmth, no roll. The warmth is the same register as the field satchel and the measuring cup — faith-generated? No: this warmth is the warmth of a permanent act. The letter is warm because what it records cannot be undone.

- [ ] **Shorthand convergence sign:** Aldric's notation mark on the ritual focus (identified in 0018) and Aldric's shorthand on the copies should match. A character who examines both: **DC 10 Investigation** to confirm the same hand wrote the focus mark and the copies. This is the session's evidence chain — Aldric wrote the focus mark (his tools), Aldric wrote the letter (his authorization), Aldric is writing the copies (his thirty-year response).

- [ ] **Reaction moment:** When the party first attempts to destroy the letter — their chosen method fails. Aldric watches. He says: *"I've tried everything."* This is the letter's reaction moment: the indestructibility demonstrated in real time, in the session.

## Required (always)

- [ ] **The letter is visible before the indestructibility test.** Party sees it in the ceramic holder before anyone touches it or tries to destroy it.

- [ ] **The holder shows the thirty years.** The ceramic holder has been cracked, repaired, and the repair is visible. Evidence of force being applied, evidence of the letter surviving it.
