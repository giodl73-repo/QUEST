# Room 06 — Crypt Passage

## Read-aloud

> A short passage leading east — toward the Crypt. Clean stone, no congregation marks, no copies on the walls. The air is cooler here than in the sacristy. A lamp is not needed: there is faint light from ahead, steady and sourceless.

*(44 words)*

## Features

- **Cool air** (from the Crypt direction). The Crypt is the only completed room in the building. The congregation finished it. It is lit, clean, and laid out correctly. The air from it is slightly cooler and cleaner than the sacristy's ink-and-stone air.

- **Sourceless light** (from east). The Crypt has light. No fire, no lamp. The congregation placed something — a smooth stone that emits a faint, steady glow — at the center of the Crypt. It was there when they finished the room. It was one of Velantha's things.

- **No congregation marks** (throughout R6). The congregation does not spend time in this passage. They finished the Crypt and they visit occasionally, but the passage itself is simply a way from the sacristy to the Crypt. No shrines, no tallies, no copies. It is clean.

## Encounter

None. Transition room toward 0020.

## Connections

- **East:** 0020 (The Crypt — Velantha)

## GM Notes

R6 is the session's final beat: the party sees where they are going next. Aldric, if he accompanies them to the passage, stops here. He has never gone further. He looks east. He says: *"She said she left something there. I never went to look. I thought it wasn't my place."*

He returns to R2 and picks up his quill.

### Memory Fragment
*She said it to him as the last thing, almost in passing: "I left something in the crypt. If anyone ever comes far enough to ask, give them the copy and tell them to go to the crypt." He had written it down. He had been waiting for anyone to come far enough.*
