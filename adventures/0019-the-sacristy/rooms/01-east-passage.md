# Room 01 — East Passage

## Read-aloud

> The cleared passage opens into a narrow corridor. The smell hits immediately: old ink and cold stone. Ahead, light from a small lamp. The sound of a quill stopping.

*(29 words)*

## Features

- **Ink smell** (immediate, throughout R1). The smell the party detected from R5 of 0018 (Renn Perception 20). Now fully present. *Relic sign: copying sign begins here.*
- **Sound of quill stopping** (from R2). Aldric heard the party enter the passage. He stopped writing. He is waiting.

## Encounter

None. Transition room.

## Connections

- **West:** 0018 R5 crossing passage
- **East:** R2 (Main Sacristy)

## GM Notes

This room delivers the ink-smell confirmation for Renn — he said "someone has been writing in there" in S4. Now he is in the room he smelled. One beat: he looks at the wall as he enters the passage and sees the beginning of the copies — the same account, over and over, in Red Robe shorthand. He has been in the Orders' system. He knows what this notation is. He knows what kind of person keeps thirty years of copies in a room no one was supposed to find.
