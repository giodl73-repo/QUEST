# Operational Pressure Table

Roll d6 at each location transition. Three rolls standard (leaving Cordwainer Lane, at the burned house, on the way to Gorren).

## Pressure Table (d6)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** The day proceeds normally. |
| 2 | **Watch patrol.** A routine Watch pair on Chandler's Cut. Stealth DC 9 (crowd; they are not watching specifically) to pass unnoticed. They do not recognise the party. |
| 3 | **A building resident.** The second-floor occupant from the tenement is on the street when the party arrives. She is incurious but present. Mira's passive social awareness (Insight +5) tells her this woman knows the building and would notice unusual interest in the ground floor. |
| 4 | **Gorren's boarding room host.** The person running the boarding room where Gorren is staying is in the corridor when the party arrives. Brief, innocuous social interaction required (Charisma check DC 8 — or just Mira's natural presentation). He goes back to his business. |
| 5 | **Pale's watcher is active.** Roll 1d4 — on a 1, the Revenue Intelligence watcher from the combat encounter is on the burned-house block. Otherwise nothing. |
| 6 | **Gorren has a visitor.** Someone knocked on his door an hour before the party arrived — a man Gorren doesn't know, asking for someone who isn't there. Gorren didn't answer. The visitor has left. But now Gorren is on higher alert when the party knocks: knife in hand, but he is slower to open the door (1 additional minute before he answers). This is a coincidence. It may not feel like one to Gorren. |
