# Improviser Lens — C5-S1

Tessaly did not comment. Everyone held. That's actually the right improvisation for this party: when something goes wrong, you don't explain it, you absorb it and continue.

Her persona-calibration behavior — the thing that makes her interesting — did not have room to fire in a heist session where everyone was playing their position. The design should give her more unstructured social space.
