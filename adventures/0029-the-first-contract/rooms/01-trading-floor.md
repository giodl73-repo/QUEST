# Room 01 — The Trading Floor

## Read-aloud
> Bolts of cloth stacked by grade, a glass case of spice samples, everything locked and covered for the night. The floor smells of salt and cedar. A single strip of candlelight shows under the store-room door to the left. Someone is awake in there.

## Features

- **The front door.** DC 14 Thieves' Tools to open silently; Darro reduces to DC 10. Forced entry makes a scrape audible on the floor above (Gale's Perception DC 8).
- **The display case.** Locked (DC 12 Thieves' Tools). Contains samples only — no value to the job.
- **The store-room door.** Unlocked, opens inward. The candlelight is visible before the door is touched.
- **The stair.** Open access to the first floor. The third step creaks. Darro notes this on entry (passive Perception 14 — his is 14 exactly).

## GM Notes

The trading floor has no encounter. It is the threshold — the commitment point. Once they are inside, the Watch patrol on the street is no longer an immediate problem.

The candle under the store-room door is the first signal that Bessa is awake. A character who moves to the store-room door and listens (passive Perception 12) hears quiet movement — not the stillness of sleep.

### Memory Fragment (optional — if the party lingers)
> *The spice samples in the case are labeled in two hands: Gale's formal script, and a second, smaller one correcting his prices downward. The smaller hand has been there for four years.*
