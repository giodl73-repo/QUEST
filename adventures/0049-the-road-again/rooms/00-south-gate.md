# Room 00 — South Gate (The Decision)

## Read-aloud

> Morning. Stonehaven's south gate. The four of you have been on this road for six sessions. The road is clear. There is nothing ahead that requires all four of you. Each of you has somewhere to go — or, in Brek's case, somewhere that is no longer where you were going.

## DM notes

- **The DM's role here is to be a witness, not a director.** Let each PC's heuristic speak.
- **Do not prompt.** Do not ask "what do you do?" Do not frame the choice. Simply narrate: *"You are at the gate. The south road to Millhaven is ahead. The west road branches off half a mile out. What happens?"*
- **Vess's heuristic:** She will not stay with the group after the road is clear unless there is evidence that the road is less dangerous with them. She said so in her character notes. The evidence is six sessions: there is evidence. Whether her heuristic updates to account for that evidence is the question. Log verbatim.
- **Brek's heuristic:** He works out what he thinks while doing the thing. He may simply take the south road. He does not need to explain this. He has no reason to take the south road except that he is already here and the others are going that way.
- **Tam's heuristic:** He pays attention to what people don't say. He will notice if Vess starts toward the west road and pauses. He will not comment. He will wait.
- **Sera's heuristic:** She delays commitment until she has more data. She has been delaying about whether this group is a group. She now has six sessions of data. She knows what the data says.

## Route E firing condition

Route E fires when all four choose the same road — specifically when Brek and Vess choose the south road despite having no destination south that requires them to be there.

## Verbatim log requirement

Whatever each PC says in this scene: log it verbatim. This is the primary campaign data point. *"I suppose I'm going to Millhaven"* counts. *"Millhaven's south"* counts. Silence followed by movement counts if the movement is documented. The DM must capture it exactly.

## Exits

South: Millhaven road (Room 01) — Route E.
West: West road — Vess's delivery route (Route A if she goes).
