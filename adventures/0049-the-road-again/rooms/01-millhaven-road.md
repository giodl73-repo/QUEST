# Room 01 — Millhaven Road (Route E)

## Read-aloud

> The Millhaven road goes south. The first day is flat country, cold air, good visibility. There is no reason to be on this road that requires any particular conversation. You are four people on the same road.

## DM notes

- **This room only exists if Route E fires.** If the group splits at Room 00, session ends there.
- **The road is the container, not the content.** The DM narrates travel. What happens is whatever the PCs put into the space.
- **Potential conversation beats (not scripted — these are what might emerge):**
  - Tam may eventually ask Brek where he's going. Brek has no destination. If asked: *"South."* That is sufficient.
  - Vess may not explain herself. She rerouted. Her next delivery contact is in Millhaven — she did have a reason. She has not mentioned it. Whether she mentions it now is her choice.
  - Sera knows what Aldric told her. She is going to find out what happened to Mikela. She has not asked anyone to help with this. Whether she asks, or whether the others offer without being asked, is the session's last Reciprocal Act opportunity.
- **RV10 bilateral attempt:** If anyone names what the group has become — *"I suppose we're something now"* or equivalent — this is the bilateral completion moment. It does not require ceremony. It requires one person naming the fact and another person not denying it.
