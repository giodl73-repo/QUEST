# Defector Pressure Table

*The party is outside the walls in the pre-dawn window. Time is the constraint. Roll once per scene transition after Room 02 (d6).*

## Pressure Table (d6)

| Roll | Event |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | **Window holds.** The patrol rotation is running late — the gap between passes is 6 minutes instead of 4. The party has a full clean scene transition without a timing crisis. |
| 3 | **Luca is on the move.** He has finished his conversation with Aldren and is heading for the return route — moving fast, back toward the cliff-gap. If the party is not at Room 03 yet, he will reach the rope before them and attempt to climb without help (DC 12 Athletics; noisy on a failure). They have one scene to intercept. |
| 4 | **Second patrol rotation begins early.** A third Reconstitution soldier, coming off early relief, joins the perimeter circuit. The encounter in Room 04 is now 3 soldiers instead of 2. The XP budget increases to Hard. The alarm-call risk increases. |
| 5 | **The sky is lightening faster than expected.** Dawn is 20 minutes earlier than the party calculated. The Stealth DC for the Room 03 crossing increases to DC 14 (no longer full dark; early grey is enough to see by). The party must move — the "decide in Room 02" window is gone. |
| 6 | **Sergeant Irr is doing an early circuit.** He reaches the northeast corner (Room 01) and finds Luca's empty post and the rope anchor. He does not yet know the party is outside — he thinks it is only Luca. He is standing at the rope, hand on it, deciding whether to pull it up. If he pulls it, the party's re-entry is blocked (they must free-climb the outer face, DC 16 Athletics; failure = 1d6 falling damage and noise). The party can signal from Room 02 if they are fast enough — DC 12 Perception for Irr to hear a controlled whistle. |

## Notes

Roll this table at each of the following scene transitions: (1) Room 02 → Room 03, (2) Room 03 → Room 04, (3) Room 04 → Room 03 (return). Three rolls maximum. A result of 5 or 6 on the final roll collapses the return window — the party must move immediately or lose the clean re-entry.

**Result 6 (Irr at the rope):** This is the session's most interesting complication. Irr is not an enemy — he is the garrison's NCO, doing his job. If the party signals correctly, he holds the rope and says nothing to Asholt (he owes the party from S1). If they miss the signal, he pulls the rope up and they have to climb the hard way. When he sees who comes over the wall with Luca, his expression closes in a way that means he is filing the information and will not be speaking about it until he decides what to do with it.
