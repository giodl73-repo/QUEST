# Luca Bent (Garrison Archer)

## Appearance

Twenty-two years old. Slight build, archer's calluses on his right hand. He has the look of someone who has been performing competence for two years at a posting where nobody asked for his opinion, and has learned to keep most of himself behind a professional face. When he came over the wall this morning, something shifted in that — he is not performing now. He did a thing and he is prepared to be accountable for it, which is different from performing.

## Role in this session

Luca's arc-completion fires in Room 05 when a PC responds to his return without a tactical frame. His token follows. His arc-completion line is the session's central character beat. Caelith's spotlight question triggers his answer, which becomes the architecture of Davan's accounting speech.

## Public knowledge

- Luca Bent has been a garrison archer at Thorngate for two years.
- He is the youngest member of the current garrison — 22 years old.
- He has been on the east wall rotation since the siege began.
- In S1, the party observed him watching the Reconstitution standard at dawn.

## Private knowledge

- His older brother Aldren, 25, serves in the Reconstitution as an archer. They joined different orders three years ago — before the schism turned the Reconstitution into the besieging force.
- Luca has known since the siege began that Aldren was in the camp across the gap. He recognized the unit marker on the Reconstitution standard from Aldren's training reports.
- He has been living with this knowledge for 24 days.
- He went over the wall to see whether Aldren was on the wrong side or the other side. These are different things. He needed to know.

## Behavior matrix

### When the party finds his empty post (Room 01)

He is not there. The rope and the empty post speak for him.

### When he returns (Room 05)

He comes over the wall without announcing himself. He sees the party. He does not apologize. He waits.

**Arc-completion condition:** The first response from a PC is human, not tactical.

### If a PC asks about his brother (human response):

He answers directly: *"He's all right. He didn't know I was here."* Then he tells Caelith what Aldren told him — Saren's stalled assault authorization, the rotating units, the exhaustion in the camp — because Caelith has already asked the right question and Luca has the information to give.

### If a PC asks a tactical question first (tactical response):

He answers it correctly and completely — date, time, route, what he said to Aldren, what Aldren told him. He gives nothing that the garrison would not have a right to know. He is not evasive. He is simply formal, and the formality means the token is not available.

### When Caelith asks "What did you need to know?":

He looks at Caelith for a moment like he is measuring the question. Then: *"Whether Aldren was on the wrong side, or just the other side."* He does not say more unless asked. He has given the thing he has been carrying for 24 days. He is lighter for having said it.

## Arc-Completion

**Condition:** The party's first response to his return is human, not tactical.

**The act:** He gives his arc-completion line to the PC who responded humanly:

*"He's all right. He didn't know I was here."*

**What closes the arc:** The 24 days of watching the Reconstitution standard from the wall knowing his brother was across the gap. He went over because he needed to know. He came back because his answer was not what he feared. The arc-completion line is him naming both: his brother is alive and is on the other side, not the wrong side. That distinction is the thing he needed. He has it now.

**Token delivery:** He gives the party his garrison archer token — a wooden disc with a bowshot carved on one face — to the PC who responded humanly or to Caelith if Caelith spoke first.

## Aldren Bent (referenced NPC, not keyed)

Aldren, 25, Reconstitution archer. He is not in the party's encounter space. He is in the camp. The party knows about him through Luca. He gave Luca:

- Saren has not filed final assault authorization — drafted three times, pulled back each time.
- Units from Solanthus have rotated out.
- Camp sergeant Harven has begun asking when they are going home.
- Aldren does not understand why Saren is stalling.

Luca's comment: *"I think I might."* He does not explain this. If pressed: *"She's waiting for something to change. She doesn't want to be the person who ordered it if nothing changes first."*

This is the Hint 4 pre-plant. Luca is 22 and is reading Saren correctly.
