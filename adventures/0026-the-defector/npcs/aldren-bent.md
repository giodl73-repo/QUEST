# Aldren Bent (Reconstitution Archer)

## Appearance

Twenty-five years old. Taller than Luca, same archer's build. Reconstitution tabard over his own armor, which is slightly mismatched — he assembled it from different sources across three years of service, not from a single issuing. His face has the particular flatness of someone who has been disciplined in a new institution long enough that he has stopped registering surprise as surprise, only as information.

## Role in this session

Aldren is not a party encounter NPC. He appears at the camp perimeter in Room 04 only as a silhouette — the party may overhear his conversation with Luca (DC 12 Perception) but does not interact with him directly. He speaks briefly, gives Luca the Hint 4 information, and retreats into the camp when combat starts or when Luca tells him to go.

## What he tells Luca

*"She drafted the final-assault request three times. Pulled it back each time. Sergeant Harven has started asking the question — 'what are we waiting for.' She hasn't answered him."*

Then: *"The units from Solanthus rotated out last week. She didn't stop them."*

He does not speculate about why. He is a soldier; he reports what he observes. He does not ask Luca to do anything with the information. He is giving it because he knows Luca is inside the keep and he does not want his brother on the wrong side of a final assault if there is some chance to prevent it.

He says: *"I don't know how long before she asks for permission. I don't know if she does."* Then: *"You should go."*

## Behavior in combat

If the party engages the patrol: Aldren moves away from the perimeter at Luca's first word. He does not fight. He has given the information; he retreats into the camp. He is not captured.

## Notes for future sessions

- Aldren is a seed for S6/S7. He is the human connection inside the Reconstitution camp — the evidence that the camp has people in it who are not the siege, who are just soldiers doing a job they may be reconsidering.
- He does not appear as a named NPC in later sessions unless the party arranges contact. But Luca knows where he is, and if the parley moves forward, Aldren is the camp's internal pressure that Saren must account for.
- His arc does not complete in S5. He is texture and seed.
