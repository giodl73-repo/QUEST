# Improviser — C4-S5

The session produced several genuine improvisations. Caur's doctrinal-texture line was constructed under DM pressure — the module said "deliver doctrinal texture via Aldren or sergeant's effects" and the DM created the interview with Caur, arriving at "a question that needed answering" organically. That line was not in the module. It is now campaign-permanent.

The stealth crossing produced two crits from the party (Caelith Stealth 22; Sava Stealth 19) and one crit from Caelith on the cliff descent — the wizard's competence outside his expected domain is a consistent surprise. The improviser enjoys this because it creates a character story that the combat log did not design: Caelith is steadier in the dark and the cold than anyone expected.

Luca's "I think I might" — the hesitation that closes the scene — was not fully scripted in the module. The module says Luca doesn't explain what he thinks. The DM found the right way to stage that non-explanation: he says the sentence and stops. The improviser loves sentences that stop before they end.

Score: 75/80
