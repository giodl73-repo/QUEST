# Tactician — C4-S5

The combat this session was deliberately harder than S4 — outside the walls, cold, dark, the party at sub-optimal initiative (Talis fumbled), no garrison support. The result was mechanically honest: Round 1 produced four misses or outright failures for the party and two hits on them. This is exactly what outside-the-walls combat at night should feel like.

Caelith Chromatic Orb was the correct choice in Round 2 — it is his highest single-target damage output at L2 and landed clean. Davan's Rally was mechanically and narratively right: Talis was the one taking hits and temp HP is better than HP for a target about to take another blow.

The enemy crit save vs. Sacred Flame (natural 20) was genuine and was acknowledged. The combat ended not through attrition but through Talis's Colossus Slayer + Davan's momentum, with the second soldier fleeing rather than fighting to zero — this is correct for a patrol soldier making a rational choice.

Minor: Davan's two misses in Rounds 1 and 2 are consistent with the cold, non-garrison-flagstone footing outside. Chain mail in snow is a liability. Mechanically honest.
