## Innovation Flags

```
INNOVATION FLAG: grief-mapped resource conservation
Dimension affected: Atmospheric Landing, Mechanical Fairness
What happened: Scene 1.2 / 1.7 — "She had said 'all in reserve unless someone is down.' They were not down. She is satisfied with this in the particular way of a person who has been correct in a way that most people won't notice." Sava conserved 0 spell slots in a Hard combat encounter. The log frames this not as tactical discipline but as grief-mapped correctness: Elara died while Sava was delayed; spending resources before they were needed would echo that pattern (too late, not enough). The conservation is motivated by character grief, not mechanical optimization.
Why the rubric missed it: Existing player-styles cover craft-witness (practice maintained as ritual), act-without-announcement (cross-session behavioral consequence), and sheet-deep-reader (PC sheet's grief paragraph driving action). This is different: the mechanical resource decision itself becomes a grief expression. The PC's relationship to their spell slots is not tactical; it is an ongoing commitment to the specific failure-mode their grief is shaped around. This has not appeared before in this workshop.
```

```
INNOVATION FLAG: independent-motivation convergence toward collective restraint
Dimension affected: Character Agency, Atmospheric Landing
What happened: Scene 1.8 — "No one reports Luca's statement to Asholt or Tomek." Four PCs, four different motivation pathways (Davan: orders don't require reporting this; Sava: this is not a triage situation; Talis: this belongs to Luca; Caelith: filing the information doesn't require routing it to command), arrive at the same non-action without coordination. The collective restraint is not a joint decision; it is four individual decisions that agree.
Why the rubric missed it: act-without-announcement (v1.5) covers cross-session consequence of a prior naming event. This is within-session independent convergence toward a collective non-action. The mechanism is different: four different heuristics, four different reasoning paths, one behavioral outcome. This is the group-level version of act-without-announcement that operates intra-session. May warrant a v1.9 amendment or a separate player-style proposal.
```
