# C4-S1 LOG — The Arrival at the Needle

## Session Summary

The Relief arrived at Thorngate Keep on the fourth day of the Reconstitution siege. The Warden received them with minimal acknowledgment and assigned them to the east wall. During the afternoon, Sava found Maret Stave inventorying her supplies with contained alarm, and the two spoke plainly; Caelith sought and received a ten-minute briefing from Asholt; Talis made a useful discovery about the oil jar positioning. At dusk, the party took the east wall's open positions without being told to. The night assault came in three waves across three ropes; the party repelled it in three rounds without taking any damage, dealing themselves all the kills while the garrison archers watched. One Reconstitution soldier at Rope C hauled himself to the lip of the wall, saw the Thorngate insignia on the garrison archer beside him, and dropped back over without attacking. At dawn, a carved wooden disc was on the northeast arrow-slot ledge. Luca Bent, on watch, said: *"My brother's out there."* He went back to watching the line.
