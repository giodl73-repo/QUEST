# Room 05 — East Wall Walk

## Read-aloud

> Sixty feet of battlements, three feet of merlons on the northeast face. The Reconstitution's siege line is two hundred yards out — you can see their fires, their standard, the shapes of men on the ridge. The wind comes from that direction. Arrows fired from there have the wind behind them.

## Features

- **Battlements:** 3-foot merlons spaced 4 feet apart. **3/4 cover** while crouching behind a merlon (AC +5, Dex saves). Standing and attacking from between merlons: no cover.
- **Arrow-slits (5):** Narrow slits cut through the solid sections of the battlement. Full cover while shooting through a slit (AC cannot be targeted), but attacks made through them have disadvantage.
- **Grapple points (3):** During the night assault, the Reconstitution places grappling hooks at three points along this wall — northwest corner (nearest the cliff face), center, and northeast corner. See encounters/night-assault.md.
- **Garrison archer positions (2):** Tomek assigns two garrison archers here at nightfall. They are competent (see stat block in encounters). They follow the party's lead only if the party establishes credibility before the assault — otherwise they act independently.
- **The token ledge (dawn):** The arrow-slot at the northeastern corner has a flat stone ledge 6 inches wide. At dawn after the assault, if Tomek's token is earned, a carved wooden disc is on this ledge. Passive Perception DC 10 to notice; it is not hidden.

## Encounter

**Scene 5 — The Night Assault.** See `encounters/night-assault.md`. The assault runs here. The Reconstitution soldiers attempt to place three sets of grappling hooks and scale the wall.

**The recognition moment (Scene 5, mid-assault):** One Reconstitution soldier hauls himself to the top of the wall at the northeast corner. He looks at the garrison archer beside him. He recognizes the Thorngate insignia on her collar. He freezes. His sergeant shouts his name. He drops back over the wall and retreats without orders. **Passive Perception DC 12 to observe this.** No explanation. He is gone before anyone can act.

## Treasure

Tomek's carved token (dawn, conditional on earning trust). Not a magical item — it is a watch-token, a wooden disc with the Thorngate device on one face and blank on the other.

## GM Notes

During the night assault, light is limited. Torches on the garrison side (two, on iron brackets at the north and south ends of the wall). The Reconstitution attacks from darkness — they have covered their approach. Effective ranged attack range for the defenders is 60 feet (beyond that: disadvantage on ranged attacks without darkvision).

**The Reconstitution standard** is visible from here at all times of day. Passive Perception DC 12: the device on the standard is an inverted Rose of Solamnia on a grey field. History DC 14 (or Solamnic background, automatic): this is not a generic Reconstitution device — it is a specific sub-unit device, associated with a commander who earned the right to modify the standard.

**Luca on the arrow-walk above (Scene 1 and Scene 6):** Luca can be seen from the east wall at the arrow-walk railings. He is always watching the northeast. Scene 1: passive Perception DC 12 to notice his expression. Scene 6 (dawn): he is on the wall when the party returns after the assault. *"My brother's out there."* One sentence. He does not elaborate.

**Token condition reminder:** The disc appears on the ledge at dawn IF: (1) the party was on the east wall for Scene 5 without being ordered; AND (2) no party member approached Asholt or Tomek after the assault to report. The DM places the disc at dawn during Scene 6, before the party's first dialogue. When the token is first picked up: it has the specific texture of something held often — not rough, not smooth. Both faces are carved; one is the Thorngate device, one is blank.

**S2 cross-session recovery path:** If the token is not earned in S1, Tomek gives it in S2 if the party demonstrates the same pattern on the supply run — acts without being ordered, does not seek credit. Note in handoff if S1 token was missed.

### Memory Fragment *(read if party stands at the battlements before the assault)*
