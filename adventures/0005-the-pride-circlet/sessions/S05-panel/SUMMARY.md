**Why the dip from S04:** S05 was a zero-dice diplomatic session. The Tactician lens is structurally disadvantaged by this — its weights favor mechanical fairness (1.6) and table readiness (1.5), but its engagement weight (0.8) combined with the low mechanical-combat score (6/10 on engagement from tactical perspective) pulled the Tactician total to 60.8. The other four lenses scored 69-73.5, showing the session quality was genuinely high — the aggregate reflects the Tactician's legitimate complaint that the session had no tactical drama, not that it was poorly designed.

**This is correct behavior.** Diplomatic sessions should score lower on the Tactician lens than combat sessions. The 68.6 mean is a structurally honest result.

**This dissent is expected for a diplomatic session.** No action needed.
