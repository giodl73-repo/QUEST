# Temporal Pressure — Korrath Urban Variant

Korrath is an Istaran city under Second House influence. Temporal pressure is political and social, not supernatural. The session spans **Day 1 afternoon → Day 2 → Day 3 morning** (~2.5 in-fiction days).

## Use

- **City Pulse** d6 every **4 hours of in-fiction time.** Atmospheric or social; rarely mechanical.
- **Estate Watch** d6 once per major scene transition inside Tiran's estate.
- Tables stop when the party is inside a single location for an extended meeting (Scenes 02, 04, 06).

## City Pulse Table (d6 per 4 hours)

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** Harbor noise. Second House flags snap in the wind. |
| 2 | **A Solamnic rider** passes through Korrath heading south. Aelric passive 13: he has a Rose Council letter-pouch. This is the trailing courier; he arrives Day 3 with Laen's letter. |
| 3 | **Felor's eyes.** Thera passive 13: a Second House retainer is watching the party from across the market. Not approaching; cataloging. |
| 4 | **A dying woman from the chapter-house.** A senior sister approaches the party near the chapter-house: *"Are you the Varduin party? Lady Tiran's people are asking if you have given her an answer."* No mechanic; pacing reminder. |
| 5 | **Tiran's reputation.** The innkeeper Sela mentions, unprompted: *"She built the harbor wall. Before her, every third winter we flooded."* Character-delivery of what Tiran built. |
| 6 | **Felor at the chapter-house gate.** If the party is inside the chapter-house when this rolls: Felor has come to pass a message — Lady Tiran requests the party's presence at the estate. This can accelerate Scene 02 or Scene 04. |

## Estate Watch Table (d6 per transition inside estate)

| d6 | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | **Nothing.** A servant passes silently. |
| 2 | **A household guard** at the corridor junction. Not actively watching; blocking by presence. Party may pass without incident. |
| 3 | **Felor's patrol.** He appears at the scene's end, 1d4+2 rounds after the party enters a restricted area. Relevant in Scene 05 unauthorized-entry. |
| 4 | **Tiran's breathing audible from the next room.** She is nearby; she knows the party is moving. She is letting them. |
| 5 | **A servant with a candle.** Lights a lamp in an adjacent room; light spills under a door. Thera passive 13: the lamp was deliberately moved to illuminate a corridor — someone is watching the party's route. |
| 6 | **Tiran enters.** She appears, composed, and asks the party what they have found. This forces a conversation before the party is ready. |

## Pacing guidance

- **Day 1 afternoon:** arrival + Scene 01 (harbor/market) + Scene 02 (Tiran first meeting). ~1-2 City Pulse rolls.
- **Day 1 evening + night:** free time; party debrief; possible Wena visit if arranged. ~1 City Pulse roll.
- **Day 2 morning:** Scene 03 (Wena briefing). ~1 City Pulse roll.
- **Day 2 afternoon:** Scene 04 (joint meeting) + Scene 05 (archive). ~1 City Pulse roll + 1-2 Estate Watch rolls.
- **Day 2 evening:** Scene 06 (vault). **Tables paused during the vault scene.**
- **Day 3 morning:** Scene 07 (aftermath). ~1 City Pulse roll.
