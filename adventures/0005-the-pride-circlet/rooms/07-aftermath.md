# Scene 07 — Aftermath (Korrath; Day 3)

**Location:** Chapter-house courtyard or harbor; morning Day 3. **Setup:** The Circlet question has been resolved; the party is preparing to leave.

**Read-aloud:**

> The harbor in the morning smells different from the harbor at noon. Quieter. The Second House ships are loading; their purple-rose flags hang slack in the windless air. Korrath wakes up slowly. The chapter-house bell has rung for morning office. Wena Laen is at the courtyard gate. She is not in her novice robe; she is in work clothes. She has a bag.

**Features:**

- **Wena at the gate.** She may be leaving with the party, leaving for another posting, or staying — per Scene 4's resolution. If the golden-path was achieved (Tiran heard without the Circlet), Wena stays at Korrath, but she is visible and present and gives the party something before they go.
- **Tiran's disposition (per Scene 6 outcome):**
  - *Attuned:* Tiran in her estate, Pride-Lock already shading her responses. Felor is managing the archive-transfer paperwork. She is composed and increasingly unreachable.
  - *Unattuned (negotiated):* Tiran is in the chapter-house chapel, talking to Wena. Neither of them is praying. They are talking.
  - *Circlet destroyed:* Tiran at the harbor-wall, alone, looking at the water. She turns when the party approaches. *"Thank you."* That is all.
- **Wena's parting gift:** regardless of outcome, Wena gives the party **Tiran's complete six-forging research** (the original; Tiran gifted it to Wena; Wena passes it on). *"You will need this more than she will."*

**The sixth-forging confirmation:**

When Kessa opens Tiran's research and cross-references with everything the party has:
- The unnamed sixth artifact is shame-keyed or self-concealment-keyed (Tiran's research)
- Aelwen could not forge it (apron-scrap: *"This will be my good work. Empty it. Break it."* — the Coin, not the sixth)
- The ingot she said she should have forged instead (#0003 journal page)
- Grom's Hint 3 (silent): *"A coil can be uncoiled"*
- The sixth is the un-forged object — Aelwen's shame is that she could not make the thing that would have released the others. The ingot is not a missing artifact; it is the **missing ending** of the grief-coil sequence.

**Brother Laen's courier (if the party checks):** A Solamnic rider has been trailing the party from Varduin, two days behind. Arrives in Korrath Day 3 with a letter from Laen: *"The Council has formally named Lady Tiran's acquisition attempts as a diplomatic concern. Whatever you have done — I hope it holds. Return when you can. — L."* The Council is watching the Second House. Aelric's actions here will be part of the official record.

**Encounter:** None.

**Treasure:**
- Tiran's complete six-forging research (0 gp; **priceless** — includes the water-reflection fragment, the six-artifact manifest, Tiran's pencil annotations).
- If the Circlet is in party hands (unattuned or attuned): its market value as a Very Rare artifact is incalculable; its weight in campaign terms is immense.
- Brother Laen's political validation letter (0 gp; Aelric formal record).
- **350 gp** from Tiran (if negotiated resolution achieved): she pays for *professional services rendered* in the language of Second House formal agreements. Felor handles the transfer with visible distaste.

**Connections:** Road back to Varduin (12 days); or Felor's ships offer faster passage (6 days by sea, if Tiran's goodwill is intact).

**GM Notes:**

- **Wena's work clothes.** She is not leaving. She is staying in Korrath, at the chapter-house, doing the work she was placed here to do. This is her choice. She is not running from Tiran; she is staying in proximity to keep watch. This is who she is.
- **Tiran's final state is not the session's verdict.** The session ends without knowing if Tiran made the right choice. That is correct. She will die in whatever configuration she chose. The party carries the research.
- **Aelric's Solamnic record.** Laen's letter means the Council knows something happened. What the Council believes happened is up to what the party's formal report says. Aelric will draft this on the road home.
