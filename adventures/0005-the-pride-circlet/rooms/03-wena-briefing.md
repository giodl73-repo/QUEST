# Scene 03 — Wena's Briefing (Chapter-House, Wena's Room)

**Location:** Revered Daughters chapter-house; Wena's private workspace (half-room; a cot, a workbench, tools). **Time:** late afternoon Day 1.

**Read-aloud:**

> The chapter-house has the specific quiet of a building that is simultaneously a home and a place of work. Wena Laen is in her room fixing a door hinge — not the room's door hinge, a spare one she is pre-fitting before installation. She looks up. Looks at Aelric specifically. Three seconds. Then: *"You came. Good. I was starting to think I would have to write a third letter."*

**Features:**

- **Wena's workbench.** Tools; a spare hinge; a small notebook (not her private thoughts — a project log for the chapter-house's current construction needs). One corner of the workbench has a folded stack of papers: Tiran's compiled research notes (Wena's copy). She will share this.
- **The research notes.** Tiran's manifest of Aelwen's six forgings. It names five: the Rose (Solamnic archive), the Reliquary (Nerakan network), the Mirror (*"currently with Mira Vaenshold-Silversmith; negotiations ongoing"* — three years out of date), the Wrath-Coin (*"with Thane Krun of Kruntharrak; status unknown"*), and the Pride-Circlet (*"in estate archive; secure"*). The sixth is listed as: *"One unnamed; suspected properties related to shame or self-concealment; provenance unknown; not in any market accessed."* **Hint 4 near-delivery.**
- **The chapter-house itself.** A senior sister passes in the corridor and closes the door (gives them privacy). The sisters know Wena has guests; they are not surprised. Tiran's money is obvious even inside the convent.

**Wena's briefing:**

She gives the party the research notes freely. She says:

*"I have known about the Circlet for four months. I told Tiran I needed time. I wrote to Aelric because I recognized the Muster from Tiran's file. She has a file. It is — flattering about Aelric. Politely dismissive about the others. I found it annoying."*

Then: *"Here is what I need you to understand. The Circlet is not in the empty case in her reception hall. I know where it actually is. I will show you. But first: what are you going to do about her?"*

**Key Wena disclosures (per PC questions):**

- **On Blank-Circlet-Trap:** *"I have read the notes. If your most-admirer is dead, the Circlet shows you the best version of yourself, and it keeps showing you until you are not looking out. That is the word she used: not-looking-out. I do not know if Lady Tiran's husband admired her most. I do not know if that question has an answer she can survive."*
- **On the sixth forging:** *"Tiran's research says 'shame or self-concealment.' I do not know more than that. If one of you has the journal page from Aelwen's last night, you may know more than Tiran does."* (This prompts Kessa to cross-reference: Aelwen's *"ingot instead"* + shame-keyed = the un-forged shame-object.)
- **On Wena's refusal to witness:** *"I will not witness an attunement I believe will harm her. I am a novice, not a priest. I have no authority to prevent her. But I will not be the Paladine-representative who watches a dying woman blind herself."*
- **On the Circlet's location:** *"The vault. Third floor of the estate, behind a door I think even Felor would need a key for. Tiran showed me once."*

**Investigation DC 12 on the research notes:** Kessa spots the Mirror entry (*"currently with Mira Vaenshold-Silversmith; negotiations ongoing"*). Tiran's network tracked Mira three years ago. The network has not been updated since Keloth's cell collapsed. **This is useful intelligence: Tiran does NOT know the Mirror was destroyed in S03.**

**Encounter:** None. This is a debrief scene.

**GM Notes:**

- **Wena's three-second look at Aelric.** She is relieved and annoyed in equal measure. She has been carrying this alone for four months. She is not fragile; she is tired.
- **The Hint 4 near-delivery is a Kessa moment.** Let her make the connection between *"shame or self-concealment"* and *"I should have forged an ingot instead"* and *"a coil can be uncoiled."* If she cross-references: note it as a SURPRISE. If not: the connection is preserved for #0007.
- **Wena's remark about Tiran's file being dismissive.** She is testing the party — specifically testing whether they are the kind of people who would correct her assumption that Thera or Grom are lesser. This shapes how she relates to them in Scene 7.
