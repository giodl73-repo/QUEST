# Scene 01 — Korrath-by-the-Sea, Arrival

**Location:** Harbor Gate + market district. **Time:** midday, Day 1.

**Read-aloud:**

> Korrath-by-the-Sea is louder than Crystalmir and more fragrant than Varduin. The harbor smells of salt, rendered tallow, and Second House money. Istaran shipping flags — the purple rose on white — hang on four ships at anchor. The market stalls run from the gate to the chapter-house district: spices from three ports south, silk from the Solamnic interior, and moon-silver jewelry in cases that Kessa will notice before anyone else does.

**Features:**

- **The harbor.** Four Second House flagged ships. A harbormaster who can be bribed for arrival manifests (Persuasion DC 12). The ships are carrying luxury imports; one carries something heavy in a sealed crate (not plot-relevant; pure Istaran-atmosphere flavor).
- **The market silver stalls.** Kessa Arcana DC 10: moon-silver pendants in the market are Vaenshold-vein work (the same lineage as Aelwen's forgings). They are shop-grade; none are artifacts. But the market-presence means moon-silver has been distributed from the Vaenshold line into Istaran commerce. Anchor-level passive (path b redundancy for Aelwen's engraving recognition in Scene 2).
- **The Purple Rose inn.** Named for the Second House sigil; Tiran endowed it forty years ago. The party can stay here. Rooms are 3 gp/night (expensive). The innkeeper, **Sela**, knows Tiran by name and with deference.
- **The Revered Daughters chapter-house** (visible from the market, fifteen minutes south). White stone; Paladine's hammer-and-shield above the door. A senior sister is at the gate.
- **The Purple Rose Estate** (harbor-facing, east; visible from the market). A proper townhouse with a harbor terrace. Second House purple trim. Felor's crest at the gate.

**Encounter:** None. This is an arrival scene.

**Connections:** street → [2] chapter-house; street → [3] Tiran's estate.

**GM Notes:**

- **Accessibility gloss on Revered Daughters:** *"The Revered Daughters of Paladine — an Order of Solamnic-adjacent clergy-women who serve Paladine through temple-construction and ritual care for the dying. They are not Solamnic knights; they are under no Measure. They have their own hierarchy."*
- **Accessibility gloss on Second House of Istar:** *"The Second House is one of Istar's old-money noble factions. They are not the Kingpriest's faction but they are not opposed to it either. They fund temples, buy influence, and maintain ships. Lady Tiran is their most visible Korrath representative."*
- **Kessa's silver-market note:** this is the only in-person evidence that Aelwen's moon-silver lineage has been commercially distributed. First time the party has seen Vaenshold work *for sale* rather than hidden or held.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

> *Korrath was a fishing town sixty years ago before the Second House built the harbor to Second House scale. Tiran remembers the fishing-town version. She endowed the inn, the chapel, the chapter-house, and the harbor wall in the forty years since. She is the reason this city is worth visiting. She will be the reason it is remembered. She knows this. It is both true and not enough.*
