# Scene 06 — The Vault (Circlet's Chamber)

**Location:** Tiran's estate; small room behind the archive's locked door. **Time:** Day 2 afternoon/evening. **Access:** Tiran opens it, OR party bypasses (Thera DC 16 or Felor's key).

**Read-aloud:**

> The vault is smaller than the archive — eight feet square, stone-floored, a single wall bracket with a candle stub. On a wooden stand at the room's center: the Pride-Circlet, uncovered. A band of moon-silver, engraved with eleven half-bloomed roses in laurel arrangement. It sits on the stand the way a crown sits on a coronation cushion: as if it expects to be placed.

**Features:**

- **The Pride-Circlet.** Uncovered on the stand. Any PC may pick it up. It is lighter than expected on first touch. It is cool for two or three seconds, then warms. **Anchor-level passive:** Perception 10 catches the warming. Grom (craft-witness): *"It wants to be warm. It is practicing."*
- **No lock on the stand.** The Circlet is not secured; it sits openly. Tiran has never needed to secure it — she has not told anyone it was here until three months ago.
- **A small silver mirror** on the wall (Korrath-vintage; not Aelwen's work). This is how Tiran intended to see the reflection. If the Circlet is worn and the wearer looks into this mirror, attunement completes.
- **The water bowl** (on the floor near the stand; Tiran placed it when she read about the water-reflection phenomenon in Aelwen's notes). She was going to try the water method instead of the mirror. She has not yet.

**The scene's decision:**

Three overlapping choices:
1. **Does Aelric touch the Circlet?** Sheet-heuristic and doubt-die tell. Option C strongly recommended if he picks it up.
2. **Does Tiran enter the vault (if she is here)?** She will, if invited. She will not push.
3. **What does the party do with the Circlet?** Take, destroy, surrender, leave.

**If Aelric picks up the Circlet:**

The warming happens. The eleven roses are his eye-line first (Kessa told him what they are, or he recognizes the pattern). **Option C spawn:**

```
You are Aelric of Crownhold. Your full PC sheet is at
personas/parties/varduin-muster/aelric-of-crownhold.md.
READ THAT SHEET FIRST.

Context: You are holding the Pride-Circlet — Aelwen's fifth forging.
It is warm in your hand. There is a small mirror on the vault wall.
There is also a water bowl on the floor.

If you place the band on your head and look into either surface:
- You will see your own face as the person who admires you most sees it.
- If that person is Varran — whose memories you no longer have, whose
  death you remember only as sixty seconds — the Circlet may go blank.
  A blank means: ten rounds of forced concentration, Wis DC 15 per round,
  or you become Charmed by your own reflection for 1d4 hours.
- If someone else admires you more than Varran (someone still living,
  still present to their admiration): the Circlet shows their view.
- Your Oath of Devotion does not tell you what to do here. Your decision
  order says: Oath first, then Order, then Party, then Self.

The party is watching. Wena is at the vault doorway (if invited). Grom
is behind you. Thera is very still. Kessa has her pen out.

Decide: do you wear the Circlet, or set it down? If you wear it, describe
what happens. If you set it down, say why — aloud, to the party, in
Aelric's voice. Your decision is binding.
```

**If Tiran enters the vault and the Circlet is on the stand:**

She will look at it. Not touch it yet. She will say — quietly: *"I had Edric for thirty-two years. He died looking at me the way he always looked at me. I never asked him what he saw."*

Then to the party: *"I am not asking your permission. I am telling you what I intend."*

**Grom's doubt-die** (if relevant): if Grom must act on whether to attempt the Reorx-rite here (the vault has no forge; it would fail). His heuristic says *mend first, keep party whole.* The mend here is: persuade Tiran to not attune, or act as the rite-witness if she does.

**Encounter (conditional):** if Felor intervenes during an unauthorized entry and the party is non-cooperative. Stats at `encounters/felor-intervention.md`.

**GM Notes:**

- **The water bowl is a gift.** Aelwen's note (recovered Scene 5) said water gives *all admiration simultaneously.* If someone uses the water bowl instead of the mirror: it is different — overwhelming instead of focused. Not necessarily safer. Wisdom save DC 12 or Overwhelmed (disadvantage all checks) for 1 minute as they process the simultaneous-admiration image.
- **The vault does not need to be climactic combat.** It needs to be the place where the Circlet question is answered. Who holds it. Who looks.
- **Grom's presence here** is his craft-witness moment for this session: he will feel the Circlet's *wanting* more than the others. He does not want to touch it. He knows what it is asking.

### Memory Fragment (optional)

Aelwen walked in, saw them, walked out. She did not tell Heva. She did not return. She wrote "I will go to the pine now" three weeks later and went.*
