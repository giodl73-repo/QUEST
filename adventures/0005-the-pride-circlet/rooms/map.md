## DM notes

- **Korrath-by-the-Sea is a functioning Istaran port.** Second House wealth is visible: the harbor has Second House flagged ships; the market has Istaran luxury goods; the inn charges three times Varduin rates. Accessibility gloss: Second House = old-money Istaran noble faction, now buying influence with the Kingpriest's coalition.
- **Tier 2 note.** Party is L4. Korrath has Istaran guards (CR ½ each; 10 on duty at any time near Tiran's estate); Felor's household guards (1d4 + Felor himself); and Second House political leverage (arrest is possible if the party creates an incident).
- **The chapter-house is neutral ground.** The Revered Daughters are apolitical in Istaran terms; neither Second House nor Kingpriest can give orders there. Tiran's influence is financial, not legal, inside the convent walls.
- **v1.3 positional redundancy:** the Pride-Circlet appears in Tiran's reception (Scene 2, in its case), is described by Wena (Scene 3), is in the archive research (Scene 5), and is physically accessible in the vault (Scene 6). Multiple positions; multiple approach paths.
