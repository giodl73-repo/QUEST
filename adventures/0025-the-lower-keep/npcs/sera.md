# Sera (Refugee Child)

## Appearance

Eight years old. Dark hair, serious face. She has Helin's posture — upright, economical with movement. She was five years old when Dene brought her family to the lower chambers, which means she does not fully remember living anywhere else. Thorngate Keep is her home. She does not know it is not supposed to be.

## Role in this session

Sera is the face at the door in Room 03. She opened it because Dene told her to: if the noise gets very loud and it sounds like soldiers are in the main passage, open the door and stand in it. She is doing exactly that. She is not frightened. She is completing a task she was given and waiting for what happens next.

## Behavior

- She does not speak unless spoken to.
- If a PC speaks to her gently or moves toward her without a weapon: she steps back into the passage and says: *"Dene said you might come."* She leads them to Room 04.
- If a PC asks her name: she answers. *"Sera."* She does not give a family name — she is eight and this is simply her name.
- If a PC asks how long she has been here: *"Always."* She means it.
- If a PC asks where she is from: she thinks about it and says: *"Here."* She is not being evasive. The Kharolis foothills where her family came from are a thing she knows from stories, not memory.

## Notes

Sera is not a campaign-significant NPC beyond the token-condition scene. She does not have an arc of her own — she is a child in an unusual situation who is managing it with the resources she has. She is fine. Her grandmother is Helin. They will be mentioned in S5 when Talis encounters the refugees outside.

After S4, Sera remains in the refugee space. If the party interacts with the refugees in later sessions, she may appear as a detail — the girl who opened the door.
