# Curse Symptoms Manifest — Clause of Grief

Curse: The weight of witness — attuned creature relives strangers' grief once/week; accumulates an archive of others' losses.

At least 3 of 4 symptoms must land:

## Symptom 1 — The cold (sensory)
The shard is always slightly below room temperature.
**Required:** When anyone first touches the stone box or the shard directly — the first thing they note is the cold. *"It is colder than the air around it."*
**Fallback:** Morreth mentions it when he describes how he found it. *"It was cold in my hand even on a warm day."*

## Symptom 2 — The re-experience (primary, Lenne)
Any creature who holds the shard for >1 minute relives their worst loss.
**Required:** Scene 03 — Lenne picks up the shard and holds it. Sixty seconds. She relives Volenn's death. She drops it. The party sees her drop it.
**Fallback (if Lenne doesn't pick it up):** Morreth describes what happened to him. *"Three days. I didn't know what was happening. I couldn't put it down. When I finally did, I couldn't say his name for a week."* (He doesn't say who "he" was.)

## Symptom 3 — The inscription challenge (textual)
*"Let no Order forget the cost of what it asks."*
**Required:** Someone reads it aloud. Calder is the most likely — his grief is about the Orders not being there when the plague came. Let him read it quietly.
**Fallback:** Lenne reads it as she examines the shard (she reads inscriptions aloud, slightly — like Thessaly).

## Symptom 4 — The Route D gift (sensory, arc-completion only)
When Sera takes the shard after naming her grief, she sees the settlement as it is now — intact.
**Required:** Route D only. The shard's surface briefly shows motion — not an image, a quality of light, like something alive happening just beyond the obsidian's surface.
**Fallback (Routes A/C):** The shard is simply cold in Sera's hands, nothing more. The gift requires the naming.

## Per-PC reception table
