## Innovation Flags

```
INNOVATION FLAG: Bilateral arc-completion (S03)
Dimension: Atmospheric Landing / Character Agency
What happened: Sera named the settlement; Morreth named Ven immediately after.
Neither naming could have happened without the other — Sera's naming was the
condition for Morreth's. The bilateral structure was designed; the execution
exceeded the design in timing and specificity.
Why it matters: NPC-arc-completion has been one-directional (party creates
conditions; NPC delivers). This is the first instance where an NPC arc-completion
fires in direct response to a PC arc-completion, creating a reciprocal naming chain.
New sub-type: bilateral-arc-completion.
```

```
INNOVATION FLAG: Lenne's empty notebook (S03)
Dimension: Character Agency / Atmospheric Landing
What happened: After the arc-completion, Lenne opened her notebook and didn't
write and put it away. She has been documenting constantly since joining the party.
This is the first time she encountered something she could not document.
Why it matters: sheet-deep-reader sub-type: the archivist who puts the notebook
away. The arc-completion landed on her as a non-documentable experience.
The notebook being open and then closed is more specific than "she was moved."
```
