# Scene 03 — Grain Mill (Optional)

**Read-aloud:**

> The grain mill is the tallest building in Thornmere — two floors, the upper floor accessible by an exterior ladder. From the upper floor: the approach road is visible for three hundred feet in both directions. A miller who does not leave her flour says: *"Do what you need to do outside. Please."*

**Features:** High point (Athletics DC 10). 300-ft sightline on approach road. Miller non-combatant.

**GM Notes:** If the combat retreats into the village (coordinator fled; agents pursuing), the mill is Sera's natural position. She will be there before anyone asks her to be. The miller will not comment; she has seen worse.

**Connections:** Scene 01 (Village Square).
