# Scene 04 — Pellan's Garden (Aftermath)

**Read-aloud:**

> The garden is behind the cottage. Pellan is on his knees checking the tomato stakes. He does not look up when you come around. He says: *"Seventy-nine days? Then you'd better go."* He has been counting too, in his way. He knew approximately when the convergence was from Vorn's note. He does not know the exact date. He has been close.

**Features:** The garden. Pellan at work. No mechanical content — this is the session's last breath before the finale.

**GM Notes:**
- Thessaly will correct his number: seventy-nine days when they arrived; now seventy-eight or seventy-seven, depending on how long the session took. She says the corrected number. He nods.
- Calder will probably say something to Pellan about the garden — the way the plants are staked, the soil quality. Pellan will answer specifically. This is two people who know what maintenance means having a conversation about it.
- Orik will check the cottage structure on the way out. He always does.
- Sera will look down the road, toward Palanthas, toward wherever the last shard is. She says nothing about it.
- Lenne will check how many notebook pages she has left. She has been filling them fast.

**Connections:** End of adventure 0013. The party returns to Palanthas; Lenne coordinates with Vorn on the finale logistics. 0014 begins.
