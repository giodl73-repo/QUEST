# Scene 02 — Pellan's Cottage

**Read-aloud:**

> The cottage is small and organized. Pellan returns from inside with a cloth-wrapped object. He holds it out. *"I've had this for twenty years. I kept it because I couldn't find its rightful owner. I found you now."* He gives it to whoever is at the front. Then: *"The inscription is about the covenant. Make sure yours isn't built on anger. You'll know if it is."* He returns to the garden.

**Features:**
- **The shard.** Room temperature. Dull finish. Feels like a stone. Pellan gives it without ceremony.
- **Pellan's garden.** Visible through the window: tomatoes, beans, some late-season herbs. He went back to watering immediately. He is not sentimental. He is satisfied.
- **The moment after.** Each PC in the room understands, at some point in the next few minutes, that they have already done the thing that earned this. The shard requires forgiveness that has already been performed. They have all performed it.

**Arc-completion — Pellan:** Already fired in Scene 01 (village square). His recognition was immediate: *"Yes. You're the ones."* The cottage scene is the handoff.

**Per-PC reception:**
- **Thessaly:** She named the Test wound to Vorn. She forgave herself enough to say it aloud to a peer.
- **Orik:** He named the hall to Lenne. He spoke it as explanation, not self-punishment.
- **Sera:** She saw the settlement safe. She forgave herself for leaving.
- **Calder:** He returned Sylaren's note. He forgave the protocol's designers enough to stop carrying it as guilt.
- **Lenne:** She filled the empty notebook page. She forgave herself for not being able to document what happened at Morreth's chapel.

None of them need to say this aloud. Pellan already knew.

**GM Notes:**
- **This scene is quiet.** The combat is over. The shard is given. Let the room be still. Nobody needs to perform anything.
- **Thessaly reads the inscription.** She will. Auto (Arcana 18). She says: *"Let no covenant hold anger as its foundation."* She will think about the compact she is building. Then: *"That's the point."*

**Connections:** Scene 04 (Pellan's Garden, optional aftermath).
