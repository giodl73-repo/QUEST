# Map — The Forgiveness Key

## Topology

A small agricultural village. The encounter happens on the approach. The shard acquisition happens at the cottage.

```
[ENCOUNTER: Splinter cell last stand — farmland approach]
        |
        v
[01 THORNMERE APPROACH / VILLAGE SQUARE]
        |                    \
        v                     \
[02 PELLAN'S COTTAGE]   [03 GRAIN MILL (optional)]
        |
        v
[04 PELLAN'S GARDEN (aftermath)]
```

**Loop:** Village square connects to both cottage and grain mill. The grain mill has a high point (grain storage second floor) — Sera's perch if the encounter moves into the village.

**Vertical:** Grain mill has a second floor. Sera can reach it (Athletics DC 10) for a sniper position if the splinter cell pursues into the village.

**Two paths to artifact:** Direct (encounter → cottage, Pellan gives shard) or via grain mill (if combat retreats into village; mill is a defensible position).
