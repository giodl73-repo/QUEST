# Scene 01 — Thornmere Approach / Village Square

**Read-aloud (encounter):**

> The farmland is flat and open — long sightlines, wheat and fallow fields, the village visible half a mile ahead. Three figures step from the treeline on the road's east shoulder. They are not hiding. They have been watching for the party for four days. The one in the center says: *"We know who you are. We know what you're carrying. And we know Vorn stood the network down. We didn't."*

**Splinter Cell Last Stand:** See `encounters/splinter-cell-last-stand.md`. Route D available if the party offers the same stand-down terms they gave the White Quill entrance agents. Route C if combat.

**Read-aloud (village square, after resolution):**

> Thornmere is quiet. Market square, grain mill, waystation-inn. A man at the north edge of the square is watering his tomatoes. He looks at you — at each of you in turn — and says: *"Yes. You're the ones."* He puts down the watering can. He goes inside.

**GM Notes:**
- **The encounter is the whole session's dramatic weight.** Pellan giving the shard is simple; the encounter on the approach is the last fight.
- **Sera's perimeter call.** She will have seen the agents from the road before the party got close (passive Perception 16: 200 feet of visible road; the agents are not hiding). She signals. The party has a round to position before the agents close.
- **Pellan watching.** He can see the road from his cottage. He knows the party is coming; he is watering his tomatoes. He will watch the encounter from his garden.

**Connections:** Scene 02 (Pellan's Cottage, after village square).
