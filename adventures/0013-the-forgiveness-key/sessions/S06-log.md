# S06 LOG — The Forgiveness Key

## Session Summary

Forty miles east of Palanthas. The farmland was open and the sightlines were long. Sera spotted the three agents from four hundred feet. They stepped from the treeline without hiding; they had been there four days and they were not pretending otherwise.

The coordinator said they knew Vorn had stood the network down and they hadn't. Thessaly invoked the same stand-down terms she had used at the White Quill entrance. Two agents lowered their weapons before she finished speaking. The coordinator asked what they had been doing it for, then. Calder answered: *"The right problem. The wrong method. The problem is still being solved. Come to Palanthas when the compact is spoken. Hear what it says."* The coordinator stood for a moment. She sheathed her sword: *"Fine."*

Pellan came out of his cottage doorway and watched this happen. He said: *"That's the answer."* He went back inside to get the shard.

In Thornmere, Pellan gave the party the Clause of Forgiveness without ceremony. He had been waiting twenty years; the waiting was over. Thessaly read the inscription: *"Let no covenant hold anger as its foundation."* She said: *"That's the point."* Orik checked the cottage doorframe (craft-witness x5). Calder noted, privately, that he has been thinking about the forty-one less. He did not announce this.

In Pellan's garden: Calder asked about the tomatoes. Pellan said the plant needs to know what it is for. Calder said "Yes." He was thinking about the compact. Thessaly wrote the opening sentence of the new compact in the margin of the reconstruction document. She has been composing it for three sessions. She wrote it now and did not show it to anyone.

Six shards. Seventy-six days.
