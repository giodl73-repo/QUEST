# Wandering Pressure — The Forgiveness Key

## Village pressure (minimal)

Thornmere is a small agricultural village. It has no criminal elements, no faction presence, no dungeon pressure. The encounter happens on the approach road. After the encounter: the village is quiet and the shard acquisition is straightforward.

## Approach Cold Pulse (d6, every 10 minutes on the road to Thornmere)

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | The road passes a farm with a well-maintained fence. Orik notes the joinery on the fence posts — correctly done; someone knew what they were building. He does not say anything. |
| 2 | Sera sees a bird that shouldn't be this far inland. She watches it for a moment. She takes a mental note. |
| 3 | Pine resin smell from a line of pine trees along the road's east shoulder — the same trees the splinter cell is watching from. Orik's hands go still. |
| 4 | Lenne finishes a page in her notebook — the accountability clause draft. She starts a new page without breaking stride. |
| 5 | Calder counts the distance to the horizon and estimates: three miles to Thornmere. He thinks about the forty-one people. He has been thinking about them less lately. He notes the change. |
| 6 | Thessaly is thinking about the compact she is going to speak. She has the seven clauses in Tessamine's reconstruction document; she has Sylaren's amendment; she has Lenne and Vorn's accountability language. She is designing the opening sentence. She does not write it down yet. |
