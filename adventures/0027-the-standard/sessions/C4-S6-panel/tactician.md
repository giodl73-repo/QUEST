# Tactician — C4-S6

The midnight assault produced the campaign's most resource-intensive combat. Caelith's Web failing on two back-to-back high saves is mechanically honest and narratively expensive — his L2 slot went to zero effect in Round 1. Talis's fumbled kill shot in Round 3 extended the combat by at least one round and forced her to draw her shortsword, which she has never done. The enemy Wave 2 Leader surviving on critical HP and then retreating when the reinforcement arrived is correct behavior for a commander making a rational assessment.

Davan's Second Wind + Action Surge combination in Round 2 is the correct fighter response when you are at 21/28 and the wave 2 leader is still up. The combination dealt 13 total damage and pushed the leader to critical. Talis's Colossus Slayer on Soldier 1 (13 total damage) closed the Wave 1 threat cleanly.

The combat ended not through decisive victory but through reinforcement — Irr's reserve arrived and the math became unfavorable for the assault. This is the correct ending for a sustained assault under these conditions.

Score: 74/80 — strong mechanical honesty; Web failure and fumbled kill shot are good combat variance.
