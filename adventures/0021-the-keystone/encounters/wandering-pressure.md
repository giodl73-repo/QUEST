# Wandering Pressure — The Keystone

*The finale has no standard wandering pressure. The building is full of people. Every congregation member, every town representative, every NPC the party has met is present. The "pressure" is the assembled weight of everything.*

*Roll this table once, at a narrative pause, to add texture:*

| d6 | Event |
|---|---|
| 1 | Morren unfolds his current copy and reads the stage he thinks Calla is about to reach. He is slightly ahead. He folds it back. |
| 2 | Mig places the measuring cup at the head of the nave where it belongs, on the shelf where it has been for thirty years. She has carried it here from the Sacristy direction. The cup is at rest. |
| 3 | Gorret descends from the gallery. He stands in the nave with everyone else. He has never been in the nave for the full assembly before. He looks at the keystone. |
| 4 | Aldric's warm copper coin in Renn's pocket has been joined in warmth by the dispensation letter, the ritual focus, and something in the room he cannot identify: Velantha's presence. Four unauthorized acts in one room. |
| 5 | The bell in the tower — the bell that has not rung in thirty years — moves once in a wind. Does not ring. Just moves. |
| 6 | The congregation's morning verse, spoken by no one in particular and everyone simultaneously: *"She came to us in the dark, and she had what we needed, and she gave it."* Once. Then quiet. |
