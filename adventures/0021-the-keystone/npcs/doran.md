# Magistra Doran of Mireth

## Appearance

A woman of approximately 55, town administrator's authority in her posture and her clothes. She came herself. She has been waiting at the building's exterior for approximately two hours. She is not visibly angry; she is visibly finished with patience.

## Public Knowledge

- She hired the party to assess and clear the cathedral site. She wants Route A.
- She has sent two messages; both were acknowledged and nothing changed.
- She came herself because the construction season is ending and she needs an answer.
- She believes in the town's Velantha-miracle story. She helped build it.

## Private Knowledge

- She knows the party has spent six sessions inside the building and has not cleared it. This means either they failed or they found something she didn't expect. She suspects the latter.
- She is prepared to be wrong about the miracle. She has been wrong before. She will not like it, but she is not incapable of absorbing a difficult truth.

## Arc-Completion

**Condition (Route D):** The party presents Doran with the full truth (Velantha's act was unauthorized High Sorcery, not divine miracle; the congregation is real; thirty years of genuine faith) AND a viable alternative (commission a second cathedral on clean ground; cede this one to the congregation).

**The act:** She signs the commission document for the second cathedral. She says: *"The town I know will not like this. I am doing it anyway."*

She does not say she believes it is right. She says she is doing it. This is her arc: she acted against the expectation of the people she serves because she understood something they haven't yet understood. She hired the party to clear a building. She is commissioning a different building. Both acts are her.
