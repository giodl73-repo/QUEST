# Room 05 — Private Consultation

## Read-aloud

> A side passage off the nave. The sounds of the assembly muffled. Space for the party and Doran to speak privately, if they need to.

*(26 words)*

## Features

- **Private space** (if needed for Route D negotiation). Doran may ask for a private conversation before deciding. The party may ask for one. This is the space.

- **The commission document** (Doran has it). A blank commission document for a new cathedral. She brought it. She did not bring it as a concession — she brought it because she was prepared for every outcome. She is a good administrator.

## Encounter

**Doran's private consultation (Route D):** See `encounters/doran-negotiation.md`.

The party presents: the full truth, the evidence (letter, journal, account), and the proposal (commission second cathedral, cede this one). Doran considers. She asks for proof. She gets it. She decides.

If Calla's account has already been spoken when Doran enters this room: the account contained the line "She never asked for a cathedral. She asked for medicine." That line was designed by Aldric thirty years ago and arrived here through Calla's synthesis. Doran heard it. The decision is made.

## Connections

- **West:** R3 (The Nave)

## GM Notes

R5 is optional — Routes A and C don't require a private consultation. Route D may or may not use it depending on whether Doran wants to decide in public or private. Either is valid.
