# Room 03 — The Nave (Assembly)

## Read-aloud

> The full nave. The factions assembled. The keystone through the main arch, visible from every point in the room. Doran and her representatives at the west end. The congregation from east to west. The party between them. Three routes are possible from this room. Only one direction will be taken.

*(51 words)*

## Features

- **The full assembly** (see R2). The session's primary social arena.

- **Three factions:**
  - *The town (Doran):* wants the cathedral cleared and completed.
  - *The congregation:* wants the building, or at least to not be erased.
  - *The party:* has the truth. Must decide what to do with it.

- **Calla's account position** (center of the nave). When Calla speaks, she stands in the center — between Doran and the congregation. The account is addressed to both.

## Encounter

**The Assembly.** See `encounters/the-assembly.md`. The session's central encounter. Three stages: (1) factions state their positions; (2) Calla speaks; (3) the choice.

**Dren's second arc beat:** During the assembly, Veth (or someone from the congregation, or Doran, or Renn) asks Dren: "What are you going to do?" — referring to the campaign's question, not to the keystone specifically. Dren's answer involves the insignia. He names what he is going to do about Aldis's family. This is the second arc beat.

**Maret's morning practice:** Before Calla speaks, Maret performs her three breaths in the center of the nave. The congregation recognizes the practice. No one says anything. The practice has been here since the campaign's first session. It is here now. Maret performs it and stands aside.

## GM Notes

The assembly is the campaign's climax. Everything this adventure has built toward is here.

**Do not rush.** The party has earned this scene through six sessions of careful engagement. Let each NPC have their position stated. Let the factions be present as people, not obstacles. Veth's quiet certainty. Sharr's watchfulness. Morren's folded paper (his current copy). Aldric's ceramic holder. Mig's cup. These are all here.

**The account fires here (Route D).** When Calla speaks, the room is listening. Every person in the assembly has a relationship to what she will say. The session-runner logs the account verbatim.
