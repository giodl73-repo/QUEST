# Map — The Keystone

## Note

The finale is a social architecture, not a dungeon. The six rooms are the spaces where the campaign's decisive events occur. They are not meant to be explored sequentially; the party moves between them as the social encounters develop.

## Spaces

```
[EXTERIOR]──[R1: COURT]──[R2: NAVE ENTRANCE]──[R3: THE NAVE (assembly)]──[R4: THE KEYSTONE ARCH]
                                                      │
                                               [R5: PRIVATE CONSULTATION]
                                               (party + Doran)
                                               
[R6: AFTER] — the building, consecrated or not, when the session ends.
```

## Connections

All connections are open. The finale has no locked doors or faction-gated routes — by this point the congregation trusts the party (Route D) or the relationship has already broken down (Routes A/C). The building is accessible.
