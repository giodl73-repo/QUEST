# Room 02 — Nave Entrance

## Read-aloud

> The factions are assembled. The congregation — all of them, from the gatehouse to the Bell Tower to the nave to the transept — standing in the nave in rows. Aldric Voss near the east wall. Velantha present, if the party invited her. Mig at the front with the cup. Sharr in the back. Morren with a folded piece of paper. Gorret watching from the gallery. The keystone is visible through the main arch: cut stone, blank face.

## Features

- **The full congregation** (all present). For the first time: every section of the building's congregation in one room. Mig (gatehouse), Veth and the nave congregation, Sharr and the Bell Tower congregation, Morren and the Transept members. They are not organized formally. They are simply present.

- **Aldric Voss** (east wall). He has his ceramic holder. He does not speak unless addressed. He is here because Velantha told him to be here, thirty years ago: "If anyone ever comes far enough to ask." They came.

- **Velantha** (present if invited). She is the same as the Crypt: direct, waiting, done performing.

- **Mig** (front, with the measuring cup). She placed her clay cup at the Nave shrine eleven sessions ago. She has the measuring cup from the shelf — she is holding it for the assembly. She does not know she is carrying the most complete expression of what Velantha's faith produced.

- **The keystone** (visible through the main arch). Cut. Blank face. Waiting.

### Memory Fragment
*The first time the congregation was all together was the day after Velantha died. They had not organized it. They were simply in the same building, and they came to the same room because there was nowhere else to go. Thirty years later, they came to the same room again.*
