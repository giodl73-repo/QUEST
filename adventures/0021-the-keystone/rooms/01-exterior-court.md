# Room 01 — Exterior Court

## Read-aloud

> Magistra Doran is at the building's exterior. She came herself. Two town representatives flank her. She says: "I've been waiting two hours. I need an answer today."

*(31 words)*

## Features

- **Doran** (waiting at exterior). She is done with patience. She is not hostile; she is finished. See `npcs/doran.md`.
- **Short rest opportunity** (before the assembly). The party has a few minutes before entering. Renn (19/22 HP) can recover 2 Warlock slots and potentially heal. **DM ruling:** if the party takes 1 minute of preparation before entering, Renn recovers 1d8+2 HP from Constitution (average 6.5 → call it 5 → Renn at 24/22 = capped at 22). Alternatively, Calla uses Healing Word (L1 slot): +1d4+3 = average 5.5 → 22/22 Renn.
- **The building behind them** (visible). The party has been inside this building for six sessions. Seeing it from outside, at the finale, is the session's first atmospheric beat.

## Encounter

**Doran's initial conversation** (brief). She wants to know: cleared or not? The party's answer leads to either immediate Route A (cleared) or the assembly (everything else). See `encounters/doran-negotiation.md`.

## Connections

- **East:** R2 (Nave Entrance) — always open

## GM Notes

R1 is the party's last private moment before everything is assembled. They have two minutes with Doran before the congregation, Aldric, and Velantha are in the same room.

**Dren's note:** He has Aldis's insignia in his breast pocket. He is about to enter a building where the question of coming when asked has been given six sessions of context. His second arc beat will fire inside.
