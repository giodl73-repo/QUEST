# Creston Ford Ambush

## Setup

8 Sorn fighters (2 roles) vs. party of 4 (L3) + unit soldiers (d8 or d6 result).

## Enemy composition

**Sorn Fighters (×4, in ford):** Thug stat block (MM p.350). AC 11, HP 32, Speed 30. Multiattack (2 attacks). Shortsword +4, 1d6+2. Pack Tactics (advantage if ally adjacent). Positioned in ford, difficult terrain impairs everyone equally.

**Sorn Archers (×2, south bank):** Bandit stat block (MM p.343). AC 12, HP 11, Light crossbow +3, 1d8+1. Range 80/320. Three-quarters cover from mule-post (+5 AC, Dex saves). Targeting visible party members — **Lenne is a priority target** (lighter armor, marked as medic by kit visible on pack).

**Sorn Flankers (×2, north bank):** Veterans (MM p.350). AC 17, HP 58. Longsword +5, 1d8+3. Parry reaction. Move from concealment on Round 2 — they were in the tree-line. Party has no warning until Round 2 when they close from behind.

## CR / XP Budget

- 4 Thugs (CR 1/2, 100 XP) = 400 XP
- 2 Bandits (CR 1/8, 25 XP) = 50 XP
- 2 Veterans (CR 3, 700 XP) = 1400 XP
- **Total:** 1850 XP base
- Party of 4 L3: Deadly threshold ~1600 XP
- Adjusted for Veterans flanking (surprise Round 2): **Deadly**. This is intentional — the hardest combat.
- **Unit soldiers help:** d8 result ≈ 4-8 soldiers. Each at HP 11, attack +3. If 6 soldiers: adds ~180 XP equivalent of actions per round. Shifts to Hard.

## Action economy notes

Veterans (HP 58, AC 17) are the real threat. They arrive Round 2. If Dorn uses Action Surge in Round 2 on a Veteran, it shifts the action economy significantly.

**Carra's incapacitation:** One of Dorn's unit bond soldiers (Carra) is targeted by a Veteran in Round 2. The Veteran rolls a hit on Carra (HP 11 soldier): automatic 0 HP. Dorn's Brace reaction triggers. Brace: "When a creature you can see moves into your reach, use your reaction to make one melee attack against it." Carra is already at 0 HP when Brace fires — Dorn attacks the Veteran who took her down.

**Lenne's arc moment:** She calls Fel's position aloud in Round 2 when the flankers close: "Fel — left flank — go wide." Heuristic: watches Fel in formation. She knows where he is.

## Escape option

If the party is at risk of TPK (all PCs below 10 HP by Round 4): the Sorn fighters are not trying to kill — they're trying to retrieve the field journal. If Varet offers the journal, they pause. Combat can end through this exchange. Varet does not give up the journal. But the DM should note this as an available beat if the session is going badly.

## Resolution

- 2-3 Sorn fighters fled or incapacitated.
- 1 Sorn fighter captured (Arren Vole holds him at the bank).
- Carra: 0 HP, stable (Spare the Dying, Lenne).
- Cohesion: degrades one die size.

## Post-combat: Captured fighter speaks

He was hired. He knows one name: Drev Ossian. "He's the one who pays Sorn. I don't know more than that." He is telling the truth — he was a hired blade, not a confidant.

**H4 full delivery:** Drev Ossian (Istaran trade factor, workshop-canon) is the origin of the blackmail chain.
