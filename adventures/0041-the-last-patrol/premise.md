# 0041 — The Last Patrol

## The adventure in one sentence

The unit's final patrol before the reckoning — hardest combat, lowest cohesion, H4 fully delivered — and every PC fires a major beat as the ensemble carries the session together.

## Setting

Day 6. The unit goes out on what should be a standard patrol. It is not. Sorn's men are waiting at Creston Ford in force — 8 fighters, coordinated, clearly briefed on the patrol schedule. The unit fights. Cohesion degrades. Soldiers die.

After the combat, in the quiet that follows, a surviving Sorn fighter reveals the final inversion: Sorn himself is being coerced. There is a document in the Keep's record-room that proves it. If Varet can get it, the chain of blackmail runs all the way up — and the person at the top is not a military officer. It's a trade factor from Istar.

## Hook

The ambush is not subtle — it is designed to end the patrol permanently. Sorn's men have specific orders: take the documents, neutralize the unit. They know Varet has a field journal. They have orders to retrieve it.

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d10 or d8 → degrades to d6 this session

## Scenes

1. **Into the ford** — ambush at Creston Ford. 8 Sorn fighters, coordinated.
2. **Combat** — hardest fight of the campaign. Multiple soldiers fall. At least one named unit soldier (one of Dorn's three — Carra, Fest, or Oln) is incapacitated.
3. **After the fight** — one Sorn fighter captured. He is the one Arren held when others fell. He talks.
4. **H4 full delivery** — the captured fighter explains Sorn's position: Sorn is being blackmailed by a trade factor named Drev Ossian (workshop-canon) who operates through the Istaran network. The chain goes: Ossian → Sorn → Veth → unit.
5. **The record-room** — document in Thorngate Keep's record-room proves Ossian's connection. Varet needs it for the tribunal. This is the session's close and the hook for S7.

## Cohesion degradation

**Cohesion d8 or d10 → d6.** At least one named unit soldier incapacitated (Carra, Fest, or Oln — Dorn's unit bond). The die degrades because a unit member is lost in combat.

**If already at d6:** degradation to d4. The unit is nearly spent.

## Spotlight

Ensemble. Every PC fires a major beat:
- **Varet:** Lay on Hands used fully (for unit soldiers, not herself). Turn the Tide reaction fires.
- **Lenne:** Preserve Life channel divinity. Spare the Dying on a fallen soldier. She calls Fel's position aloud.
- **Taris:** Hunter's Mark on lead fighter. Favored Terrain: mountain advantage fires.
- **Dorn:** Action Surge. He sees Carra go down. He uses Action Surge for the line — not for efficiency; for the contract.

## NPC

- **Carra** (one of Dorn's three unit bond soldiers) — goes down in combat. Stable, alive, but out of the fight. Dorn's Brace reaction fires when she takes the hit.
- **The captured Sorn fighter** — H4 delivery mechanism. Not a major NPC. A man who knows one thing and is scared enough to say it.
