## Combat zone detail (Room 02)

- **Ford:** 40 ft across, difficult terrain (costs 2 ft per ft). Creatures at 0 HP in water: DC 10 Con or drowning begins.
- **North bank rock shelf:** half cover for PCs advancing.
- **South bank mule-post cover:** three-quarters cover for archers there.
- **Sorn flankers (north bank):** positioned behind party when combat opens — they close from behind on round 2.

**Tactical priority:**
1. Flankers on north bank: must be turned or they get free attacks from behind.
2. Archers on south bank: targeting Lenne (she's in the lighter armor, visible as medic).
3. Fighters in ford: standard melee threat, but difficult terrain slows approach.

This is designed to be the campaign's hardest tactical encounter — three threat vectors simultaneously.
