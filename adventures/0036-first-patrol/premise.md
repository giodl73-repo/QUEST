# 0036 — First Patrol

## The adventure in one sentence

The unit's opening patrol of the Irenvale Crossing goes cleanly — four route waypoints, one brief skirmish — but Sergeant Arren Vole notices something that doesn't make it into the formal after-action report.

## Setting

**Irenvale Crossing** — a mountain pass 40 miles south of Thorngate Keep (workshop-canon, Kharolis border region, PC 188). The crossing is a trade route that command has classified as a "border irregularity corridor." The unit's orders are to patrol, observe, and report. Three waypoints on day one: the Ridgehead Marker, the Burned Waystation, and the Creston Ford. At Creston Ford the patrol encounters a small smuggler's party — 4 men, 2 mules, unlicensed goods.

The unit has 12 soldiers. Sergeant Arren Vole is at the head of the file. The PCs ride at the command position.

## Hook

This is a first patrol. Standard assignment. Varet has her orders. They are clear. The first entry in the field journal reads: *"PC 188, Day 1 patrol, Irenvale. Weather: overcast, passable. Route: Ridgehead–Waystation–Creston. Incident: one contact, four irregulars, goods confiscated. Outcome: clean."*

The last line of the orders — *"confiscated goods to be held at staging cache, coordinates attached"* — has coordinates Taris does not recognize.

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d12

## Scenes

1. **Briefing at Thorngate Keep** — Varet receives orders from Captain Osren Veth (workshop-canon). The coordinates are in the order. No anomaly is raised.
2. **The Ridgehead Marker** — nothing. The marker exists. Taris notes the condition of the trail.
3. **The Burned Waystation** — evidence of a fire, several months old. Not logged anywhere. Arren Vole asks who reported it.
4. **The Creston Ford Encounter** — four smugglers, two mules, resisting confiscation. Brief combat. Goods confiscated.
5. **End-of-patrol staging** — goods delivered to the cache coordinates. The cache is a well-supplied permanent structure. Lenne notes: it is stocked with more than the unit brought.

## Hint placement

- **H1 seed (pre-plant):** The cache coordinates in the written orders. Taris's map doesn't have them. The structure is there. It is not new.

## NPC

- **Sergeant Arren Vole** — appears in formation, speaks once (about the Waystation fire), does not raise it formally.

## Research notes

Baseline session. RV6 test begins: does Varet write something in the journal beyond the minimum? The discrepancy column is empty. The question is whether she writes anything in the unmarked margins.
