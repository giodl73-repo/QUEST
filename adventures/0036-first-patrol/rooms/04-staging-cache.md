# Room 04 — Staging Cache

## Read-aloud

> The cache coordinates lead to a structure set back from the trail in a shallow hollow, screened by a stand of pine. Stone walls, solid roof, iron-banded door, and a padlock. The key is on a cord at the bottom of Varet's written orders — she finds it only because she looks. Inside: three rows of supply shelves, half full. Military-grade provisions, medical stores, equipment. More than this unit carried out of Thorngate. More than any single patrol would have brought.

## DM notes

- **Lenne's Medicine or Investigation DC 12:** the medical stores include injury supplies for more casualties than a standard patrol would expect. Triage kits. Tourniquet sets. Three times the standard allocation.
- **Taris's Investigation DC 13:** the supply manifest is pinned to the inside of the door. The most recent entry is three days ago. A different hand from the unit's manifest.
- **Investigation DC 15 (any PC):** a second small ledger is tucked behind the shelf brackets. It records incoming goods by date, quantity, and origin: "CF ford," "RH toll," "BW burn." CF = Creston Ford. RH = Ridgehead. BW = the Burned Waystation.
  - **Passive Perception 15:** the ledger exists even without the active check — the shelf brackets are visibly thicker on one side; a trained eye notes it.
- **Anchor-level symptom:** The second ledger. If found, it is the H1 confirmation: this cache receives goods from the patrol route. The unit's confiscations are part of the supply cycle.
- **v1.3 fallback:** (a) Passive Perception 15 for ledger; (b) the ledger is here again in S2 if missed (positional redundancy); (c) Arren Vole can remark on the supply volume in S2 (NPC recall).

## What this room establishes

The cache is part of an operational supply system, not an emergency reserve. The unit's patrol route feeds it. This is the H1 confirmation if the party looks.

## Exits

North: trail to Creston Ford (2 miles).
Return: Thorngate Keep via full route.
