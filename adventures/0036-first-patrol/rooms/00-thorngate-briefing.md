# Room 00 — Thorngate Keep Briefing

## Read-aloud

> Captain Osren Veth is a compact man, precise in his speech, with the look of someone who has read the same reports so many times that the words are furniture. He hands Varet a sealed packet without ceremony. "Standard patrol. Ridgehead, Waystation, Creston. Any contact — irregulars, traders off-route, anything — confiscate goods and hold at the staging cache. Coordinates and key are in the packet. You're back by nightfall." He does not wait for questions.

## DM notes

- Varet may ask questions. Veth will answer two:
  - "What are we expecting at Creston?" — "Border irregulars. Smugglers. The usual."
  - "What's at the staging cache?" — "Forward supply point. Your confiscations go there. Keeps the keep's inventory clean."
  - Third question: Veth's expression does not change, but he says: "The orders are clear, Commander." That is the end of the briefing.
- **Arren Vole** is in the courtyard when Varet emerges. He reads her expression without comment. He says: "Ready when you are, Commander." This is the first time the party hears his voice.
- The key at the bottom of the packet: small, iron, on a cord. It fits the staging cache lock. Varet finds it when she opens the orders fully — it falls out of the folded paper onto the floor.

## What this room establishes

Chain of command is clear. Captain Veth exists. The orders are specific. The key's placement (at the bottom, like an afterthought) is the first small wrong note.

## Exits

South: patrol route begins.
