# Room 03 — Creston Ford

## Read-aloud

> The ford is wide here — forty feet of flat water, knee-deep, running clear over gray stone. On the far bank two mules are tied to a root-post, loaded with canvas-wrapped bundles. Four men. One on this bank with a short blade already drawn. One up on the slope behind him, crossbow visible. Two at the mules, hands moving. They see you at the same moment you see them.

## Combat encounter

**Enemies:** 4 Border Smugglers (use Bandit stat block, MM p.343)
- Near bank: 1 smuggler (melee, blade, AC 12, HP 11)
- Slope: 1 crossbowman (range, AC 12, HP 11, +3 to hit, 1d8+1 piercing)
- Far bank: 2 near mules (melee fallback, AC 12, HP 11 each)

**Terrain:**
- Ford: difficult terrain, half movement. Any creature at 0 HP in the ford begins drowning if not stabilized.
- Rock shelf near bank: half cover (+2 AC, Dex saves)
- Mule baggage far bank: three-quarters cover (+5 AC, Dex saves) for adjacent creatures

**Unit soldiers (d12 = 12):** 4 soldiers can be directed into the ford to assist. Each acts on Varet's command (initiative 10, attack +3, 1d6+1).

**Tactical note:** The crossbowman is the priority target. If the near-bank smuggler is engaged first, the crossbowman fires freely. Taris's Hunter's Mark should go on the crossbowman.

## Resolution options

- Full subdual: PCs can attempt to grapple/restrain rather than kill. DC 13 Athletics.
- Full combat: standard. The smugglers fight for 2 rounds and then one attempts to flee east (into the hills, not toward any settlement).
- Parley: DC 15 Persuasion. The near-bank smuggler is scared, not committed. He will answer one question before his companions force his silence.

## If the smuggler answers

*"We're not irregulars. We pay the toll. We always pay the toll. Ask at the staging cache."*

He does not know what the staging cache is called. He means the structure at the coordinates in Varet's orders. He has been paying into it for two years.

## What this room establishes

The smugglers pay a toll. There is a toll system operating on this route that is not in the formal record. The goods confiscated here go to the cache where the toll is collected.

## Exits

West: trail back toward Burned Waystation.
East: hills (no patrol objective; fleeing smuggler route).
South: Staging Cache (2 miles).
