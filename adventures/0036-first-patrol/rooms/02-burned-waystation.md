# Room 02 — Burned Waystation

## Read-aloud

> The waystation was a four-wall structure once, stone base, timber walls and roof. The roof is gone. The walls stand to waist height where they haven't collapsed entirely. The fire was months ago — the char is weathered, lichen starting at the base stones. The floor inside is ash-black. There were supplies here once. The storage shelves are metal frames, still standing, empty.

## DM notes

- Arren Vole speaks unprompted: *"Who reported this? This isn't on the patrol log."* He waits. Nobody answers. He does not pursue it.
- Investigation DC 14: the structure was not burned by accident. The fire accelerant was applied from inside — scorch marks go outward from a central point. Someone burned what was stored here and left.
- History DC 12: the waystation was on older patrol maps as a resupply point. It was removed from current maps three years ago with notation "decommissioned." No fire incident recorded.
- **Anchor-level symptom:** The gap between the decommission record and the burn evidence. Nobody reported the fire. The waystation was removed from maps before it burned.
- **Passive Perception fallback 14:** the storage frames are military-spec — same manufacturer as the staging cache the unit will visit at end of patrol. The forge-mark is the same.

## What this room establishes

The waystation was part of a supply system. Something changed three years ago. The burn was deliberate. The gap between fact and record is the first discrepancy Varet doesn't have words for yet.

## NPC presence

Sergeant Arren Vole asks his question here. He will not ask it again. His asking is the arc seed.
