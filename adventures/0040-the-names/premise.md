# 0040 — The Names

## The adventure in one sentence

The unit learns who is being protected and why — not a corrupt commander, but a blackmailed one — and Varet writes the most important discrepancy entry yet.

## Setting

Day 5. Captain Veth calls Varet in privately. He tells her things he should not tell her. He tells her because Dorn's contract reading and the formal objection have put him in a position where silence is more dangerous than speech. He tells her: Lord Herath Sorn has documentation proving Veth accepted a payment seven years ago. He has been compliant since. He does not know who Sorn works for. He was told the unit was assigned because they were expendable — because Varet's record includes a dismissed complaint from three years ago (workshop-canon: she filed a report that was suppressed), which makes her look manageable.

Varet closes her journal. She opens it. She writes.

## Hook

Veth is not the villain. He is the person who got trapped. He is telling Varet because he is done. The payment documentation exists. The complaint record exists. The unit being "expendable" is not a metaphor — there is an order in Sorn's correspondence that references the unit as a "temporary asset."

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d10 or d8 (depending on S4 outcome)

## Scenes

1. **Veth calls Varet in** — private meeting, no witnesses (except Arren Vole, standing outside, who hears nothing and says nothing until asked).
2. **Veth's disclosure** — H3 delivered (unit chosen because they were expected to fail/disappear) and H4 planted (Veth is being blackmailed, not corrupt).
3. **Varet writes** — extended journal scene. The party is present for this in abstract — they see Varet writing. They do not see the entries. They wait.
4. **Taris shows the map** — after Varet emerges from the private meeting, Taris shows her the full map: routes, cache, toll marks, the east valley holdout site, the Sorn coordinates. He has been making it legible for this moment. He does not explain it. He shows it.
5. **Lenne hands over the medical log** — "These are the ones I couldn't explain with the briefing I was given." Pre-arc-completion beat.

## Hint placement

- **H3 delivered (full):** The unit was chosen because it was expected to fail or disappear. Varet's complaint record was the selection criterion.
- **H4 planted (partial):** Veth is being blackmailed. The full H4 inversion (Sorn is also under pressure from above) comes in S6.

## Spotlight

Commander Varet Senn. The journal is the mechanism. This is the session where the discrepancy column has more entries than the outcome column. The ratio inverts. Her arc fires at the next session — but this session is the accumulation completing.

## NPC

- **Captain Veth** — discloses. His arc fires here.
- **Arren Vole** — at the door. When Varet emerges, he reads her face. He says: "I'll be ready when you are." Not a question.
