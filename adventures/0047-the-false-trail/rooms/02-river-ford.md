# Room 02 — River Ford / Weighing Station

## Read-aloud

> The weighing station is a low shelter with a pole-and-rope toll gate across the ford. Two men at the station; one more at the rope. On the far bank, a fourth man. The man running the operation — sitting at the table, writing in a ledger — looks up when you approach. He recognizes Brek before Brek has spoken.

## DM notes

- **Torven Aldric:** See npcs/torven-aldric.md. He knows why Brek is here. He is not surprised. He has been expecting this for three years.
- **Opening:** Torven does not pretend. He says: *"Ashfall. I wondered when the road would bring you."* He puts down his pen. He does not reach for a weapon.
- **His offer:** He has documentation connecting his operation to Evren Doss's broader network — records that would serve as evidence in Cassel's investigation. He is prepared to offer this in exchange for Brek agreeing to take the money (the financial value of the mill contract) and leaving him operational. He is not asking for mercy — he is negotiating.
- **Brek's decision options:**
  1. **Take the money, leave** — the financial debt is resolved. The wrong his father suffered is compensated. Torven continues operating. Brek's flaw: he assumed force was the answer; this is the evidence there's another one.
  2. **Take the money and the documents** — gives Cassel what he needs; Torven's operation ends via legal route. Requires Torven to hand over both. He will do this if he believes the alternative is worse.
  3. **Violence** — possible. Torven has 4 armed men. This is a Hard combat (medium threat). Brek can handle it, but the others would need to fight. If they fight for Brek without him asking: log as Reciprocal Acts.
  4. **Walk away with nothing** — valid. Not satisfying. What the dice and heuristics produce.
- **Tam's reading:** He watches Torven during the negotiation. Insight DC 12: Torven is genuinely afraid — not of Brek specifically, but of the investigation closing in. The offer is real. He is trying to buy his way out of both problems at once.

## Exits
