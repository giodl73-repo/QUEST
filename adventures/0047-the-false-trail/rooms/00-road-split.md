# Room 00 — The Road Split

## Read-aloud

> The road forks. Left: a well-worn track with fresh wheel-ruts, heading south toward Stonehaven. Right: a narrower track, less traveled, heading southwest toward a river crossing. Vess says the left fork is the Stonehaven trade road. She knows this road. She has been through it before.

> Brek takes the right fork.

## DM notes

- **Brek's choice:** No discussion, no announcement. He takes the right fork. Per his heuristic: works out what he thinks while doing the thing, not before. Talking first feels like stalling.
- **The others have three options:**
  - Follow without asking (strong relationship signal — log immediately)
  - Ask before following (weaker but still a follow)
  - Stay on the left fork (do not follow; this is the alternative RV9 data)
- **Research note:** The DM does not prompt following. If the others follow, it is because of three sessions of operational familiarity. This is the PRIMARY RV9 TEST measurement point. Log trigger type for each following PC.
  - Vess: does she follow someone going off her planned route? Her heuristic is about professional commitment to the delivery. Following Brek is not on her contract.
  - Tam: his heuristic is social fluency and paying attention to what people don't say. He has been watching Brek since the market district in Greyford. He follows.
  - Sera: she was going to the Millhaven archive. Millhaven is not down this fork. Does she follow?
- **If nobody follows:** Brek continues alone. The DM has Tam call after him: *"I know that crest too now. Where are you going?"* This creates a dialogue opening. Brek stops. He turns. He says: *"Wages owed."* It is the deflection he has been using. Tam waits.

## Exits

Right: right fork approach (Room 01).
