# Room 01 — Right Fork Approach

## Read-aloud

> The right fork narrows to a track through scrub oak. A quarter-mile in, the river is audible. There is no other traffic on this track. The weighing station is visible at the ford — a low structure with a rope across the water. Three men visible from here.

## DM notes

- **H1 and H3 disclosure scene.** If the others followed: this is where Brek tells them what is actually happening. He does not plan a speech. He is walking and he says it.
- **Brek's H1 disclosure:**
  - *"The money isn't wages. There's a man at that station who took something from my family. I'm collecting what he owes."*
  - If asked what he took: *"He bought a contract on a mill my father owned. The contract was fraudulent. My father is dead. The mill is gone. The money would have kept him through the winter."*
  - He does not deliver this with emotion. He states it as he would state the survey findings from the roadblock. It is information. He has been waiting until it was relevant.
- **H3 connection (if Vess is present and the package dispute is known):**
  - Vess: passive 16 Perception. She looks at the station, looks at the toll-rope, looks at the crest on the station's post. Same crest as the Greyford Warden's documents. She says: *"The man who sent my package runs through here."*
  - Brek: *"Evren Doss?"* (He does not know the name. But Doss is the upstream name; Torven is the local operation.)
  - If Tam connects the dots: *"Cassel said the road between Doss and the delivery was what he was checking. This is that road."*
  - H3 fires: the package situation and Brek's personal debt are part of the same network. Neither knew.
- **Vess's position:** She was going to deliver a package through a network that turns out to be the same one that wronged Brek's family. Her professional commitment to the delivery is now uncomfortable. She has not opened the package. She does not know what is in it. She is standing at the edge of knowing.

## Exits

South: River Ford (Room 02).
North: Road split (Room 00) — retreat option.
