Key:
- `[00]` Road approach — where travelers first encounter the blockage
- `[01]` Rubble survey point — east face has one climbable angle (Athletics DC 12)
- `[02]` Passage area — where clearing work happens; dangerous stone (Room 03)
- `[03]` South road — clear once passage is opened; camp site visible 200 yards
- `###` Rubble — impassable without work

**Canyon walls:** 20 feet high on both sides; no climbing route around without 1+ hour of rope work. The road is the only option.

**Shifting stone:** One large limestone slab in the passage area (Room 02), leaning against the canyon wall. If disturbed from the wrong angle, it falls toward whoever is in the passage. Athletics DC 14 to secure it properly; DC 10 to Dodge out of the way (DEX save).
