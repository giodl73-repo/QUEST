# Room 00 — Road Approach

## Read-aloud

> The road bends into a short canyon — limestone walls on both sides, forty feet high, no purchase for climbing. Twenty yards ahead, the road is gone. A shelf of rock has come down, filling the canyon mouth with rubble. It is not dramatic. It is simply there, and there is nowhere else to go.

## DM notes

- Each PC arrives within 15 minutes of the others. They arrive as individuals, not as a group. No introductions are required; the blockage is the introduction.
- Carter Dolen arrives from the north with his wagon just as the third PC arrives. His mule will not approach the rubble. He sets the brake and sits.
- Merchant Havel is already there — arrived an hour earlier. He is sitting on a stone eating bread. He has assessed the situation and decided to wait.
- First NPC dialogue: Havel, without looking up: *"It came down in the night. I walked the full width. You cannot go around."*
- PCs may ask each other questions. The session does not require introductions; if PCs do not introduce themselves, that is fine — log it as baseline social data.

## What this room establishes

Four strangers at a dead end. The situation is the same for all of them. The first cooperation opportunity is the survey (Room 01).

## Exits

East: rubble face (surveyable, impassable).
