# Room 03 — South Road

## Read-aloud

> The passage is three feet wide at the narrowest — enough for a person, not a horse. Carter Dolen's wagon will not fit; he will need to unload and carry. The south road beyond is clear. Two hundred yards ahead there is a flat section with a firepit ring, old ash in it.

## DM notes

- The road is open. The four strangers are standing at the south end of the passage. There is no reason to stay together — except they're going the same direction and it is two hours until dark.
- **Nightfall decision:** The DM does not prompt this. Let the PCs decide what to do. If nobody speaks: Tam will say, *"There's a camp site ahead. Two hours to dark. I'm going there."* He does not invite anyone. He states his plan.
- If PCs camp together: log as social baseline. No ceremony needed.
- If PCs camp separately despite it being obviously sensible to camp together: log this too — it is valid baseline data.
- **Merchant Havel** will ask where they're headed before they separate. If asked in return, he says: *"Stonehaven. Same as most of you, I'd expect."* He is not alarmed if they're going to different places. He is gauging the road traffic, not them specifically.
  - **DM note:** Havel's Stonehaven comment may interest Brek. Brek has not told anyone he is going to Stonehaven. His heuristic is to not explain. Log whether he reacts or stays silent — this is H1 data.
- Carter Dolen passes through and continues south without stopping. He waves.

## What this room establishes

The road is clear. The four are not a party. They happen to be in the same place. Session ends here.

## Session close beat

If anyone is sitting at the firepit, Vess eats without speaking, watching the south road. She shifts position twice — first at the edge of firelight, then slightly further back. She does not explain. This is her act-without-announcement instance: she is maintaining watch without announcing she is maintaining watch.

## Exits

South: The road continues.
