# 0039 — The Wrong Order

## The adventure in one sentence

Orders arrive to relocate all confiscated goods from the staging cache to a new location — the coordinates are outside the patrol zone, in civilian territory — and Dorn reads the relevant clause of his contract aloud in the presence of an officer.

## Setting

Day 4. New orders: transport everything in the staging cache to a second location, coordinates attached, by sundown. The unit loads the mules. Taris checks the coordinates. The destination is a private estate — Lord Herath Sorn's factor-house, 8 miles west. Not a military installation. Not a garrison. A civilian building in a private estate.

## Hook

Transporting military-confiscated goods to a private civilian estate is not an irregularity. It is a violation — of Dorn's contract (Clause 4: no military resources to civilian private custody without a tribunal order) and of Solamnic military law. Dorn knows. He reads it.

## Unit Cohesion Die at start: d10

## Scenes

1. **Briefing with Veth** — orders delivered. Transportation order. New coordinates. Veth does not explain.
2. **Cache loading** — unit loads mules. Taris checks coordinates. He shows Varet the map: the destination is civilian.
3. **On the road** — 4 miles toward Sorn estate. A mounted officer (Lieutenant Cael Ossren, workshop-canon) intercepts to "supervise the transfer."
4. **The confrontation** — Ossren is present when Dorn reads his contract. Ossren does not have the authority to override the clause. The scene turns on this.
5. **Resolution** — Party decides: comply (Cohesion d10→d8 if forced), refuse outright (Cohesion d10→d8 if ordered and defied), or find a middle path.

## Cohesion degradation trigger

**If the party defies the transport order, Cohesion d10→d8.** If they comply fully, Cohesion holds (d10). The middle path (partial compliance, formal objection logged) is a third option that holds Cohesion and files a discrepancy entry.

## Hint placement

- **H2 confirmation:** Ossren references "the arrangement" when he thinks no PC can hear. Taris hears it. He does not react. He marks his map.
- **H3 seed:** Ossren says to Dorn: "This unit was assigned to this route for a reason. You should think about what that reason is."

## Spotlight

Veteran Dorn Ashfeld. This is his arc. He reads the clause. That is the session's climactic beat.

## NPC

- **Lieutenant Cael Ossren** (workshop-canon) — young officer, nervous, carrying orders he doesn't fully understand. Not corrupt. A messenger. His arc fires when he realizes he has been used.
- **Arren Vole** — present. Silent until Dorn reads. Then: "That's the contract, Lieutenant."
