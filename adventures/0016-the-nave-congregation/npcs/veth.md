# Veth, the Keeper

## Appearance

Older male gully dwarf, approximately 55 years (old for his kind). Compact. His hands are calloused from the morning sweep — a specific callous pattern on the right palm from holding the same broom handle the same way for twenty-two years. He moves through the nave without looking where he places his feet. He knows every stone.

## Stat Block

Non-combatant. HP 8, AC 10, Speed 20 ft. He does not fight. If directly threatened, he will place himself in front of the measuring cup and not move. The congregation will defend him.

## Public Knowledge

- He has been the daily keeper of the nave for twenty-two years.
- The morning rite has seven steps. He knows them in sequence; he learned them from the previous keeper, who learned them from Dova.
- The measuring cup has been at the head of the nave for thirty years. He has used it every morning for twenty-two. It always holds the right amount.
- Velantha came here thirty years ago. He was not alive. He knows the story through the rite and the oral transmission.
- There are congregation members who are not peaceful. They are in the tower. He says this without judgment.
- There is a man in the sacristy who writes. He has been there longer than most. He does not join the rite.

## Private Knowledge

- He suspects the measuring cup and the field satchel are connected — he noticed the satchel from the gatehouse when it came through the inner door with Mig, and felt something similar to what he feels holding the cup. He has not shared this with anyone. He does not have the vocabulary for it.
- He knows the rite's seven steps are being imperfectly transmitted. He knows the practitioners in R4 are getting it wrong. He has not corrected them because he is not certain his version is entirely right either — three generations of oral transmission. He holds this doubt privately.

## Information Readiness Condition

Veth holds two key seeds: the orders-man seed and the aggrieved-faction seed. He will give them when:

- **Orders-man seed:** The party asks about the congregation's full composition — who is here, where does everyone live in the building. He answers: *"Most of us are in the nave. Some are in the tower. One stays in the sacristy. He came later than most. He writes."*
- **Aggrieved-faction seed:** The party asks why the congregation is divided or whether everyone is content here. He answers: *"Some of us came here angry. Some are still angry. They are in the tower."*

The condition for both: the party must ask directly. Veth does not volunteer this information. He is not concealing it — he simply does not offer what is not asked.

## Behavior Matrix

| Party action | Veth's response |
|---|---|
| Party enters | He says: "You came through. Good. Come in." |
| Party asks what he is doing | He describes the morning rite. Seven steps. He is specific. |
| Party asks "what are you trying to understand?" back at him | He is quiet for a moment. Then: "Whether the practice is what she intended." He does not explain further. |
| Party participates in the morning rite | He watches them. At the end, he brings them to the cup. |
| Party asks what the cup was *for* | He brings them to R4 directly. He holds the cup in front of whoever asked. |
| Party asks about the congregation's composition | He gives the orders-man seed (see above). |
| Party behaves disrespectfully | He stops moving. He looks at them. He says: "Why did you come here?" He waits for an answer. |
| Party moves toward R5 without asking | He watches. Gorret will handle R5; Veth lets this play out unless Gorret is overwhelmed. |

## Arc-Completion

**Condition:** The party asks Veth what he is trying to understand — specifically the second-level question, not just "what do you do" but "what do you want to know."

**The act:** He says: *"Whether the practice is what she intended. I didn't know her. I learned from someone who learned from someone who knew her. I don't know if I am doing it right. I do it anyway."*

He does not say this as a confession or a breakdown. He says it the way you say a thing you have thought about for twenty-two years and have not said aloud because there was no one to say it to. The arc completes when he says it. He returns to the morning preparations immediately after.

**Information readiness note:** This arc-completion also unlocks additional information: Veth will tell the party that the elder in the Transept (deeper in) was present the night Velantha performed the ritual. *"He knows what actually happened. I know what was passed down. They are not the same."*
