## Questions Raised About the Module or the System

1. **Gorret's private rite (Wandering Encounter result 3) fires before his R5 confrontation.** If the party witnesses Gorret's private devotion at the cup before his confrontation, his question reads differently. Note in the module: the wandering pressure result 3 should be flagged as potentially informing the R5 confrontation — if it fires first, Dren's honest answer is enriched.

2. **The debris blocking R6 east passage.** Improviser identified this correctly. The module does not specify what the debris is (construction material, congregation-placed, or something else). Design the answer and put it in R6's GM Notes for the next module revision.

3. **Veth's arc-completion information disclosure.** The additional information (*"the elder in the Transept was there"*) was in Veth's readiness conditions but not his arc section. Future npc-architect work should explicitly link arc-completion to information readiness: "when the arc fires, also deliver [specific information]."
