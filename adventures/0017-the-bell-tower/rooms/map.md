## Scale note

The Bell Tower is approximately 12 ft square internally at each level, narrowing slightly toward the top. Ceiling heights: R1/R2/R3 ~10 ft. R5 (Bell Rope Chamber) ~15 ft (to accommodate the rope shaft). R6 (Bell Platform) open sky.

| NPC/Group | Location | Notes |
|---|---|---|

| **4 congregation members** | R2 (first landing) | Initial contact; not immediately hostile |
| **Sharr** | R3 (second landing) | Bell Tower leader; promise-cord on left wrist; moves to R2 if warned |
| **3 aggrieved members (combatants)** | R3 (with Sharr) | Dangerous; follow Sharr's lead |
| **3 members** | R5 (bell rope chamber) | More recently arrived; perform the braiding rite |

*Sharr repositions on return visit:* If the party was hostile but not victorious, Sharr moves to R5 and positions at the ladder base. If the party handled S2 socially and returned honestly, Sharr descends to R2 to meet them.
