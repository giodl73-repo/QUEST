## Questions Raised

1. **Hint 2 recovery path:** The ritual focus in 0018 provides the mechanical confirmation of what Velantha used. The elder's account of the ritual will also deliver Hint 2's content. One of these two delivery mechanisms should be auto-delivered (passive check rather than active roll) so a second miss doesn't strand the hint. Recommend: elder's account delivers Hint 2 at passive DC = active DC + 2, per v1.2/v1.3 guidance.

2. **Faerie Fire pattern:** Three sessions, Calla has tried concentration spells, all failed at or above DC. She needs a strategic adjustment or a backup option designed into 0018's prep.

3. **Dren's bilateral arc:** Two PC sheets list the bilateral condition. The bilateral fired. Both PCs named their people. Is the arc complete? The Roleplayer says no (second beat needed); the module design says yes (the campaign-permanent fact is the naming). Recommended: treat the naming as the arc's first completion; the insignia's disposition is a bonus beat the campaign rewards if it emerges.
