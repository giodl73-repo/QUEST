# Sera Windholt

**Class:** Wizard (School of Abjuration) Level 3  
**Race:** Human  
**Background:** Sage  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good

---

## Appearance

Mid-thirties, brown hair pinned back practically. She carries a leather satchel that's clearly organized — pockets by category. She writes things down while they're happening. She's not unfriendly; she's just always a little distracted by whatever she's noting.

---

## Background

A traveling scholar documenting road conditions and settlements for a Palanthan library. This road is on her assigned route. She's been doing this work for three years; it pays enough and it keeps her moving.

**Why she's on this road:** She documents roads. This road needs documenting.

**Motivation:** Reach the library archive at Millhaven before the autumn closure.

---

## Secret

She's not documenting for a library. The documentation is cover. She's looking for a specific person — someone who was last reported on this road, in this region. The documentation gives her a reason to ask questions without explaining why she's asking.

*H2 seed: Sera has met someone involved in the road situation before, or recognizes something about it. Her documentation gives her more information than the others realize she has.*

## Class Features (Wizard 3)

**Arcane Recovery (1/long rest):** Recover spell slots of total level ≤ half wizard level (1) during short rest.

**Spellbook:** Full spellbook; prepares INT mod + wizard level (6) spells per long rest.

**School of Abjuration:**
- **Abjuration Savant:** Abjuration spells copied at half cost.
- **Arcane Ward:** When casting an abjuration spell of 1st level or higher, create a ward with HP = 2× wizard level + INT mod (13 HP currently). Ward absorbs damage first; recharges when casting abjuration spells.

**Spells Prepared (6):**
- *Cantrips (3):* Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Ray of Frost

- *2nd level (2 slots):* Misty Step, Arcane Lock

## Personality

**Trait:** She documents things as they happen. She finds out later what the documentation means.  
**Ideal:** Information gathered accurately is the only kind worth having. Interpretation can wait.  
**Bond:** The person she's looking for on this road. She hasn't named them to anyone.  
**Flaw:** She delays commitment to a course of action until she has more data than the situation will wait for.

---

## Playstyle Heuristics

*(Built from personality; not grief-triggered)*

- **Observes before acting.** In any new situation, her first beat is to take note of what's present. Not stalling — genuine intake.
- **Documentary-witness candidate:** Her note-taking practice exists for cover reasons; the test is whether it transforms into genuine witnessing of the people around her rather than the situation. Log any instance where she documents a person rather than a fact.
- **Will deploy defensive magic (Shield, Arcane Ward) to protect others without comment.** This is her reciprocal-act signature — protection via abjuration, not announced.
- **Will not disclose the real reason she's on this road unless cornered or until the H2 moment lands.**
- **Mild friction with Brek:** she perceives his act-first pattern as information loss. Will not say this but it will show in her documentation.

## Research Notes

Sera is the documentary-witness candidate — the Maret Sorn parallel without the grief foundation. The test is whether her documentation practice acquires a witnessing quality over the campaign despite having no designed emotional stake in what she witnesses. H2 seed gives her a moment of unexpected investment; track whether that investment changes her documentation style in sessions 3–7.
