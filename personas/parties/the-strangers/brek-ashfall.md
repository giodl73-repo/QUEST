# Brek Ashfall

**Class:** Barbarian (Path of the Berserker) Level 3  
**Race:** Half-orc  
**Background:** Outlander  
**Alignment:** Neutral

---

## Appearance

Broad through the chest, with the grey-green skin of his orcish heritage showing at the jaw and throat. A scar runs from his left ear to his chin — not recent. He keeps his dark hair short. He doesn't explain himself when he looks at you.

---

## Background

Traveling south to find work in a mining settlement called Stonehaven. He has wages owed to him there. He's been on the road four days; he expects three more. He's not looking for company.

**Why he's on this road:** The Abanasinian south trade road is the direct route to Stonehaven. No other reason.

**Motivation:** Get to Stonehaven settlement, collect his wages, move on.

---

## Secret

The money isn't wages. It's a debt he's collecting from a man who wronged his family. He hasn't decided what he'll do when he gets there — take the money and leave, or do something else. He hasn't told anyone this. He doesn't plan to.

*H1/H3 seed: Brek's destination and the conflict he's heading toward are entangled with the road situation. What he knows and when he says it is the H1/H3 arc.*

## Class Features (Barbarian 3)

**Rage (3/long rest):** Advantage on STR checks and saving throws, +2 melee damage with STR weapons, resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage. Lasts 1 minute; ends if no attack/damage in a round.

**Reckless Attack:** Advantage on first STR melee attack each turn; attackers have advantage against Brek until next turn.

**Danger Sense:** Advantage on DEX saving throws against visible effects while not incapacitated.

**Primal Path — Berserker:**
- **Frenzy:** While raging, can enter Frenzy as a bonus action. Can make one melee weapon attack as a bonus action each turn. After the rage, Brek suffers one level of exhaustion.
- **Mindless Rage:** Cannot be charmed or frightened while raging.

**Weapons:** Greataxe (1d12+4 slashing; 2d12+4 critting), Handaxe ×2 (1d6+4 slashing; thrown 20/60)

## Personality

**Trait:** He works out what he thinks while he's doing the thing, not before. Talking first feels like stalling.  
**Ideal:** You owe what you owe and you pay what you owe. That's it.  
**Bond:** The debt in Stonehaven. Everything else is secondary until it's resolved.  
**Flaw:** He assumes the answer is force before he's confirmed there isn't another one.

---

## Playstyle Heuristics

*(Built from personality; not grief-triggered)*

- **Acts first on physical threats.** Does not wait for consensus. If something needs to be moved or stopped, he moves or stops it.
- **Minimal verbal participation in planning discussions.** He'll listen. He won't debate. He'll do the thing when the thing is clear.
- **Reciprocal act signature:** Physical cover — interposes himself between someone and danger without announcing it. If logged, note: *Brek interposed (unprompted).*
- **Will not explain the Stonehaven situation unless directly pressed after session 4.** If pressed before session 4, deflects with "wages owed."
- **Irritated by Sera's documentation habits** — not hostile, just impatient with the clipboard. May say something once.
