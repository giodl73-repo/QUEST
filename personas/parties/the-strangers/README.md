# The Strangers

**Party name:** The Strangers (no formal name; they haven't given themselves one)

**One-line identity:** Four people on the same road for different reasons who couldn't get past the same problem alone.

**Formation:** Pure contingency. They were at the same roadblock. That's all.

**Cohesion:** Minimal. The situation keeps requiring all four of them. That's the only cohesion designed in.

**Group dynamics:** Nobody is in charge. Decisions are made by whoever acts first. This produces disagreements. The disagreements are the campaign.

## Research Design Notes

These PCs carry no grief paragraphs, no loyalty objects, and no professional codes. This is intentional — Campaign 7 tests whether player styles emerge from situational pressure alone. Do not add designed personal-stakes content to these sheets retroactively. If a player develops depth organically in play, document it in the session log under emergent character development, not as a designed feature.
