# Orik Flintback

**Mountain Dwarf Battle Master Fighter, Level 5.** 43.

## Stat block

**AC** 18 (Chain Mail + Shield) | **HP** 52 | **Speed** 25 ft.
**STR** 18 (+4) | **DEX** 10 | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 10 | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 8

**Saves:** STR +7, CON +6
**Skills:** Athletics +7, Perception +5, History +3, Intimidation +2

**Attacks:** War Pick +7 (1d8+4) | Hand Crossbow +3 (1d6)

**Action Surge:** Once per short rest, take one additional action.

**Battle Master Maneuvers (4 superiority dice d8, 4/short rest):**
- **Menacing Attack:** Add die to damage; target must save WIS DC 15 or be frightened until end of next turn.
- **Parry:** When hit, reduce damage by die + DEX mod as reaction.
- **Commander's Strike:** Forego one attack; one ally uses reaction to make an attack.
- **Rally:** Use bonus action; ally regains die + CHA mod HP.

**Dwarven Resilience:** Advantage on saves vs. poison; resistance to poison damage.
**Stonecunning:** Double proficiency on History checks related to stonework.

## Appearance

Orik is stocky even by Dwarven standards — built like a doorframe, which is appropriate because he has spent most of his adult life standing in them. His armor is maintained meticulously; his weapons are not flashy but are maintained to the same standard. He has a forge-scar on his right forearm from an apprenticeship he left abruptly. His beard is iron-grey, plaited in a working-knot rather than a decorative one. He smells faintly of pine resin.

## Grief paragraph

**What he lost:** He was hired as hall-guard for a Dwarven metalworking hall in Thorbardin's outer holds — a legitimate commission, seven apprentices under a master smith. He left his post for two hours to buy a specific oil the master had requested from a merchant who was only in the hold that morning. He returned to smoke. The hall had caught fire from an overturned lamp. Seven apprentices died. The master survived; he does not blame Orik. The Thorbardin records list the cause as accidental fire during unsupervised work.

**The specific sensory memory:** The smell of pine resin — the oil he was sent to buy — every time he opens a new door. He still smells it for a moment on every threshold. He has never stopped buying pine resin when he doesn't need it, just to make the smell ordinary again. It has not worked.

## Background: former hall-guard, currently unaffiliated

Left Thorbardin twelve years ago. Has worked security contracts since. Is not in this campaign because he cares about the Conclave Compact; he is in it because Thessaly hired him and she seemed reliable. He has become more invested than he expected, primarily because the compact-shards keep being held by people who are protecting something they think deserves protecting, and he understands that.

## PC sheet: decision-order in play

1. **Party guard-radius first:** Wherever the party is, Orik has noted the exits, the ceiling height, the load-bearing walls. If anyone in the party is at risk, he responds before thinking about it. The party is his hall.
2. **Room topology:** He reads every new space structurally before anything else. This is not a special ability — it is what twelve years of hall-work does to a Dwarf's eyes.
3. **What did I miss?** His grief makes him check twice. He is not paranoid; he is thorough. He will circle back for what other people walk past.
4. **Non-violent resolution preferred:** Fights cost resources and create enemies. He would rather negotiate, intimidate, or wait than fight. When he fights, he fights to end it, not to demonstrate something.
5. **In doubt:** Back to the wall, count exits, count heads. Then decide.

## Interplay with the compact

The Clause of Ambition — the adventure 2 shard — will connect to Orik's grief through an indirect path: the mage's apprentice who holds the shard is ambitious in the specific way that the Thorbardin apprentices were ambitious. They worked harder than they should have, faster than was safe, because they wanted to prove themselves. Orik recognizes this. He does not want her to be punished for it. His arc in adventure 2 is whether he can prevent the cost he didn't prevent twelve years ago.
