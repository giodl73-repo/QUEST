# The Relief — Shared Session Log

*Appended by session-runner after each session. One entry per session.*

---

**C4-S1 — The Arrival at the Needle** (2026-04-22): The Relief arrived at Thorngate Keep on siege day four. Warden Asholt assigned them to the east wall without welcome. Sava found Maret Stave counting supplies she had not told Asholt about. Caelith got ten minutes with the Warden and noticed map annotations in two ink colors. The party took the open east wall positions at dusk without being asked. The night assault came in three waves; the party repelled it in three rounds, taking no damage. One Reconstitution soldier looked at the garrison insignia and dropped back over the wall without attacking. At dawn, a carved wooden disc was on the arrow-slot ledge — Tomek's watch-token, earned. Luca Bent, on watch, said: *"My brother's out there."* Party HP: full. Sava spell slots: full (Maret's token available S2). Caelith Portent [6, 7] carried forward. Supply Die: d10 heading into S2.

**C4-S2 — The Supply Run** (2026-04-22): Maret asked Sava to find the supply cart before Asholt needed to know it was missing. The party left before dawn. Talis spotted cold smoke on a ledge above the choke point and the party climbed — except Talis, who failed the climb three times and took ground-level position. On the ledge: a Reconstitution regular named Pren who had tried to stop the cart ambush. Caelith spoke with him alone; Pren gave the partial: "She came back for something that wasn't finished. Not the pass." They descended with Surprise. Two rounds, both scouts driven off. First real party damage: Davan took 5 from Scout A's longsword. Sava spent 1 spell slot on Aldric (1 HP, stable, alive two days). The cart was repaired. They came home. Maret gave Sava her token — healer to healer. Party HP: Davan 23/28; others full. Sava: 3×L1, 2×L2. Caelith Portent [6,7] still carried. Supply Die: d8. 1 potion for S3.

**C4-S3 — The Saboteur** (2026-04-22): Hessa Var asked the party to find who sabotaged the west wall arrow stock. Talis read the targeted rot (Survival 21). Caelith found Wil Fen in the postern log (Investigation 20) and recovered his coercion letter (Investigation 17). In the confrontation, Davan grappled Wil (initiative 21) and Caelith fired his Portent [7] — first deployment after 3 sessions — replacing Wil's escape attempt (natural 15→7, failed). Davan named the coercion. Wil stopped. Hessa disclosed to Asholt voluntarily, was reinstated, gave the party her token: *"I knew what I was doing and I told myself it didn't matter. That's the part I can't fix by telling him."* Davan stood in the corridor holding the burned letter. 0 party HP damage. Caelith Portent [6] carried to S4. Supply Die d6. Davan accounting speech begun.
