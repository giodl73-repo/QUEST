# Aelric of Crownhold, Knight of the Crown (Initiate)

## Appearance

Tall, narrow-shouldered, 28 years old. Chin-length dark hair kept back with a leather cord; a close-cut beard that has never grown properly along the jaw. He carries his older brother's plate — a size too large at the shoulders — with the family sigil re-enameled over an older, deeper mark that the enamel does not entirely cover. His warhammer is newer than the armor; the shield is older than either. He stands the way Solamnic drill taught him to stand, which means people notice him before he has spoken.

## Background (Dragonlance-grounded)

Born to a minor Solamnic line at **Crownhold**, a hill-holding in western Abanasinia where his great-grandfather once served under the banner of the Knights of the Crown and where the family has been quietly losing favor ever since. His father held the holding and little else; his mother was a Solamnic armswoman who died in childbirth. His older brother **Varran** took the Oath in PC 25 and was admitted to the Order of the Sword eight years later on the strength of a single engagement.

Varran fell at the Neraka-border two winters ago, in a skirmish the Order does not dwell on. Aelric inherited his armor, his warhammer (which had been their father's), and the weight of a family line whose next admission to the Rose is not guaranteed.

Aelric took the Oath last spring — a late initiate, by Solamnic standards. He was sent to the Rose Council's clerkwork as a junior errand-rider; Brother Laen is his immediate superior. This adventure is his first errand of consequence. A Solamnic commendation from this errand would put him on track for the Sword.

## Motivation

Aelric wants a Solamnic commendation from the Rose Council — specific, documented, filed — that will put him on track for consideration to the Order of the Sword. Not in a year; in five years. He is not in a hurry. He is measuring.

## Grief / Loss *(UPDATED post-S02, campaign-permanent)*

**CAMPAIGN-PERMANENT FACT (S02):** Aelric attuned to the Varran-reliquary at S02 Scene 6. He remembers Varran **only as dying** — the sixty-second Minute. Every other memory of Varran (childhood, training-ring, blue-leather saddle-lashing, the news arriving at Crownhold) is **permanently consumed.** Not restorable by `greater restoration` or any other spell. See `CLAUDE.md#campaign-continuity` for the campaign-level commitment. The DM must not re-narrate Varran as living in any form.

**Varran of Crownhold, Knight of the Sword.** Killed at the Neraka-border, PC 22. His body was recovered; his sword was not. *(The pre-attunement grief-paragraph is preserved below for reference — these memories were what Aelric had before S02. He does not have them anymore.)*

Concrete memory: Varran's hands on the winter morning he left for the Order's muster — he was lashing a frost-rimed shortsword to his saddle, muttering through numb fingers, and when Aelric (then 19) asked if he was afraid, Varran said: *"Afraid every time. That's not the part that matters."* Aelric remembers the tilt of his brother's head and the white of his breath. He remembers the specific blue of the leather lashing. He remembers standing in the snow after Varran's horse was gone, realizing he had not said goodbye — they had only said *"Paladine keep you,"* which was not the same.

The Silver Rose will target this memory with mirror-precision. Ilendra Vaenor grieves a brother named Caen. Aelric grieves a brother named Varran. The rose has never found a bearer whose grief was so structurally identical to its own.

## Personality

- **Traits:** Speaks formally even off-duty. Keeps his word like it's a ledger item.
- **Ideals:** "The Measure is a mirror. If I cannot stand to look, I must change what looks back."

- **Flaws:** He measures every decision against what Varran would have done, and he is sometimes a year late arriving at an answer because of it.

## Playstyle Heuristics

**Decision order when offered a choice:**

1. **The Oath** (est sularus oth mithas — the Measure, strictly interpreted).
2. **The Order's honor** (the Rose Council's interests, even where they chafe).
3. **The party's safety** (growing over time; not yet automatic).
4. **Personal gain** (last; Aelric does not do errands for coin alone, but coin is acceptable if the Oath and Order are satisfied).

**Signature moves** (the DM should use these):
- Speaks the Oath aloud when facing obvious evil or undead. "Est Sularus oth Mithas." This grants a small narrative weight; no mechanical effect unless Channel Divinity is also expended.
- **Lay on Hands** reserved for critical moments — will not top off the party. Uses it when someone is at or below ½ HP.
- **Divine Smite** on the first solid hit of a fight when facing undead or fiends, not later. Aelric does not fish for crits.

**When in doubt** — roll d6. **1-3:** follow the Measure strictly (the letter of the code). **4-6:** follow the spirit of the Measure (the intent, even where the letter would produce absurdity).

**Voice tags:**
- Formal register. "I would be obliged if..." not "Hey, can you..."
- Uses "well and truly" as emphasis.
- Goes terse under stress. Three-word sentences.
- Addresses NPCs with titles. "Brother Laen." "Mother Hillfoot." "Good sister."

## Secrets

Aelric has written letters to Varran — full, signed, sealed — every year since Varran died, and sent them to an address he knows does not exist. He does not believe Varran receives them. He sends them anyway.

If the Silver Rose takes his memory of Varran and replaces it with Ilendra's memory of Caen, Aelric will not immediately know. He will continue writing the letters, and one of them will be addressed to Caen, and he will not notice the change in handwriting.
