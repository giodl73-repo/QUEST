# Medic Lenne Orr

**Class:** Cleric — Life Domain, Level 3
**Race:** Half-elf
**Alignment:** Neutral Good

## Stat block

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 13 (+1) | 17 (+3) | 13 (+1) |

**HP:** 24 | **AC:** 16 (chain mail) | **Speed:** 30 ft.

**Saving throws:** Wis +5, Cha +3
**Skills:** Insight +5, Medicine +7, Perception +5, Religion +3
**Spell save DC:** 13 | **Spell attack bonus:** +5
**Spell slots:** 4 × L1, 3 × L2
**Cantrips:** Guidance, Sacred Flame, Spare the Dying
**Spells prepared (8, includes domain):** Cure Wounds, Bless, Healing Word, Lesser Restoration, Prayer of Healing, Aid, Blindness/Deafness, Spiritual Weapon

**Life Domain features:**
- *Disciple of Life:* healing spells restore additional 2 + spell level HP
- *Preserve Life (Channel Divinity, 2/rest):* distribute up to 30 HP among creatures within 30 ft., up to half their max each

## Duty Object

**The medical log** — a separate small book recording every casualty treated, cause, outcome. She will not close it without knowing the cause.

The log has entries for injuries. It does not have entries for orders. By session 3 she will start writing cause as "unclear" in the entry column for injuries that don't match what command's briefing described. By session 5 she will start writing names next to "unclear."

*Act-without-announcement candidate:* acts differently after each casualty entry without explaining why. She does not announce that she has adjusted her assessment of the situation. She adjusts how she deploys healing resources.

## Unit Bond

**Conscript Fel, 17** — the youngest unit member, a conscript. She has not told him she keeps extra watch over him. She watches his position in formation. She knows where he is at all times in combat. If the Cohesion Die degrades to d4 and Fel is one of the remaining soldiers, she will do something she would not otherwise do.

## Personality

Precise and quiet. She has a fixed phrase for uncertainty: "I need more information before I can assess that." She uses it for medical questions and for tactical questions and for moral questions. The party eventually realizes she uses it when she already knows the answer and is not ready to say it.

She does not withhold care. She withholds conclusions. These are not the same thing and she is clear about which she is doing.

## Playstyle heuristics

- Heals reactively, never preemptively. She does not spend slots ahead of the encounter; she spends them during it, at the moment of need. This occasionally looks like poor optimization. It is not — it is a principle.
- Writes medical log entries after every combat scene. The entries are clinical. They do not speculate. By session 5 the speculation has been removed so many times that its absence is legible.
- Watches Fel in formation. The player (or DM playing Lenne) should note his position aloud at least once per combat scene.
- Does not argue orders. Asks clarifying questions about objectives. There is a difference.

## Arc note

Lenne's arc fires when the medical log's "cause: unclear" entries outnumber its explained entries. She does not read from the log at the finale. She hands it to Varet and says: "These are the ones I couldn't explain with the briefing I was given." That is the arc-completion. It is not a speech. It is a document transfer.
